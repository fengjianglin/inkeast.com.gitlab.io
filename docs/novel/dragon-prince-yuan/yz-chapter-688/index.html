The blood asura light that shrouded Wu Huang’s emitting a hissing and roaring noise, and a strong bloody scent had filled the world.

From the countless horrified gazes, it was clear that the power of the Genesis Qi that burst forth from Wu Huang’s body was so strong that it makes their heart pound with fear.    

“Wu Huang is really fierce. If we were to measure his Genesis Qi in terms of Genesis Qi stars then it’s most likely over 250,000, right?”   

“Is the strength of 250,000 Genesis Qi stars still considered the Alpha Origin stage? If he was any stronger, he can compete against experts of the Divine Dwelling stage.”    

“He’s a monster.”  

Outside the city, Chu Qing, Li Qingchan and other people's faces had become somewhat strange. Looking at the soul of the blood asura around Wu Huang’s body, even if they were a distance away, they could still feel a strong oppressive feeling.   

“Wu Huang is that strong?” Li Qingchan knitted her brows in a frown. Previously, Zhou Yuan’s 180,000 Genesis Qi stars, in her view, were already able to destroy all experts of the Alpha Origin stage. She did not expect Wu Huang to possess even more terrifying strength!

Chu Qing also wore a solemn expression. 

“This is the strength of the blood asura body, which you guys simply can’t imagine.” Jiang Taishen stated indifferently. 

The expression in his eyes was somewhat complex. That day, when Wu Huang summoned the soul of blood asura during their battle, he already knew that he did not have the slightest chance of winning.    

The distance between the two was too great. 

Chu Qing glanced at him without saying a word. The fact that the arrogant Jiang Taishen said something like that was enough to show Wu Huang’s strength. It seemed that it would be really difficult for Zhou Yuan to win the battle.   

Boom!    

While countless people were dumbstruck by the appearance of the soul of blood asura, Wu Huang did not say anything more but fixed a cold stare at Zhou Yuan. He stepped forwards, and his figure vanished instantly. 

And when his figure disappeared, Genesis Qi erupted from Zhou Yuan's body as he rapidly retreated.

Swoosh!  

However, Wu Huang’s speed was frighteningly fast. He reappeared in front of Zhou Yuan like a shadow with a sneer on his face and thrust out his blood crystal spear.   

On the blood crystal spear, the sticky blood qi had condensed into liquid, dripping constantly.

As the spear whizzed out, the sound of an explosion caused by the spear, shook the air. 

The golden Genesis Qi in Zhou Yuan’s body was surging out without any reservation, and the Heavenly Yuan brush in his hand erupted in a blaze of golden light and met the spear head on.   

Clang!    

The spear and brush collided once again.

Shock waves visible to the naked eye swept out with the two people as the centre, causing the entire ground to collapse.   

Zhou Yuan's body violently trembled, and was directly blasted out by the tremendous force. He stabbed the Heavenly Yuan brush into the ground, tearing long marks across the ground to stabilise himself. 

With this attack, Wu Huang had revealed the terror of the blood asura soul, and had almost completely suppressed Zhou Yuan. 

“Haha, Zhou Yuan, is that all you have?” 

“If you're just able to do that, then what are you returning to the Great Zhou for? To die?”

Wu Huang roared with laughter. His attacks did not diminish, but endlessly struck Zhou Yuan like bolts of thunder. 

Clang!  

Facing Wu Huang's storm-like offensive, Zhou Yuan constantly retreated. If it weren’t for the Heavenly Yuan brush providing an impenetrable defense, Wu Huang’s blood crystal spear most likely would have pierced through.  

Watching this scene, countless people secretly shook their heads. Wu Huang had undoubtedly gained the upper hand. If Zhou Yuan had no other trump card, then the outcome of the battle would soon be decided. 

Swoosh!   

Wu Huang’s figure was like a ghost, skimming across the sky. The blood crystal spear suddenly vibrated, forming a blood red crescent moon on the tip of the spear.    

“Blood Moon!”    

Buzz!   

As the blood moon shot out, even the air itself was torn apart.

Wu Huang’s attacks were more and more ferocious, and clearly intended to utterly defeat Zhou Yuan. 

Watching the blood moon rapidly enlarging before him, Zhou Yuan’s eyes also turned fierce. He suddenly released the Heavenly Yuan brush and clenched his fist.   

Swoosh!    

The blood moon came in the blink of an eye.   

Just as the blood moon was about to strike, Zhou Yuan, under countless exclamations of shock, slammed out his fist.    

Countless people were dumbstruck by this sight. Zhou Yuan was only able to withstand Wu Huang's violent offensive because of the strength of the Heavenly Yuan brush. But he had let go of the brush and intended to fight with his fist! He wanted to die?! 

 Boom!    

Before the exclamations of shock had faded, Zhou Yuan's fist and the blood moon had already collided.   

Rumble!    

Violent Genesis Qi raged out. 

However, what made countless people’s eye shrink was that the scene of Zhou Yuan’s arm being cut off did not appear. Instead the blood moon was directly blasted apart by the punch. 

And this time, Zhou Yuan's figure only quivered ever so slightly. 

In mid-air, Wu Huang’s pupils shrank a little. 

The blood light gradually dissipated, revealing Zhou Yuan's figure. All eyes were gathered on his fist as it was there where silver liquid was gushing out from his body.

The silver liquid first wrapped around his fist, and finally spread all over Zhou Yuan’s body.

In just a few seconds, the silver liquid had turned into a streamlined silver armour. 

Wu Huang looked coldly at the familiar silver armour, which was the key point in his defeat against Zhou Yuan two years ago. 

On the ground, shrouded in silver armour, Zhou Yuan was kneeling on one knee and gently touching the ground with the palm of his hand.

Earth Saint Rune!    

The ground quaked again, and in the depths of the ground were dense and majestic Genesis Qi of the earth whizzing out like a torrent, pouring into Zhou Yuan’s body. 

Boom!    

The infusion this time was much more majestic than before!

There were strict requirements that his body needed to meet during the infusion of Earth Genesis Qi. When Zhou Yuan summoned the silver shadow, which merged with his body, it had undoubtedly enhanced the ability of his body to withstand.

If previously Zhou Yuan had held back slightly when he operated the Earth Saint Rune, this time he operated the Earth Saint Rune to its limit this time.   

Rumble!   

Rumbling thunder noises were constantly being emitted from Zhou Yuan’s body, and a mysterious faint yellow light was faintly visible around his body, and finally formed ancient yellow light patterns on the silver armour.   

It was also at this moment that countless people inside and outside Distant Saint City were shocked to find that the Genesis Qi being emitted from Zhou Yuan’s body was rising at a terrifying speed.   

That degree had overtaken Wu Huang once again! 

The ground, unable to bear any longer, began to crack constantly.

In the sky, Wu Huang's face was icy cold and the corners of his eyes were slightly twitching.

Under countless gazes of shock, Zhou Yuan, clad in silver armour, slowly lifted his head and fixed his piercing eyes on Wu Huang in the air. He clenched his hand and the Heavenly Yuan Brush fell into his hand. The sharp tip of the brush slowly tilted up, and finally pointed to Wu Huang in mid air.

“Bring out all your trump cards.” 

“Otherwise you won’t have the chance to escape from Distant Saint City with your spirit like last time.”
