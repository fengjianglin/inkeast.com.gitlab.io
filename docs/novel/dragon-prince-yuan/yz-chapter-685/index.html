The centre of the Distant Saint City.  

When the two figures were facing each other, countless people from all over the city darted their eyes over. Even in the sky, the air was trembling slightly. It was unknown how many powerful experts used their perception to break through the air from distant places in order to take in the battle as much as possible.

The battle between Zhou Yuan and Wu Huang could be said to have become the focus of the Shengzhou Continent.   

The two were the top experts of the young generation of the Cangxuan Heaven. Wu Huang had defeated Jiang Taishen, and was ranked first on the Chosens List. 

Zhou Yuan also wasn’t someone easy to deal with. He had completed the feat that nobody in the Cangxuan Sect had ever managed to do. He initiated the Chosen Competition Battle, and gathered all seven arts of the sect. His achievement was equally dazzling.    

The two strongest young people of the Cangxuan Heaven were about to face each other. Who would have the last laugh in the end?   

Countless people were curious about this result. 

Of course, if it were purely in terms of the strength of the two people, it wouldn’t possibly attract the attention of so many powerful forces, but everyone knew that this battle not only represented the two youngsters, but also the two great sects behind them.   

Everyone knew that even Sect Master Qing Yang and the Palace Master of the Sacred Palace were paying attention to this battle. 

“Thieves?”  

“Zhou Yuan, so many years have passed, but why are you still so naive?” Standing on the stone pillar, Wu Huang had a playful expression on his face as he studied Zhou Yuan.

“Back then the Qi of the Sacred Dragon had split into three, one part is with me, one in Wu Yao and the last in the Great Wu.”

“If you want to live as the Lord of the Sacred Dragon, wait until the day you assemble the Qi of the Sacred Dragon. Otherwise, as I had said in the past, you are just a container for the Sacred Dragon Qi.”

Zhou Yuan’s eyes drooped, “I naturally will get back what belongs to me, just as two years ago I took back half of the Sacred Dragon Qi from you.”  

Wu Huang narrowed his eyes, and the surrounding temperature plummeted.

“Don’t worry, what I lost last time, I'll get it back this time.”

“I'm afraid that not only won’t you get it back, but you will also lose your life here. Wu Huang, last time I carelessly allowed you to escape with your spirit, but this time, you won’t be as lucky.” Zhou Yuan smiled coldly.

“Nonsense!”   

The eyes of Wu Huang became sinister. “I would like to see what qualifications you have to say this?”  

Boom!  

When his voice faded, the blood-red Genesis Qi, like a torrent, erupted from his body. A rich stench of blood flooded the world, and sent the air shaking.    

Hiss!    

Wu Huang abruptly shook his hand, sending blood-red Genesis Qi tumbling in the air and directly transformed into a giant blood-red python. It whizzed across with a chilling fiendish aura, aiming for Zhou Yuan with lightning speed.

He already revealed an extremely astonishing Genesis Qi foundation with his first attack. The level was most likely not any weaker than Zhou Yuan's 180,000 Genesis Qi stars! 

Zhou Yuan studied the blood python roaring over and he could sense the strength of the Genesis Qi contained in it. He slightly narrowed his eyes and sneered, “It seems that the Palace Master of the Sacred Palace is really good to you.”

Wu Huang’s original body was destroyed, and his cultivation in the past two years should have been slow, but his attack was surprisingly strong. It could be imagined that the Palace Master had spent a lot effort on him in the past two years.   

“Your strength is just donated by someone else. While I, in the two years you were hiding in the Sacred Palace, relied on myself to reach this level!”

Buzz!    

Golden Genesis Qi rushed into the sky from Zhou Yuan’s body. As the Genesis Qi was roaring it gradually transformed into a giant golden python with sharp claws and emitted a terrifying imposing manner.    

Rumble!  

The golden giant python shot out, directly meeting the blood python head on. Waves of violent Genesis Qi raged and instantly destroyed the surrounding buildings.  

The shock waves wreaked havoc, but Zhou Yuan and Wu Huang did not budge an inch, even the corners of their clothes weren’t lifted in the slightest.

Wu Huang’s face was devoid of expression as he said indifferently, “I only know that the winner is the king, who would care how I succeeded? After I kill you, I will destroy your Great Zhou so the people there can keep you company.”   

They were both evidently trying to mess up each other's mood with words, thereby infuriating each other and waiting for an opportunity to find a flaw.    

Zhou Yuan shook his head with a smile on his face. There wasn’t the slightest warmth in that smile. Given his years of training, Wu Huang's words did not provoke him, but only intensified his killing intent.   

Dong!   

He suddenly stamped his feet.    

Golden Genesis Qi soared into the sky, and the next moment, the golden light gathered together and turned into thousands of golden torrents that whizzed down, like meteorites, bombarding the place where Wu Huang stood.

Within each golden torrent was incredibly strong Genesis Qi. In the face of such an offensive, ordinary Chosens most likely couldn’t cope and would be destroyed instantly.  

Countless people in Distant Saint City were all moved upon seeing this scene. There were very few people of the Alpha Origin stage who could display such a powerful offensive.

Looking at the flood of mighty golden genesis qi overhead, the corners of Wu Huang’s lips curved in a cold smile. “Your attack would most likely be invincible against the useless Chosens of the Cangxuan Sect, but in front of me, it has no effect.” 

His lifted both palms and the blood-red Genesis Qi surged. Spheres of blood-red light formed densely around his body, and within those sphere there was equally violent Genesis Qi.    

Swoosh!    

Wu Huang flung his sleeve and the blood-red balls whizzed out, colliding with the golden torrents.

Boom!   

At the same time of a loud noise resounding across the sky, like fireworks, a storm of Genesis Qi was constantly raging.

The Genesis Qi of both sides continued to clash, and was then annihilated.

This was purely a competition of who had a stronger Genesis Qi foundation! 

Inside and outside Distant Saint City, many people of the Alpha Origin Stage felt their scalp going numb. If it were them, they most likely would have exhausted their Genesis Qi long ago, but Zhou Yuan and Wu Huang both showed no signs of their strength weakening. 

Are these two men really that strong? 

Boom!    

A beam of golden Genesis Qi was intercepted by a blood red ball one foot away from Wu Huang. Violent Genesis Qi collided and rays of dazzling light exploded in front of Wu Huang.

Wu Huang narrowed his eyes, but at the next moment-----

Whoosh!   

Within the bright light, a ghost-like figure sped out and directly appeared in front. It was Zhou Yuan.

Zhou Yuan’s fists were clenched, and his body was glowing like translucent jade. Then, a ray of silver light flashed across his bones. 

A berserk beast-like power rushed through his blood and flesh.

Mythic Saint Body, mythic-jade skin, star-silver bone! 

Boom!   

Zhou Yuan expressionlessly slammed his fist out, causing the air to tremble.

That punch was seemingly simple, but the terrifying power caused the air to shake, and was far more powerful than the flood of Genesis Qi before. 

The previous Genesis Qi attack was only to distract Wu Huang.  

As the fist roared out, a deafening sonic boom echoed throughout. 

The astonishing fist light rapidly enlarged before Wu Huang's eyes. At that distance, it was too late to even activate his Genesis Qi. 

However, at this moment, Wu Huang did not reveal a trace of panic in his eyes, and instead, the corners of his mouth curved in a sneer.    

“Zhou Yuan, I have long heard that you made some progress with your bodily strength, how could I not increase my guard against you?”

“But do you really think I am the same Wu Huang as before?”  

Hiss! 

The moment his voice faded, his skin turned blood red at an astonishing speed, wisps of blood red mist rose from his pores, and a gush of violent aura erupted from his body.

A sinister smile came to his face. He clenched his fist, and under countless gazes, his punch met with Zhou Yuan’s fierce fist light.

“Boom!”  

Violent shock waves swept out, and at the same time, the shrill cry of Wu Huang could be heard.  

“Thanks to you my body was destroyed, and now I will let you try the strength of my new body!”

“Blood Asura body!”
