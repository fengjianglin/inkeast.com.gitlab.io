Dong! 

On the Broken Dragon River, the beating of war drums echoed throughout heaven and the earth, setting off waves on the vast river.

The great army of the Great Wu also began to move. The giant ship raised its sails, and, like a giant beast, cut through the waves. 

Murderous spirit pervaded the world.

And the movements of the Great Wu were immediately detected by the Great Zhou in the Broken Dragon City. The rapid beating of war drums also sounded on the city walls, and countless soldiers tightened their grip on their bows and spears. 

Gushes of Genesis Qi, both strong and weak, like flames, bloomed above the city walls. 

In the main building of the city walls, Zhou Qing also looked solemnly at the movements of the Great Wu in the distance. It was clear that the Great Wu was about to launch the final attack. 

“Prepare for battle!”    

He raised his palm, and his voice echoed across the city walls.

Wei Canglan tightly clasped a long spear. Genesis Qi surging out all over his body as he roared: “Fight to the end!”  

“Fight to the end!”   

Countless soldiers roared. 

On that side, Black Venom King’s face had become utterly pale. Watching the Great Wu army get into battle formation in the distance, he felt his scalp going numb. He murmured, “It’s over, it’s over.” 

“It’s all that kid Zhou Yuan’s fault, I’m going to get killed because of him.” 

Wei Canglang shot a glance at him, but was lazy to pay any attention to him. Next to him, Wei Qingqing bit her red lips and whispered, “Father, do we have a chance of victory?” 

Wei Canglang deeply looked at her and said, “If my Great Zhou is defeated in the end, I will find an opportunity for you to flee. Remember, your little brother is at home, so you have to live to protect him!” 

Wei Qingqing’s heart sank. For Wei Canglan to say this, he obviously felt that Great Zhou had no chance of winning.

Her eyes reddened as she reassured, “Father, we may not lose. Wasn’t there news that His Highness Zhou Yuan will come back? He is now a Chosen of the Cangxuan Sect!” 

Black Venom King interjected, “What's the use of him coming back? So what if there’s another person of the Alpha Origin Stage? Is he coming here to be killed?”

His words were filled with resentment. 

Wei Canglan glared at him, and then said to Wei Qingqing, “Silly girl, if His Highness don’t return, given his talent, he will be able to slaughter King Wu and avenge the Great Zhou in the future.” 

Wei Qingqing bit her red lips, feeling a little despair. At that time, even if King Wu was killed, it would be difficult to rebuild the Great Zhou. 

Dong!   

The sound of war drums continued to reverberate. 

Multiple huge ships rapidly cut across the river, gradually nearing Broken Dragon City with an earth shaking momentum.

And as Great Wu’s endless army gradually approached, numerous figure rose into the sky, emitting powerful Genesis Qi. 

They were all Alpha Origin experts of the Great Wu Empire

At that moment, the Great Wu experts all had their arms crossed, playfully looking at an important city in the distance. From that look, it was obvious that they did not attach any importance to the Great Zhou. 

In Broken Dragon City, the Alpha Origin stage experts of the Great Zhou also erupted with numerous streams of powerful Genesis Qi in response, but their faces were particularly grave. It was evident that they felt the oppression brought about by the Great Wu. 

A ship began to slow down, stopped, and then everyone saw a golden giant battleship, coming slowly from behind. Where it passed, other battleships immediately gave way. 

All eyes were gathered on the golden battleship.

Immediately after, they saw that on a throne on the golden ship sat a figure with his eyes closed.

That figure, clad in a golden Emperor’s robe, wore an indifferent expression. As the Emperor’s robe fluttered in the wind, a majestic aura filled the air

When this figure appeared, the sound of the war drums gradually subsided, and even the turbulent waves also quietly calm down under that pressure. 

The world fell quiet. 

Countless gazes were fixed on that figure. 

On the city walls, Zhou Qing's hands were firmly clenched, veins throbbing. The biting cold killing intent in his eyes seemed to be piercing through the throne. 

That figure was too unforgettable for him. 

When Great Zhou was at its peak and respected by the entire continent, it was precisely because of the rebel in front of him that caused the collapse of the Great Zhou. In the end, they were trapped in such a remote corner. 

Moreover, the thief not only took the Great Zhou’s Empire, but even plotted to seize Zhou Yuan's Sacred Dragon Qi, causing him to suffer constantly. Adding to this, Qin Yu, in order to protect her child, repeated transferred blood to him, which led to her life span reduced drastically. 

The person in front of them could be said to the biggest culprit in the destruction of their family and Empire!

“Wu Xuan!”   

Zhou Qing stared intently at the figure, his bone-chilling voice came through his clenched teeth.

The figure on the throne was the King of Great Wu, King Wu Xuan!

Under countless gazes, King Wu slowly opened his slightly closed eyes. It was also at that moment that an astonishing Genesis Qi pressure directly erupted from his body. 

The golden ship below his feet made creaking noises.

And behind him, the countless soldiers of the Great Wu were knelt down respectfully, as if they couldn’t withstand that kind of Emperor power. 

“Zhou Qing, it's been a long time.”

King Wu lifted his head, eyes fixed on Zhou Qing from a distance, and his faint laughter resounded like thunder. 

“A thief of the Empire!” Zhou Qing's eyes were filled with intense hatred. 

King Wu chuckled, “An incompetent man like you wants to occupy the throne? Zhou Qing, the reason the Great Zhou is in its current state is because of your incompetence.” 

Zhou Qing’s eyes were red with rage.    

 “Wu Xuan, you made the ancestral oath that the Great Wu wouldn’t step foot into the Great Zhou within one hundred years. Do you want to violate the ancestral oath? Do you want the world to laugh at you?” Wei Canglan roared out. 

King Wu narrowed his eyes. “If your Great Zhou had acted honestly like a small country, I would have ignored you pitiful creatures, but you, Zhou Qing, you still have other thoughts, so I have no choice but to eliminate the roots.”

“The Ancestral Oath—”   

His lips twisted in a sneer, “Do you really think I have limits? In fact, my most regretful thing is that back then, after gaining the Qi of the Sacred Dragon, I was too excited and forgot about you all. If I had gotten rid of you all then, how would there be such trouble today?” 

Wei Canglan hissed through clenched teeth, “You violated the ancestral oath, there will be retribution!” 

King Wu waved his hand dismissively, “The anger of the weak is especially ridiculous.” 

He looked condescendingly at Zhou Qing and said, “Zhou Qing, don’t talk nonsense. You should know the purpose of my Great Wu’s attack.” 

“If you take your life in front of the two armies, then my Great Wu can peacefully take over the Great Zhou.” 

His narrowed eyes were filled with overwhelming killing intent.

“But if you stubbornly refuse, then my Great Wu will have no choice but to dye the river and mountains of the Great Zhou with blood.”
