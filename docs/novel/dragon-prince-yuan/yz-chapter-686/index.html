Clang!

When Zhou Yuan and Wu Huang collided with each other, there was a loud clang of metal striking one another, and forces that were visible to the naked eye rippled out. 

The huge stone pillar underfoot broke off inch by inch, and eventually shattered into powder.

That solid ground was also lifted up layer by layer and swept out like waves.

And the two figures also quivered very slightly, and then shot back and left long marks across the ground with their feet before they could steady their bodies.

Zhou Yuan’s face stiffened slightly as he stared at Wu Huang in the distance, whose body was blood red all over, as though his skin had been removed.    

Moreover, blood-coloured qi had wrapped around Wu Huang’s body, giving off a fiendish aura.  

During the previous collision, Zhou Yuan could clearly feel that Wu Huang's bodily strength was unexpectedly powerful. 

The power of the mythic-jade skin and star-silver bone that he had activated was completely engulfed by Wu Huang’s blood red body.

“The Body of Blood Asura.” Zhou Yuan’s eyes flashed, thinking it must be the masterpiece of palace master Sheng Yuan.

Zhou Yuan could sense that Wu Huang did not specialise in the practice of bodily strength like him, but his Blood Asura Body was in no way inferior to his Mythic Saint Body Art.  

It was no surprise that Wu Huang could beat Jiang Taishen, who was listed first on the Chosen List. His strength was indeed extremely terrifying.

When the fight between Zhou Yuan and Wu Huang began, the chaotic battle outside Distant Saint City had unknowingly weakened. Numerous figures hovered in the sky, intently watching the center of the city.    

Both Chu Qing and Jiang Taishen knew very well that, in today's battle, the disciples of the two sects were just the side characters, including them.    

And the real protagonists were the two people in the centre of the city. 

So when the two collided, their battle was no longer important. Both sides unanimously slowed down their offensive and fixed their eyes on the city. 

“It seems that Zhou Yuan has already cultivated a true Mythic Saint Body.” Jiang Taishen turned to Chu Qing not far away and smiled, “But Wu Huang has the Body of Blood Asura, which is in no way inferior to Zhou Yuan’s.”  

Chu Qing narrowed his eyes and laughed, “junior brother Zhou Yuan just doesn’t have the Mythic Saint Body.”

Jiang Taishen remained indifferent as he peered down at Wu Huang's body, silent for a while, and shook his head. 

“It's no use, you don't know how strong the Blood Asura Body is.”

“Your Cangxuan Sect is bound to lose this time.”  

In the other direction of the city wall, Zuoqiu Qingyu, Luluo, Li Chunjun and other people were also quietly watching the centre of the city with a solemn expression on their faces.    

“That Wu Huang is so strong now.” Li Chunjun’s eyes that were wrapped in black cloth shifted in the direction of Wu Huang. Although he was unable to see, he had an extremely strong perception ability. To him, Wu Huang was like a blood asura creeping out from the ninth underworld. The bloodthirsty aura he emitted was enough to make one’s scalp tingle.

“Can Zhou Yuan win?” Luluo asked worriedly.    

No one answered. Even Zuoqiu Qingyu bit her lips. The two men could be said to be the strongest two of the Alpha Origin stage in the Shengzhou Continent.

Nobody could predict who would win.

“Zhou Yuan, you can't live past today, give up now.” Wu Huang’s eyes were filled with blood as he grinned at Zhou Yuan.

Zhou Yuan’s face was devoid of expression. He clenched his hand and the Heavenly Yuan Brush emerged with a flash of light. The tip of the brush made of snow-white hairs pointed diagonally to the ground. 

Buzz!    

Golden Genesis Qi frantically swirled around the surface of Zhou Yuan’s body, and finally gradually turned into a mysterious light that enveloped Zhou Yuan’s body and then stretched out its wings.

Genesis Qi of the universe was gushing out and engulfed by the mysterious light. 

Omega Saint Spirit Art! 

Zhou Yuan's imposing aura was dramatically strengthened! 

Evidently, after the previous test, Zhou Yuan had understood that Wu Huang was indeed no longer the same as before. If he wanted to win today he must fight with all his strength. 

Bang! 

Genesis Qi rushed forth while Zhou Yuan’s eyes suddenly turned icy cold. As his body turned into a shadow and shot out, the solid ground instantly collapsed.  

His speed was incredibly fast, and coupled with the wings of the Omega Saint Spirit, his strength had doubled. While countless people just felt everything blurred in front of them, Zhou Yuan reappeared next to Wu Huang. 

Boom!   

The snowy white brush was thrusted down.

Wu Huang stared at the tip with a chilling smile on his face. He clenched his hand and a blood red crystal spear formed in his hand.

Around the body of the spear, there was blood qi swirling around, as though it had been soaked in a pool of blood for many years. 

Clang!    

The blood crystal spear turned into a beam of blood light to meet the tip of the snowy white brush.

Where the collision was, the space shook and ripples of shock waves rapidly spread out.

The Heavenly Yuan Brush and blood crystal spear both quivered, but the next moment, the brush and blood spear both trembled violently, transforming into thousands of shadows.

Clang!   

The brush and spear collided over thousands of times in less than ten seconds, and every time they clashed, it sent the air trembling. 

The figures of Zhou Yuan and Wu Huang also flashed continuously, and the spacious streets where they passed were instantly destroyed. 

Countless gazes stared unblinkingly at the city.

Everybody could feel the ruthlessness of the battle between the two, and every attack was directed at a vital area. Although they both possessed incredible bodily strength, once either of them were struck by Zhou Yuan's Heavenly Yuan Brush or Wu Huang's blood crystal spear, which were both mighty Genesis Weapons, they would still suffer severe injuries. 

Boom!   

Violent Genesis Qi wreaked havoc across the city.    

The blood crystal spear in Wu Huang's hand blocked a sharp brush shadow that was whizzing over. There were seemingly sticky blood qi all over his body like a snake coiling around him. 

“Zhou Yuan, I told you that you can't get past me!”

“Haha, I won’t kill you, but I will stop you so that you can’t go save the Great Zhou. I want you to watch your Great Zhou be destroyed by my Great Wu Empire!” Wu Huang’s figure flew out, his eyes were blood red, and his smile sinister.    

Zhou Yuan was also blasted away by the force. His eyes flashed with strong killing intent. He suddenly bent over and clutched onto the ground. 

An ancient talisman emerged from the palm that touched the ground. 

Earth Saint Rune!    

Invisible ripples broke out with Zhou Yuan’s body as the centre. The ground quaked violently and cracked. Majestic and boundless Genesis Qi were frantically rushing into Zhou Yuan’s body. 

Given that Zhou Yuan had cultivated the Mythic Saint Body Art, his bodily strength had grown stronger once again and the Genesis Qi that his body could withstand had also dramatically increased.

Boom!    

With the stamp of his feet, Zhou Yuan caused the ground within thousands of feet to collapse, and his figure once again sped towards Wu Huang.

“Still won’t give up?” Wu Huang sneered. 

Boom!  

Zhou Yuan did not answer, but tightened his grip around the Heavenly Yuan Brush, and forcefully swung down. 

“Million whale!”   

Following his deep voice, the Genesis Qi within his body and the Earth Genesis Qi came roaring out. The two gathered and directly caused the space to vibrate.    

Roar!   

As the brush swung down, an ancient and resounding whale cry could be heard echoing across the universe, and dozens of ancient whale shadows appeared around the brush! 

Every ancient whale shadow contained astonishing power.   

This was the first time that Zhou Yuan was able to increase the number of ancient whale shadows to this level ever since he activated the Million Whale Rune! 

The power of this brush could destroy even a mountain.

Wu Huang was also aware of the terrifying power that broke out under Zhou Yuan’s brush. His pupil shrank, but the Heavenly Yuan Brush was already rapidly aiming towards him along with numerous ancient whale shadows. 

He could only let out a loud roar. Blood red qi rose and the blood crystal spear erupted in a blaze of blood-red light.

Boom!

Under countless gazes, the Heavenly Yuan Brush struck the blood crystal spear heavily.

Destructive power broke out from the brush, and blood mist directly shot out from the pores on Wu Huang’s arms.

The blood crystal spear also emitted a tragic cry.

Rumble!    

In the face of Zhou Yuan’s terrifying attacks, Wu Huang could only withstand for a few moments before his body shot out like a cannonball, destroying several buildings along the way. 

Boom!   

A road of rubble and waste continued to appear. In the end, Wu Huang’s figure was deeply buried amongst the rubble.

 Distant Saint City instantly fell silent at this moment.

Countless people were dumbstruck at the sight. Zhou Yuan and Wu Huang seemed to be evenly matched moments ago. Who would have thought that Zhou Yuan would explode with such terrifying power and forcibly suppressed Wu Huang.   

Under the countless astonished gazes, Zhou Yuan clasped the Heavenly Yuan Brush tight with both hands. There was blood flowing down his hands, which was caused by impact of the terrifying power before.   

But he did not seem to care. He lifted his head, peering at the ruins and deep marks left in front of him with a chilling look in his eyes.    

“You want to stop me---”    

“With your life!?”
