The next day, outside the main hall of the Cangxuan Sect.

Sect Master Qing Yang was standing together with several peak masters. Their eyes were fixed ahead, where a slender young figure was releasing a sharp and powerful energy.    

Once the Mythic Saint Body was completed, even if Zhou Yuan didn’t activate his Genesis Qi, his body was still exuding an indescribable ferocious aura, like a beast.

Today was the day that Zhou Yuan would leave the Cangxuan Sect and head to the Cangmang Continent.

So at this moment he was like a world-class sword. He would soon be unsheathed and show off his ability.

“Zhou Yuan, according to the information we received, the disciples of the Sacred Palace had already came out of their nests in full strength a few days ago. They are lying in ambush on the path of returning to the Cangmang Continent, with the intention of intercepting you.”

Sect Master Qingyang reminded, “Your journey back will be filled with danger.” 

Faced with the attack from the disciple of an entire sect, even if Zhou Yuan was considered invincible in the Alpha Origin stage, it would still be difficult for him to match their strength. 

But fortunately, Zhou Yuan wasn’t alone. 

Behind him, Chu Qing, Kong Sheng, Li Qingchan and other Chosens were standing tall, and further behind him were the disciples of various peaks of the Cangxuan Sect, standing closely and solemnly together. 

“Zhou Yuan, just focus on moving forward on your journey. The disciples of the Cangxuan Sect will help you withstand the interception of the Sacred Palace.” Chu Qing grinned, his shiny head unusually eye-catching.

“Senior brother Zhou Yuan, the disciples of the Saint Genesis Peak will do their best to protect you!” In front of the disciples of the Saint Genesis Peak stood a girl called Mu Xiaoman. Holding a spear in one hand, she stared at Zhou Yuan with her huge bright eyes.    

Zhou Yuan turned his head to look at the countless Cangxuan disciples, who, at this moment, were all looking at him with blazing eyes. This scene brought a current of warmth flowing to his heart.    

How would he not know that there would be many casualties amongst the disciples in the fight against the disciples of the Sacred Palace? But these disciples did not have any complaints at all.   

“Zhou Yuan, this isn’t just your fight, but a fight between my Cangxuan Sect and the Sacred Palace. As disciples of the Cangxuan Sect, we will naturally fight with all our strength.” Li Qingchan said, her red lips moving slightly.    

Zhou Yuan nodded, but did not say anything pretentious. He just pressed his lips and solemnly bowed to the many disciples.  

“Zhou Yuan, go ahead.” Sect Master Qing Yang urged. 

Indeed, as Li Qingchan had said, the current matter had already become a contest between the Sacred Palace and the Cangxuan Sect. Although this content was suppressed to only involve the disciples of both sides, it still had a huge impact.    

The other four major sects, as well as forces of the Shengzhou Continent, most likely had already focused their attention on them.    

Although there weren’t any high-level experts intervening in the battle between the two sects, the fight between disciples could be even more brutal and tragic. 

“I hope senior brother returns victoriously!”  

Countless disciples shouted simultaneously. Their voices echoed across the world like thunder.

Boom!   

Majestic Genesis Qi gushed from Zhou Yuan's body, and his figure, at the same time, shot skywards. It transformed into a beam of light that pierced through the clouds and away from the Cangxaun Sect.   

“Go!”   

Chu Qing, Kong Sheng, Li Qingchan and other Chosens also immediately made a move, following closely after Zhou Yuan. 

Swoosh!   

And behind them, countless Cangxuan disciples also sped out with Genesis Qi. The mighty and imposing appearance clearly displayed the power of one of the strongest sects.   

Sect Master Qing Yang stood with hands clasped behind his back, gazing at the distant countless shadows as they left, “My Cangxuan Sect hasn’t had a battle of this scale in many years.”    

“The Sacred Palace has acted overbearing for many years, and now it's time for them to see the power of my Cangxuan Sect!” 

As he finished, his gaze gradually turned fierce. 

The countless disciples of the Cagnxuan Sect guarded Zhou Yuan as he left the sect. This sort of movely was naturally earth shaking. It attracted the attention of countless forces of the Shengzhou Continent. 

The people of various forces secretly smacked their lips. From their stance, the Cangxuan Sect evidently intended to fight the Sacred Palace to the end. 

The new and old overlords of Cangxuan Heaven were to face each other once again after many years. Even if the battle was only between disciples, the shock caused by such a battle was still unimaginable.  

The other four major sects restrained their disciples to prevent them from getting involved in the battle between the Sacred Palace and the Cangxuan Sect.    

Even the four major sects were like that, meaning other forces were even more unlikely to participate.

Where Zhou Yuan passed by, other people immediately retreated.

Everyone knew that Zhou Yuan’s path to the Cangmang Continent would be dyed red with blood. 

A tragic battle that was watched by many forces across the Shengzhou Continent was about to begin. 

Zhou Yuan and the army of Cangxuan Sect disciples headed straight three hundred miles away from the Cangxuan Sect.

Within three hundred miles, it was calm and peaceful, and without the slightest of disturbance. The reason for this was that no matter how bold the disciples of the Sacred Palace was, nobody would dare run to the door of the Cangxuan Sect to cause trouble in fear of angering any one of the Peak Masters.

However, once they left the three hundred miles limit, the world was filled with an intense murderous spirit.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        

In the high altitude, Zhou Yuan hovered in the air as he indifferently studied the mountains ahead. In between the mountains, it was unknown how many hidden figures he saw, Moreover, it was impossible to conceal the waves of Genesis Qi.    

And Zhou Yuan could also clearly feel that the countless gazes filled with killing intent were quietly locked onto his body.    

“You don't have to care about those hidden ghosts.” 

Li Qingchan ran her icy cold eyes across, stretched out her beautiful hand and gave it a gentle wave.    

“Kill.”    

A voice filled with killing intent echoed. 

Buzz!    

Behind them, the eyes of countless disciples of the Cangxuan Sect were immediately blazing with killing intent. Battle cries rang out like thunder and countless shadows whizzed out, racing into the mountains.    

In an instant, numerous bombardments of Genesis Qi could be heard

The entire mountain seemed to be rumbling at this moment.

“Continue ahead, the path won’t be peaceful, but you don’t need to do anything. Your opponent is Wu Huang of the Sacred Palace. I believe he is also waiting for you.” Chu Qing said.  

Zhou Yuan took a deep look at the mountain ranges, did not say anything, and continued to move forward quickly. He knew that this was just the beginning.

His journey home was destined to be a sea of blood. 

But neither the Sacred Palace nor Wu Huang could stop him.

“Wu Huang, two years ago, you stopped me, and I destroyed your body.”  

“Two years later, you once again attempt to stop me from returning home. I’m going to destroy your spirit!”
