I patted the Sky Obsidian Greedy Wolf's head and said, "Work hard and there’ll be meat!"

The wolf wagged its tail and rubbed hard against He Yi's arm. I was furious. Seriously? When Lin Yixin was present, it went to Lin Yixin. Now that Lin Yixin wasn't here, this wolf's entire mind was on our beautiful leader.

I grabbed the wolf's tail and pulled it out of the city, to the floating sky stairs. Its head banged on the stair steps as it howled and still kept on looking at He Yi. It desperately wanted to wag its tail so its behind waggled around instead. The girls laughed, and said that the wolf definitely had my habits.

I was accused wrongly. When was I ever like this?

……

We walked down the sky steps. When we came to the border of Sky Forest, 1500 Ancient Sword Dreaming Souls members were already waiting for us. Basically, every player was above Level 80. These were the core, guild’s most elite players. Under my command, the newly joined High Fighting Spirits and Eighteen Steeds of You and Yun were promoted to team leaders in the guild. Each of them had 100 people under their command. These people would slowly be nurtured.

Xu Yang came over with his sword and said, "You’re finally here!"

I nodded.

Chaos Moon looked at the time and said, "There are three minutes before the spawn. I don't know which divine site will be the first to appear."

"Haha, no matter. No guild will be able to take the site over. There will be guardian monsters around, so it doesn’t matter who discovers them first; we will all have a chance to compete."

I looked at the sky above Sky Forest and said, "Everyone has to work hard. This time, we can’t rely on the heavens or the support of our allies to get the divine sites. We can only rely on our weapons. Ancient Sword Dreaming Souls' future is in our grasp right now!"

Everyone nodded. "Yes, let's work together!"

……

Two minutes later, a string of bells sounded in the sky above Sky City—

Ding~!

System Notice: All players, please be informed that the Four Divine Sites are officially open. War Holy Temple, Pegasus Spring, Dragon Blood Fortress, and Giant Rock Cave have appeared within the borders of Sky City. Players, go search. The guild that takes the divine site will receive very powerful buffs!

……

The guild chat exploded. Everyone was asking, "Have they appeared? Have they appeared? Has anyone seen a divine site..."

But it seemed that no one in Ancient Sword Dreaming Souls found the divine site.

A few minutes later, someone shouted in the guild channel, "Damn, they found the Dragon Blood Fortress. It is less than five miles north of Sky City!"

Xu Yang's gaze turned cold. He looked at me and asked, "What do you think?"

I said, "Continue to wait, be calm."

"Okay!"

Soon after, news came. "The Giant Rock Cave has been found. Over in Blackwater Bog. Gods of Destruction and Mad Dragon have sent people there!"

At my side, Murong Mingyue and He Yi exchanged a look as though they could not stay calm.

I pressed my hand on the hilt of the Purgatory Sword and maintained my calm. If War Holy Temple did not appear, Ancient Sword Dreaming Souls would not move. Otherwise, we would get nothing.

At this time, an exciting news came in the guild chat. A player called Broken Bridge Remnant Snow shouted, "I found the War Holy Temple!"

Xu Yang was shocked. "Where?"

"South of Sky Forest, about fifteen minutes away from Sky City. The coordinates are (12754, 9843), everyone, come quick. I'm not the only one who found it!" Broken Bridge Remnant Snow's voice was urgent. "Fuck, I've been found. It's Warsky Alliance! Damn, you want to kill me? Not so easy..."

Broken Bridge Remnant Snow's voice disappeared. A few seconds later, he said, "I was killed, it was Farewell Song of Warsky Alliance..."

Gui Guzi and I stared at each other. This Broken Bridge Remnant Snow wasn’t a weakling. A heavily-armored Level 82 had been killed in just a few seconds. Warsky Alliance's strength was not a joke. The CGL Hall of Famer Farewell Song wasn't just a leather armor killer, he was also a heavy armor killer. To top assassins, armor type didn’t seem to matter. All that meant was just taking a bit longer to kill.

……

Clang!

I pulled out the Purgatory Sword and shouted. "Brothers of Ancient Sword Dreaming Souls, the coordinates are (12754, 9843). Go with me. War Holy Temple is ours!"

The melee players pulled out their swords and laughed. "Take War Holy Temple, kill!"

I was the first to charge out. On the map, the marked target was not far. We probably wouldn’t need even 15 minutes, more like 10. This divine site was so close to the city. This would mean there would be many fierce battles. After a player was killed, they could revive and run over in 10 minutes. They would be endless!

At the same time, I contacted Lin Yixin, "Yiyi, the coordinates are (12754, 9843). Come quick with Snowy Cathaya. Many people are going to fight for this spot. Warsky Alliance has already appeared. I don't know if other guilds will come. Just come quickly. Ancient Sword Dreaming Souls will try to reach the divine site's hall immediately. Snowy Cathaya will be responsible for clearing the enemies around the perimeter."

"Alright!"

Lin Yixin's reply was clean and brisk. I felt a sense of trust. This girl was both stubborn and brave. I felt that she would accomplish whatever she promised me!

The large crowd of Ancient Sword Dreaming Souls players flashed through the forest, sweeping through almost everything. The Level 90 monsters along the way were routed, they didn’t pose a threat.

He Yi, Gui Guzi, Xu Yang, Chaos Moon, Wolf Fang, High Fighting Spirits, Eighteen Steeds of You and Yun were all in high spirits beside me. With such a strong line-up, there probably wouldn't be anyone in Sky City that could stop us. Also, there was the archer camp led by Beiming Xue with their powerful attacks!

As we got closer to the target, my heart started to jump faster. I was excited. This was a fight between the guilds of the super cities!

But when I went a bit ahead, another guild appeared and blocked our path. This was a guild called Horizon Sea Pavilion and had about 1000 people!

A Level 84 magic knight called Zero Degree Temptation with a sharp blade appeared in front of us and looked at our forces. He snickered, "The area ahead is forbidden. Everyone, please stop. Horizon Sea Pavilion is taking this area. Friends, please make a detour!"

Clearly, this so-called guild activity was just taking the divine site. There were many guilds in Sky City. This Horizon Sea Pavilion seemed to have branches. Sending a few thousand people didn’t seem like a problem to them. With more than 10 million players in Sky City, guilds with a few thousand people in their community were too common!

Seeing us not respond, Zero Degree Temptation chuckled and said, "What, you’re not willing to detour?"

Clang!

I pulled out the Purgatory Sword and pointed in the direction of Horizon Sea Pavilion. I shouted, "Detour my ass, kill and use their blood to pave the way!"

Xu Yang, Gui Guzi, Du Thirteen and the others already had red eyes. At my order, many people charged. Did we have to be polite to an arrogant guild like Horizon Sea Pavilion?

Charge!

Whoosh!

I immediately flashed ahead of Zero Degree Temptation, successfully stunning him. I swung my sword. Thousand Mirage Slash!

Boom!

Nine streaks of sword energy attacked the crowd, and an enormous swarm of damage numbers rose. Horizon Sea Pavilion fighters were either injured or killed at the front line. Zero Degree Temptation gaped, and looked in shock at the last thread of his HP!

Crack. I easily disposed of him in the next moment, felling the vice leader of Horizon Sea Pavilion. His presence didn’t slow my advance at all. Before long, I flashed into the crowd of Horizon Sea Pavilion players. My blade turned blood-red as I leapt up and thrust the sword into the ground!

Boom! Boom! Boom!

War Crush robbed the frontline players of Horizon Sea Pavilion of their courage. After the three-hit combo, all the players within five yards were instantly killed, with no exceptions!

At the same time, Gui Guzi charged up, and thrust his spear, shouting, "Poison Dragon Thrust!"

Puchi!

A sharp thrust caused the enemy players on the line to all fall to the ground dead. Tsk tsk, Gui Guzi also had an AoE skill. While the range was small, it was still very strong. This proved that Ancient Sword Dreaming Souls possessed a second fighter with an AoE skill. It was a source of great deterrence!

Beiming Xue was in the rear as eleven of her powerful arrows howled out. At the high rank, Evil Spirit Volley’s damage was extremely terrifying and domineering!

He Yi released the Firelight Mouse. The rodent, almost at Level 90, squeaked and waved its small tail as it conjured flames that covered the crowd. The Horizon Sea Pavilion crowd cried and howled!

Xu Yang, Chaos Moon, High Fighting Spirits, and Eighteen Steeds of You and Yun also started to attack, their weapons cutting on the shields of the enemy magic knights, forcing them back. Ancient Sword Dreaming Souls' attack line was too strong. Just our numbers were enough to destabilize them!

Bang!

My sword flashed across two attacking swordsmen before I raised it high into the air. Magic flashed from behind me, and a cyan wolf leaped over me. Puchi! The Sky Obsidian Greedy Wolf bit into an enemy archer’s neck and tore left and right. The archer was instantly killed!

"Roar roar!"

Accompanied by the enormous roar, the Level 71 Sky Obsidian Greedy Wolf displayed its Epic Rank boss strength. Its paws flew as it unleashed its Flurry Dance at two Horizon Sea Pavilion magic knights, each combo consisting of three consecutive hits. Both of them were crushed into meat pulps, dead. One of them managed to let out one last line before his demise: “Heavens, what kind of pet is this?"
