Chapter 957 - The Strengthened Nine Nether Palace

The Hunting War of the Northern Region had finally ended after several months. When it came to an end, an uproar unfolded in the Northern Region.

The fall of the Divine Pavilion Master!

When this news spread out, the entire Northern Region was shocked to the point that they were speechless. Every single force was dumbfounded to the point that they felt a little disbelief. The expert that practically stood at the pinnacle of the Northern Region actually fell.

Although there would be the destruction of peak forces in every single Hunting War, perhaps no one had expected that the destruction this time would be the most powerful force in the Northern Region…

The entire Northern Region trembled as an uproar rang out. That’s because anyone knew that without the Divine Pavilion Master, the Divine Pavilion would definitely fall. With their current strength, it was impossible for them to protect such huge properties.

At the same time, such an ample force would also attract wolves to gather around it.

But this time, even that had exceeded everyone’s expectations, once again. The competition for the Divine Pavilion’s territories wasn’t the same as it was in the past, where rivers of blood would form. On the contrary, everything had come to a quiet end.

Under the gazes of countless forces, everyone discovered once again that half of the Divine Pavilion’s territories were taken by the Great Havenlaw Domain.

At this moment, everyone understood who the biggest winner of this Hunting War was.

At this moment, the news of Mandala’s cultivation reaching the Quasi-Greater Earth Sovereign Realm, and everything that occurred in the Hunting War, had spread out like wildfire in the Northern Region.

Quasi-Greater Earth Sovereign Realm?

When many forces heard this news, they had undoubtedly sucked in a cold breath, since they must have all been clear that this meant that the strength of the Great Havenlaw Domain had exceeded the other peak forces.

Perhaps in the future, the competition between peak forces would come to an end, whereas the Great Havenlaw Domain would become the strongest peak force.

With the situation coming to this, many people had started to understand what kind of attitude should they have when facing the strongest peak force in the Northern Region…

In the future, the Great Havenlaw Domain would definitely become the Overlord in the Northern Region and no one would dare to provoke it.

At the same time when the fame of the Great Havenlaw Domain had spread out, Mu Chen’s name had also spread out because, judging from the information, the reason why the Great Havenlaw Domain could become the biggest winner was greatly related to Mu Chen’s contributions. Without him, the entire situation might have been completely different…

Furthermore, Mu Chen had become a Battle Formation Master in the Hunting War, leading the troops of the Great Havenlaw Domain and it was so much so that even a Pavilion Lord of the Divine Pavilion couldn’t do a thing to him.

Even Fang Yi of the Divine Pavilion and the Netherworld Prince of the Netherworld Palace had become stepping stones for Mu Chen.

Therefore, when the Great Havenlaw Domain sat firmly at the top, Mu Chen had also ascended in the Dragon-Phoenix Record and replaced the Fang Yi, becoming the genuine Overlord of the record and also the pinnacle amongst the younger generations of the Northern Region.

With all his stunning contributions, no one could say a thing in the Northern Region about him being the Overlord amongst the younger generations of the Northern Region.

…

When the commotion caused by the fall of the Divine Pavilion settled down, the Great Havenlaw Domain had boiled. There wasn’t any peace after the Hunting War came to an end.

That’s because they had to devour nearly half of Divine Pavilion’s territories. With such a huge amount of territory, it was even troublesome for the Great Havenlaw Domain to devour it at once.

Furthermore, there were some forces that were unwilling to pledge allegiance to them amongst the territories of the Divine Pavilion, and thus, they had used underhand methods to cause chaos, which further increased the difficulty of the Great Havenlaw Domain taking over the territories.

However, all of these problems were settled after the three Emperors made a move and pulled those weeds that caused the chaos from the background out.

After all, those forces weren’t fully loyal to the Divine Pavilion, they just feared that after the Great Havenlaw Domain took them over, there would be a change in their benefits.

But after so much bloodshed from the Great Havenlaw Domain, they finally understood that this wasn’t something that they could resist. What they could do was willingly pledge allegiance or there wouldn’t be a place for them in the Northern Region.

Thus as time passed, the fame of the Great Havenlaw Domain grew with more forces submitting to them, causing the Great Havenlaw Domain’s power to dramatically grow.

Thus, in just a few months’ time, the Great Havenlaw Domain had gradually devoured half of the territories of the Divine Pavilion. Although there was the occasional chaos, things had started to settle down.

As they gradually devoured half of the Great Havenlaw Domain’s territories, they had undoubtedly become fattening. But fortunately, Mandala and the three Emperors still had their rationale. Although more and more experts submitted to them, they did not casually give out posts. Some of the more important posts were still given to the veterans of the Great Havenlaw Domain. This prevented internal problems from occurring that would cause great trouble for the Great Havenlaw Domain…

But, as long as the Great Havenlaw Domain rode through this period and gradually digested the experts from such huge territories, the power possessed by the Great Havenlaw Domain could definitely look at the entire Northern Region with disdain.

At that moment, only then would the Great Havenlaw Domain be the Overlord of the Northern Region!

…

Great Havenlaw Domain, Greatlaw Sky, the Nine Nether Palace

The current Nine Nether Palace had also grown along with the Great Havenlaw Domain, becoming stronger than before. Furthermore, the number of experts in the entire Nine Nether Palace had grown as well.

As the Great Havenlaw Domain grew, the benefits that the Nine Nether Palace had obtained was the best amongst the Kings. The cities under them had also grown from 100 to 600!

Such an ample harvest had undoubtedly caused the Nine Nether Palace to become the top amongst the Kings. Even the powerful Asura King couldn’t be compared to the Nine Nether Palace.

But they had no comments towards Mandala’s treatment for the Nine Nether Palace. After all, everyone was clear that it was all due to Mu Chen’s contributions that their Great Havenlaw Domain could become the biggest winner and their contributions were all pale in comparison.

Furthermore, Mu Chen even gave the Starlight Demon Suppression Pyramid over to Mandala at the end…

Therefore, even if they were jealous of the Nine Nether Palace’s growth, no one had spoken any unhappiness, since these were all the things that the Nine Nether Palace, or perhaps Mu Chen, deserved.

Although the Nine Nether Palace was filled with joy from the expanding territories, troubles followed as well. But fortunately, with Tang Bing’s help as the housekeeper of the Nine Nether Palace, and the numerous experts that had joined the Nine Nether Palace, the Nine Nether Palace completely gained control of the cities after one month…

With 600 cities, the annual tributes to the Nine Nether Palace alone would be a few hundred thousand drops of Sovereign Spiritual Liquid, which was several times more than it was in the past.

The depths of the Nine Nether Palace. This place was still the forbidden land of the Nine Nether Palace, despite the expansion of territories, and only a few in number could enter here.

At this moment, Mu Chen stood, with his hands behind his back, on the roof of the palace as he looked around the Nine Nether Palace. There were constant streaks of light flying about that showed that the entire Nine Nether Palace was currently extremely busy.

Mu Chen also did not slack from the large number of cities that the Nine Nether Palace had to control. After all, not all of those cities were willing to submit to them. So Mu Chen had no choice but to pay a visit with the Nine Nether Troop and sweep up those obstructions. So, over the past month, even he couldn’t endure the numerous battles that he had to go through…

But fortunately, after so much hard work, he had finally stabilised the situation of the Nine Nether Palace. As long as nothing went wrong, it was just a matter of time for the Nine Nether Palace to grow.

“Truly incomparable to the past…”

Mu Chen stood on the roof as he looked at the Nine Nether Palace and couldn’t help sighing. Two years ago, when he came to the Nine Nether Palace, the Nine Nether Palace was nearly forced to disband. But who could have thought that after two years, it had actually grown to be so strong.

“Yeah… and most of it was because of your contributions.”

A peal of gentle laughter rang out by Mu Chen’s rear. He turned his head over and saw a beautiful figure. Nine Nether was chuckling as she stood behind him.

Nine Nether took a step forth and stood side-by-side with Mu Chen. She had also cast a gaze at the distant sky and was a little absent-minded. “I never thought that you would grow so much in just two years.”

Two years ago, when Mu Chen came to the Great Havenlaw Domain, he had not even formed his Sovereign Celestial Body. But right now, he had already become the Overlord amongst the younger generations of the Northern Region. His growth had made others sigh in admiration.

“It’s all thanks to Big Sister Nine Nether for guiding me.” Mu Chen lightly smiled. If it wasn’t for Nine Nether guiding him to the Great Havenlaw Domain, he would probably still be wandering around in the Great Thousand World without a home.

Nine Nether lightly smiled in response to his words.

Mu Chen looked at Nine Nether and noticed that her smile was somewhat forced, so he lightly furrowed his brows. “What’s wrong?”

Nine Nether briefly hesitated, before she helplessly said, “I have received the news from the clan. Five days from now, Elder Tianque will come to take me back to the clan…”

When Mu Chen heard her words, he squinted his eyes. 

The Nine Netherbird Clan was finally coming?
