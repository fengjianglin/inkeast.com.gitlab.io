Monarch of Evernight

Prologue

Twelve hundred years have passed since the War of Daybreak.

That was a war of fate, a collision between humans and the dark races. It was a struggle of Daybreak origin power, burning black stones, gushing steam, rumbling firearms, and enormous rough machinery against Darkness origin power!

That was an era that truly belonged to the strong!
