Chapter 0396: Thoroughly Refined Midasthefloof's Thoughts

Action Fantasy Tragedy Xianxia Chinese Ongoing 2019/05/08

Heaven's Devourer


Right before his eyes was the largest Subterranean Treasured Moon Furnace within the Ravaging Ghost Volcano. 

This Subterranean Treasured Moon Furnace was much bigger than the one Wu Yu had encountered previously. If he had devoured the Treasured Moon furnace here to forge his Invincible Vajra Body, his body would’ve been even stronger! 

It was as though there was a heavenly furnace before him. The boiling water erupted with great might and the heat wave was pressurizing. Ordinary steel and even the majority of precious treasures with spiritual marks would be vaporized if they fell into it. 

Even the Yan Huang Immortal Armor wouldn't be able to withstand the heat. 

As such, Wu Yu removed his Yan Huang Immortal Armor. His current body was forged by devouring the Subterranean Treasured Moon Furnace. Therefore, he was like a fish in water when he entered it. 

For him, the surrounding golden lava wasn't much different from the seawater. It was just a little warmer. 

However, the Black Fiend couldn't do the same. The moment he entered this place, he was forced to use several Heaven Earth Void techniques to protect himself. He acted like a mortal falling into flames. He was drenched in perspiration and was panting violently. 

As for Cangxue Qingfeng, she was slightly better. As her lion body moved within the furnace, she created tremendous waves from within the golden lava.

"Wu Yu, why are you still thinking of escaping?" Cangxue Qingfeng caught up with Wu Yu.

 

At this moment, Wu Yu still didn't know about the depth of the furnace. 

"If I engage them and pretend to fall below and fake my death, they would definitely go after the Yan Huang Warship. I have to make them doubt it so as to hold them back here for a few days!" 

At the thought of this, Wu Yu smirked and remarked, "Of course I'm escaping to a place you guys can't follow. Want to catch me? It isn't going to be simple." 

As he laughed, he dived deeper without pausing. 

"You are still diving deeper?" Cangxue Qingfeng was starting to feel the strain on her physical body and dao technique at the current depth surrounded by golden lava. She had not expected Wu Yu to be able to dive deeper! 

"You must be crazy!" chanted Cangxue Qingfeng furiously. 

"This is my territory! It's only natural for me to go wild here! If you aren't satisfied, just come for me." Wu Yu's tone was calm and composed. He was like a fish swimming deeper. His golden skin had almost become one with the surrounding golden lava. The giant furnace around him was just like his body. Cangxue Qingfeng indeed wasn't as used to an environment like this as Wu Yu. 

Nonetheless, the Black Fiend was still behind her. Despite having the upper hand, Wu Yu didn't take the risk. What he wanted to know was if there was something he had been searching for for a long time below the furnace....

"If you dive deeper, you are courting your own demise!" Cangxue Qingfeng snorted coldly. 

"I'm going to dive deeper. What are you going to do about that?"

From Wu Yu's words, one could infer that he was trying to delay the two of them for a period of time. Judging from Cangxue Qingfeng’s resistance against the Subterranean Treasured Moon Furnace, she likely couldn't enter the hotter region below. 

At this point, he was in fact close to the bottom of the Subterranean Treasured Moon Furnace. It was insanely scorching but it could get even hotter if he dived deeper. Wu Yu knew clearly there was something even hotter below the bottom! 

"This must be it! There's an even larger furnace below, and these eight Subterranean Treasured Moon Furnaces are just extensions of it. Considering the large scale and temperature, it must be a Subterranean Blazing Sun Furnace! Who would have expected the Subterranean Blazing Sun Furnace to appear here instead of on land. What a miracle!" Ming Long exclaimed. 

Wu Yu heaved a sigh of relief but got agitated soon after. He had even forgotten about the two terrifying ghostly cultivators who were chasing behind him. 

"If you have the guts, come over!" 

Wu Yu roared out loud. At this point, he could see a furnace at the bottom that glowed with a dimmer shade of gold. The dense color resembled flowing molten metal, and the flames spewing from it resonated and reverberated out. This was the core of the entire Ravaging Ghost Volcano! The Ravaging Ghost Volcano spanned over thousands of miles, and all the lava had originated from this place. 

Wu Yu could feel his blood boiling as he charged into the rarely seen Blazing Sun Furnace. 

RUMBLE!

When the dull gold lava got into contact with his skin, Wu Yu instantly felt like his body was on fire. He couldn't hold back the pain and screamed out loud. The difference was stark. The moment he entered, his body was torched black. The terrifying heat even penetrated into his physical body, resulting his inner body being burnt to toast and vaporizing! 

Screams of pain reverberated constantly from the bottom of Cangxue Qingfeng's feet. 

Cangxue Qingfeng formed a golden sphere around her and tried to close the distance between her and the furnace. However, she couldn't last long. She was drenched in perspiration. Her human body was exceptionally alluring from the reflection of her perspiration, while her lion body was shimmering in dazzling flames. This created an exceptional contrast. 

The truth was that her body hosted both male and female parts. No one could really definitively determine her gender. 

Cangxue Qingfeng felt miserable. However, she broke into laughter upon hearing Wu Yu. "Black Fiend, that Wu Yu must be a fool to be trying to lure us to a battleground he excels in. However, he has overestimated himself and will likely lose his life here." 

"What!?" 

The Black Fiend was just halfway into the Treasured Moon Furnace. He was completely drenched and looked pale at this point as he struggled to withstand the surrounding golden lava. As the golden lava crept onto the black sphere he had created to protect himself. It could be seen that he was in a perilous situation. 

"Wu Yu has dived into a place that even I don't dare to go. He's screaming in pain now and will likely not last long." Cangxue Qingfeng chuckled. 

"This is going to be troublesome. If his corpse is reduced to dust, we wouldn't be able to get his legacy!" The Black Fiend didn't dare to dive deeper, as he was wary of facing the same fate as Wu Yu. 

"It is indeed as such. Now we can only wait and see if this brat dares to surface. Let's open a path for him and let him out." As Cangxue Qingfeng spoke, she moved beside the Black Fiend and the two of them looked below them. 

If Wu Yu still had some strength in him, he should be rushing up. 

The Black Fiend was feeling a little bottled up as he remarked with melancholy, "Why isn't there any commotion? Has he been burnt to death?" 

Cangxue Qingfeng was about to nod her head when she vaguely heard a faint voice from below. "It's not that easy to kill me! If the two of you have the guts to come down, I'll fight with both of you at the same time!" 

Cangxue Qingfeng exchanged glances with the Black Fiend. Seeing that the other party wasn't dead, they felt a little relieved. Otherwise, they would have gained nothing. 

At this point, they had the time to play along with Wu Yu. 

As such, the Black Fiend said, "Stop acting tough. You can probably only last a while longer below." 

As he spoke, he moved up a little with Cangxue Qingfeng and left enough space for Wu Yu to move should he come up. Since they were here, they naturally would want to kill Wu Yu with their own hands and gain something from it rather than seeing him being burnt to death. 

Wu Yu smiled and answered, "Alright, let's see who can last longer!" 

Keeping the two of them here was his motive. 

While in the incomparably hot Subterranean Blazing Sun Furnace, Wu Yu couldn't release his doppelgangers, as they would be reduced to dust the moment they appeared. 

After succeeding in keeping them here, Wu Yu acclimatized quickly in the extreme heat while arguing and observing them. After getting a sense of the size of the Subterranean Blazing Sun Furnace, he noticed that he was still in a rather exterior region. Therefore, he was able to withstand the heat after short periods of burns. 

"This might very well be the only one in the divine continent. You are really lucky to have found this place." 

The third tier of the Invincible Vajra Body could be separated into two finer levels. Wu Yu had to reach the Nanwu Blazing Sun Golden Vajra Body to be considered as achieving great mastery. With the vast and scorching Blazing Sun Furnace below his feet, Wu Yu finally possessed the opportunity and condition of achieving great mastery of the third tier of the Invincible Vajra Body. 

However, the difficulty wasn't much different from the first time he fell into the Subterranean Treasured Moon Furnace. At this moment, the dull golden lava was just like a sharp spike piercing throughout his body as it raged brutally within his blood. Wu Yu constantly moved within the region to relieve the pressure he felt by the movements of the body. What he was striving for was the slow burn of his body.

After successfully withstanding the heat, he started devouring the dull gold lava using the spiritual art of the third tier of the Invincible Vajra Body. He wasn't just using his mouth to devour. In addition, he was using his ears, nose, and even the pores of his skin to hone and refine his body. 

The very first time he devoured, Wu Yu screamed out pitifully. At that instant, he felt as though the inner organs of his body were being reduced to dust. It was the feeling a mortal would feel when he devoured flames. To put it simply, he felt drained. Moreover, every single inch of his body could feel the pain that resembled being sliced by knives coded with spice. That pain could knock anyone unconscious. 

Rumble.

In his mind, a buzzing sound reverberated. 

Above him, Cangxue Qingfeng and the Black Fiend seemed to have heard it too. The two exchanged glances and smiled. The Black Fiend said, "Wu Yu, why are you so insistent? Come out now and we will grant you a quick death." 

"As you have said, this is your territory. We can't dive any deeper, so why are you torturing yourself below?" asked Cangxue Qingfeng. 

"If you have the balls, just come down. Otherwise, stop nagging there," Wu Yu responded loudly while screaming in pain. After shouting out, he felt a little better. 

"What a stubborn guy." 

"I have heard about that too." 

"In that case, let's see how long you can last before being reduced to dust." 

Wu Yu no longer paid attention to them. Currently, his entire focus was on refining and honing the Nanwu Blazing Sun Golden Vajra Body. 

The day he succeeded would also be the day both of them would die! 

For this mission, Wu Yu would have reaped handsome rewards if he slew three fourth tier Violent Kingdom of the Inner Sea cultivators! Even if he didn't collect the 150,000 merits, it wouldn't matter. 

Persevere! 

While forging the Invincible Vajra Body, the only way forward was to persevere. 

Moreover, one couldn't just do so for a short while. Instead, he had to persevere at maximum intensity over a long period of time! 

From the very beginning, honing the Invincible Vajra Body was never going to be easy. 

However, the effects of it were incomparably immense! If not for the Invincible Vajra Body, Wu Yu would have died several times over. 

While devouring the dull golden lava and forging his body, every second and every breath brought unimaginable pain and misery. 

While burning, Wu Yu looked towards the sky from the depths of the sea. 

"To become an immortal, one has to be thoroughly refined!"
