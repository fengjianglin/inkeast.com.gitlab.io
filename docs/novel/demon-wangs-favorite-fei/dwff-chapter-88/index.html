Chapter 88 The shy Nan Lin Wang (2)

“Wangye[1. Wang/wangye: prince of the first rank], let’s find a place where you could see the mountains and rivers to bury Guli!”

Just now, they were hostile towards each other. Now, as a woman, Murong Qi Qi’s soft spot had been awakened by Guli’s last tears.

Which woman was not a pure flower at first, when going into a man's arms with a heart full of love? But, only after experiencing betrayal would the evil in the heart of a woman breed and make them do deviant things. Guli was nothing but a pitiful person.

“Good!”

Feng Cang helped Murong Qi Qi up and told the servants to bury Guli. When Helan Lian Yi saw that Guli had died, he only frowned and then left hurriedly with Long Duo.

This woman indeed had a talent to shock people and also had something that attracted the eyes of people. The dance of just now almost let him lose his soul and be in a daze…….

While saying that, suddenly in Jia Lan’s mind appeared the image of Murong Qi Qi dancing. Don’t know if that woman would participate in the tournament of the four countries or not. If she participates, then the outcome will really be something to look forward to!

The duel ended with Guli’s death and Helan Lian Yi losing a hundred thousand golden teals.

Just when Feng Cang and Murong Qi Qi were about to leave, suddenly Longze Jing Tian appeared in front of Murong Qi Qi. He stared deeply at her. In his eyes was a strong love.

“Xian Er, is it you? I’ve searched a long time for you!”

It turned out that Murong Qi Qi’s dance made Longze Jing Tian think about the day Du Xian Er saved him in the forest. When the two figures overlapped, they had actually matched so well.

“I don’t know what you’re talking about! If this is Jing wang’s way to strike, I can only tell Jing wang that this way is a failure!”

Even when Feng Cang, who was holding Murong Qi Qi’s hand, moved far away, Longze Jing Tian still looked infatuatedly at Murong Qi Qi.

“Xian Er, is it you? In the end, is it really you?”

Longze Jing Tian’s words went into Murong Qing Lian’s ears, making her dislike this rubbish third big sister even more. Today, Murong Qi Qi let people be surprised. It’s just, why was it that everywhere this woman went, she was able to seduce men? Feng Cang’s eyes almost dropped honey and now Longze Jing Tian was also like that. Where was there anything good about this woman?! Murong Qing Lian bit her lips and mercilessly stamped on the ground.

When Feng Cang and Murong Qi Qi arrived at Meixiang yuan[2. Yuan: wing of a residence], Helan Lian Yi already let people send over a hundred thousand golden teals.

“Did you count it? Inspect it?”

Murong Qi Qi drank a mouthful of tea and asked Su Yue.

“Answering miss, everything was real and the amount is also right!”

Su Yue laughed while answering. Her mood was very good. Before, Fang Jing was worried that building the world’s best place for business would need a lot of capital. Now, miss only went away for a bit and came back with a hundred thousand golden teals. If Fang Jing saw it, he’ll certainly be happy enough to die.

“Mm, be careful. Put it away in a safe place.”

When she finished talking, she turned and looked smilingly at Feng Cang.

“Wangye, you have wealth equivalent to that of an entire nation. I’m afraid that these hundred thousand golden teals certainly won't enter your eyes. Take it as if I won some pocket money, alright?”

Ruyi, who was listening to this, almost spouted out blood. Wangfei[3.Wangfei: main wife/consort of first-rank prince] really has an appetite of a lion, ah! Where in this world was there a woman who would take a hundred thousand golden teals as pocket money? Even the princesses in the palace didn’t have this much pocket money, ah!

Feng Cang smiled, and Ruyi almost bit off his tongue. Master pampers wangfei too much! But having said that, this money was indeed earned by wangfei herself.

Murong Qi Qi quickly stood up and bowed towards Feng Cang. She didn’t even bow yet, when she was pulled into Feng Cang’s arms.

“Qing Qing, don’t you need to confess something? Hmm…...”

Feng Cang stretched out his voice. Su Yue and the others saw that, and they knew that Feng Cang and Murong Qi Qi had something to talk about, so they retreated. When they left, they specially closed the door.

Murong Qi Qi blinked her eyes innocently as if she didn’t understand what Feng Cang meant.

“You, ah……”

Seeing Murong Qi Qi like that, Feng Cang didn’t want to force her. As long as she’s safe and happy, it’s enough!

Feng Cang pinched Murong Qi Qi’s nose. His eyes were full of pampering and love.

“You having a little secret, I don’t mind that. I’m willing to wait for the day you completely open your heart. Just, next time when you encounter such a thing again, tell me in advance, lest to let me worry so much at one side!”

Feng Cang didn’t continue to pursue the matter, but let her keep some private space, making Murong Qi Qi very moved. She took the initiative to go into Feng Cang’s arms.

“Feng Cang, you really are good to me!”

“Qing Qing is also not bad towards me, ah! Today, who repeatedly said ‘Feng Cang is my man’ in front of everyone?”

Murong Qi Qi’s face turned red when Feng Cang mentioned this. Originally, she said those words to anger Guli and also at the same time, beat back those ill-intentioned women like Murong Qing Lian, who wanted to covet Feng Cang. She didn’t think much about it. Unexpectedly, Feng Cang let those words enter his mind. Really embarrassing, ah.

“Before, you said them so righteously, how come you’re shy now? My reputation has been ruined by you. Qing Qing, you say, shouldn’t you compensate me?”

Feng Cang’s devilishly beautiful face moved closer to Murong Qi Qi. That pair of quiet eyes was full of spring, making Murong Qi Qi couldn’t help but put her hands on Feng Cang’s face.

“Feng Cang, you look really charming……”

“Oh…...”

Murong Qi Qi saying such a praise, made Feng Cang give her a knowing smile. He put her jade-like hand against his lips and kissed it gently.

“Qing Qing, if I was a woman and you praised me like that, I will feel happy. But, I’m a man. Being praised like a woman, I’m very sad. Could it be that Qing Qing is saying I’m very sissy?”

“No, no, no!”

Murong Qi Qi immediately gestured with her hand and quickly denied.

“I just never have seen such a man like you. You’re really a devilish beauty!”

The moment he heard ‘devilish beauty’, Feng Cang raised his head and casted a flirtatious glance at Murong Qi Qi.

“Since Qing Qing praise me as a devilish beauty; if I don’t do something to tempt you, then wouldn’t I not be living up to Qing Qing’s praise?”

Not waiting for Murong Qi Qi to understand, Feng Cang’s handsome face moved over. Red lips leaned closer, seizing Murong Qi Qi’s petal-like soft lips and gently sucked on them.

Sweet with a taste of green apples making a person float. Moreover, Murong Qi Qi’s lips were extremely soft. It was like kissing soft clouds. Feng Cang gently bit on her soft lips. He didn’t dare to use force in fear of hurting her

Murong Qi Qi’s face turned red because of shyness. She closed her eyes and moaned once. She didn’t think that the heat on her lips would stop. She opened her eyes. Feng Cang’s handsome face was red, but his eyes were full of worries.

“What’s the matter? Qing Qing, did I hurt you with the biting?”

So, it turned out, that the moan of Murong Qi Qi, in Feng Cang’s ears, it sounded like he had hurt her. That’s why he stopped.

Looking at Feng Cang’s uneasy expression and his red face, Murong Qi Qi’s heart jumped badly. This man is too cute! What kind of hearing is that, ah? To actually turn comfortable into pain. Is it that he has never kissed before?

Thinking till here, Murong Qi Qi once again looked at Feng Cang. Feng Cang’s face turned redder. He didn’t dare look at Murong Qi Qi. Their gaze just met for a moment, and then he quickly turned to look somewhere at the roof. Now, Murong Qi Qi was more certain about her thought.

“Wangye, could it be that you’ve never kissed a girl before?!”

Murong Qi Qi asked carefully. Her eyes were locked on Feng Cang’s face.

Now, Feng Cang’s face burned completely. So hot, it was painful.

What should he do? Could it be that just now, he had hurt her while biting? So embarrassed, he wanted to die. Why did such a thing happen? The first time he kissed and he left such a bad impression on Murong Qi Qi. Will she laugh at him?

“Haha!”

It’s the first time that Murong Qi Qi saw such a shy Feng Cang. She didn’t think that he would be this shy. Blood could now be pinched out of that handsome face. He looked so brightly red, very devilishly beautiful. It’s just that this man was shy because of a kiss, really too cute! Just now, he just sucked her lips like a baby. Could it be that he thinks that this is kissing?!

So, kissing is like this, ah! Feng Cang finally understood that the dabble and stop of just now was not considered kissing. Such a lingering was a real kiss. So, it’s like this!

Obviously, he wanted to get it so much, but he still bore with it. She really didn’t know what to say anymore. She could only nestle quietly in his arms and enjoyed this beautiful sweetness……

Using Croton powder wouldn’t harm people’s life, but would make people need to go to the bathroom till their legs turned weak. They wouldn’t be able to participate in the tournament.
