Chapter 98 Cousin brother, you this beast (2)

At this time, Feng Cang changed his modest behavior. Inside his phoenix eyes was laughter. He pulled at the snow-white fur on his body. Then he took off his coat, revealing the white inside it. When he was about to take off his underwear, Murong Qi Qi was so scared she grabbed Feng Cang’s hands.

“No need!”

Murong Qi Qi shook her head and bit her lips. This time, the joke went too far. If Su Yue and the others saw Feng Cang’s clothes being untidy, they would think they did something in the carriage. By then won’t she feel shameful to death?!

“Really, don’t want?”

Feng Cang smiled with a smile that was yet not like a smile at Murong Qi Qi; looking at her blood red face, her red neck and that pair of beautiful eyes full of tension.

“Don’t want!”

‘Hua……’

The curtain has been opened, revealing Wanyan Kang’s handsome face.

“What doesn’t cousin in law want?”

When Wanyan Kang saw the scene in front of him clearly, he was truly stunned. His eyes widened and his finger pointed while trembling at Feng Cang.

“Cousin, you, this beast!”

The original sweet fragment between a couple, in the eyes of Wanyan Kang turned into Feng Cang took of his clothes and wanted to force himself on Murong Qi Qi. Plus, with the scream from Murong Qi Qi, Wanyan Kang could imagine how Murong Qi Qi, this little white rabbit, ‘suffered’ at the hands of Feng Cang, the wolf.

“Cousin brother! No matter how impatient you are, you should wait till the wedding night, ah!”

“Get lost……”

Seeing Wanyan Kang deliberately distort the right from wrong, Feng Cang’s palm went at him. Wanyan Kang was scared and quickly retracted

“Cousin brother, you want to silent (kill) me, ah?!”

Wanyan Kang’s scream came from outside the carriage.

“If you say one more word, when we go back, I’ll let uncle find ten women for you!”

Feng Cang put on his clothes, pulled the curtain and looked at Wanyan Kang. The threat in his voice was very strong. Wanyan Kang quickly closed his mouth.

“I didn’t see anything. I don’t know anything. You, continue! Aiya, the weather is so good today. The sun is really big, ah! Haha! Being young is so good!”

The adapting to the circumstances Wanyan Kang, quickly looked at the sky and laughed.

“Humph!”

Feng Cang pulled down the curtain. At the time, Murong Qi Qi truly wanted to faint. Her face was burning and red. Her head buried completely in Feng Cang’s chest.

“That kid is used to being like this! When I go back, I’ll deal with him!”

Feng Cang held Murong Qi Qi and softly apologized. This little woman’s face is thin skinned.  He also is. Now, his heart is still jumping very hard. Damn! When they go back, they must marry quickly! By then he can bill and coo with Murong Qi Qi openly!

Till they arrived at the venue, did Murong Qi Qi leave Feng Cang’s embrace. After they came down the carriage, Wanyan Kang whistled at them. Murong Qi Qi blushed once again. Until Feng Cang’s eyes swept over Wanyan Kang, did he touch his nose and hide at a side.

The venue for the finals was like a stage. In the middle was stone build round platform. It was surrounded by a circle of semicircular two story small building. It’s specifically for people to watch the game.

Because the contestants of Nan Feng and Dong Lu had been eliminated- Nan Feng’s contestant already returned to their country- so, this time only Xi Qi’s, Bei Zhou’s contestants and the people of Dong Lu who came to watch at the venue.

The people of Dong Lu have long arrived. The one in the lead was Jia Lan.

“Wangye, princess!”

Jia Lan waved from his seat at Feng Cang and Murong Qi Qi.

“You’ve arrived!”

“Mm!”

Feng Cang nodded in answer. He took Murong Qi Qi to the seats of Bei Zhou’s contestants. Not long after, Xi Qi’s contestants also arrived.

Because it was a once in every five years’ tournament between the four countries, many citizens also came to the venue. They wanted to watch this rare tournament. Fortunately, the venue was big enough. So, the citizens who came also had a place to sit.

Wanyan Bao Zhu also came to see the tournament. Since Feng Cang got to know from Murong Qi Qi that Wanyan Bao Zhu was a fake, he let people ‘serve’ her more attentively. They served her like a real princess, made Wanyan Bao Zhu forgot the shock of before and thought that she really got away with it.

When she arrived at the venue, with one glance, Wanyan Bao Zhu saw Longze Jing Tian sitting at Xi Qi’s side.

Dup, dup! Wanyan Bao Zhu’s heart beat very fast. She never thought that she’ll ever be able to see Longze Jing Tian again. She also never expected to see him at this place.

Although this man had hurt her, but he was the first man she loved. He was her first love and also her first man. So, after seeing Longze Jing Tian, the emotions in Wanyan Bao Zhu’s heart were difficult to contain. The extinguished flamed burned again. She forgot that she was at the venue and also forgot that Feng Cang and Murong Qi Qi were at her side. She actually walked to Longze Jing Tian.

“Hey, who are you? What do you want?”

Not waiting for Wanyan Bao Zhu to get to Longze Jing Tian, she has been stopped by Murong Qing Lian. Murong Qing Lian didn’t know this Bei Zhou’s princess, but she came from over where Murong Qi Qi was. Murong Qing Lian didn’t have good feelings for Murong Qi Qi, so, she stopped Wanyan Bao Zhu even before she (WBZ) had the chance to get close to Longze Jing Tian.

The moment Wanyan Bao Zhu saw the person who stopped her was Murong Qing Lian, she frowned. How come it’s her? Why is fourth little sister here? Thinking again, Murong Qing Lian was an excellent figure among the young generation, she must’ve come to participate in the tournament. However in her (MQL) eyes were no kindness. This made Wanyan Bao Zhu somewhat unhappy.

“I would like to ask, are you Xi Qi’s Jing wang?”

Ignoring Murong Qing Lian, Wanyan Bao Zhu went around her and came to Longze Jing Tian. The way she looked at him was full of infatuation as well as memories from the past.

“You are?”

Longze Jing Tian lowered his head. Looking at that infatuated expression of Wanyan Bao Zhu, he immediately frowned. Why is every woman around him so infatuated? Why is it that aside from Murong Qi Qi, there’s no one who isn’t infatuated?”

“I’m…...”

The words were about to leave her mouth, when Wanyan Bao Zhu suddenly stopped. How should she tell Longze Jing Tian? Should she say that she’s Murong Xin Lian? That’s impossible! Right! Now she’s Bei Zhou’s princess! She must come up with a princess’s style!

“Bengong[2. Bengong: this palace, I, used by the females of the imperial family] is Bei Zhou’s princess Bao Zhu!”

“Oh……don’t know what brought princess here?”

Longze Jing Tian narrowed his eyes. He was somewhat impatient. It’s just Wanyan Bao Zhu didn’t notice it. She was just thinking about another problem.

Now, she’s is not Murong household's second miss Murong Xin Lian anymore, but she’s Bei Zhou’s princess Bao Zhu-----Wanyan Bao Zhu. So, she had a new identity. Did this also mean that she could begin a new life? For example, marrying Longze Jing Tian as Bei Zhou’s imperial princess? Now her identity is noble. She’s enough to be comparable with Longze Jing Tian. She doesn’t have to be afraid of stepping a tail and beg for pity anymore.

Wanyan Bao Zhu remained silent. She just stared at Longze Jing Tian’s face in daze, making Murong Qing Lian very unhappy.

“Princess, our wangye is talking to you!”

“Presumptuous!”

After her thoughts was cut by Murong Qing Lian, Wanyan Bao Zhu immediately snapped.

“Bengong is princess Bao Zhu. I’m talking to Jing wang. What right do you have to interrupt?!”

Have to say, when a person is placed at a position, she will naturally have the aura of that position. Such as Murong Xin Lian, now her identity is a princess. Then she naturally would emit an imposing aura. Although this imposing aura couldn’t be compared to Murong Qi Qi’s, but to Murong Qing Lian, a daughter of a subject, it still had a certain effect.

Wanyan Bao Zhu snapped at his country’s people, made Longze Jing Tian even more unhappy. At other countries, protecting your own is a must. Immediately Longze Jing Tian opened his mouth: “What did princess come to find benwang[3. Benwang: this king. I used by the prince of first rank] for?”

Although Longze Jing Tian didn’t clearly speak up for Murong Qing Lian, but the coldness in the words and the tone of keeping people a thousand miles away, Wanyan Bao Zhu caught on with them in just a moment.

“Nothing. It’s just wangye looks like an old friend of mine. So, I came to say hi.”

Having Longze Jing Tian’s ‘support’, Murong Qing Lian got more daring.

“Yo! Princess’s way to strike up with someone is very peculiar, ah! It’s just don’t know what the name of that old friend of princess’s is. How is he like our wangye, ah?”

This time, Longze Jing Tian didn’t bring Lu Yuan and Lu Ye on the journey. Plus, the fact that his feet got injured in the pagoda, it was inconvenient to apply medicine. Murong Qing Lian in the name of ‘wangye got hurt because of me, I should ho take care of him’ applied medicine every day for him.

Murong Qing Lian wanted to take this opportunity to do a good job and also wanted to get Longze Jing Tian’s attention because of this. Because of her hard work, Longze Jing Tian gave her a bit better expression. This also became Murong Qing Lian’s confidence to be smug. That’s why she dared to talk like that to Wanyan Bao Zhu.

Being rebuked by Murong Qing Lian openly, Wanyan Bao Zhu’s face immediately turned red. She wanted to use her identity of princess to teach Murong Qing Lian a lesson and let her know who is the boss, but the time for the contestants to draw had arrived.

Looking at Murong Qing Lian following Longze Jing Tian diligently and happily, Wanyan Bao Zhu clenched her fists. Having a Murong Qi Qi to ruin her good things, she’s already very gloomy and unhappy. Now, a Murong Qing Lian appeared and she’s looking so love struck at Longze Jing Tian. Wanyan Bao Zhu didn’t need to think to know what’s in Murong Qing Lian’s head.

Slut! Truly a slut! She even wants to snatch her own brother in law!

Wanyan Bao Zhu gritted her teeth. She wished her glare could burn a few holes on Murong Qing Lian’s body. From her point of view, the behavior of Murong Qing Lian thinking about Longze Jing Tian was truly shameless! Because Longze Jing Tian is her (WBZ) man, is her (MQL) brother in law.

She has long forgotten that from beginning to end, Longze Jing Tian had never put her in his eyes. She also forgot that she got Longze Jing Tian after using extreme means. It should be that she ‘snatched’ him from Murong Qi Qi. Now, Wanyan Bao Zhu’s heart is full of hate towards Murong Qing Lian……

Slut! I’ll definitely find an opportunity to deal with you!

Before, at Murong residence, Murong Qing Lian relied on the fact that she was favored plus the fact that Liu Yan Zhi was also favored, she was always cynical in front of Murong Xin Lian. From time to time, she (MQL) would also gave her (MXL) a bad look.

At that time, although she was beautiful but Murong Tai put more emphasis on the level four Murong Qing Lian. Plus, the fact that Liu Yan Zhi had the backing of a son, so every time the two fought, Murong Tai was always biased towards his youngest daughter. Now, it’s different. Now she isn’t Murong Xin Lian, but princess Bao Zhu from Bei Zhou’s imperial family. She now has a noble identity. Killing people for revenge is just a small matter!

Murong Qing Lian didn’t know this Wanyan Bao Zhu is Murong Xin Lian and also didn’t know that someone already set her eyes of her (MQL).

Now, the thing she’s most concerned about is the drawing of the sticks. In Murong Qing Lian’s heart, she wished very much to draw Murong Qi Qi. By that time, she wanted to cut many scars in that proud face of Murong Qi Qi!

The drawing was split into two groups. One group would draw the number about which order they will compete. The other group would draw the name of the opponent. How they would be allocated was decided by drawing sticks.

The stick Longze Jing Tian draw was to select the opponent and the one Murong Qi Qi draw was the order of the tournament. Longze Jing Tian knew the principle of letting women go first, so he let Bei Zhou draw first. Su Mei would compete first, Ruyi the second, Wanyan Kang the third and Murong Qi Qi the fourth.

Now, it’s Xi Qi’s contestants turn to draw the opponents. Longze Jing Tian’s opponent was Wanyan Kang. Li Yung Qing’s was Su Mei. Bai Yi Yue would go against Ruyi. Murong Qing Lian’s opponent was Murong Qi Qi.

Murong Qing Lian triumphantly shook the stick with Murong Qi Qi’s name on it. What she thought off happened. Just now, she was thinking about how she must become a pair with Murong Qi Qi. She didn’t expect that she was so lucky and really picked Murong Qi Qi. It seemed that even heaven dislikes Murong Qi Qi. That’s why heaven sent her (MQL) to uphold virtue and condemn evil!

Seeing Murong Qing Lian being so proud, Murong Qi Qi smiled. And Murong Qi Qi’s smile, in Murong Qing Lian’s eyes was a way of Murong Qi Qi’s expressing goodwill and wanting her to be easy at the tournament.

Thinking till here, Murong Qing Lian turned more proud.

“Big sister, I also want to take our relationship of sister into consideration, ah. However, there’s no sisterhood on a battle. I’m Xi Qi’s contestant, naturally I should bring glory to the country, ah! Big sister, by then, don’t blame me, ah!”

Act, continue act! Seeing that face of Murong Qing Lian, Murong Qi Qi was too lazy to bicker with her. She yawned. White jade like hands settled on Feng Cang’s neck.

“Alright! Do whatever you like! How you’ll do, I’ll follow!”

Just now, Murong Qi Qi had a modest attitude. Now, she (MQQ) pushed her (MQL) to a side. The attitude was so different, making Murong Qing Lian a bit unable to adapt.

“Of course! No matter if it’s fighting with or without inner energy, bring it on. This big sister is not afraid of you!”

Murong Qi Qi raised an eyebrow and looked provocatively at Murong Qing Lian.

“However, taking into consideration that you still call me big sister, I’ll let you have three strikes first. It could also be considered as taking our sisterhood into account.”

After Murong Qi Qi said she’ll let her have three strikes, Murong Qing Lian felt great insult. Only when the opponent is very weak, would people let off three strikes as expressing mercy and sympathy to the other party. Now Murong Qi Qi, this ‘rubbish’ actually yielded strikes to her and it’s three strikes. How could Murong Qing Lian who has been always hailed as genius accept it?

“No need to yield strikes to me!”

Murong Qing Lian sneered.

“If big sister doesn’t beg me to yield strikes, then I’ll be satisfied. How would I let big sister yield strikes?!”

“Hehe, not bad. You’ve got backbone!”

Murong Qi Qi gave a chic smile without the coquettish of a woman. Instead it had a heroic aura. And the graceful bearing between her eyebrows, fell head-on on Longze Jing Tian and Li Yun Qing who were behind Murong Qing Lian, making them be dazzled.

“Exactly what I want!”

Even others could feel the thunder and lightning between the two daughters of Murong family. Wanyan Bao Zhu was far away. Although she couldn’t hear anything they said, but she could see that the two went against each other. Very good! Let them be like dogs eating dogs. Then she could benefit from it without doing anything!

One Murong Qi Qi, one Murong Qing Lian; these two people are the thorns in her heart. If she could get rid of two thorns through this tournament, won’t that satisfy everyone? The thought that at the coming finals, these two people would either be finished or both would suffer, Wanyan Bao Zhu felt happy from the bottom of her heart. It seemed that it was a correct decision to come watch the tournament today.

Four competitions. Three wins means victory. If two wins and two loses, then they need to select one contestant to compete once more. Everyone knew the rules very well. Now, they’re only waiting to see the process and results.

The first competition was Su Mei against Li Yun Qing.

After snowing for a few days, today is a rare clear day. An orange sun lazily hung at the horizon. Smilingly, it looked at the earth. Because the sun exposed its face, the snow began to melt. The temperature was very low. Su Mei’s face became red because of the cold air, like a blooming an Indian Azalea.

“Little Mei er[4. Er: term of endearment], fighting! I’ve your back! I support you!”

The competition hadn’t started yet, Wanyan Kang made a trumpet from his hand and began to shout, making Su Mei feel ashamed.

“Fighting, fighting! Little Mei er is the best! Little Mei er must win! Beat him!”

Wanyan Kang’s shouts reverberated at the venue. It happened that this venue was semicircular. The sound echoed here and there. One words seemed like a cry of a lot people, making Su Mei lose patient.

“Before, I come down the platform, if you make one more noise, I’ll sewn your mouth!”

Su Mei’s voice was very hard. Coupled with the help of the semicircular venue, it appeared increasingly ‘aggressive’.

“Little Mei er is so fierce! Very frightening!”

He’s not someone easy to deal with.

Although Su Mei wasn’t afraid of Li Yun Qing, but she was also very careful. This is the opening competition. She must make a beautiful battle of it to encourage the people after her. After making up her mind, Su Mei took out her weapon. It’s a half-moon-shaped dagger.
