Die or meet Wanyan Hong’s needs. She didn’t want to die. She was timid, afraid and still had hatred that she hadn't yet avenged. So, she couldn’t die! In contrast to Wanyan Bao Zhu, Wanyan Hong was satisfied like never before. He had never experienced such happiness. Whether it was men or women, none of them gave him such a wonderful feeling.

Don’t worry, next time, this crown prince wouldn’t be so rude and would definitely be very gentle. This thing, you will like it if you do it more. In the future, you won’t feel pain like today!”

There’s still in the future?! Wanyan Bao Zhu truly wanted to die. Originally, she thought there was only one time. She didn’t expect that this pervert still want to do it in the future. Didn’t this mean that she could never escape him?

As if seeing the grayness in Wanyan Bao Zhu’s eyes, Wanyan Hong went to her. His big hand grabbed her waist. The pain made Wanyan Bao Zhu wake up. Then her eyes met Wanyan Hong’s gloomy eyes.

“Don’t think of escaping! Even if you grew wings, this crown prince will break them!”

Wanyan Bao Zhu already had a profound understanding of Wanyan Hong’s personality. Towards such a pervert, she could only go along with him first and wait till she could find an opportunity to escape. Now, she doesn’t have any power to contend him. She could only go along with him.

Seeing that Wanyan Bao Zhu had a clear view of how things are, Wanyan Hong’s mood turned slightly better.

Wanyan Bao Zhu carefully nodded. The pain on her waist stimulated her. Why did this man like to pinch someone’s waist? Could it be that he didn’t know it would hurt?

Wanyan Hong loosened his hand and tightly hugged Wanyan Bao Zhu.

“Say, who provoked this crown prince’s most beloved little sister?”

“It’s…”

Wanyan Bao Zhu originally wanted to say Murong Qi Qi; however, now she thought about it, the only thing that could save her was the marriage to Longze Jing Tian. However, even a fool knew what kind of attitude Murong Qing Lian had towards Longze Jing Tian. Let’s first begin with Murong Qing Lian! After clearing all obstacles and marrying Longze Jing Tian, she would finally get away from Wanyan Hong, this pervert!

“Xi Qi’s Murong Qing Lian!”

“Murong Qing Lian? Ah, this crown prince knows. How do you want to do it? Directly kill her or…”

Of course, Wanyan Bao Zhu was willing to let Murong Qing Lian die. However, her dying wouldn’t solve many problems. If she could let Wanyan Hong transfer his target to Murong Qing Lian, how great would that be? In that case, she wouldn’t have to bear the pain of the body anymore! And she also can be together with Longze Jing Tian!

After determining her thoughts, Wanyan Bao Zhu snuggled in Wanyan Hong’s arms.

“Imperial big brother, Murong Qing Lian is a little pretty beauty. Beauties come from Murong family. Now, Murong Qing Lian is also very famous in Xi Liang (capital of Xi Qi). Why don’t imperial big brother take her into your palace and enjoy her?”

How could Wanyan Bao Zhu know that Wanyan Hong had long heard from Zhao Lang that her identity was Murong fu’s second miss Murong Xin Lian. Now, seeing her push her little sister out, Wanyan Hong had of course guessed what she was really thinking.

It was just, the mantis stalks the cicada, unaware of the oriole behind. Wanyan Bao Zhu only wanted to let Wanyan Hong get Murong Qing Lian, so he would change his target and let herself get free. How could she know that Wanyan Hong wanted to kill two birds with one stone; to let this pair of sisters stay with him!

She wanted to beg for mercy, but her head was pressed fiercely on the table by Wanyan Hong. Tears made the black hair, which hung loosely on the table, wet. This demon, this pervert! Wanyan Bao Zhu could only curse Wanyan Hong in her heart! However, all this hate and curses couldn’t change her current situation. She passed this night very sorrowfully…

Feng Cang’s and Murong Qi Qi’s wedding was set on the twenty-eight day of the first month. After Feng Cang came back, this news was spread out. After one night, everyone in the capital knew that demon wang[1. Wang/wangye: prince of first rank] was getting married again. Immediately, Yan Capital soared. Everyone was talking about Feng Cang’s ninth bride.

“Miss, just now, the bets were flying everywhere outside. Everyone was betting that you wouldn’t live past the wedding night.”

Su Yue peeled the walnut and put it in front of Murong Qi Qi.

“The gambling dens once again began to set bets!”

“Oh?” Murong Qi Qi yawned lazily. “Did you get the money that I won before? How much is it?”

The moment they heard Murong Qi Qi mention the money, Su Mei and Su Yue laughed. Those fools bet that Bei Zhou would lose! Ah, why didn’t they look at what kind of person their miss is?!

“It’s someone from Fo Sheng Men.”

Su Mei was surprised for a moment and told the result of Wu Qing’s investigation to Murong Qi Qi.

“Fo Sheng Men?”

Murong Qi Qi’s beautiful brow frowned. Eventually, it turned back to normal.

“It seems that Long Ao Tian has brains. This time, he has earned sixty million!”

“That’s right, ah, miss! Now, everyone at the gambling dens are betting that you won’t live past the wedding night, but Long Ao Tian’s people once again bet ten million, betting that you will survive the wedding night!”

Now, Murong Qi Qi became interested. She sat up and repeatedly thought about the issues of Long Ao Tian.

“Could it be that he knows my identity?”

“How could that be?! Aside from our people, no one would know miss’s identity. Our own people won’t tell anyone. Long Ao Tian’s news couldn’t be so fast and abundant!”

Now, Murong Qi Qi felt it was strange. Since, her identity wasn’t exposed, then why would the leader of Fo Sheng Men repeatedly bet on her? Could it be that he predicted or did he bet according to his mood? Really strange!

She didn’t understand the reason so Murong Qi Qi simply changed into clothes to travel. She left Tingsong building, left wangfu[2. Wangfu: residence of the prince of first-rank] and went shopping.

Now, the thing they lacked to build the number one place of business was money. Every day, they needed to spend a lot of money because Moyu had so many people.
