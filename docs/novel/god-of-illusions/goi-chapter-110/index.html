I’m finished, I’m finished, I f*cked it up!

“Haha! I was right! You really are Bai Xiaofei!” The young woman exclaimed once more with surprise, and it turned out that she was just probing him earlier….

Bai Xiaofei wished for nothing more than to give himself a fierce slap. I was wise at all times, but why did I do such a foolish thing!?

“Err…. Fellow Student, you said you would help me!”

I’m finished, the prying soul of this girl has been lit!

But, Bai Xiaofei couldn’t care about all this now, and he only hoped that this girl would keep to her word and help him with this ‘small task’.

“Boss Bai, wait here. I’ll go bring Big Sister Xian’er here for you! The young woman smiled as if she’d found the most amusing thing in the world, and she skipped along back towards the dormitory.

Bai Xiaofei heaved a long sigh of relief while he waited with a perturbed feeling in his heart. He kept having the feeling that this young woman wasn’t so simple, and he would probably have to experience some sort of terrifying thing in a moment.

As he thought like this, Bai Xiaofei had already started to anticipate various situations because preparedness averts peril!

On the other hand, just as Bai Xiaofei had imagined, after the young woman returned to the dormitory, she really didn’t look for Hu Xian’er at the first possible moment, and she ran back to her own dorm room with extreme excitement instead before exaggerating about the matter of Bai Xiaofei being downstairs to everyone within her dorm room.

After that, everyone in the room were lit up by the prying flames of the young woman, and then all of them charged out excitedly to spread the news to all their other friends….

How enormous was the network of relationships possessed by women? Thus, in less than 10 minutes, various versions of ‘Bai Xiaofei chasing after Hu Xian’er without letting up’ became a matter than was known to all within this entire building.

Of course, the young woman could be considered to have completed her mission because Hu Xian’er received this news as well.

With a trace of bewilderment in her heart, Hu Xian’er wore he clothes with slight hesitation before walking down the dorm.

On the other hand, at this moment, Bai Xiaofei felt his head was on the verge of exploding.

He’d indeed anticipated numerous situations, but this situation was really not within his expectations! An entire 100 plus chattering female students had encircled him like they were visiting the zoo while various questions emerged endlessly, causing Bai Xiaofei to not dare make a single sound.

If he were to make a sound now, then he would be courting death!!

God damn it! The words of a woman really can’t be trusted! Especially when she says she’ll absolutely not spread news of something!!!

“Boss Bai, you just spoke with me earlier, but it’s troublesome for me if you don’t speak now!” The young woman that he spoke to earlier moved over to stand before him, and her face carried a trace of anxiety because if Bai Xiaofei didn’t speak, then it would seem like she was spreading a rumor.

However, Bai Xiaofei still didn’t make any reaction.

You f*cker! You f*cking trapped me like this and you expect me to answer you?!

It’s best if you’re abandoned by everyone!

However, Bai Xiaofei had thought of the matter in a way that was too simple. The methods of torture that a young woman possessed was much more than a man. Not to mention Bai Xiaofei was in her territory now.

“Aunty is just outside, so if we don’t cover for you, then do you think she’ll be able to see through your disguise if she’s standing right in front of you?” A single sentence caused Bai Xiaofei to instantly concede….

“What do you want me to say!?” Bai Xiaofei practically gnashed his teeth as he spoke, whereas, just a simple sentence like this caused an explosive reaction in the surrounding girls.

“Wow! It really spoke!”

“Based on the voice, it really is like that Bai Xiaofei!”

“Wow! How romantic! He actually pursued her all the way to the female dormitory!”

Various voices sounded out in a clamorous wave while Bai Xiaofei even had the heart to kill himself now. There was no need for him to think about it because he would absolutely become famous by tomorrow morning….

But, right when Bai Xiaofei was in despair, Hu Xian’er walked over.

As he looked at the slim and graceful Hu Xian’er, Bai Xiaofei suddenly had a feeling of relief, and he forgot all adverse impacts this would cause as he directly removed Blackie’s simulation….
