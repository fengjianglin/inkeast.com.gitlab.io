“Looks like you’ve chosen the latter! Then I can only let Huskie benefit.” Bai Xiaofei grabbed Huskie up as he spoke while his left hand pressed down upon the opening at the top of the cage, and he didn’t say another word before opening the cage and pushing Huskie in.

The black cat tried to run out instantly when Bai Xiaofei opened the cage, but the opening on the cage was too small, and it was directly pushed back in by Bai Xiaofei.

“Motherf*cker! You actually dared to scratch me! Just see how I deal with you!” As he spoke, the scene inside the cage was in a mess. Within the small space, the black cat scratched Huskie without end, and in the blink of an eye, Huskie was already shivering and forced to a corner.

But, right at this moment, Bai Xiaofei stretched out his hand and placed it on the black cat while he circulated the Lunisolar Brilliance cultivation technique, causing a strand of Origin Energy that carried the pink colored energy to be extracted from his Origin Core.

Bai Xiaofei didn’t dare utilize too much and just controlling this strand already exhausted Bai Xiaofei to the point he was drenched in sweat. But, this strand was already sufficient for the black cat’s build!

Meow!!! Meow!!!

Right at the instant when Bai Xiaofei’s Origin Energy was about to invade the black cat’s body, the black cat instantly let out two miserable cries, and then it stopped attacking Bai Xiaofei and Huskie.

“What is this meaning of this? You’re submitting? But, what if I don’t have the mood anymore?” As he hurriedly withdrew the energy back into his Origin Core, Bai Xiaofei wiped the beads of sweat that seeped out of his forehead.

Motherf*cker! This energy is really f*cking dangerous!

In merely that instant from before, Bai Xiaofei was already exhausted to the point his mind was slightly dazed….

Meow! Meow! Meow!

The black cat cried out anxiously, and then it looked at Huskie before raising its claw and placing it on Bai Xiaofei’s hand.

Its round eyes were closed tightly, and it seem extremely alike to criminal that was sent to the execution grounds.

You bastard! I told you that I’d surely be able to deal with you!

Even though he was unable to understand what the black cat meant now, its reaction was sufficient to explain everything.

As he sneered with extreme complacency, Bai Xiaofei gradually calmed down.

The process of forming a contract wasn’t as simple as one would imagine. Especially when one formed a contract with an animate puppet because the slightest carelessness would cause one to go mad!

But, with the black cat’s cooperation, the entire process was completely rather smoothly.

After it was completed successfully, Bai Xiaofei heaved a long sigh of relief, and as he felt the connection that appeared within his mind, he couldn’t help but reveal a slight smile.

“Good girl!” As he spoke, Bai Xiaofei grinned as he opened up the cage. In the next second, the black cat shot out from within like a bolt of lightning, and it directly swung its claws at Bai Xiaofei. However, its figure became extremely stiff before it could even approach Bai Xiaofei.

The relationship between a puppet and a puppet master was always one of being controlled and control. This was something that was determined at the instant the contract was formed!

“Little Fellow, if you dare do that again, then I guarantee that the incident from before will occur once more. Moreover, I don’t even have to put you in heat now!” Bai Xiaofei spoke fiercely, causing the black cat to instantly become listless.

“Since you’re completely black, I’ll call you Blackie in the future. Don’t hope that I’ll give you a better name because this is already my limit!” As he spoke, the black cat didn’t dare make the slightest sound, causing Blackie to become its name from now on.

“Huskie, you did well. I’ll give you an extra meal when we get back.” Subsequently, Bai Xiaofei carried Huskie out, and a single sentence from him caused the originally upset Huskie to instantly become excited.

The nearby Blackie couldn’t help but give Huskie a glance of disdain.

Stupid husky, you were bribed with just a single meal!

“Come, let me see what ability you’ve given me!” As he spoke, Bai Xiaofei whose tone was filled with anticipation placed his hand on Blackie because all his hopes were placed here!

“Haha!!! The heavens are kind to me!!!” Bai Xiaofei howled towards the sky before holding up Blackie and rubbing it furiously while complacency was written all over his face.

At the same time, a new plan had appeared within Bai Xiaofei’s mind!

……

“Vice Principal Luo?! What’re you doing here?” The Great Barrier God was patrolling at her post when Luo Xi who appeared abruptly caused her to be slightly shocked.

But, when facing her question, ‘Vice Principal Luo’ didn’t reply.

“Principal? Did you come to look for someone?” Along with the Great Barrier God’s approach, a trace of panic obviously appeared on Luo Xi’s face, and such an obvious change in expression was naturally unable to escape the eyes of  the great god.

“You aren’t Vice Principal Luo!” She decided instantaneously, and then the aunty directly raised up her broom. In the next second, Luo Xi instantly transformed into Bai Xiaofei. At the same time, he assumed the posture of intending to charge into the female dorms.

“You stinking brat! If I don’t teach you a proper lesson today, then you won’t know how the word death is written!” The aunty raised her broom and struck towards ‘Bai Xiaofei’ to the point ‘Bai Xiaofei’ fled all the way back. But, the aunty didn’t notice that a slight change had unknowingly occurred in the surrounding scenery.

For example, there seemed to be an extra tree at a certain direction….

The ability Blackie gave Bai Xiaofei was scenery simulation!

Within a certain area, Blackie was capable of changing the scenery at will. Moreover, it was capable of causing a portion of the created scenery to become material. At the same time, it could change the outer appearance of some things in the area.

Thus, at a corner that the Great Barrier God wasn’t paying attention to, Bai Xiaofei had transformed from a tree into a trash can, and then into a water bucket….

All along the way, Bai Xiaofei made Blackie help him turn into anything that might be there, and every single time the aunty felt something was wrong, Blackie would make the surroundings materialize, causing the aunty that touched a material object to dispel her doubts.

Just like this, Bai Xiaofei snuck all the way into the female dorms!

Moreover, fortunately, the division of the female dorms was basically similar to the male forms, and he just had to move towards the area that the new students would be in the male dorms.

Hu Xian’er was the 21st amongst those that were recruited internally, and Bai Xiaofei moved according to the division of the male dorms to quickly lock onto the dorm building that Hu Xian’er lived in.

But, a new problem followed along with this.

He didn’t know which floor Hu Xian’er was at! And he was unable to find her!

Entering the building to look for her wasn’t realistic because he didn’t dare do that even if Blackie was able to help him accomplish it. Trespassing into the female dorms was already liable for the ‘death penalty’, and if he were to enter the dormitory building, then he would probably have to be dismembered at the very least!

After that, Bai Xiaofei who’d already turned into a trash can fell into deep contemplation.

It just so happened that right at this moment, a female student that wore her pajamas walked out from the dorm building, and she was carrying a bag of trash.

“Eh? When was there a trash can here?” The female student muttered to herself, and her tone carried a trace of bewilderment.

“Cheh, who cares!? Isn’t it better that I can walk a few steps less!?” The female student walked over towards Bai Xiaofei as she spoke, and she assumed the posture of intending to toss it over from afar.

“If it’s possible, I hope you don’t toss trash on me.” Bai Xiaofei who was anxious because he didn’t have a way to find Hu Xian’er had a sudden flash of inspiration when he saw the young woman, and a bold plan surfaced within his mind.

Life and death, success and failure, I can only take a gamble!

He was betting that this female student was an idiot!
