At the Allheaven, there was no true ruler, and there was only Brahma Qi that enveloped the universe.

The Golden Immortal Realm was also known as the Allheaven Golden Immortal Realm, and this was a form of praise towards this realm. It described that after an Immortal attained the Golden Immortal Realm, the Immortal Energy in the body would transform and contain the true qi of Brahma. It was capable of enveloping the universe and creating a new world. It possessed boundless ability, and it was like the lofty and boundless sky.

In the Immortal Dimension, existences at the Golden Immortal Realm were already considered as central pillars and overlords of an area!

Zhu Yunsou was only a Heavenly Immortal and was even inferior to a Mysterious Immortal, so how could he have imagined that his opponent would be a Golden Immortal? If he knew this at the beginning, then he would have probably fled for his life a long time ago.

Unfortunately, there was no room for regret in the matters of this world.

Right after he howled loudly, a vast energy of light enveloped down, and the dazzling and resplendent light covered his body, causing others to be unable to see exactly what was happening.

“AH!!! How could this be possible? How could a Golden Immortal be allowed to exist beneath the Laws of the Heaven Dao in the Mortal Dimension!? How could this be possible!!?” A miserable and terrified shrill cry sounded out, and it was extremely horrifying and caused everyone to be shocked.

The men and women by Zhu Yunsou’s side originally intended to lend a hand, yet they were sternly stopped by Granny Fei Jiu because that was the laws of light, and it contained Brahma Qi. Merely being tainted slightly by it would cause one’s body and soul to be destroyed!

In next to no time, Zhu Yunsou’s  shrill cries stopped abruptly.

The light dispersed, causing everyone’s fields of visions to recover its clarity, yet they were unable to find a trace of Zhu Yunsou any longer. He seemed as if he’d evaporated into thin air, and he didn’t even leave behind the slightest trace of aura and had been completely obliterated.

This sort of scene was even more shocking that a bloody one because that was a Heavenly Immortal, yet he’d vanished so suddenly, so what sort of pain had he suffered before he died?

No one was able to imagine it, and it was precisely because of this that it was even more shocking and terrifying.

A Golden Immortal!

In an instant, the gazes Fu Yun and the others looked at Qing Xiuyi with carried heartfelt respect and admiration. If this happened in the mortal world, then she could have even been taken to be a god and worshipped in a temple.

On the other hand, the expressions of Granny Fei Jiu and the others was bleak and filled with shock. They seemed to have become completely weak, and they’d lost their ferocious and formidable auras from before.

A terrifying existence like a Golden Immortal wasn’t allowed to exist in the Mortal Dimension at all. This was an iron law that was publicly acknowledge in the three dimensions. Yet it just so happened that such an existence was right before their eyes, so how could they dare hold any hope in their hearts?

“All of you seem to be waiting for someone?” Qing Xiuyi lowered her head to look at the ground. An hourglass stood there all alone while the dreamlike and multicolored grains of sand within it had almost flowed completely.

This was a very baffling sentence, and it caused Fu Yun and the others to be bewildered.

Only Chen Xi vaguely noticed something. The grains of sand within the hourglass on the floor was roughly able to persist for the time for an incense stick to burn. These fellows from the Immortal Dimension had come in an overbearing manner, yet they intentionally set up a deceptive battle array and used the time of an incense stick to burn as the time limit for Fu Yun and the others to submit. Wasn’t this a little unnecessary?

The only explanation was that they were ordered to the Spirit Radiance Hall, yet they hadn’t received orders on how to deal with Fu Yun and the others. So they used the time of an incense stick to burn as the limit in order to await the return of the person that pulled the strings behind the scene.

Sure enough, the expressions of Granny Fei Jiu and the others turned pale as soon as Qing Xiuyi finished speaking, and the last remaining trace of persistence in their hearts crumbled with a bang as they revealed expressions of despair.

Even this had been seen through by Qing Xiuyi, so could any good outcome be awaiting them?

“Seize this opportunity and speak of your objective for coming here. All of you can keep quiet, yet I guarantee that all of you’ll die before your backup arrives.”

But to their surprise, Qing Xiuyi didn’t kill them and asked about their objectives instead.

Yeah, why did these fellows descend from the Immortal Dimension?

This was what Fu Yun and the others were puzzled about.

No matter what, Qing Xiuyi’s words caused Granny Fei Jiu and the others to heave a sigh of relief. They firmly believed that so long as they waited for their backup to arrive, then the situation before their eyes could surely be turned around, allowing them to escape death.

“Actually, we’re from the same sect. But we’re from the Immortal Dimension’s Nine Radiance Sword Sect, and we received the orders of the Immortal Monarch to reclaim the sect’s Saint Artifact, the Dao Calamity Sword…” Granny Fei Jiu took a deep breath and restrained the terror in her heart as she spoke slowly.

According to what she said, Mei Luoxiao and Yu Zhongxia from the sect had came over some time ago yet hadn’t returned after a long time. Later on, they noticed that the Soul Plates of Mei Luoxiao and Yu Zhongxia had shattered, and they’d perished in the Mortal Dimension.

This discovered caused the Nine Radiance Sword Sect in the Immortal Dimension to be shocked, and the Immortal Monarch was furious. Thus he sent them to the Mortal Dimension to investigate the reason and reclaim the Dao Calamity Sword.

Everyone was stunned and didn’t dare believe their ears after Granny Fei Jiu finished speaking. Only Fu Yun revealed a cold smile. The Nine Radiance Sword Sect in the Immortal Dimension? Who’re you trying to fool!?

“You’re lying!” Fu Yun immediately spoke bluntly.

But when he was about to speak of the reason, he forcefully refrained himself instead because this matter was only known to the higher-ups of the sect. Moreover, news of it had been locked down a long time ago. If this information were to be revealed, then it would probably cause great waves to arrive in the sect and everyone would feel they were in danger.

Granny Fei Jiu frowned from being criticized by a Earthly Immortal, and she said, “This is the truth.”

“She really is lying.” Meanwhile, Chen Xi spoke coldly.

He was clearly aware of this matter, and he’d even personally witnessed Evil Lotus kill Mei Luoxiao and Yu Zhongxia. Perhaps everything Granny Fei Jiu said was true, yet what she said about the Nine Radiance Sword Sect in the Immortal Dimension was a lie.

Because at this moment, the forces of the Nine Radiance Sword Sect in the Immortal Dimension had been annihilated by the sect’s enemies a long time ago!

“Looks like you’re looking for death.” Perhaps Qing Xiuyi wouldn’t believe Fu Yun, but she’d never doubted what Chen Xi said.

So as soon as he finished speaking, she’d already made a move. Light surged as it converged into surging energy of the laws, and it enveloped down at Granny Fei Jiu.

Granny Fei Jiu’s expression changed rapidly. She was unable to figure out how they’d seen through her lies, and she’d never imagined that Qing Xiuyi would actually make a move so decisively without giving her the slightest chance to explain.

“Don’t kill me! I’ll tell you! I’ll tell you!!” Granny Fei Jiu intended to flee yet was utterly incapable of escaping the restraint of light. Her entire body was instantly enveloped as she let out a miserable and horrified sharp cry.

Unfortunately, Qing Xiuyi wasn’t interested to listen to her at all. Light surged and directly incinerated Granny Fei Jiu, causing her to vanish into thin air and follow in Zhu Yunsou’s footsteps.

Instantly, only those men and women that followed behind Zhu Yunsou and Granny Fei Jiu remained here, and all of them were terrified to the point their souls almost left their bodies and were in a constant state of anxiety.

Before Qing Xiuyi could speak, one of them couldn’t refrain from crying out. “I’ll speak, I’ll speak. I beg Senior to spare my life…”

Bang!

He’d only spoken halfway when a strand of Light Flames fluttered down onto him and instantaneously incinerated him into nothingness.

“Time’s up.” Qing Xiuyi pointed to the ground. Within the hourglass, the multicolored grains of sand had piled up on the bottom, and there weren’t any grains of sand pouring down anymore.

Her icy cold, indifferent, and emotionless attitude almost tortured them to the point of falling into madness.

“Bastard! Golden Immortal my ass! All of you can dream of continuing to live after you kill members of my Misty Immortal Mountain!” A man couldn’t restrain the terror in his heart any longer, and he cried out madly.

Misty Immortal Mountain! 

A bright light flashed in Chen Xi’s eyes as he firmly remembered this name.

If he wasn’t wrong, then the power that this Misty Immortal Mountain represented was the culprit that annihilated the Nine Radiance Sword Sect!

Coupled with what Dao Lotus and Evil Lotus had said, the person that controlled this power might even be one of the enemies that schemed against the Chaotic Divine Lotus all those years ago!

When he thought up to here, Chen Xi made a signal to Qing Xiuyi, and she instantly came to an understanding. She stopped wasting time and casually flicked her sleeve, causing all of those men and women to be instantly annihilated.

Her direct and decisive methods of slaughter and casual expression caused Fu Yun and the others to be horrified, and they felt even more reverence towards Qing Xiuyi.

They were truly unable to imagine how Qing Xiuyi had actually leaped up to become a Golden Immortal in such a short period of time.

Not to mention them, even Chen Xi felt indescribable shock when he found out about this, and if it wasn’t for him having witnessed Qing Xiuyi’s ability, he would almost not dare believe it.

“Since you’ve come, do you think you can leave?” Right at this moment, Qing Xiuyi seemed to have noticed something, and her clear eyes were suffused with a wisp of a piercingly cold sheen. As she spoke, she’d already vanished on the spot.

Everyone was stunned, and then they came to an understanding that Qing Xiuyi had probably gone to pursue the backup that Zhu Yunsou and the others had been waiting for all along.

But no matter what, as soon as Qing Xiuyi left, everyone present here heaved a sigh of relief in unison. It couldn’t be helped, the might of a Golden Immortal was too formidable. Even if it wasn’t directed at them, it caused them to feel pressure that came from the depths of their souls.

“Mother will…” Chen An spoke in a low voice, and his handsome face revealed a wisp of worry.

“It’s fine, she’ll be back soon.” Chen Xi consoled with a warm voice.

“Uncle, when did Aunty become so formidable? I remember that during the Allstar Meeting all those years ago, Aunt’s strength seemed to be inferior to you.” The nearby Chen Yu spoke with admiration.

“The Allstar Meeting…” In his daze, Chen Xi recalled that all those years when he participated in the Allstar Meeting in Silken City, he’d defeated Qing Xiuyi to win the position of first.

At that time, this little fellow, Chen Yu, was only around seven years old. In the blink of an eye, the little fellow from all those years ago had already grown into a valiant young man.

On the other hand, hadn’t An’er grown up like this as well?

“Your Aunt isn’t an ordinary person.” Chen Xi sighed emotionally with a smile on his face.

These words were something that Fu Yun and the others agreed to.

Qing Xiuyi was naturally not an ordinary person. She was able to overcome nine levels of the Heavenly Tribulation a few thousands of years ago, and she was publicly acknowledged as a proud genius of the Dark Reverie. Now that she’d experienced one hundred reincarnations and eliminated the karma of her previous lives, her footsteps on the path of cultivation had already arrived at a height that others could only look up to!

Thump!

Meanwhile, a black shadow was tossed into the hall, and it fell to the ground and rolled like a gourd.

At the same time, Qing Xiuyi’s figure appeared once more by Chen Xi’s side, and she said indifferently, “This fellow is their backup, an expert at the Mysterious Immortal Realm. I think that he probably knows even more, so I brought him back.”
