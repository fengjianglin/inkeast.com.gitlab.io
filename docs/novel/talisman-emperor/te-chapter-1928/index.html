Chen Xi! Donghuang Yinxuan and Leng Xinghun’s pupils constricted when they saw Chen Xi’s figure, and a wisp of gloominess appeared on their faces.

They’d discerned that Chen Xi was a Region Lord now, and he’d become an existence that was equal to them!

However, there were still unable to figure out how Chen Xi had actually advanced successfully at the last minute when his body had clearly been destroyed and only his Soul Divine Flame remained!

This doesn’t make sense!

But no matter what, all of this had occurred. Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan were clearly aware that it was impossible for them to kill Tu Meng now.

But fortunately, Tu Meng was already on the verge of death while Gu Yan and A’Liang weren’t Region Lords in the end, so they weren’t threats as well.

So, the most difficult to deal with was naturally Chen Xi!

While they were still at the Universe Enlightened Ancestral God Realm, both Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan had lost miserably at Chen Xi’s hands, and they’d taken it to be an extraordinary humiliation.

This time, they thought they wouldn’t have to do anything, yet Chen Xi would definitely perish in the end. But who would have imagined that Chen Xi would actually advance into the rank of Region Lords in such an inconceivable manner and instantly become an extraordinarily great threat to them?

Fortunately, they weren’t facing him alone this time as they were both together, so they weren’t afraid of Chen Xi.

Even if we’re unable to kill this fellow. How could we possibly be defeated by him?

This was the point of view that both Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan held.

So, in merely an instant, both of their gazes carried a wisp of arrogance and indifference once more as they gazed at Chen Xi.

“I never expected that you would be so tenacious. But unfortunately, you’ve encountered the two of us again, and you’re bound to face a horrible end.” Donghuang Yinxuan smiled lightly while his voice carried a wisp of confidence and provocation as well.

As he spoke, Leng Xinghun and him split up to stand at two sides of this starry sky to stand at a position to launch a pincer attack against Chen Xi, and then they faintly entered into confrontation with Chen Xi who stood in the distance.

The atmosphere here emanated an oppressive and suffocating aura of slaughter.

Merely the imposing aura created from a confrontation between Region Lords was sufficient to throw the world into disorder and turn it upside down!

However, Chen Xi seemed as if he hadn’t noticed all of this at all.

He flicked his sleeve and brought out Tu Meng who he’d just rescued. As he gazed at Tu Meng who was covered in injuries and drenched in blood, Chen Xi said, “Watch properly as Martial Ancestral Uncle takes revenge for you.”

His voice was calm and didn’t contain even a trace of emotion.

For some unknown reason, Tu Meng’s heart suddenly felt warm when he heard these words, and his eyes turned red while he was actually almost unable to restrain himself from tearing up.

The body and mind of boorish fellow that had an irritable and straightforward disposition had suffered extreme devastation after experiencing the extremely cruel torture inflicted by Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan. He was already on the verge of death, and despair had arisen in his heart.

But who would have imagined that he would actually be saved at the critical moment? This simply felt like he was on the verge of death and experienced a cycle of reincarnation, causing him to feel uncontrollably happy.

So, when he heard Chen Xi, he’d revealed such an expression.

The killing intent Chen Xi had suppressed in his heart grew even thicker when he witnessed this scene, yet his expression grew even more calm and indifferent.

He patted Tu Meng on the shoulder and didn’t say anything before he turned around to look at Donghuang Yinxuan and Leng Xinghun who stood in the distance.

In an instant, Chen Xi seemed as if he’d become a completely different person. His entire body emanated an invisible murderous aura while his eyes were deep, icy cold, and completely devoid of emotion.

“Let’s put an end to the enmity between us!” His indifferent voice drifted through the starry sky.

Leng Xinghun’s eyes narrowed slightly before he suddenly roared with laughter. “Put an end to it? With your life? This suggestion isn’t bad.”

“Perhaps, this fellow thinks that he can run wild and look down upon everyone after he became a Region Lord.” Donghuang Yinxuan laughed with disdain.

Put an end to it?

This fellow, Chen Xi, really dares to brag!

Leng Xinghun started laughing, and so did Donghuang Yinxuan.

But Chen Xi didn’t laugh. His expression grew even more calm and indifferent. He was like an unconcerned party that locked onto the two of them with a completely emotionless gaze.

For some unknown reason, Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan felt slightly uncomfortable when Chen Xi stared at them with such a gaze, and it was like they were dead to Chen Xi.

“Since you’ve finished laughing, then it’s time to be on your way….” Amidst his icy cold and indifferent voice, Chen Xi’s clothed fluttered as he strode through space in a neither fast nor slow pace, and he was like an imperial monarch who was patrolling his territory.

“Bastard! Let me see where you got the gall to actually be so arrogant!” Amidst a ghastly and loud shout, the killing intent throughout Leng Xinghun’s body seethed as he launched the first attack.

Bang!

Leng Xinghun’s blood red hair fluttered while he seemed mighty and majestic, and the blade, Thundervoid, appeared in his palm. The black colored blade flowed with clear colored Region Energy as it soared into the sky and shattered the space before it.

After that, it slashed down forcefully!

In an instant, a storm and violent winds raged, and the world instantly turned black. It was like the end of the world was here.

Crack!

Space completely collapsed, causing countless rifts to split open. These rifts were huge and boundless, and they densely covered the surroundings as they swept out, causing the surroundings to be thrown into disorder.

At this moment, a completely round screen of light had appeared 1m above Leng Xinghun. The screen of light glistened with clear radiance, and it seemed like a region was floating within it while a myriad of stars had converged in it. It caused Leng Xinghun’s imposing aura to seem mighty, dignified, and supreme to the extreme.

This was the imposing aura of a Region Lord!

The completely round screen of light was the Region Energy possessed by Region Lords!

Bang!

At practically the exact same time, Chen Xi attacked as well. He stood towering on the spot without moving at all, and he merely slapped his palm out lightly, causing brilliant violet gold divine radiance to effuse out.

In an instant, Leng Xinghun’s attack that revealed terrifying might was shattered into powder by this strike of Chen Xi’s palm, and it transformed into nothingness!

“Kill!” Leng Xinghun’s pupils constricted while he struck once more with Thundervoid. The peerless Region Energy he possessed transformed into burning light that converged onto the edge of his blade, and then it swept out like an unstoppable ocean as it shot towards Chen Xi.

“Kill!” Before this attack could hit, Leng Xinghun roared loudly once more, and he executed thousands of slashes with Thundervoid in an instant.

His blood red hair fluttered while two brilliant blood red arcs of lightning shot out from his eyes, and he seemed to be mysterious and terrifying amidst the illumination of this divine radiance.

At this moment, he’d fully displayed the might of a Region Lord, and his imposing aura filled the starry sky, crushed all things, and revealed a terrifyingly dignified aura.

There was no doubt that Leng Xinghun didn’t underestimate Chen Xi at all at this moment, and he’d utilized his true strength as soon as he’d attacked!

Bang!

At practically the exact same time, Donghuang Yinxuan attacked ferociously from the other side.

He held the Dao Control Brush in hand while he activated the Taixuan Codex. Every single stroke of the brush of like the embodiment of the Grand Dao, and it formed a myriad of profundities of the Grand Dao, emanated a brilliant glow of the Dao, and possessed boundless might.

At this moment, Donghuang Yinxuan had utilized his full strength as well. His aura was terrifying while his entire body was suffused by powerful Region Energy, and he formed a link with the Grand Dao, carried the might of the world, and could destroy everything that stood before him!

Rumble!

When two Region Lords attacked at the same time, such a terrifying attack caused this expanse of the starry sky to seem as if it was on the verge of collapse and destruction, and it rumbled violently.

It was too terrifying!

It was beyond imagination!

When the might of a Universe Enlightened Ancestral God was compared to this, it was simply like a tiny pearl trying to compete in brilliance with the moon.

Tu Meng’s heart trembled violently as he resided in the distance. These attacks were too terrifying, and if it was him in Chen Xi’s place, then not to mention resisting them, he would be instantly obliterated by these attacks.

Will Martial Ancestral Uncle be able to… resist these attacks?

…

In an instant, Chen Xi was covered by boundless divine radiance, and his entire figure was drowned beneath all sorts of terrifying and peerless attacks.

His figure couldn’t be found anymore!

Gulp!

Tu Meng’s expression changed abruptly. How could this have happened?

“Looks like this fellow just advanced and has clearly not fully grasped the strength of a Region Lord. Hahaha!” Leng Xinghun roared with laughter while his blood red hair fluttered behind him. His laughter shook the world, and his gaze was brilliant like the sun. He seemed horrifying.

“That’s very normal. Even if it was any other Region Lord, that person would probably find it difficult to resist our joint forces a swell.” Donghuang Yinxuan laughed coldly.

Even though they spoke like this, they felt a slight lack of confidence in their hearts because according to their knowledge, Chen Xi would definitely not make such a weak display.

Swish!

Sure enough, in the next moment, a strand of brilliant violet gold divine radiance suddenly shot into the sky, and it seemed extremely dazzling.

It transformed into a completely round screen of light, and an extremely enormous region circulated within it. Numerous universes circulated while a myriad of stars flickered. It was linked to the heavens and the earth, and it simply seemed like a real region. Moreover, it emanated a supremely dignified aura that was sufficient to make all living being submit before it!

What’s that? Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan’s hearts shook fiercely while they felt disbelief.

A completely round screen of light had surged into appearance above them as well, and it was their Region Energy. But when compared with the Region Energy before them, it simply paled in comparison!

It was even to the extent of being utterly impossible to compare!

Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan almost wondered whether it was really Region Energy because it was too vast and magnificent.

“Is that the extent of your abilities?” Amidst his indifferent voice, Chen Xi’s figure appeared within the blazing divine radiance. He was handsome, mighty, and completely bathed in violet gold divine radiance. He seemed like a peerless imperial sovereign, a supreme overlord!

Along with his appearance, the extremely grand Region Energy had started to circulate according to his vital energy, and as it circulated, it caused Chen Xi’s imposing aura to seem even more boundless.

“Dammit!” Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan’s expression changed simultaneously. They finally dared to confirm that not only was this fellow, Chen Xi, completely unharmed, the strength Chen Xi possessed had completely exceeded their expectations!

“Kill!”

“This kid is difficult to deal with! We must go all out!” Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan practically instinctively attacked ferocious once more. They circulated their entire cultivations and were like two primeval gods of war that revealed monstrous might.

At this moment, they fully understood that Chen Xi wasn’t weak like they’d guessed, and he was strong to a point that even they were filled with extreme fear instead!

The inconceivably grand Region Energy revealed by the completely round screen of light as the best proof.

Bang!

Leng Xinghun held Thundervoid in his hand while his entire body was covered by strands of clear colored divine chains as he attacked. His imposing aura had been raised to its peak, and his essence, energy, and spirit seemed as if they were on fire.

This strike was clearly much stronger when compared to the attacks from before, and it seemed like he was fighting desperately.

Bang!

But in merely an instant, a deafening sound of collision resounded. A huge palm that carried violet gold divine radiance directly pierced through the layers of clear colored divine chains before slapping onto Thundervoid!

After that, Leng Xinghun seemed as if he’d been struck by lightning. His entire body trembled violently before his figure was forcefully blasted flying.
