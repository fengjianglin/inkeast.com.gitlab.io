After Heaven Trampler joined the group, they continued towards the depths of the God Attainment Region.

On the way, Chen Xi couldn’t help but ask Heaven Trampler about his father.

However, Heaven Trampler’s answer was slightly disappointing to Chen Xi.

Because Heaven Trampler had only occasionally heard some information about Chen Lingjun a few years ago, and he didn’t know of Chen Lingjun’s exact whereabouts.

This caused Chen Xi to have no choice but to put this worry aside and ask about Zhen Liuqing and the others.

“They’ve all cultivated well. When you’re returning to the Immortal Dimension, you can make a trip to the Divine Abyss of Darkness, and you can naturally meet them there.” Heaven Trampler didn’t speak too much about them and just gave him a simple reply.

“Alright.” Chen Xi nodded and went silent.

He was able to sense that they were getting closer and closer to the God Attainment Altar. So, Shi Yu, Xiangliu Li, Dian Dian, and Heaven Trampler had become much more careful, and they naturally didn’t have the time to bother about other trivial matters.

Two days later.

“We’ve arrived!” Shi Yu who led the group suddenly stopped moving, and he actually revealed a rare trace of burning anticipation.

We’ve arrived?

The spirits of everyone was refreshed, and they raised their heads in unison.

The expanse of space in the distance was completely chaotic, and it enveloped their entire path forward. It was like the end of this expanse of space, and there was an ancient palace surprisingly standing within the chaotic mist!

The palace was lofty and imposing, and it possessed a powerful aura. It possessed multiple layers of eaves, and it was like a pagoda that was formed from a single continuous beam.

This ancient palace was extremely large like a divine palace established within the chaotic mist. It towered into the sky while its surroundings were suffused with chaotic qi, and numerous dazzling and resplendent stars surrounded it while emanating blazing light.

When looked at from afar, this palace was extremely lofty, sublime, and unreachable.

When they stood before it, they were simply tiny like ants, and it was even to the extent they were even inferior to a tile of the divine palace!

There was an arched bridge before the divine palace, and it formed a Heaven Ladder that linked this area to the divine palace.

It was a true ladder of the heavens. Every single step seemed like an expanse of the heavens and the earth, and it rose up high to a height that aroused awe in the hearts of all!

The God Attainment Palace!

As they gazed at this ancient palace that seemed like a divine manifestation, Shi Yu, Xiangliu Li, Dian Dian, and Heaven Trampler faintly revealed a trace of excitement at this moment.

This was a mysterious and ancient place that had existed for countless years since the prehistoric times. Since the ancient times, it had drawn over innumerable Immortal Kings that intended to find the method to become a god and surpass the three dimensions.

Presently, it had actually appeared before their eyes!

Chen Xi was similarly shocked speechless by this sight. This divine palace was too lofty and ancient, and when his gaze looked over at it from afar, he instantly felt as if he’d stepped into an independent world. Various strange and obscure auras assaulted his face, and they surged like the tide on an ocean!

In an instant, Chen Xi’s entire body was bathed within the Laws of the Heaven Dao, and the vital energy in his entire body was suppressed to the extreme!

This was an almost suffocating feeling. It was like the Grand Daos he possessed were like subjects that had encountered their king, and they were suppressed to the point of being unable to raise their heads before their king.

Om~

However, right at this moment, the River Diagram fragments in his sea of consciousness shook lightly in a casual manner, and an obscure fluctuation spread throughout his body. It instantly wiped out the uncomfortable feeling in him, allowing his entire body to feel relaxed.

Merely taking a glance at it from afar almost suffocated me. Isn’t the energy of the Grand Dao within that divine palace too terrifying? Chen Xi was astounded in his heart.

He was naturally clearly aware that this was a place that only Immortal Kings could step foot on, and it would be strange if a Golden Immortal like him wasn’t suppressed upon arriving here.

“This is the God Attainment Palace. According to legend, it’s a divine treasure born within the chaos at the beginning of the world, and it was left behind by the Chaotic Dao Ancestor, Pangu. Only by passing through this palace will we be able to see the true God Attainment Altar.” Dian Dian’s voice transmission sounded out by Chen Xi’s ear.

The Chaotic Dao Ancestor, Pangu!

A wave of shock arose once more in Chen Xi’s heart when he heard that this divine palace had such great origins because Pangu was the number one expert when the world was created!

“Could it be that there’s something to take notice of when passing through this palace?” After he calmed down, Chen Xi acutely noticed that the expressions of Dian Dian and the others had calmed down, and even carried slight heaviness. Moreover, they weren’t in a hurry to set out.

“Yes. The Restrictions of the Gods are set up outside the God Attainment Palace, and it isn’t easy to pass through them.” Dian Dian answered casually.

The Restrictions of the Gods?

Chen Xi couldn’t help but sigh in his heart. That’s a Divine Restriction that contains the energy of the gods.

He was currently restrained by his cultivation, so no matter how high his attainments in the Talisman Dao was, he was helpless against such Divine Restrictions.

“Hmph! Looks like many people have indeed arrived before us.” Right at this moment, Shi Yu suddenly grunted coldly and said, “Come, let’s head over first and see what ability our competition possesses.”

As he spoke, his figure flashed, and he led all of them directly towards the ‘Heaven Ladder’ in the distance.

Om!

When they stepped onto the first step of the ‘Heaven Ladder’ a terrifying force rumbled as it surged over.

Shi Yu was prepared long ago, and he withdrew the Five Color Rock that swiftly created a barrier of light, and it directly resisted the terrifying fluctuation that assaulted them.

“If we weren’t in a hurry, we could rely on the might of the restrictions on this Heaven Ladder to cultivate. The greater the pressure, the more of our potential will be stimulated. I heard Elder Su of the Sect say that many great figures in history utilized the Heaven Ladder as a paradise to temper themselves, and they more or less obtained some benefit from it.” Shi Yu shook his head before his figure flashed, and he kept the five colored screen of light up as he flashed up the Heaven Ladder.

Chen Xi couldn’t help but be astounded when he heard this. Probably only Immortal Kings would dare do that.

Because of the oppressive force of the divine restrictions and the Heaven Ladder being truly too high, Shi Yu utilized the Five Color Rock and led all of them up for the time for an incense stick to burn before they finally reached the end of the ladder.

The end of the Heaven Ladder was a limestone path. It started from here and stretched all the way to the divine palace, and it was concealed within the chaotic mist.

Gusts of cold wind blew by and dispersed the mist. The extremely ordinary limestone path emanated an ancient and desolate aura, and it seemed as if it had experienced the baptism of countless years.

At this point, Chen Xi finally saw the God Attainment Palace clearly, and the first thing that entered his eyes was a resplendent and magnificent door that was inscribed with countless mysterious talismans.

The talismans were extremely ancient and obscure, and they moved and twisted about like numerous tadpoles. Chen Xi was only able to faintly distinguish some words. “Sky, Ground, Annihilate , Restrict, Destroy, Seal, Move, Kill, Fire…

Chen Xi had actually never seen or heard of most of the other talismans, and they were like the language of gods.

Besides that, the Divine Palace’s walls, eaves, stone pillars… Everything was forged from unknown divine rocks. After experiencing the corrosion of countless years, they still shone brilliantly and weren’t tainted by dirt at all, and they emanated a solemn aura that aroused reverence in the hearts of all.

“Heaven Trampler!”

“Nuwa’s Dao Palace’s Shi Yu and Xiangliu Li?”

“Midnight Immortal King?”

When Chen Xi’s group appeared on the limestone path before the divine palace, a wave of surprised voices instantly resounded.

After that, Chen Xi noticed that numerous figures were standing amidst the chaotic mist. There were around five of them, there were both men and women, and all of them possessed monstrous imposing auras that were supreme like overlords.

When he looked at them from afar, Chen Xi felt a piercing pain in his eyes.

Obviously, this was a group of Immortal Kings!

On the other hand, the expressions of Shi Yu and the others sank slightly when they saw this group of people. They noticed that the Sovereign Sect’s Suiren Ting and Jiang Lingxiao weren’t amongst them.

It was even to the extent that the Devil Spirit Sect’s Wu Xiangchi who was almost jointly annihilated by them wasn’t there either.

But according to Xiangliu Li’s analysis from before, amongst the members of the Prehistoric Sect that had come to the God Attainment Region and besides those that had already fallen, there were only four Prehistoric Sects which they were unable to determine if these sects had joined forces with the Sovereign Sect.

Yet the scene before their eyes had already exceeded the number of four!

Simply speaking, the number of Immortal Kings that had come to seek the God Attainment Altar this time had exceeded their previous estimations.

“Old monkey, why haven’t all of you gone in?” Heaven Trampler spoke with a rumbling voice while his gaze shot towards one of the Immortal Kings there. This Immortal King had a thin figure, a sharp mouth, a chin that was like that of an ape, and eyes that surged with a bright light and revealed a ruthless and wild expression.

At the same time, Heaven Trampler sent a voice transmission to the others. “That old monkey is an independent cultivator from Hollowice Mountain, and he’s called Sun Wuhen. The other five people by his side are all the masters of various Prehistoric Sects.”

Actually, there was no need for him to introduce the others because Shi Yu and the others roughly recognized them.

After all, it wasn’t their first time here at the Prehistoric Ruins. Coupled with all of them being Immortal Kings as well, a brief deduction would allow Shi Yu and the others to roughly guess their origins.

However, it was impossible to determine exactly how many amongst these six Immortal Kings had sided with the Sovereign Sect.

“Hmph! Three old geezers have already died miserably in there just now. So would anyone dare go in rashly without a certain level of confidence?” When Sun Wuhen who Heaven Trampler called ‘old monkey’ heard this, he spoke with disdain.

Some have already died in there?

Shi Yu and the others were shocked in their hearts, and their expressions became heavy because those experts were Immortal Kings, and the death of every single Immortal King was sufficient to cause a mighty uproar in the three dimensions.

Presently, three Immortal Kings had actually perished in the God Attainment Palace, so how could they not be shocked?

“Oh? Which unfortunate fellows were they?” Heaven Trampler seemed to be rather familiar with Sun Wuhen and continued.

“The Darkcloud Sect’s Fu Xiaochen, the old bear from White Emperor Ridge, and Wu Xiangchi from the Devil Spirit Sect…” Sun Wuhen answered casually.

Wu Xiangchi!

Shi Yu and the others were slightly stunned once more when they heard this name.

“Did they enter along with the Sovereign Sect’s Suiren Ting and Jiang Lingxiao?” Xiangliu Li couldn’t restrain herself from asking this question.

“I don’t know.” Sun Wuhen was stunned when he heard the words ‘Sovereign Sect,’ and then he shook his head. It was a mystery if he really didn’t know or he was feigning ignorance.

“They were together indeed.” Right at this moment, a grey robed and dignified middle aged man spoke in a deep voice. “When I arrived here, I just happened to see them entering the God Attainment Palace together.”

Shi Yu raised his eyes and looked over, and he instantly recognized the grey clothed and dignified middle aged man as the master of Upper Sky Palace, Yi Ranfeng.

“So besides those Immortal Kings that have perished, Suiren Ting and Jiang Lingxiao have passed through the God Attainment Palace and arrived at the God Attainment Altar?” Xiangliu Li asked with a frown on her face.
