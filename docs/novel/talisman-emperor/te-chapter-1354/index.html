The Eye of Heavenly Tribulation, a forbidden force that came from the Heaven Dao.

Even if it was Suiren Ting, he could only rely on the Divineking Soul Streamer as a catalyst and sacrifice the Blood Essence of Immortal Kings in order to utilize the might of the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation, yet was unable to control it.

This obviously showed how terrifying the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation was.

So when all the Immortal Kings in the surroundings saw Chen Xi was actually capable of going against the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation, they felt shock and even disbelief.

It felt that like they suddenly saw an ant leap out and actually shake a towering tree. The extreme contrast caused them to feel unparalleled shock.

But in practically a short moment, Shi Yu and the others instantly recovered from their shock, and they realized that they couldn’t waste any time now!

“Attack together and kill those two fellows!” Shi Yu had a murderous look at he withdrew the Five Color Rock, and he charged at Suiren Ting.

Presently, they weren’t threatened by the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation any longer, so if they didn’t make the best use of their time to kill their enemies, then they would simply be utterly idiotic.

“Kill!”

“I’ve been fucking waiting for this moment!”

“Attack together!”

Xiangliu Li, Heaven Trampler, Sun Wuhen, and even Dao Yao and Pang Du attacked furiously at this moment.

Earlier, they were forced deeply into a hopeless situation and were almost annihilated by the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation. Now that they had such a superb opportunity to kill their enemies, how could they possibly let it slip by?

Om~

The Five Color Rock emanated boundless radiance as it transformed into five colored divine lights that swept out.

Swoosh!

The Nine Clarity Jade Crown revolved in midair before it emanated Nine Clarity Divinelight.

Bang!

Heaven Trampler held the black and enormous axe as he charged out directly.

…

For a time, various Divine Artifacts soared through the sky while supreme Immortal techniques danced about. All of them were shocking abilities that would flabbergast others if one of them appeared at any ordinary time, yet various supreme and profound techniques had appeared in unison at this moment!

This was the ability of Immortal Kings. They stood at the peak of the three dimensions and illuminated the ages.

This time, it was Suiren Ting and Jiang Lingxiao’s turn for their expressions to turn grim.

The Eye of Heavenly Tribulation that they’d exhausted great effort to prepare didn’t even have the chance to reveal its might when it was obstructed by an unknown little fellow.

This had exceeded their imagination, and it caused them to instantly fall into a bad situation.

When they saw Shi Yu and the others charge over, their expressions became gloomy to the extreme while their hearts almost bled. They were on the verge of going mad.

They’d set up the Nine Heaven Calamity Dao Annihilation Divine Formation, yet it had failed.

They’d activated the full might of the 36 Restrictions of the Gods in the God Attainment Palace, yet it amounted to failure in the end as well.

Now, they’d paid a great price and exerted great effort in order to the Divineking Soul Streamer as a catalyst to utilize the might of the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation. Could it be that…we’re going to fail once more?

Suiren Ting and Jiang Lingxiao’s faces were gloomy to the extreme.

However, the situation obviously didn’t allow them to think too much about it. In the next moment, they were already locked in battle with Shi Yu and the others.

Even though the situation was severe at this moment, it wasn’t impossible to turn around yet. They still placed their hopes on the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation’s ability to annihilate Chen Xi.

At that time, it would be the moment they launched their counterattack!

Kill!

Kill!

Kill!

The situation fell once more into chaos. Peerless divine radiance rumbled ceaselessly in this world shaking fierce battle between Immortal Kings. Various terrifying Divine Artifacts fought for supremacy with each other, and the grand scene simply seemed like days of chaos had descended and a great calamity had erupted.

It was very obvious that Suiren Ting and Jiang Lingxiao had quickly been firmly suppressed, and the situation was gradually turning bad and precarious for them. It was even to the extent that if they didn’t possess a few peerlessly formidable Divine Artifacts to rely on, both of them would have been annihilated a long time ago.

…

The God Attainment Altar was bound to not be at peace today.

On the extremely long limestone path, numerous Immortal Kings were fighting, whereas Chen Xi was facing the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation head-on by himself in the sky!

At this moment, Chen Xi’s mind and heart were occupied by rage and ruthlessness, and his entire body was enveloped by the obscure and strange aura of the River Diagram fragments.

He charged without end, and every single Dao Technique he utilized was mysterious and terrifying. They were utterly not techniques that he possessed, and merely a shred of its supreme might was sufficient to crush an entire world and collapse the universe. Even if it was the Immortal King Realm, it was probably impossible for Immortal Kings to possess such might.

It was precisely because of this that he was capable of ceaselessly facing the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation head-on. If he utilized his own cultivation instead, then he would probably be instantly obliterated into nothingness.

In other words, instead of saying that Chen Xi was fighting the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation, it could be said that the River Diagram fragments were fighting the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation.

One of them was a forbidden force that came from the Heaven Dao. It caused the Immortal Kings of the three dimensions to be filled with fear towards it, and even the primeval gods were unable to resist it. It was the energy of Heavenly Tribulation, and to the living beings of the three dimensions, it was a true supreme and paramount force.

The other was the River Diagram fragments. This precious treasure’s origins were unfathomable, yet it contained supreme profundities. The Master of Oracle Mountain, Fuxi, had relied on the River Diagram to see through the profundities of the workings of the heavens, and then he ascended to the extremes of the Grand Dao. This obviously showed how terrifying the River Diagram was.

Presently, the River Diagram fragments were utilizing Chen Xi’s body to resist the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation, and the conflict that erupted between them could be considered to be paramount. If it was in the outside world, then it would probably cause an extraordinary calamity.

Rumble! Rumble!

In the chaotic sky, the mysterious, indifferent, and emotionless Eye of Heavenly Tribulation surged and revolved. Its pupil revealed the myriad of worlds in the universe, the changes in the universe, the river of eternity…and various other grand scenes.

These scenes transformed into numerous terrifying ‘Heavenly Tribulation Chains’ in the end, and they shot down like a myriad of chains that were falling down from the depths of the universe with the intention of transforming the world into a cage that restrained everything and annihilated all variants.

Every single black divine chain contained the forbidden energy of the Heaven Dao, and they formed mysterious patterns that crushed the Grand Dao, collapsed the sky, and obliterated the surroundings.

If any Immortal King was here in Chen Xi’s place, the Immortal King would probably instantly be shackled by these divine chains, and then his Immortal King Laws would be completely extracted before being crushed to death!

It was even to the extent that even if primeval gods were here, they would be powerless to resist these chains because it was the forbidden energy of the Heaven Dao, and so long as it was within the three dimensions, no one could compete against it!

Chen Xi wasn’t an Immortal King nor was he a god. However, his body was occupied by the River Diagram fragments at this moment, and he was capable of fighting for supremacy with the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation!

If news of this were to be spread to the Immortal Dimension, then it would definitely cause a mighty uproar.

Of course, Chen Xi wasn’t aware of all of this. His consciousness had been completely occupied by the obscure fluctuations of the River Diagram fragments, and it transformed into emotions of ruthlessness and rage. Besides that, he didn’t have any other thoughts.

After an unknown period of time, an enormous bang that shook the heavens resounded. In the sky, the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation suddenly trembled violently before it actually rippled and vanished swiftly!

Yes, it had vanished!

It wasn’t defeated by Chen Xi, and it suddenly left by itself…

However, at the instant that it left, the seemingly boundless pupil of the eye suddenly revealed that expanse of chaotic space once more. The space was densely filled with countless Cages of the Grand Dao, and every single cage contained a figure that possessed a monstrous aura of divinity.

This scene was shocking, and Chen Xi had witnessed it in the past. The only difference was that a blurry figure had suddenly appeared in the chaotic space, and the figure inadvertently glanced at Chen Xi…

Chen Xi felt as if he was struck by lightning!

After that, the aura of the River Diagram fragments that enveloped his entire body actually seemed as if it had suffered a severe blow, and it instantly collapsed completely!

It was merely a single glance of that blurry figure that caused all of this!

Who was he?

Why would he appear in the pupils of the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation?

Chen Xi hadn't realized at all that he’d recovered his consciousness…

…

Bang!

At the instant the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation vanished, the Divineking Soul Streamer in Suiren Ting’s hand rumbled before the boundless divine light it emanated suddenly dimmed down to the extreme.

At the same time, Suiren Ting suddenly spat out a mouthful of golden blood, and his face instantly turned ghastly pale.

Obviously, the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation vanishing had caused the energy within the Divineking Soul Streamer to lose balance, and it caused Suiren Ting to suffer a certain amount of backlash.

After all, he was only an Immortal King while he’d relied on the Divineking Soul Streamer to utilize the might of the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation, and he wasn’t able to truly control it. If it was the Sovereign Sect’s Master who was here in his place, then the situation would be different.

“Senior Brother!!” Jiang Lingxiao exclaimed with shock, and a wisp of despair couldn’t help but arise in her heart.

They’d been suppressed by Shi Yu and the others earlier to the point they were unable to catch their breaths, and they were suppressed in all aspects. They were in a precarious situation. Now that the Eye of Heavenly Tribulation had vanished as well while Suiren Ting suffered the backlash from it, it caused them to instantly fall into dire straits.

“Kill!”

“Let’s kill the two of them at once!”

The spirits of Shi Yu and the others was refreshed when they saw this, and their attacks grew even more violent.

“Kill us? All of you aren’t worthy of that!” Suiren Ting started laughing instead from extreme rage. He seemed to be in a frenzy as his entire body blazed with flames. At the same time, a bang resounded as an ancient, obscure, and mysterious jade tablet floated above his palm, and it actually revealed the terrifying scenes of gods dying miserably, Buddhas bathed in blood, Fiendgods wailing, and skeletons piled up into the sky.

As soon as it appeared, merely its imposing aura actually forced Shi Yu and the others back repeatedly!

“The Sovereign Jade Tablet!” All of them exclaimed with surprise.

Before they could make any reaction, a terrifying fluctuation droned through the surroundings. The ancient and mysterious jade tablet glowed, and it carried Suiren Ting and Jiang Lingxiao along with it as it instantly vanished into thin air!

This was the God Attainment Altar that was suffused with restrictions, and it was utterly impossible to teleport here. Yet Suiren Ting and Jiang Lingxiao had actually vanished into thin air, and this obviously showed how shocking the ability of the Sovereign Jade Tablet was!

It was indeed a fact. The Sovereign Jade Tablet belonged to the Master of the Sovereign Sect. Even though it didn’t possess monstrous might like the Divineking Soul Streamer, yet it was said to have the title — ‘The Universe In An Instant.’

Obviously, it meant that no matter where one’s destination was, one could instantly traverse the universe by relying on this ancient jade tablet!

“The day of the upheaval of the three dimensions will be the day of your deaths!” Suiren Ting’s voice that was filled with resentment, rage, and unwillingness still drifted through the sky, and it didn’t vanish for a long time.

They actually fled successfully… Shi Yu and the others were disgruntled as well, and they stood on the spot with extremely gloomy expressions.

None of them had expected that the Sovereign Sect would actually prepare so many things for their trip to the God Attainment Region this time, and even their path of retreat was fully prepared.

“No wonder the Sovereign Sect was able to survive through the countless years even if it was the public enemy of the three dimensions…” Xiangliu Li sighed lightly, and her tone was filled with a sense of loss.
