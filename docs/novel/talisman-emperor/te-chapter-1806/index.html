This man wore black clothes, had a tall and graceful figure, jet black long hair that hung loosely on his shoulders, skin that was crystalline and fair, and his face was angular as if it was carved and chiseled into form. He was extremely handsome.

Especially his eyes, they were like an eternal veil of night that gave others a tranquil, ancient, and indifferent feeling.

He didn’t possess a world shocking imposing aura, and his aura was extremely flat and ordinary. Moreover, his entire body was suffused with an aura of returning to simplicity like the Grand Dao.

Chen Xi remembered him, and Chen Xi had a very deep impression of him. It was during the Treasure Exhibition. Chen Xi had chosen a Goldwave Rock from a stall, but he’d sold it off to this black clothed man right after that.

Chen Xi had never imagined that he would actually encounter this man again now in the boundless starry sky.

Moreover, the man seemed to be in a slightly sorry state and seemed to be avoiding something.

“Fellow Daoist recognizes me?” The black clothed man acutely noticed the change in Chen Xi’s expression, and he couldn’t help but be stunned. A strange dark ripple suddenly arose in his eyes, and it was like the eternal veil of night was reflected within his eyes.

After that, his eyes lit up as he roared with laughter. “Haha! So, it’s you! The world really is small! After that hasty parting all those years ago, I never expected that we would actually meet here at this moment. The matters of the world really are indescribably miraculous and profound.”

Chen Xi’s heart shook. He’d changed his appearance and even utilized the Daoseal Mark to conceal his aura, yet who would have imagined that this man was actually able to see through Chen Xi’s true appearance with a single glance!

This had never occurred in the past.

Obviously, this man definitely possessed some sort of supreme technique that could see through all disguises!

“It really is a coincidence.” Chen Xi quickly recovered his calm, and he nodded indifferently before he suddenly said, “You’re being pursued by someone that intends to kill you?”

The black clothed man immediately restrained his smile, and then he sighed with a troubled expression, “It’s far from that. It’s merely a crazy girl that just wont leave me alone. Neither fighting her nor scolding her works. She’s simply like a ghost that refuses to go away. It’s truly gives me a headache.”

Chen Xi was stunned. He’d never expected that the black clothed man would actually give him such a reason.

The black clothed man seemed as if he’d found someone to vent to, and he poured a stream of complaints to Chen Xi. “Fellow Daoist, tell me. Isn’t a relationship something that stresses upon consent? But that crazy girl took the marriage agreement made by our seniors as a sacred law and has constantly been pestering me to get married with her. But I don’t intend to get married in my entire lifetime! But she just refuses to listen and insists on abiding by the marriage agreements. She’s simply… simply a mad woman that’s impervious to reason!”

Chen Xi was instantly astounded. A marriage agreement again? But based on the circumstances, this fellow seems to have been forced to flee from it….

“It’s even to the extent that for the sake of getting married with me, that woman actually came all the way to my clan and seemed to swear to not give up until she accomplished her objective. How infuriating!” The more he spoke about it, the more annoyed he was, and his voice was filled with helplessness.

“What happened after that?” Chen Xi couldn’t help but ask this question.

“After that, I could only flee. I couldn’t be helped, even my family really approves of that crazy girl’s actions, so if I still didn’t flee, then it would really be too late.” The black clothed man shrugged and seemed extremely depressed.

When he spoke up to here, his brows suddenly raised. He seemed to have noticed something, causing his expression to instantly turn unsightly while he gritted his teeth and said, “Dammit! That crazy girl has caught up again! Fellow Daoist, you must help me. If that crazy girl comes over, then act as if you don’t know anything. We can have a proper chat once I escape this calamity.”

He spoke extremely quickly, yet his movements weren't slow at all. As he spoke, his figure spoke and actually transformed into strands of light that seemed dark like the veil of night, and he swiftly vanished into thin air.

At this instant, even Chen Xi was actually unable to sense his existence, and it was like his traces and aura in the world had been wiped off while he’d evaporated into thin air.

Such a concealment technique could truly be said to be shocking!

But fortunately, after merely an instant, Chen Xi’s Daoseal Mark noticed a trace of obscure fluctuation in space at his side, and it was extremely difficult to detect. Obviously, it was the black clothed man.

This allowed Chen Xi to heave a sigh of relief. Because if this fellow intended to harm him, then at the very least, Chen Xi wouldn’t be taken by surprise by this fellow’s concealment technique.

Suddenly, a fragrance that was clear like ice and faint like perfume faintly drifted through the air, and it refreshed the soul.

Chen Xi’s heart instantly shook.

Right at this moment, a chilly and low voice sounded out from outside the treasured vessel.

“Fellow Daoist, did you notice a black clothed man passing by here?” Along with this voice, a slender and graceful figure stepped foot on the treasured vessel and directly arrived at the cabin. From the beginning until the end, she didn’t request Chen Xi’s agreement at all and entered without invitation.

Chen Xi couldn’t help but frown because of this. He raised his eyes to look over, and he saw that woman had a graceful aura and wore a white dress that fluttered around here. She seemed like she was about to transcend the world and enter the nine heavens.

She had an extremely gorgeous appearance, jet black hair that flowed down like a waterfall, moist cherry lips, picturesque brows that were clear and bright, and an otherworldly bearing. Her peerlessly graceful bearing and unparalleled elegance was breathtaking.

Even Chen Xi had no choice but to admit that this woman was extremely beautiful. She possessed a form of unique and otherworldly bearing, and it caused others to be unable to arouse the intent to disrespect her.

But in next to no time, Chen Xi’s eyes narrowed. He noticed that the aura emanated from every single move this woman made was actually unfathomable, obscure, and extremely powerful!

Chen Xi was sure that she’d attained the Imperial Monarch Realm, and since she was able to possess such a formidable aura, she was presumably an extraordinary figure.

Actually, no matter if it was that black clothed man or this matchlessly beautiful woman standing before him, both of them were rare and unsurpassed geniuses. When Chen Xi compared them to the Universe Enlightened Ancestral Gods he’d encountered in the past, he felt that they weren’t existences of the same level at all.

After a short period of silence, Chen Xi suddenly smiled and said, “Miss, it’s slightly rude to come uninvited.”

“This is the compensation for it.” The white clothed woman’s peerlessly beautiful face remained composed, and she waved her fair hand to toss a storage pouch to Chen Xi.

Chen Xi was stunned. When he took it and had a look, he noticed that there were actually 100,000 Divine Crystals within it. This was absolutely a huge amount of wealth, and it was sufficient to purchase a sixth-grade mid-rank Artificial Spirit Treasure!

Yet this was merely compensation for ‘coming uninvited’, and this made this woman’s origins seem even more extraordinary.

But Chen Xi was clearly aware that this woman hadn’t done this because she was extremely wealthy, and it was because she couldn’t be bothered to argue with him and apologize.

While he thought in this way, Chen Xi couldn’t help but smile. He pointed at the area of space at the side and said seriously, “I really don’t welcome guests that come uninvited. So, seize this opportunity to take your fiancée and leave immediately. The further you go the better.”

As soon as these words were spoken, the white clothed woman couldn’t help but be slightly stunned, and then a wisp of a cold glow arose in her clear eyes while she raised her fair hand.

Om!

Strands of brilliant golden bolts of lightning interweaved together, and they were dazzling and resplendent as they flowed within her palm. After that, she swiftly pressed them down forcefully towards the place that Chen Xi pointed at.

This attack seemed to be executed casually, yet it carried a peerless and invincible aura, and even Chen Xi’s eyes couldn’t help but narrow when witnessed the might it created.

Swoosh!

However, right when the woman was about to attack, a shadow that was ethereal like the veil of night had suddenly appeared out of thin air, and it flashed out of the cabin before the attack could descend.

“Chen Xi, you were actually bought over by a few lousy divine crystals. You truly disappoint this Young Master!” The black clothed man’s exasperated voice sounded out from outside the treasured vessel.

This fellow actually recognizes me? Chen Xi’s heart shook, and then he laughed as he said, “I’m sorry, but while someone is pursuing you for the sake of a marriage, I’m being pursued with the intent to kill. So as to avoid it affecting you, I can only act in this way.”

“You’re… being pursued with the intent to kill?” The black clothed man seemed to be slightly surprised.

“Yea Chen! It’s already at a time like this, yet you still have the time to pay attention to the business of others? This time, let me see how long more you can hide for!” Before Chen Xi could answer Yea Chen, the white clothed woman’s figure had flashed, and it shot out of the treasured vessel like a ray of light.

“Xue Qingge! You’re simply a stubborn crazy girl! No one in the world forces another to get married like you’re doing! Dammit! It’s truly damnable!”

“It’s dictated by both our parents and matchmakes. If you’re a man, then come back with me!”

“What a joke! Do you have any authority to speak about whether this Young Master is a man or not?”

“In my opinion, you’ve been unworthy of the word ‘man’ since the moment you fled from this marriage agreement.”

“You! You! You!” The sounds of intense argument resounded in the starry sky, and it was accompanied by the deafening sound of battle. The scene seemed to be extremely chaotic.

When Chen Xi walked out of the treasured vessel, he saw the man and woman were fighting while in a chase in the starry sky. They seemed like life and death enemies, and the sight of this caused Chen Xi to be astounded.

No wonder. So that fellow is Yea Chen…. Chen Xi seemed to be lost in thought. Yea Chen was the peerless genius that was ranked at the first position on the Domain Enlightened Chart all those years ago. He was a descendent of the Imperial Region’s Yea Clan, and he seemed to be the number one genius in the Imperial Region!

When Chen Xi was at the Treasure Exhibition, he’d heard that Yea Chen and another extraordinary figure, Yu Jiuyue had advanced in succession into the Universe Enlightened Ancestral God Realm. Moreover, they caused extremely grand phenomenon of the heavens and the earth to descend when they advanced. For a time, they’d become the hottest topic that the entire Ancient God Domain took delight in talking about.

However, Chen Xi had never imagined that Yea Chen would actually be this black clothed man who was fleeing from marriage, and Chen Xi couldn’t help but feel that this was slightly absurd.

As for that woman called Xue Qingge, Chen Xi had never heard of her name. However, based on how she was pursuing Yea Chen to the point he was in such a sorry state, she was clearly an extremely extraordinary figure.

“AH!!! AH! AH!!! I can’t take this any longer!” Suddenly, Yea Chen who was fighting extremely far away in the starry sky had let out an extremely sorrowful and furious cry, and then his figure flashed as he fled towards the distance.

“Chen Xi! It won’t be long before the curtains to the Dao Discussion held by the five extremes of the Imperial Region would be drawn. You despicable fellow, you participate in it! Remember this! This Young Master has wanted to fight you since a long time ago. I hope you don’t disappoint this Young Master!” His voice sounded out from afar, and it caused Chen Xi to be unable to help but be stunned. A Dao Discussion held by the five extremes of the Imperial Region?

“You actually still have the mood to think about all of that. How detestable! If you don’t abide by the marriage agreement, then you can just dream about all of that!” Xue Qingge’s icy cold voice sounded out while her figure had already chased after Yea Chen’s figure that had flown into the distance and vanished in the boundless starry sky.
