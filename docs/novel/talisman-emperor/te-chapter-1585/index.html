Han Zhen was gone, yet an unknown middle aged man had appeared, and he’d brought Zhu Dongting and Moli Chou along while seeming to be extremely confident in dealing with Chen Xi.

When facing all of this, Chen Xi was naturally clearly aware that Han Zhen’s disappearance was probably related to Zhu Dongting and Moli Chou.

When he thought about how this profligate disciple of the Violethell Divine Sect refused to leave him alone just for the sake of a mere abode, even with Chen Xi’s good temper, he couldn’t help but be infuriated at this moment.

“Looks like no matter what I say today, I’ll be unable to rent this abode any longer?” Chen Xi’s gaze was piercingly cold as he gazed at the middle aged man.

“These are the rules, and I’m just acting according to the rules. Fellow Daoist, don’t think too much about it.” The middle aged man spoke with an emotionless expression.

“Bastard! Cut the crap and get the fuck out of here!” Moli Chou berated coldly, and his expression was icy cold and vicious.

He could restrain his arrogance when facing Zhu Dongting, but he couldn’t be bothered to restrain his temper when facing a little fellow at the World Enlightened True God Realm.

Their Violethell Divine Sect was the number one power in Jaderock Universe, and as the servant of the Violethell Divine Sect’s Grand Elder, there were very few people in Jaderock Universe that Moli Chou feared. However, it didn’t include this young man who stood before him.

Chen Xi’s gaze swept over and glanced deeply at Moli Choi before he said, “Fellow Daoist, if you continue acting arrogantly like this, you’ll suffer an accident sooner or later.”

As he spoke, he placed his hands behind his back and walked swiftly out of the abode.

Since he couldn’t stay here, then he would naturally not continue disputing it. Because even though Chen Xi wasn’t afraid of becoming enemies with Jade Drop Paradise because of this, it was too troublesome in the end.

On the other hand, Chen Xi’s style was to try his best not to cause trouble if he could.

Of course, all of this depended on the situation he was in. Presently, he was all alone, new here, and was suffering the pursuit of the Sovereign Sect’s Ye Yang, so it was truly inadvisable for him to incur enmity with others.

“What an arrogant kid! If you don’t kneel down and apologize to me today, then don’t even think of leaving!” Moli Chou exploded with rage because a little fellow at the World Enlightened True God Realm had actually dared to insult him, and this kid was simply courting death.

As he spoke, he suddenly stepped forward and obstructed Chen Xi’s path, and he seemed as if he would attack on the slightest sign of disagreement.

“Haha! A’Chou, you did well. This kid is truly too damnable, and he must be taught a proper lesson so that he’ll wake up!” Zhu Dongting clapped and roared with laughter at the side.

Chen Xi’s deep and black eyes narrowed slightly, and he suddenly started laughing lightly. He said to the unfamiliar middle aged man. “This is the Jade Drop Paradise. I’d like to ask if there still are any rules here? If there aren’t then allow these two bastards to make a move against me, and I’ll have nothing to say about it. However, if there are, then do as you see fit!”

His expression was calm, carefree, and composed, and he seemed to be completely indifferent towards all threats. In actuality, he’d already discerned that Zhu Dongting and Moli Chou would absolutely not dare make a move in Jade Drop Paradise. Because no matter how great their background was, Jade Drop Paradise’s background was no joke.

Moreover, even if a member of Jade Drop Paradise has come forward with the intention of driving Chen Xi out, but Chen Xi still hadn’t left Jade Drop Paradise, right?

Even though Chen Xi wasn’t willing to become enemies with Jade Drop Paradise, he would absolutely not mind or miss the chance to annoy Jade Drop Paradise.

Two bastards? Zhu Dongting and Moli Chou’s faces turned grim, and they revealed furious expressions while killing intent had arisen in their hearts.

“Please give me some face. Breaking the rules of Jade Drop Paradise won’t be tolerated.” The middle aged man’s face sank while he glanced coldly at Chen Xi, and then he spoke to Zhu Dongting and Moli Chou. “Perhaps once this Fellow Daoist leaves, the all of you can spar with each other.”

“Alright! Then this Young Master will exercise forbearance for now.” Zhu Dongting’s face sank, and his voice seemed as if it was squeezed out from the cracks between his teeth.

On the other hand, Moli Chou glanced coldly at Chen Xi while a wisp of undisguised ridicule arose on the corners of his mouth. He seemed as if he was looking at a dead man.

Chen Xi just smiled when facing thing, and he gazed at the middle aged man and said, “I heard that besides providing abodes, the Jade Drop Paradise has many places for its guests to amuse themselves. I’m free and have nothing to do, so why don’t you accompany me around? Don’t worry, you’ll be given the benefits you deserve.”

As he spoke, he flicked the tip of his finger and tossed out a Divine Crystal as if he was dismissing a beggar.

The middle aged man’s face froze while the corners of his mouth couldn’t help but twitch. Dammit! This kid is intentionally humiliating me!

But in the end, he still received the Divine Crystal with stiff movements, and he pulled up a trace of a stiff smile on the corners of his mouth before he said, “Thank you for your kindness, Fellow Daoist.”

After all was said and done, he was only an attendant, and he didn’t dare to make a move against Chen Xi because of this.

Zhu Dongting and Moli Chou who stood at the side were even dumbstruck. They’d seen shameless people, yet they’d never seen someone so shameless. Could it be that this bastard kid is utterly shameless?

After that, a strand of extreme rage arose in their hearts. If this bastard does this, then when would we be able to teach him a lesson?

After all, they’d already been waiting bitterly for three months of time, and it was all for the sake of waiting for Chen Xi to leave before trampling on him to vent the rage in their hearts!

“What? You feel a single Divine Crystal isn’t sufficient?” Chen Xi looked at the middle aged man and frowned as he spoke.

“How could I dare? It’s just that this lowly one is only in-charge of providing abodes to guests, and there are others in-charge of the other areas.” The middle aged man was furious to the point his chest almost exploded, yet he had no choice but to explain patiently.

“Oh? Then forget it. I thought you’d be of help, yet it turns out that you’re completely useless. Then give me back that Divine Crystal I rewarded you with,” said Chen Xi with a smile.

“You….” The middle aged man was utterly unable to restrain his rage, and he spoke in a grim voice while being on the very of exploding with rage.

Even Zhu Dongting and Moli Chou were stunned when they heard this. He actually intends to get back a single Divine Crystal he gave as a tip!? My god! Where exactly did this shameless freak come from?

“What? Fellow Daoist, do you have some sort of opinion of me?” Chen Xi was still calm and indifferent as before, yet he was sneering in his heart. I just like to watch your aggrieved appearance from detesting me yet not daring to make a move against me. Everyone knows how to sicken another!

In the end, the middle aged man gritted his teeth and suddenly withdrew another 10 Divine Crystals before handing them over to Chen Xi, and he said, “Fellow Daoist, take it. Since you still refuse to feel regret when you’re facing imminent death, then take these Divine Crystals as me paying for your funeral expenses.”

Chen Xi smiled even more brilliantly, and he received the Divine Crystals and said, “Fellow Daoist really is considerate and warmhearted. Thank you, thank you. If I have the opportunity, I truly wish to treat you to some food and drink. Perhaps you’d give me even more funeral expenses then. As for who’ll be buried in the end, that’s something that needs to be discussed.”

When he spoke up to here, he placed his hands behind his back and left slowly, and he paid no attention to that middle aged man’s gloomy and livid expression.

“A’Chou, follow him!” Zhu Dongting spoke in a ghastly tone when he saw this.

“Don’t worry Young Master. No matter who comes this time, it’s impossible for them to save this bastard!” Moli Chou spoke gloomily before leaving as well.

…

“Young Master this Exquisite Five Qi Divine Pill is the best medicinal pill in my Jade Drop Paradise. Its effects are capable of bringing the dead back to life, benefitting the soul, and replenishing Divine Energy…. Its price is 300 Divine Crystals each.”

“I’ll take 10.”

“Young Master, this Jadelight Divine Armor was refined from Chaotic Jadelight Iron, Cold Spirit Golden Silkworm Thread, Divine Hardcloud Sand, and another 75 precious divine materials. Moreover, 108 layers of restrictions were set-up on it be a Talisman Formation Grandmaster of Jade Drop Sword Sect. When it’s worn, its defensive ability is so formidable that it’ll absolutely be able to add an impregnable layer of protection to you. Its price is 3,900 Divine Crystals. If you think it’s expensive….”

“Cut the crap, I’ll take it!”

“Young Master, my Jade Drop Paradise sells all the 99 divine materials that you need, and their total price is 4,800 Divine Crystals.”

“Young Master….”

“I’ll take it.”

“I’ll take it.”

“I’ll take it.”

At the medicinal pill area, divine treasure area, divine artifact area… and various other areas in Jade Drop Paradise, the sounds of conversation between Chen Xi and the attendants there frequently resounded, and in less than 10 minutes of time, Chen Xi had already spent over 15,000 Divine Crystals.

The way he spent money like flowing water caused many nearby guests to feel extremely astounded and envious. It was even to the extent that the eyes of some female guests glowed while they were almost intoxicated by him.

In their hearts, Chen Xi seemed to have become that sort of wealthy young master, and they truly wished for nothing more than to curry favor with him and beg him to bring them along with him.

During this entire process, Moli Chou had always been following behind Chen Xi, and he noticed all of this down to the slightest detail.

As he continued watching, even a Domain Enlightened Spirit God like him couldn’t help but be slightly envious. That was 10,000 Divine Crystals! Even an existence like him didn’t dare spend lavishly like that!

I never expected that this bastard kid is really rich. Unfortunately, no matter how many divine pills and divine artifacts he buys, it’s impossible for him to escape death! Moli Chou gritted his teeth while killing intent seethed in his heart. On one hand, it was out of hatred, and on the other hand, it was because he coveted the wealth in Chen Xi’s possession.

Moreover, as far as he was concerned, everything Chen Xi was buying now was for the sake of self-protection. Then, why did Chen Xi act in this way? Obviously, Chen Xi was worried about the safety of his own life, and Chen Xi was worried about being killed by him, Moli Chou!

Hmph! Laughable, truly laughable! Could it be that he thinks the gap in cultivation realms can be closed up with external forces? He truly is an idiotic bastard! Moli Chou sneered without end, and he was thinking about how the wealth in Chen Xi’s possession should be shared once he killed Chen Xi.

Hmm? Right at this moment, he suddenly noticed that Chen Xi had glanced at him while a wisp of a spurious and ridiculing smile hung on the corners of Chen Xi’s mouth.

This caused Moli Chou’s face to instantly turn grim because being silently ridiculed by a fellow he felt was an idiot was extremely upsetting to him.

Fortunately, this sort of feeling didn’t continue for long, and his spirits were refreshed because he shockingly noticed Chen Xi’s figure within his field of vision had turned towards the entrance of Jade Drop Paradise.

Our opportunity has finally arrived! The blood in Moli Chou’s entire body seethed, and he’d never felt such an urgent desire to kill someone.

He started to send a voice transmission to Zhu Dongting.

At practically the exact same time, Chen Xi muttered in his heart as well. After a year of closed door cultivation, my cultivation has already almost recovered to its peak state. Now that I’ve prepared so many things, even if I encounter Ye Yang once more, I would at least be able to flee….

If Moli Chou knew that everything Chen Xi had purchased in Jade Drop Paradise was purchased for the sake of dealing with the threat of a Universe Enlightened Ancestral God and not for himself, one could only wonder how Moli Chou would feel.
