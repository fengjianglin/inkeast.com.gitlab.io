Chen Xi’s arrival here didn’t make all of them shocked like it was an extremely remarkable and heaven-shaking scene, and it caused countless experts to feel astounded and furious.

It was mainly because no one had expected his arrival.

After the Time Daolord forcefully restrained the annoyance in his heart, he swept his icy cold gaze towards the Dao Defiants who remained.

Since it wasn’t the first ancestor of the Dao Defiants who’d arrived here, then he would naturally not pay too much attention to Chen Xi.

Moreover, the most important thing for him right now was undoubtedly to break through the obstruction of his enemies and search for the whereabouts of the Dao Defiants’ first ancestor at the depths of the Spring.

A Savior was nothing when compared to that.

“Everyone, I bet you didn’t expect this, right? The person all of you were hoping would save you has actually turned out to be this Savior who has killed numerous members of your clans.” The Time Daolord spoke indifferently. “Now, do all of you intend to continue fighting stubbornly, or will you move aside obediently?”

As soon as these words were spoken, the expressions of those Daolords from the Dao Defiant Clans changed indeterminately, and their faces were gloomy and unsightly.

“We would rather die in battle than submit to our enemies!” An old man flared up as he howled furiously.

The murderous atmosphere in the surroundings condensed once more.

“What impervious fools! Let’s stop wasting our breaths and attack.” The Lightning Daolord spoke murderously, and then he suddenly spoke to Chen Xi who stood in the distance. “Little Fellow, the events here are of no concern to you, so return to the campsite right now!”

While he didn’t sound like he was scolding Chen Xi, his words were extremely overbearing and blunt. It seemed like he was ordering Chen Xi, and Chen Xi would have no choice but to obey him.

He’s said all of this because Chen Xi just happened to be standing between the 3 of them and the Dao Defiants. If it wasn’t for that, then the Lightning Daolord wouldn’t even be bothered to pay any attention to Chen Xi.

Moreover, Chen Xi was still considered as a member of the Dao Protectors right now, so it was inadvisable for them to attack Chen Xi right now.

However, if Chen Xi was stubborn and refused to leave, then the Lightning Daolord didn’t mind killing this potential threat right now.

“From today onward, I, Chen Xi, will be taking control of this place, and everything here will be under my protection.” At this moment, Chen Xi finally raised his head, and he gazed at the 3 Dao Servants while he spoke in a calm and composed tone.

Along with these words, the nine colored divine lotuses around him transformed into rays of divine radiance that surged into Chen Xi’s body, causing his imposing aura to suddenly change.

His black pupils were like two abysses that casted light upon the universe.

His shoulders were like mountains that stood towering into the sky.

His backbone was like a pillar that held up the heavens.

His heart was like the sun and moon, and it was eternally radiant.

His body was like the Dao, and it was brilliant and eternal!

As he stood there casually, his invisible and mighty aura oppressive the world to the point it wailed and all Daos fell into sadness.

Everyone in the surroundings was shocked by this scene.

The members of the Dao Defiant Clans were stupefied and filled with disbelief. What is that fellow saying? He intends to take control of our clans?”What high-sounding sentiments!

Some of the Daolords were furious and ashamed. Earlier, the 3 Dao Servants had run amuck through their territory and killed countless members of their clans while completely disregarding them. Now, even a Savior dares to speak so arrogantly and intended to encroach on everything they possessed. He’s simply going too far!Could it be that he thinks he can run wild just because he has advanced into the Daolord Realm?

Some of the Dao Defiants couldn’t help but be overcome by feelings of sorrow. When have we Divine Children of the Heavens suffered such humiliation?Some were even unable to restrain themselves anymore, and they intended to charge forward and fight Chen Xi to the death. However, they were stopped by one of the senior Daolords who said via voice transmission, “Don’t be rash! Let that kid and those 3 Dao Servants fight against each other first.”

At this instant, all the experts of the Dao Defiant Clans had come to an understanding. Even thought hey were still furious in their hearts, since that Savior had stepped forward and intended to go against the 3 Dao Servants, then they were naturally happy to watch the battle.

…

On the other hand, the Light, Time, and Lightning Daolords almost thought that their ears had failed them when they heard Chen Xi. Has this kid gone mad?

However, their hearts shook when they noticed the change in Chen Xi’s imposing aura. They instantly determined that this fellow was clearly not joking.

Moreover, they couldn’t avoid feeling slightly surprised by the imposing aura which Chen Xi revealed. It’s truly rare for a little fellow who has just advanced into the Daolord Realm to possess such an imposing aura.

They’d paid attention to Chen Xi’s battles in the past, and they knew that he’d been able to surmount a realm of cultivation and kill over 10 veteran Daolords of the Dao Defiant Clans while he was still at the Ninth Star Region Lord Realm.

Now that Chen Xi had advanced into the Daolord Realm, it was obvious how shocking the transformation of his combat strength would be.

However, they were still extremely infuriated from being provoked by a little fellow like this, and a wisp of gloominess appeared on their faces.

Who were they?

They were the Dao Servants who had resided on and protected Godrank Mountain for countless years!

Numerous Daolords from the Dao Defiant Clans had died miserably at their hands just now, so how could they possibly fear someone who’d just advanced into the Daolord Realm?

“What a little bastard who doesn’t know what’s good for him! Are you sure you want to go against us?” The Time Daolord spoke with an indifferent expression, and his voice carried a wisp of piercingly cold killing intent and was filled with a threatening tone.

“No.” Chen Xi shook his head.

The Lightning Daolord shouted loudly. “Then why the fuck are you still standing here for?”

Chen Xi glanced at him and ridiculed him. “Fool! I meant that all of you aren’t qualified to go against me. Understand?”

Hiss!

All the experts of the Dao Defiant Clans who were watching the show couldn’t help but gasp. Isn’t that Savior being a little too overconfident?

Even the experts of the Dao Defiant Clans felt it was absurd for Chen Xi to say that those 3 Dao Servants who’d protected Godrank Mountain for countless years and regarded all living beings as ants weren’t qualified to go against him.

If even those 3 Dao Servants aren’t qualified, then what about all of us?

After all, over 60 Daolords had died miserable deaths at the hands of those 3 Dao Servants just now! Yet they still weren’t qualified?

That kid is a madman!

This was just the reaction of the experts from the Dao Defiant Clans, and one could only imagine how the cultivators of the outside world would react if they found out about this.

As the concerned party, the Lightning Daolord was instantly infuriated to the point killing intent erupted from within him.

“Little Bastard! I’d intended to let you live for now, but I’ll send you on your way right now since you intend to court death!” The Lightning Daolord’s robust and mighty figure suddenly flashed amidst his loud shout that rumbled like thunder, and his palm carried a terrifying bolt of crimson red lightning as it slapped down forcefully.

Bang!

The crimson bolt of lightning that was formed from the energy of Destiny disregarded space as it instantly appeared above Chen Xi and struck down.

Its might was too terrifying, and numerous Daolords of the Dao Defiant Clans had perished before such an attack.

Now, this attack was directed at Chen Xi!

How could a fellow who has just advanced into the Daolord Realm possibly resist that?

Many experts of the Dao Defiant Clans couldn’t help but reveal a cold smile. Only Chi Qingying’s heart constricted, and he couldn’t bear to watch. Because Chen Xi had saved his life just now, so regardless of whether it was intentional or not, how could he disregard that fact?

Moreover, when he saw Chen Xi stand before the 3 Dao Servants by himself in an arrogant and proud manner while speaking in a domineering tone, Chi Qingying felt extremely excited by such a sight.

So, Chi Qingying had a slight impulse to go help Chen Xi.

Rumble!

In an instant, Chen Xi’s entire body was covered by the extremely dazzling crimson bolt of lightning, and it seemed like Chen Xi hadn’t been able to dodge in time.

No!!! Chi Qingying howled in his heart.

The experts of the Dao Defiant Clans couldn’t help but reveal a wisp of disappointment. They were hoping that Chen Xi would be able to engage the 3 Dao Servants in a fierce and desperate struggle, but they hadn’t imagined that he would be so weak to the point of being unable to resist a single attack.

They couldn’t help but feel speechless when they recalled the arrogant words he'd spoken earlier. A fellow like him was dreaming of taking control of our clans?

“What a weak clown!” The Lightning Daolord spoke with disdain. He’d thought that Chen Xi would be able to struggle for a while, but he hadn’t expected that this fellow only seemed strong on the outside and was actually weak. So, he was truly filled with contempt towards Chen Xi.

Even the Light and Time Daolords couldn’t help but feel slightly surprised because that Savior was much weaker than they’d expected.

Right at this moment, the crimson lightning which enveloped Chen Xi’s entire body had suddenly surged violently, and it drew the attention of everyone in the surroundings.

After that, the surging crimson red lightning actually seemed like a myriad of streams returning to the ocean they were form, and they were completely devoured. After that, Chen Xi’s tall and completely unharmed figure appeared before everyone’s fields of vision.

There was a strand of dazzling crimson red lightning still coiled around the tip of his right index finger, and it seemed extremely meek.

The hearts of all the spectators couldn’t help but thump when they witnessed such an inconceivable scene. That fellow is actually alive!?

Nice! Chi Qingying roared in his heart, and he was extremely excited. I should have realized that it would be absolutely impossible for him to be killed with such ease. Otherwise, he couldn’t have saved me from that river of time with such ease, right?

Chen Xi gazed at the Lightning Daolord with a calm expression on his face, and he seemed to have thought of something as he said, “So this is the energy of Destiny that belongs to the Grand Dao of Lightning? Looks like all of you Dao Servants aren’t true living beings of this world, nor are you innate gods that were born from within the Chaos. If I’m not wrong, all of you should have been born from within the Order of the Heaven Dao.”

The Lightning Daolord’s pupils constricted. He seemed to be unable to believe that Chen Xi had been able to survive, and he seemed to be unable to believe that his attack had been dispersed with such ease.

Moreover, he seemed to be slightly surprised and bewildered because Chen Xi had exposed the greatest secret within his heart.

“There’s something off about all of this. This kid is slightly more troublesome than we imagined. Prepare yourselves to attack.” The Time Daolord’s aged eyes flickered repeatedly. He’d acutely noticed that Chen Xi was too unusual. Regardless of whether it was Chen Xi’s aura of strength, they were both completely different from an expert who’d just advanced into the Daolord Realm.

“It’s slightly strange indeed.” The Light Daolord nodded while killing intent faintly appeared within his heart.

“Little Bastard! Try another attack of mine!” The Lightning Daolord was the most violent and unbridled amongst all the Dao Servants. It made him feel like he’d suffered an extraordinarily great provocation when Chen Xi dispersed his attack with such ease, so he suddenly shouted loudly while his figure transformed into a myriad of crimson red divine chains of lightning before the divine chains took the form of a lightning prison which was dazzling like a volcano.

The Crimson Lightning Prison!

This was the Lightning Daolord’s Highgod Domain, and it could be said to possess extraordinarily terrifying strength!

Obviously, the Lightning Daolord had decided to eliminate Chen Xi with this attack, and he didn’t intend to hold back at all.

At practically the exact same moment, killing intent flashed through Chen Xi’s black pupils as a dark blue sword silently appeared within his grasp.
