At the fourth level of the arenas.

This was the level that perfection-stage Heavenly Immortal Realm experts fought, and experts that could compete here were mostly able to be ranked in the top thousand ranks of the Continental Ascensio Rankings.

It was just as Chen Xi had expected, there was no lack of existences at the intermediate-stage and advanced-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm at this level, and there were only no experts at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm.

In other words, it would at least seem that Chen Xi had become the one and only existence at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm amongst everyone present here. So he seemed to be extremely offensively conspicuous here. As soon as he arrived here, he received the attention of many gazes.

“Little Fellow, this isn’t a place you should come to.” Someone couldn’t help but laugh with surprise and shake his head endlessly.

“Haha! Has this friend at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm come to observe and learn? In my opinion, you’ll be unable to obtain any experience from observing battles of such level, so it’s better to return to the first three levels to observe and learn.” Someone ridicule and advised Chen Xi to leave.

“Oh, you’re still not leaving? Why don’t we play later? I guarantee that I’ll make you wake up completely from your errors and turn back from your misguided path.” Someone laughed as he mocked Chen Xi.

Chen Xi disregarded all of this, and he just walked forward as he observed the battles on the arenas. His expression had become much more serious, yet unfortunately, they were still unable to arouse his desire to do battle.

“That kid over there! Stop!” Right at this moment, a furious shout sounded out from below. It was the yellow clothed young man and the others. They flew over along with this voice while revealing gloomy expressions.

All the experts at the fourth level were stunned when they saw this, and then the gazes they shot at Chen Xi more or less carried pleasure from watching him suffer misfortune.

They frequently tempered themselves in the Martial Emperor Domain, so they’d naturally seen this sort of scene on many occasions. They noticed with a single glance that this kid at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm had probably offended a formidable figure and had been pursued all the way to the Martial Emperor Domain.

This caused them to come to a sudden understanding, and they understood why this kid didn’t stay at the third level and had come to the fourth level instead. He’d obviously come to here to take refuge.

Even though causing trouble was prohibited according to the rules, conflicts still frequently occurred here. They used all sorts of despicable methods to infuriate their enemy to the point of leaping up onto the arena to fight them, and in this way, it naturally wasn’t considered as breaking the rules.

Most importantly, when they occasionally intended to deal with an enemy that they had deep enmity with, some experts would even disregard the risk of being expelled by the Laws of the Heaven Dao in the Martial Emperor Domain and make a move against their enemy.

But this sort of situation was extremely rare, and unless they had no other choice, no one would choose to do this.

“Hey! Little Fellow, do you want me to help you deal with them? I’ll do it if you give me a Darkspirit Grade Immortal Artifact.” Someone chuckled.

“Enough, did you not see that those are the members of the Four Divinity City’s Yin Clan?” Someone frowned as he warned the other person.

“The Yin Clan? My god! It really is them.” The person that spoke earlier instantly shut his mouth. Obviously, he was rather afraid of the Yin Clan.

“This kid is probably going to suffer misfortune unless he’s able to restrain himself from making a move. But in that way, he’ll be a pussy, right?” Someone intentionally fanned the flames.

As soon as these words were spoken, an uproar broke out in the surroundings. They didn’t recognize Chen Xi who was new here, yet they recognized the clansmen of the Yin Clan, so they naturally wouldn’t assist Chen Xi and even didn’t even reveal any pity.

Meanwhile, the yellow clothed young man and the others had arrived at the fourth level as well, and they surrounded Chen Xi before an arena. However, out of consideration for the rules of the Martial Emperor Domain, none of them dared to make a move.

Chen Xi finally started to size them up when he saw this.

There was a total of thirteen in their group. Most of them were experts at the intermediate-stage and perfection-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm. Only the yellow clothed young man in the lead possessed a cultivation at the initial-stage of the Mysterious Immortal Realm.

Such strength was sufficient to crush most Heavenly Immortal Realm experts, and this obviously showed that Liang Bing was right. Yin Feng’er was indeed someone that sought revenge for the smallest grievances, and for the sake of taking revenge on Liang Bing, she didn’t hesitate to shift the blame and hatred onto Chen Xi as well.

She really thinks highly of me… Chen Xi sized them up briefly before withdrawing his gaze. His expression was indifferent and composed, and he didn’t reveal any anxiousness at all.

“Kid, you should know why we’ve come. Now, quickly kneel down, admit your crimes, and then swear to the heavens that you’ll draw a boundary between yourself and Liang Bing in the future, then we’ll spare you this time!” A robust young man spoke coldly. His gaze was fierce, and he stared at Chen Xi as if he was looking at a dead man.

Chen Xi’s eyes narrowed before he said with a smile, “This is Yin Feng’er’s idea?”

“How audacious! How dare you disrespect the name of my Young Miss?” Someone rebuked furiously.

Chen Xi shrugged and shook his head. “How boring.” As he spoke, he turned around with the intention of leaving.

His relaxed attitude that seemed as if he practically disregarded them caused the yellow clothed young man and the others to be stunned. Never had they imagined that a little fellow at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm would dare to act in this way!

“You’re courting death! Stop right there!” The robust young man moved forward and obstructed Chen Xi’s path, and then he said gloomily, “Kid, do you think you can leave today?”

Chen Xi replied with a question. “Do all of you dare to break the Martial Emperor Domain’s rules?”

The robust young man’s face froze while his expression turned gloomy, and then he said, “Liang Bing dared to break the rules and slap my clan’s Young Miss, so do you think we wouldn’t dare to break the rules and kill you?” 

As soon as these words were spoken, the experts spectating in the surroundings were shocked. They seemed to have never imagined that the hatred between these two parties would have arrived at such a level, and it seemed as if they wouldn’t hesitate to break the rules so long as they could deal with that kid.

No one would take these words to be a joke because these words were spoken by a member of the Yin Clan. Since he dared to speak in this way in public, then he was surely prepared to act in this way.

Chen Xi’s expression remained unchanged instead, and he still remained carefree as he said, “Oh, then feel free to try.”

This sort of reply caused the yellow clothed young man to be stunned. In his expectations, Chen Xi would either feel dejected and despaired, or stamp his feet with rage, yet never had the young man in yellow clothes imagined that Chen Xi would actually be so calm and indifferent at this moment.

It wasn’t just him, even the nearby experts were surprised in their hearts. Only now did they notice that perhaps this little fellow at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm had a low cultivation, yet his courage to remain composed before danger was shocking.

“You better forget about leaving the Martial Emperor Domain now. I guarantee that unless you don’t enter the Martial Emperor Domain forever, otherwise you’ll never be able to escape the palm of my hand.” The yellow clothed young man pondered briefly before he spoke coldly.

Chen Xi went silent for the first time.

If it was only for the sake of tempering his strength, then leaving the Martial Emperor Domain was inconsequential. After all, the Immortal Dimension was enormous, and there surely was a suitable place to temper himself.

But if he wanted to ascend into the top thousand of the Ascensio Immortal Rankings, then he had to come to the Martial Emperor Domain because the battles on the training arenas could directly affect the rankings on the Ascensio Immortal Rankings.

On the other hand, the yellow clothed young man and the others obviously displayed their intentions to keep at it until the end, and this caused Chen Xi to finally make a decision.

In the next moment, he’d flashed up onto the nearest empty training arena, and then his gaze swept the yellow clothed young man and the others before he said calmly, “Didn’t all of you want to take revenge for Yin Feng’er? Now, I’ll give all of you a chance. I’m in a hurry, so all you better hurry up.”

Chen Xi’s actions of directly leaping up onto the arena caused the yellow clothed young man and the others to be delighted. But their expressions sank when they heard what he said.

He’s in a hurry?

Is this something a little fellow at the fucking initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm should say?

He simply doesn’t know the limits of his ability!

Even the surrounding experts were stunned as they never imagined that Chen Xi who was at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm would be so brave to actually intend go against the Yin Clansmen that came to look for trouble with him!

Especially since it was at the arenas on the fourth level.

“Bastard! Is the fourth level a place a Heavenly Immortal at the initial-stage like you should come to? Get the fuck down!” The robust young man was the first to be unable to refrain himself, and he flashed up onto the arena with a ghastly expression as he walked step by step towards Chen Xi.

“Big Brother Yin Shan, quickly kill this bastard that’s full of nonsense!” 

“Big Brother Yin Shan possesses a cultivation at the advanced-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm. Wouldn’t killing a little fellow like this be as easy as playing a game?”

The Yin Clan disciples raised a clamor when they saw this.

“Should we make a move?” Extremely far away from the training arenas on the fourth level was two young men that stood side by side. One of them had a handsome appearance, snow white hair, and a gaze that was fierce and sharp like a sword. The other wore green clothes and white shoes, he has a clear appearance, a graceful figure, and an unrestrained bearing.

If someone was here, the person would surely be able to recognize to their surprise that these two were Liang Dongyuan who was ranked 14th on the Continental Ascensio Rankings and Liang Ren who was ranked 3rd!

These two young men were called the twin stars of the Liang Clan. Especially Liang Ren, since he was able to be ranked as the 3rd, it obviously showed how shocking his cultivation and combat strength was.

But the person that spoke earlier wasn’t Liang Ren, and it was the white haired Liang Dongyuan instead.

“Have you forgotten the Eldest Young Miss’s instructions?” Liang Ren spoke indifferently before he turned around and left. “Come, let’s go temper ourselves in the higher Martial Emperor Domain. I’ll be having a battle with the 2nd ranked Gu Yueming in a few days.”

“Gu Yueming? Hmph! In my opinion, only the 1st ranked Jiang Zhuliu is Big Brother’s opponent. As for everyone else, they’re utterly unworthy.” Liang Dongyuan grunted coldly.

Liang Ren shook his head and didn’t speak any further before transforming into a flowing ray of light and left swiftly.

Their departure was silent, and it didn’t arouse the notice of anyone.

But Chen Xi who stood on the arena seemed to have noticed it. He glanced towards the distance before withdrawing his gaze, and he said in his heart, What a formidable aura. Their rankings on the Continental Ascensio Rankings are probably extremely high up…

“Bastard kid! You actually dare to be distracted while fighting your Uncle Yin Shan? You’re simply courting death! Kneel!” Meanwhile, the robust Yin Shan had charged over explosively with a gloomy expression on his face. His entire body was like ferocious primeval beast that crushed everything before his path, and his imposing aura was ferocious and vicious to the point it caused the surrounding air to buzz.

Bang!

Before he even approached Chen Xi, his large hands that were like cattail leaf fans slapped down swiftly like a bolt of light, and it was like a mountain that crushed down from outer space and carried an extremely ferocious and overbearing imposing aura.

When facing this, Chen Xi simply raised his head while revealing a calm expression, and he lightly spat out two words from his lips. “Fuck off!”
