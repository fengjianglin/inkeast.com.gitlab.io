He’d blasted Leng Xinghun flying with a casual slap of his palm!

When they witnessed this scene, no matter if it was Tu Meng or Donghuang Yinxuan, both of their hearts couldn’t help but shake because this was simply unbelievable.

Early on while he was still at the Universe Enlightened Ancestral God Realm, Leng Xinghun was known for being the best in the Imperial Region. His natural talent and physique were extraordinary, and they were sufficient for him to look down upon most cultivators.

For the sake of attaining the Dao and becoming a complete Region Lord, he hadn’t hesitated to exercise forbearance and suppress his cultivation for around 10,000 years. Now, he’d finally succeeded, so it was obvious how formidable his might would be, and it was absolutely not something an ordinary Region Lord could compare to.

However, at this moment, he was blasted flying by a single strike of Chen Xi’s palm!

If the cultivators of the outside world witnessed this scene, then their jaws would definitely hit the floor from shock.

Bang!

The battle continued. Donghuang Yinxuan didn’t have the time to think too much about it at all. At the instant Leng Xinghun was blasted flying, Chen Xi had suddenly turned around and shot towards him.

At this moment, Chen Xi was like an imperial sovereign who had descended to the world. Violet gold divine radiance flowed throughout his body, and his imposing aura was indifferent and supreme.

Bang!

Donghuang Yinxuan roared loudly, and he used every ounce of strength he had to go head-on against Chen Xi. However, in merely an instant, he was blasted flying by a single strike of Chen Xi’s palm as well, and the bones throughout his body had almost been completely broken.

“How could this be possible?”

“Impossible!”

Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan’s expressions changed. Their faces were covered in disbelief, shock, and fury.

They’d utterly never imagined that while they’d advanced into the ranks of Region Lords and had even joint forces, they were actually unable to resist a single strike from Chen Xi!

Isn’t this a little too terrifying!?

Both of them were almost unable to accept this!

Why? Why is the gap between us so huge when we’re all Region Lords?

Bang!

Before they could recover from their shock, Chen Xi had attacked once more.

This expanse of the starry sky seemed as if it was on the verge of collapse while Chen Xi was like the overlord here. He possessed unparalleled divine might, and every single step he took caused space to be obliterated.

“Bastard!!” Leng Xinghun was utterly infuriated, and his pupils were blood red.

He withdrew the Heavenmatch Checkerboard, and it formed numerous black and white pillars of night before instantly transforming the world into a board. He intended to trap Chen Xi and forcefully take the edge off Chen Xi’s spirit.

Bang!

Chen Xi flicked his sleeve. The impetus of this wasn’t really great but rumbling resounded before the board was instantly obliterated. Moreover, the Heavenmatch Checkerboard wailed and shook without end.

On the other hand, Leng Xinghun was pushed back again, and he coughed up blood.

Swoosh!

At the other side, the Taixuan Codex flipped open before rows of profound markings of the Dao surged out from within it. They emanated divine might as they formed a literary piece, and then they were controlled by the Dao Control Brush with the intention of crushing Chen Xi.

“A sneak attack? Fuck off!” Chen Xi frowned as he swiftly turned around, and then he stretched out his hand and grab. He actually forcefully tore apart the literary piece that was formed by the mysterious markings of the Dao, causing a rain of light to spray throughout the surroundings.

Donghuang Yinxuan’s eyeballs had almost burst out from their sockets from the shock he felt!

How could he have imagined that Chen Xi was actually able to resist the might of the Dao Control Brush and Taixuan Codex with his bare hands? Isn’t he a little too ferocious?

Bang!

In the next moment, Donghuang Yinxuan suffered backlash from this, and he staggered back while his vital blood roiled.

At this moment, no matter if it was Leng Xinghun or Donghuang Yinxuan, they simply seemed as if they couldn’t withstand a single strike and were easily crushed by Chen Xi!

it wasn’t that they weren’t strong, and it was because the gap between them and Chen Xi was too huge!

In the Ancient God Domain, existences like them could be considered as overlords of a region, and they possessed boundless might that could rule over the lives of countless living beings.

However, they dimmed in comparison before Chen Xi.

After all, it wasn’t just any Region Lord who could refine and absorb the nine Region Quintessences like Chen Xi had, and the scale of the region he established in his body was an entire nine times wider than other Region Lords!

Nine times!

This seemed like an insignificant number. But if it was converted into cultivation, then it was astounding. At the very least, in the history of the entire Ancient God Domain, there was probably not a single existence like Chen Xi.

It could be said to be unprecedentedly rare!

“Dammit! Truly damnable!” Leng Xinghun’s heart was filled with grievance and rage, and he seemed like he’d gone mad as he charged madly at Chen Xi.

At this moment, he utilized all the supreme techniques he’d cultivated and divine treasures he possessed as he fought with all his strength, and he didn’t hold back at all.

He wasn’t willing to be suppressed just like that!

After he advanced into the ranks of Region Lords, he thought that it would be sufficient to make up for the gap between him and Chen Xi, but never had he imagined that he hadn’t just failed to offset the gap, it had grown even larger….

How could Leng Xinghun who’d always been confident and proud be able to accept this?

“Kill!” Donghuang Yinxuan was the same. His expression was livid and gloomy while his eyes revealed extreme rage. Just Chen Xi alone had caused them to fall into such an embarrassing state, and he felt that it was an extraordinary humiliation!

Rumble!

This expanse of the starry sky fell into extreme chaos. The stars were obliterated into powder, space was in disorder and collapsed, blazing and terrifying techniques shot about, and divine artifacts collided and interweaved together. It seemed like scenes of the end of the world.

This was a battle between three Region Lords. Every single move they made was capable of throwing world into chaos and obliterating the sun and moon!

If such an unprecedented battle had occurred in the Ancient God Domain, then it would have probably caused a world shocking calamity.

Fortunately, they were at a mysterious expanse of starry sky in the Forgotten Grounds of Chaos, and it allowed the spread of such a calamity to be avoided.

Bang!

Divine radiance surged, the heavens collapsed, the earth split apart, a rain of blood, and various other terrifying phenomena appeared here.

This was created by Chen Xi’s imposing aura. Every single move he made was accompanied by all sorts of terrifying phenomena like the gods perishing and ghosts wailing.

He was still fighting barehanded, but every single palm of his was like a tidal wave or landslide. The starry sky trembled violently while boundless violet gold divine radiance surged towards the surroundings, and they tore space apart, illuminated the world, and caused one’s heart to tremble.

“AH!!!” Suddenly, Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan’s shouts resounded, and it carried rage, unwillingness, shock, frustration, and hatred.

At this moment, they were utterly unable to withstand Chen Xi, and they ceaselessly coughed up blood while they found it impossible to go against Chen Xi’s peerless might.

Chen Xi’s movements weren’t very fast. He strode forward step by step like an imperial sovereign patrolling his territory.

However, every single movement of his was terrifying. Because they carried the supreme strength of a Region Lord, and they crushed everything before them!

Up until this point in the battle, Chen Xi’s expression had remained indifferent and calm while his black and icy cold eyes didn’t reveal any fluctuations of emotion at all.

While Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan were letting out shrill cries, Chen Xi seemed as if he hadn’t heard it at all.

Because… Chen Xi’s hate had risen to its limits a long time ago!

Since the Dao Discussion began, they’d been constantly provoking and humiliating him, and they’d repeatedly utilized all sorts of despicable methods to eliminate him from the Dao Discussion.

Later on, when they’d just entered the Forgotten Grounds of Chaos, Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan had led all the disciples of their respective sects to set up an ambush against Chen Xi and the others. If Chen Xi’s reaction wasn’t quick, then Tu Meng would have probably perished at that time!

Earlier, while Chen Xi was refining and absorbing the Region Quintessences, the two of them had even utilized cruel methods to torture and humiliate Tu Meng….

This string of enmity had been accumulated in Chen Xi’s heart for a very long time, and it was ceaselessly growing and seething like lava. So, it was irreconcilable since a long time ago.

Under such circumstances, how could Chen Xi show mercy once he attacked?

He’d hated them to the bone!

If he didn’t kill Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan today, then he felt that he would be unable to control his flames of rage!

…

Bang!

Chen Xi had a cold and indifferent expression on his face as he pressed forward incessantly. He pressed down upon space to the point it rumbled explosively while peerless violet gold divine radiance surged out like a torrent and swept towards the surroundings.

This sort of strength was too terrifying. Even Leng Xinghun wasn’t able to evade it in time, and he was forcefully blasted flying like a rocket. Blood flowed from his entire body while numerous bones in his body were broken.

At this moment, Leng Xinghun wasn’t mighty and arrogant as he was just now. He was being ceaselessly blasted flying and coughed up blood without end. His hair was disheveled, his entire body was bathed in blood, and he seemed miserable to the extreme.

As the leading figure of the Sovereign Sect’s younger generation, when had he ever been in such a sorry state? But now, he’d suffered the greatest setback in his entire lifetime!

He wasn’t a match for Chen Xi, and he was utterly unable to resist Chen Xi at all!

It was even to the extent that he felt helpless and tiny before Chen Xi as if Chen Xi was unshakeable.

This caused Leng Xinghun to be furious, and his heart was filled with grievance!

“AH!!!” Leng Xinghun roared loudly while he resisted Chen Xi with all his might. However, a large and bloody hole was blasted open on his chest by a single strike of Chen Xi’s palm, and he almost lost his life.

“We must leave quickly! The Region Energy this fellow possesses is an entire nine times of ours. We can’t fight him at all!” At this critical moment, Donghuang Yinxuan charged over from the side, and he rescued Leng Xinghun who was blasted flying before he intended to flee.

“You want to flee? This region is my territory.” Chen Xi’s gaze was like a bolt of lightning as he gazed indifferently at them in a superior and unparalleled manner.

He wasn’t exaggerating. After he refined and absorbed the nine Region Quintessences of this mysterious expanse of the starry sky, the energy of this region was under his control.

Under such circumstances, he would naturally not just watch as his enemies fled.

Bang!

As he spoke, Chen Xi pressed both hid hands downward in the space before him. Suddenly, numerous clear colored divine chains descended from all around this expanse of the starry sky, and they were like chains of order that completely sealed off this expanse of the heavens and the earth.

“NO!!!” Donghuang Yinxuan’s expression changed violently. He was like a madman as the spirit, energy, and essence within his body seethed, surged, and burned. He intended to fight desperately and risk his life to break open the divine chains that enveloped the area before him.

But in the end, it was in vain.

Bang!

Right at this moment, Chen Xi attacked once more, and his movements seemed otherworldly as he moved his palm.

Donghuang Yinxuan let out a shrill cry while the sound of breaking resounded from the bones throughout his body. Blood flowed from all seven of his apertures, and his extremely handsome face had warped and become hideous at this moment.

He was unable to flee!

At this moment, Donghuang Yinxuan experienced despair and helplessness for the first time, and this sort of feeling caused him to almost go mad.

He’d just advanced into the ranks of Region Lords, and it was the period that he should feel satisfied with his success. So, how could he possibly be willing to perish in the Forgotten Grounds of Chaos?

“Chen Xi! Let us go this time and we’ll swear to not go against you again, alright? Could it be that you insist on being ruthless and eliminating both of us?”

“Give us a chance. We disciples from the five extremes of the Imperial Region have definitely not joined forces and entered the Forgotten Grounds of Chaos in order to kill each other!”

Donghuang Yinxuan and Leng Xinghun retreated as they cried out loudly, and their voices carried a wisp of panic and terror.

Peerless geniuses like them were extraordinarily conceited and proud. But when they faced death at this moment, they actually didn’t hesitate to beg for mercy in order to save their lives. This clearly showed how despaired and helpless they felt in their hearts at this moment.

Did anyone in this world not fear death?

Of course!

But it was absolutely not Donghuang Yinxuan and Leng Xinghun!

They were disciples from one of the five extremes of the Imperial Region, they were leading figures in the younger generation of their respective sects. They possessed monstrous authority and reputations, and they’d even stepped into the ranks of Region Lords now. So, their future could be said to be limitless.

Thus, existences like them were naturally unwilling to die just like that!

When faced with this, Chen Xi finally spoke for the first time since the battle had begun. His gaze was icy cold and indifferent as he glanced at Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan, but he pointed at Tu Meng who resided in the distance as he said, “Did the two of you give him a chance just now?”

He paused for a moment, and then Chen Xi’s voice suddenly became low and gentle amidst the gazes of despair from Leng Xinghun and Donghuang Yinxuan. “Don’t be cowards. I’m taking revenge, so please respect me, your enemy. Alright?”
