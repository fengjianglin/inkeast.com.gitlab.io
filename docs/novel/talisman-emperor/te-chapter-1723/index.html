Little Junior Brother?

Everyone in the surroundings was astounded. When did we offend a personal disciple of Oracle Mountain?

After they recovered from their initial shock, all of them found out about Wu Xuechan’s identity by conversing via voice transmission.

However, it was precisely because of this that their reactions would be so strong when they heard what Wu Xuechan said.

However, in next to no time, all of them shuddered. The imposing aura Wu Xuechan emanated was too terrifying. It was deep like an abyss and boundless powerful, and it caused them to feel suffocated.

Right when all of them were surprised and bewildered, Wu Xuechan had taken a stride forward and swiftly arrived before Chen Xi.

“Little Junior Brother, I’m sorry. Some ignorant fools wasted quite a bit of my time on the way here, and that’s why I’m late.” Wu Xuechan had an apologetic expression when facing Chen Xi.

Hiss!

Everyone in the surroundings gasped and felt their scalps go numb. Never had they imagined that Chen Xi was actually Wu Xuechan’s Little Junior Brother!

It wasn’t just them, even Imperial Monarch Chong Dou had never expected this, and his expression changed abruptly. How could this be possible? When did Oracle Mountain have another personal disciple called Chen Xi?

“I’m fine.” Chen Xi shook his head.

Wu Xuechan sized Chen Xi up from top to bottom, and then he suddenly frowned before he sighed. He patted Chen Xi on the shoulder and sighed. “You’ve been through a lot. There’s no need to worry any longer, Senior Brother is here now.”

Merely these words alone caused Chen Xi to be unable to help but feel warm in the heart, and he felt the feeling of meeting a fellow disciple after a long time.

“How could this be possible!?” Suddenly, someone cried out loudly. It was Gongye Nanli that spoke these words. He’d watched helplessly as all of this occurred, watched helplessly as Chen Xi escaped calamity over and over again, and he was aggrieved to the point he almost coughed up blood.

Yeah!

How could this be possible?

The other great figures of the Imperial Region were unable to accept such a situation as well. Chen Xi was just about to be annihilated when Empress Yu Che arrived abruptly, and then after she was suppressed by Imperial Monarch Chong Dou, the Grand Lord of Oracle Mountain made an appearance!

How could they accept this string of unexpected events?

“Earlier, it was you that wanted my Little Junior Brother to kneel, right?” Suddenly, Wu Xuechan turned around, and his clear and ordinary face was already covered in a cold expression.

His gaze seethed with divine radiance, and it was like the sun and moon that illuminated the world as it locked coldly onto Gongye Nanli.

“So what if I did? Could it be that your Oracle Mountain dares…?” Gongye Nanli braced himself and spoke in a grim voice.

Before he could even finish speaking, his entire body suddenly shook, and then a crack resounded as his knee bones were directly crushed into pieces.

Thump!

He fell on his knees in front of everyone!

From the beginning until the end, not to mention Gongye Nanli himself, even the others hadn’t noticed how Wu Xuechan did it.

This sudden and unexpected event caused the expressions of many others to change. There had always been rumors about the Oracle Mountain’s Grand Lord possessing extraordinary ability and an unfathomable cultivation, and now that they’d witnessed it with their own eyes, they felt that it was even more terrifying than the rumors.

“AH!!!” Gongye Nanli let out a shrill cry as he struggled with all his might to get up. However, he was suppressed by an invisible and enormous force, and all his efforts seemed powerless before it.

A Universe Enlightened Ancestral God ranked on the Universe Enlightened Chart, a great figure from the Imperial Region’s Gongye Clan had his knee bones shattered at this moment and was on his knees before everyone. This scene caused many great figures in the surroundings to feel extremely horrified in their hearts.

“Wu Xuechan, we respect you because you’re the Grand Lord of Oracle Mountain, but aren’t you being a little too haughty by attacking without even discerning right from wrong?” Someone felt disgruntled and spoke with a grim voice in Gongye Nanli’s defense.

“Exactly. Could it be that you’re not going to ask exactly what sort of monstrous crimes this kid has committed? You’re going too far by just protecting him blindly!”

“This kid didn’t just kill my clan’s disciple Kunwu Qing who’s a Spirit God Exalt, other disciples like Luo Shaonong, Gongye Zhefu, Di Jun, Yue Ruhuo, Jin Qingyan, and Pei Wen were killed by this kid as well. Such a murderer that has no conscience is simply an embarrassment for your Oracle Mountain!”

The other great figures spoke with fury, and they condemned Chen Xi.

Chen Xi sneered as he watched this. These old bastards are simply despicable to the extreme. Earlier, they were utterly arrogant and unreasonable when dealing with me earlier, and they even acted unscrupulously. Now that Eldest Senior Brother has arrived, they’ve suddenly started to be reasonable. They’re simply shameless to the extreme.

Right and wrong? All of that’s bullshit!

If we’re being reasonable, then on what basis did Luo Shaonong and his group besiege me? And why couldn’t I kill them?

Most detestable to him was that Zhen Liuqing was harmed exactly because of Luo Shaonong’s group, so it caused Chen Xi to be utterly unable to forgive those bastards!

He took a deep breath and intended to describe the sequence of events to his Eldest Senior Brother, Wu Xuechan.

However, Wu Xuechan waved his hand and signaled that Chen Xi didn’t have to say anything.

In the next moment, Wu Xuechan glanced coldly at all those great figures that spoke furiously in succession, and his face was emotionless as he lightly spat out a single word. “Kneel.”

It was merely a single word, yet it was like a thunderclap that shot through the sky.

In the next moment, a wave of the sound of bones breaking rose and fell. All these great figures that possessed monstrous might in the past let out shrill and miserable cries in succession, and then they fell uncontrollably to their knees.

Such a scene was simply shocking to the limit.

It caused others to be unable to help but recall that Imperial Monarch Chong Dou had once spoke just like this when dealing with Chen Xi, and it had created such a terrifying scene as well.

However, Chen Xi hadn’t knelt in the end, whereas, all of these great figures from the Imperial Region didn’t even have the time to react and were even unable to resist before they’d been forced to their knees!

Over 10 people were kneeling on the ground, and it caused the hearts of the other cultivators in the surroundings to shudder with fear. Moreover, their eyelids twitched while their entire bodies stiffened on the spot.

It was too terrifying!

And he was too domineering!

The Grand Lord of Oracle Mountain hadn’t show himself for numerous years. However, as soon as he did, he revealed such domineering, overbearing, and cold methods, and it simply caused the others to feel disbelief.

“If all of you were reasonable, then all of you wouldn’t have caused such harm to my Little Junior Brother. So, I can only be even more unreasonable when dealing with all of you.” Wu Xuechan glanced coldly at all those great figures who remained kneeling on the ground, and then he glanced at the others in the surroundings. “Is there anyone else that wishes to speak about being reasonable with me, Wu Xuechan?”

All of them were silent like cicadas in the winter, and the surroundings were deathly silent.

At this moment, Wu Xuechan had simply become the imperial monarch that ruled over this expanse of the heavens and the earth. He looked down proudly onto the world, and he revealed a supreme graceful bearing.

“Grand Lord, you’re going a bit too far by acting in this way!” Imperial Monarch Chong Dou’s expression was extremely unsightly, and he spoke in a low voice.

“I’m going too far?” Wu Xuechan raised his eyes and gazed coldly at Imperial Monarch Chong Dou.

Imperial Monarch Chong Dou’s eyes narrowed, and he took a deep breath before he said, “What? Could it be that your Oracle Mountain intends to go against all of us?”

Wu Xuechan suddenly started laughing instead, and he glanced indifferently at Imperial Monarch Chong Dou before he said, “I’ll give you a chance. If you’re able to resist three attacks of mine, then I’ll leave immediately with my Little Junior Brother, otherwise, leave your life behind. What do you think?”

It was just a simple sentence, yet it was peerlessly domineering.

Imperial Monarch Chong Dou’s expression suddenly turned grim, and he said in a low voice, “What if I don’t agree?”

Wu Xuechan restrained his smile, and then his expression became indifferent. “If you don’t agree, then kneel down and apologize.”

He wanted an Imperial Monarch to kneel down and apologize!

The hearts of everyone in the surroundings shook fiercely when they heard this, and they almost didn’t dare believe their ears. Why has the Grand Lord of Oracle Mountain become so overbearing?

According to rumor, Oracle Mountain had always been known for being mysterious and keeping a low profile. Moreover, the rumors said that the Grand Lord, Wu Xuechan, was a wise man with an extremely warm disposition. Yet now, he’d become so domineering and overbearing. Could it be that it’s because of that Chen Xi?

“You’re going too far!” Imperial Monarch Chong Dou was utterly infuriated. He had a furious appearance as he pointed at Wu Xuechan. “Since it’s like that, then allow me to experience the Grand Lord’s ability!”

bang!

His imposing aura surged, and he emanated boundless might.

At practically the exact same moment, the 24 Divine Boundary Pearl were activated. They formed into a barrier that seemed like a universe that lay before him, and the terrifying and grand scenes of stars circulating and alternation between the sun and the moon was reflected within it as it smashed down ferociously at Wu Xuechan.

He’s utilized his full strength as soon as he attacked, and the imposing aura of an Imperial Monarch instantly enveloped an area of 500,000km, causing all things to be obliterated while the Grand Dao itself was destroyed!

However, all of this vanished in merely an instant.

When everyone in the surroundings returned to their senses, they noticed to their shock that the 24 Divine Boundary Pearls were actually within Wu Xuechan’s grasp!

This scene caused their eyeballs to almost fall out from shock, and it was simply inconceivable!

Even Chen Xi was astounded in his heart. Isn’t Eldest Senior Brother’s combat strength too heaven defying? He actually turned the situation around in such a casual manner!

“You…. You…. You’ve actually already….” Imperial Monarch Chong Dou’s pupils suddenly dilated, and he seemed to have realized something, causing him to be shocked and furious to the extreme.

“Kneel.” Wu Xuechan stretched out his slender and broad palm, and then pressed it down lightly in midair.

Bang!

The area Imperial Monarch Chong Dou stood at was blasted into pieces, whereas, he himself seemed as if he’d been struck by a sledgehammer. Blood flowed from all the pores on his body while his face warped. In the end, he was unable to endure such terrifying pressure, and a thump resounded as he knelt in midair.

An Imperial Monarch knelt before everyone just like that!

At this instant, the surroundings were deathly silent. Everyone had practically forgotten to breathe. Their pupils were dilated and filled with disbelief.

Such a scene was something that was impossible to come by in a million years!

After all, it was an Imperial Monarch! Even in the Imperial Region, an Imperial Monarch was a peak existence that commanded great authority and could rule over a region.

Yet now, such an existence had actually been forced to his knees….

Empress Yu Che’s pupils couldn’t help but constrict, and she seemed to have realized something, causing her to gaze at Wu Xuechan’s figure with shock while her heart couldn’t calm down at all.

“Wu Xuechan! I’ll never forget the humiliation I suffered today!” Imperial Monarch Chong Dou roared furiously, and his roar shook the heavens and was filled with boundless rage and resentment.

“Feel free to come looking for me.” Wu Xuechan glanced at him, and then couldn’t be bothered to pay any further attention to him.

He turned around to look at Chen Xi, and then he said with a smile, “Little Junior Brother, are you satisfied?”

Chen Xi shook his head and said, “In the end, all of this was only possible with Senior Brother’s help. It’ll only be satisfying when I crush them again one day in the future when I’ve become accomplished in my cultivation.”

Wu Xuechan couldn’t help but smile, and then he seemed to have noticed something, causing him to look towards the distance while his brows knit together imperceptibly. He said in a low voice. “Nevermind, let’s leave first.”

Chen Xi was stunned. “Then what about them….”

Wu Xuechan said with a smile, “I’ll leave them for you to satisfy yourself in the future.”

As he spoke, he frowned once more, and then he didn’t ask for Chen Xi’s agreement before he flicked his sleeve and took Chen Xi and Empress Yu Che along as he vanished instantly on the spot.
