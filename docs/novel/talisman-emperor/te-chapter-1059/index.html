Yin Shan was an expert at the advanced-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm that had grasped the energy of three Laws of the Grand Dao. His rankings on the Continental Ascensio Rankings was the 764th rank, and he could be considered to be a rather outstanding expert of the Yin Clan.

Especially since his battle style was violent, ruthless, ferocious, and vicious. It caused his enemies to die rather miserably at his hands, and they either had their bodies torn open, had their bodies crushed into pieces, had their heads smashed apart…

He practically never left an intact corpse.

It was precisely based on this understanding that when they saw Yin Shan step foot on the arena and charge towards Chen Xi that was a the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm, regardless of if it was those Yin Clansmen or the surrounding spectators, all of them revealed a wisp of being unable to bear to watch.

Even if it was the Martial Emperor Domain and killing would only heavily injure the soul, that feeling of having one’s head smashed apart wasn’t nice at all.

But to the surprise of everyone present here, the young man actually didn’t dodge under this extremely dangerous situation, and he even lightly spat out a pair of unbelievable words — fuck off!

This young man really tries his best even if it’s of no use. He’s clearly aware that he’ll lose for sure, yet he doesn’t have any intention to dodge, is he courageous and fearless, or is he just ignorant?

Everyone sighed emotionally like this in their hearts.

Yin Shan even started laughing from extreme rage. The force of his fist became even more violent and emanated a rumbling explosive soundwave.

It was at this moment that Chen Xi’s eyes opened abruptly while the imposing aura in his entire body suddenly rose. Powerful Immortal Force emerged from his body and grew stronger and stronger, and in the blink of an eye, it had become a surging ocean!

Yin Shan’s large hands had just arrived above Chen Xi’s head and hadn’t smashed down when he suddenly seemed to have been stunned on the spot. His mouth gaped while he seemed to have seen an enormous wave of Immortal Force covering the sky as it swept towards him with unstoppable might.

Yin Shan that possessed abundant combat experience suddenly returned to his senses and realized that he’d actually been intimidated by his opponents imposing aura! He let out a fierce howl as his fist sped up and smashed down!

At practically the exact same time, Chen Xi circulated his strength and struck out with his fist, and it struck firmly onto Yin Shan’s fist.

A wisp of astonishment instantly surged onto Yin Shan’s face while his arm suddenly emanated explosive cracking sounds, and then it twisted together like a twisted fried dough.

Yin Shan’s right arm was actually crippled in this head on collision!

Moreover, the remaining strength of this punch shook Yin Shan’s robust figure to the point of flying up into the air, and then he fell heavily towards the area outside the arena. During this entire process, his chest had collapsed rapidly while he bled from all seven orifices before he fell to the ground with a thump. He fell head first to the ground, causing over ten of his teeth to fall out, and then his head tilted to the side as he fainted. 

The outcome was decided instantly with only a single punch!

Not only had Yin Shan fucked off out of the arena just like Chen Xi had said, he’d passed out directly!

The Martial Emperor Domain really is realistic like the outside world. Even the scene of blood and bones breaking can appear when one suffers an injury… Chen Xi praised in his heart.

When they saw this scene, everyone in the surroundings was completely stunned. They almost didn’t dare believe their eyes because a fellow at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm had actually dealt with an expert at the advanced-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm with a single punch? 

“How could this be possible?” A Yin Clansman cried out in a sharp voice.

“He cheated! This kid surely cheated!” 

“Big Brother Yin Shan is at the advanced-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm. How could he lose at the hands of a kid at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm? And he even lost so swiftly…”

The Yin Clansmen spoke successively with surprise and bewilderment, and they seemed to be both shocked and terrified.

“Idiots! Can anyone even cheat on the training arenas? All of you shut up!” The yellow clothed young man frowned and shouted furiously with a gloomy expression.

Everyone went silent like cicadas in the winter and didn’t dare speak another word. However, the gazes they shot at Chen Xi faintly carried a wisp of bewilderment and seriousness.

“Kid, what’s your name?” The yellow clothed young man directly shot his gaze at Chen Xi, and his voice was extremely gloomy.

The nearby experts revealed a wisp of astonishment when they heard this. These Yin Clan disciples arrived menacingly, yet they actually don’t know the identity of their enemy?

The yellow clothed young man felt slightly helpless in his heart. He was only someone Yin Feng’er had sent over to take revenge. When he heard that the target was a little fellow at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm, how could he possibly investigate his target’s identity and background?

But Yin Shan’s loss allowed him to suddenly realize that since a little fellow at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm was able to defeat Yin Shan so easily, how could this fellow be an ordinary nobody?

“I’m in a hurry. I’ll be leaving if all of you don’t want to fight.” Chen Xi frowned and was slightly impatient. He’d come to the Martial Emperor Domain to temper himself and not to play games with a group of people that were ranked far below him, and it was because he really didn’t have much time.

Again!?

The expressions of the Yin Clansmen turned extremely gloomy when they heard this.

“Kid, don’t get presumptuous! I, Yin Jian, will seek advice from your great skill!”

“Hmph! Big Brother Yin Jian, wait a moment! Allow me to deal with this rampant kid, I guarantee not to torture him to death!”

“Stop fighting, let me do it!”

All the Yin Clan disciples roared furiously in succession.

Chen Xi couldn’t help but be amused. These Bastards really can’t differentiate between black and white. It’s obviously they who came looking trouble with me, yet they say I’m rampant. I’ve seen shameless people, but I’ve never seen people that are so shameless. 

He couldn’t be bothered to make a fuss about it and said directly, “All of you come at me together so as to avoid wasting time.”

As soon as these words were spoken, it caused everyone present to be astounded. 

How arrogant!

This kid is simply boundlessly arrogant!

Could it be that he thinks he can go against that group with his cultivation at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm? There’s even an expert at the initial-stage of the Mysterious Immortal Realm residing amongst them!

But hearing this called those disciples of the Yin Clan to be delighted in their hearts. After they witnessed the scene of Chen Xi defeating Yin Shan, it would be a lie if they said they weren’t afraid. At this moment, when they saw Chen Xi actually put forward such a request, the apprehension in their hearts instantly vanished without a trace.

Bully others with numbers was something they were masters at!

Hmph! Kid! Aren’t you being too arrogant? Do you think there’s no one capable in my Yin Clan? Even though he thought like this in his heart, the yellow clothed young man feigned a cold grunt as if he disdained to bully Chen Xi with numbers.

Chen Xi felt even more contempt towards these fellows and said directly, “Are all of you going to fight or not? Fuck off if you aren’t!”

“What a kid! You’re simply tying yourself to a pole and asking to be slapped. We have to satisfy such an arrogant request! All of you go together, and don’t allow him to die so easily!” The yellow clothed young man laughed coldly and waved his hand when he saw this.

Swoosh! Swoosh! Swoosh! 

Those Yin Clansmen were growing restless since a long time ago, and they instantly charged up the arena when they heard this. As soon as these ten plus figures ascended the arena, they encircled Chen Xi while revealing ghastly expressions that carried a savagery and hostility.

The nearby experts shook their heads without end when they saw this. Even though this young man at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm was arrogant, yet the actions of these disciples of the Yin Clan was slightly dishonorable. 

“Looks like this request made all of you very happy, huh? Then why cover your desires with lies?” Chen Xi ridiculed them bluntly.

“You’re about to die, yet you still dare talk nonsense? Kill him!”

Bang!

An expanse of blazing divine radiance flew over like a rising cloud, and it enveloped Chen Xi. These clansmen of the Yin Clan moved out in unison, and they didn’t hesitate in the slightest to attack with lethal blows.

However, Chen Xi was faster. He took a step to the side before his hands shook, and then two divine moons appeared. One was black, the other white, and it was like Yin and Yang co-existed. They rubbed together before merging into a black and white millstone that smashed forward.

Bang! Bang! Bang!

The divine radiance was blasted apart and shattered. It transformed into a rain of light that covered the sky, whereas, the group of Yin Clan disciples seemed as if they’d been struck by a myriad of large mountains, and they let out shrill howls as they were blasted flying.

The scene was extraordinarily magnificent. In a single strike, over ten figures were blasted out of the arena at the same time while blood sprayed from their mouths and noses and shrill cries shook the sky, and it instantly caused everyone present to be stirred.

The eyes of everyone was opened to the point of being completely round, and they were flabbergasted while their jaws almost hit the floor.

Originally, they thought that a fierce battle would occur, yet never had they imagined that this battle would actually be just as it was before. It ended with a single move, and it was unbelievably fast!

After all, that was over ten Heavenly Immortal Realm experts. Every single one of them had cultivations at the intermediate-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm or above. Moreover, since they were able to enter the Martial Emperor Domain, they were surely figures that were ranked on the Continental Ascensio Rankings of Southbridge Continent. 

Yet now…they’d actually been easily defeated like pieces of paper by a young man at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm!

Could anyone have expected such a scene would occur?

Such an unexpected event even drew the attention of the experts that were in battle on the nearby arenas, and there was no lack of figures that were flying over here.

Clang! Clang!

Right at this moment, a figure flashed onto the arena like a shadow. With a flick of his sleeve, sixteen crimson red streaks shot out violently while emanating a buzzing noise, and they were coiled by a large expanse of immortal mist. Moreover, they emanated a shocking aura that contained a fierce sharpness that terrified the soul.

“A Darkspirit Grade Immortal Artifact!”

“Sixteen immortal swords of the same attribute, and it’s even an entire set of Darkspirit Grade Immortal Artifacts!” All the experts exclaimed with shock. When they looked carefully, it was surprisingly sixteen immortal swords that were coiled by rising flaming mist, and every single sword was suffused with a peerless sword light and a shockingly ferocious and sharp aura.

Obviously, since it could be brought into the Martial Emperor Domain, it was surely an Immortal Artifact at the Darkspirit Grade or above. At this moment, it suddenly shot out explosively and covered the arena, and it obviously intended to annihilate Chen Xi and cut off all his paths of escape.

The Crimsonray Immortal Sword!

The spirits of those Yin Clansmen that were blasted flying out of the arena was refreshed, and they sneered without end. They knew that Yin Hun had made a move because only his cultivation at the Mysterious Immortal Realm was capable of bringing forth the might of this set of Darkspirit Grade Immortal Artifacts.

Yin Hun was the yellow clothed young man.

Chen Xi’s eyes lit up. Has an expert finally come to join the battle? 

He wasn’t apprehensive but delighted instead, and a strand of monstrous battle intent suddenly blazed within his chest. Talisman markings wove around his body as he charged forward while howling through the air, and he collided head on with a crimson red immortal sword that assaulted him.

Clang!

Sword and palm collided, and they erupted with a ball of dazzling light. Crimson light filled the sky while fierce killing intent rumbled as it spread towards the surroundings, and the restrictions on the arena were completely activated before it was able to disperse the aftershock of this collision.

This was an extremely terrifying fluctuation. If it was in the outside world, even the Immortal Dimension would suffer 500km of land being transformed into ruins.

“The Crimsonray Immortal Sword! Doesn’t it belong to Yin Miaomiao who’s ranked at the 4th on the Continental Ascensio Rankings? Why has it appeared in this young man’s possession?” Someone finally recognized the set of sixteen immortal swords and spoke with surprise and bewilderment.

But in next to no time, he couldn’t be bothered to pay attention to all of this because a fierce battle that was extremely rare in the recent years had erupted on the arena.

The reason it was rare was because it was a battle that occurred between an expert at the initial-stage of the Heavenly Immortal Realm and an expert at the initial-stage of the Mysterious Immortal Realm!
