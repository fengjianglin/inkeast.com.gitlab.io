Bang!

Imperial Monarch Xue Ying was an extraordinary figure, yet at this moment, he was actually struck by this 4m long iron rod to the point he staggered back in midair.

This caused his expression to change.

At this moment, even Chen Xi, Ye Yan, Imperial Monarch Kun Mu, and Shaohao Yu were shocked.

An Imperial Monarch was shaken from a single strike of the rod?

Exactly how monstrous would the force behind this attack be?

Right at this moment, they finally saw it clearly. The one that had swung this iron rod was actually a monkey that possessed brilliant fur and an appearance that seemed clever and bright. It was exactly the Gold-eyed Rhesus Monkey, Little Bao.

At this moment, he seemed to be very domineering. He had one hand on the iron rod and the other on his waist, and he grinned while he shouted at Shaohao Yu. “Grandson, did Grandpa not beat you enough just now?”

In an instant, Shaohao Yu’s expression changed, and he roared. “It’s you! Damnable monkey! Kill! Kill it!!”

Imperial Monarch Kun Mu and Imperial Monarch Xue Ying exchanged glances. Both of them realized that this Gold-eyed Rhesus Monkey was extraordinary, and they very tacitly chose to attack in unison.

“Kill!”

“We’ll deal with this monkey first!”

When two Imperial Monarchs attacked jointly, it instantly caused the surroundings to dim down while space was thrown into disorder. Moreover, a strand of an indescribably terrifying auras covered this area, and it seemed extremely shocking.

Bang!

Imperial Monarch Kun Mu flicked his sleeve, causing a myriad of black vines to grow madly from within space. The stretched out and twisted together to form what seemed like a whip of the heavens, and it danced wildly in midair as it whipped down forcefully towards Little Bao.

This was a supreme technique called Spirit Dao Illusory Vine technique, and it was passed down in the Goumang Clan. Once one was touched by the black vines, they would madly absorb the spirit, energy, and essence within one’s body, causing even one’s soul to be unable to escape. It was extremely overbearing and vicious.

Clang!

At the same time, Imperial Monarch Xue Ying withdrew a blood red sabre, and then his figure flashed like a blood red bolt of lightning that flowed indeterminately through space. Strand after strand of terrifying sabre lights seemed like blood red crescents that descended from outer space, and they crushed the landscape as they swept over violently while whistling through the air.

When they witnessed this scene, Chen Xi and Ye Yan practically instinctively dodged towards the distance. This force as too formidable, and it exceeded the scope of their ability to resist it.

On the other hand, Chen Xi immediately felt worried for Little Bao.

Earlier, he’d confirmed that this monkey was clearly only at the Universe Enlightened Ancestral God Realm. If it wasn’t for that, he wouldn’t be so surprised when Little Bao shook Imperial Monarch Xue Ying with a single strike of the rod.

Yet now, how would Little Bao face the full forced attacks of two Imperial Monarchs?

These thoughts merely flashed in Chen Xi’s mind, and Chen Xi even intended to enter the battlefield and fight desperately alongside Little Bao.

However, right at this moment, the fur on Little Bao’s body stood on end.

Roar!

Little Bao emanated a roar that shook the heavens and the earth. In an instant, his nimble little body had actually erupted with a myriad of strands of golden radiance.

In the next moment, his body suddenly rose violently by 300m and grew to possess three heads and six arms. Moreover, the fur throughout his body emanated brilliant gold light while piece after piece of muscle bulged up from its body like rocks, and its entire body emanated a terrifying and violent aura that was almost monstrous.

Especially his pupils, they had completely transformed into bright golden pupils, and his gaze was like a golden bolt of lightning that shot through the heavens and the earth!

At this moment, Little Bao simply seemed completely different from the past. It was like a primeval divine ape had descended here, and his might was formidable to the extreme.

Most shocking of it all was that even Little Bao’s cultivation had actually instantly risen explosively to the Imperial Monarch Realm!

All of this took a long time to describe, yet it actually occurred in an instant.

Amidst his roar, the iron rod in Little Bao’s hand seemed to have transformed into a pillar capable of holding up the heavens, and he forcefully smashed it in a violent, direct, and even savage manner towards the attacks directed at him.

Rumble!

Rumble!

Terrifying sounds of rumbling and explosions resounded. The black vines that covered the sky were forcefully smashed into bits by this strike, and they flew throughout the surroundings.

On the other hand, the strands of blood red sabre light actually rumbled and transformed into powder like extremely fragile glass, causing a rain of light to descend throughout the surroundings.

The joint forces of these two Imperial Monarchs had actually been forcefully blasted apart by Little Bao with the iron rod in his hand, and they dispersed into nothingness.

Chen Xi was flabbergasted by this scene, and his heart shook violently. He finally understood why Senior White said this Gold-eyed Rhesus Monkey was unique in the world. It was a monster!

Ye Yan gasped. Never had she imagined that this stinking monkey that peeked on her while she changed would actually be so strong and ferocious.

“Kill it! Kill it!” Shaohao Yu was shocked and furious. It was unknown whether it was out of terror or rage, but he shouted in a practically insane manner.

There was no need for Shaohao Yu to remind them before Imperial Monarch Kun Mu and Imperial Monarch Xue Ying were clearly aware that they would be helpless against this monkey if they didn’t go all out.

Swish!

Imperial Monarch Kun Mu formed profound seals with his hand, and the image of a towering three was reflected from his body. It was suffused with holy radiance while glistening and verdant leaves that were like strands of Divine Chains of Order dropped from it.

The image of the tree was the Innate Form of the innate god, Ju Mang. Now it had been utilized by Imperial Monarch Kun Mu via a secret technique, and every single branch and leaf of it carried a force capable of crushing all Daos and obliterating the world.

Swoosh!

A bloody glow shot into the sky from Imperial Monarch Xue Ying, and it was like he’d transformed into a blood red ocean. Moreover, there were faint sights of platinum colored bolts of lightning flickered within it, and then all of these bolts of lightning coiled onto the sabre in his hand.

The sabre seemed as if it had awakened from deep sleep, and its might rose steadily. In the end, it actually transformed from blood red to platinum in color, and it illuminated the surroundings while its murderous aura caused the world to tremble.

This was the Gold God Combat Technique! It possessed unprecedented murderous might, and it was a supreme technique passed down from the Gold God, Ru Shou. It’s might was immeasurable.

When both Imperial Monarchs attacked with their full strengths, how could the phenomena created by this be ordinary?

In an instant, the restrictions within Divine Arambha Garden were alarmed, and the violet stars in the sky suddenly emanated a myriad of strands of violet divine radiance.

It was like a violet waterfall was descending from the starry sky.

However, to Chen Xi’s surprise, this strand of the energy of the restrictions didn’t passively resist the aftershock of the battle like it had in the past, and it seemed as if possessed intelligence and fully surged into Little Bao’s body.

In an instant, it caused Little Bao’s imposing aura to rise explosively without end, and he revealed a ferocious and haughty aura of supremacy. It seemed as if so long as Little Bao was willing, then he would be able to crush the heavens and the earth!

It felt like Little Bao had become the master of Divine Arambha Garden, and all the restrictions here and the energy of the Grand Dao here were in complete control!

Bang!

He struck with a swing of his hand, and nothing could stop it as it surged through the surroundings. All attacks were like paper before this strike of the rod, and they were easily crushed into pieces.

He seemed as if he was capable of sweeping through the world.

This scene was too terrifying, and it shocked Imperial Monarch Kun Mu and Imperial Monarch Xue Ying to the point the corners of their mouths twitched. How could this animal have become so terrifying?

Imperial Monarch Xue Ying was just about to withdraw his sabre and dodge the brunt of this attack, yet Little Bao suddenly strode through space and his iron rod smashed down once more.

It was extremely simple, rough, and savage, yet its aura could be said to be world shocking.

Bang!

Merely a single strike of the rod blasted away the sabre in Imperial Monarch Xue Ying’s hand, and it wailed violently and endlessly. On the other hand, Imperial Monarch Xue Ying himself was struck to the point blood sprayed from his mouth, and he smashed forcefully onto the ground like a meteor.

No wonder the Goddess said that I should seek help from Little Bao if I encountered trouble that was difficult to deal with. So, it turns out that this monkey can utilize the energy of the restrictions in the entire Divine Arambha Garden…. Chen Xi finally came to an understanding. Even then, he still couldn’t help but exclaim repeatedly with surprise as he watched Little Bao display invincible martial prowess.

As for Ye Yan, she’d been dumbstruck since a long time ago.

“How dare you, animal!” Imperial Monarch Kun Mu’s heart shook, and his eyes almost split apart from rage while he did all he could to rescue Imperial Monarch Xue Ying.

“Old Bastard! Who do you think you are to call your Grandpa an animal?” Little Bao roared loudly while he seemed extremely violent. His 300m tall body swung the iron rod down forcefully, causing strong winds to whistle past it.

Imperial Monarch Kun Mu’s entire body went cold as he’d sensed danger. He couldn’t be bothered to rescue Imperial Monarch Xue Ying any longer, and he suddenly flashed with the intention of traversing space and avoiding this attack.

However, in merely an instant, his figure froze. The space seemed like a wall that was extremely hard, and it had actually trapped him within it!

This unexpected event terrified Imperial Monarch Kun Mu to the point his soul almost left his body.

Shit! He roared loudly in his heart as he circulated his entire cultivation, and he did everything he could with the intention of escaping.

Bang!

Right at this moment, Little Bao’s iron rod tore through the sky and arrived before him. Even though Imperial Monarch Kun Mu utilized a technique to block it, the violet impact from it shook the bones in his entire body to the point of cracking apart. Fresh blood seeped out from every single pore on his body, and he was instantly covered in blood.

After that, his entire body shivered while he seemed as if he couldn’t hold on any longer, and he felt to the ground while his entire body twitched incessantly.

In the blink of an eye, two Imperial Monarchs had been defeated successively, and they’d been completely crushed by the iron rod!

This scene could simply be said to be breathtaking and world shocking. It was utterly unimaginable that the Gold-eyed Rhesus Monkey would actually possess such monstrous might.

It could be said to be peerless!

Chen Xi couldn’t help but be astounded in his heart. Little Bao was too formidable, and it was even to the extent that Chen Xi was unable to link the nimble and obedient Little Bao from before to the ferocious figure standing before him.

The impact that Chen Xi felt from the divine might Little Bao revealed was obvious.

As for Ye Yan… she was still in a stunned state….

“How could this be possible!? How could this be possible!?”

At this moment, Shaohao Yu was on the verge of going mad because of the scene that occurred before his eyes, and he was on the verge of complete collapse.

That was two Imperial Monarchs!

Yet they were actually crushed by a damnable monkey!

If word of this were to spread, would anyone dare to believe it?

“Grandson!” Little Bao pointed the iron rod in his hand at Shaohao Yu. “Didn’t Grandpa warn you before throwing you out?”

Shaohao Yu’s entire body trembled, and he almost wet himself from fear. He forcefully braced himself and gritted his teeth as he said, “What? Could it be that you really dare to kill this Young Master?”

“You dare talk back!? You’re looking for a beating!” As he shouted, Little Bao’s swung the rod horizontally and blasted Shaohao Yu flying, and Shaohao Yu let out shrill cries incessantly as he flew.

Thump!

After that, he fell to the ground, yet his butt was already mangled, and even the bones within were shattered, causing him to be unable to stand up.

Coupled with his disheveled hair and swollen appearance he wasn’t just in a miserable state, he was simply miserable beyond compare.

At this point, Imperial Monarch Kun Mu, Imperial Monarch Xue Ying, and Shaohao Yu were heavily injured and defeated, and they’d completely lost that imposing aura of supremacy that they had when they’d just arrived here.

At this moment, even Chen Xi was slightly unable to bear the sight of these three fellows. It was too miserable. The Gold-eyed Rhesus Monkey, Little Bao, didn’t know how to hold back at all, and he was violent, savage, and ferocious to the extreme.

“Chen Xi, should we kill them or let them go this time?” Suddenly, Little Bao turned around and grinned as he asked Chen Xi this question.
