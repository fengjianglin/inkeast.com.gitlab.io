Chapter 1467 Defying The Heavens

Action Cooking Crafting Fantasy Mystery Xuanhuan Chinese Ongoing 2017/09/22

Talisman Emperor

Amongst the people present here, besides Tie Yunhai and the Old Scholar, Meng Xinghe, Li Yang, Zhao Taici, Ao Jiuhui, Chi Cangsheng, Yuan Che, Kong Lin, Yuchi Wan, Yun Su, Shi Yu, and Xiangliu Li had attained the Godrank Realm.

There was a total of thirteen of them.

The others were all beneath the Godrank Realm.

According to Li Yang, when this calamity erupted, the existences at the Godrank Realm would be the first to bear the brunt of it. Moreover, unless all the existences at the Godrank Realm within the three dimensions were annihilated, otherwise this calamity would temporarily not affect existences below the Godrank Realm.

So as soon as Tie Yunhai finished speaking, the expressions of the existences at the Godrank Realm turned solemn. They circulated their cultivations, withdrew their respective Divine Artifacts, and then accumulated strength as they waited.

“The Last Days Domain is behind that door in the sky. If we’re able to pass through it, then we’ll have a chance to enter the ancient God Domain, whereas if we’re unable to pass through it, then we’ll either perish or be eternally imprisoned within it.” Tie Yunhai controlled the divine formation to resist the Divine Chains of Order that smashed down without end while he swiftly spoke via voice transmission and warned everyone that they had to be careful.

“As for you, Little Junior Brother, you don’t have to be nervous. The Divine Chains of Order won’t affect you for now.” As he spoke, Tie Yunhai seemed to have realized something, and he sent a voice transmission to Chen Xi alone. “Little Junior Brother, even though the Iris Immortal Prison has been destroyed, there’s a safe place within it. You can head over there once this calamity falls still. At that time, you’ll understand everything.”

Chen Xi’s heart shook, and he nodded.

“Little Junior Brother, this is our Oracle Mountain’s Star Spirit Jade Disk. You’ll be able to arrive at the area where the sect resides by relying on this treasure. After we part ways this time, I don’t know when we’ll be able to meet again, so you have to take good care of yourself.” Suddenly, Li Yang spoke as she passed a jade disk that was suffused with piercingly cold starlight to Chen Xi, and then she revealed an expression that showed her sorrow and reluctance to part with Chen Xi.

Chen Xi’s heart shook. “Senior Sister, you’re leaving as well?”

“It isn’t just me. All the Senior Brothers and Senior Sisters of our Oracle Mountain are leaving. No one is able to escape this calamity in the end…” Li Yang sighed lightly and was slightly dejected.

A wisp of complicated feelings suddenly arose in Chen Xi’s heart, and he took a deep breath and put the Star Spirit Jade Disk away in the end. After that, he said in a serious and solemn manner, “Don’t worry Senior Sister, I’ll take care of everything in the sect.”

Li Yang said with a smile, “All of these are only external belongings, and there’s no need to attach too much importance to them. One day, this calamity will affect you in the end. Perhaps, we’ll be able to meet once more in the ancient God Domain.”

“Little Junior Brother, I left the sect hastily this time, and there are numerous books of mine still within the sect. If you have the free time, then please keep them safe and don’t allow them to be destroyed in this calamity.” The Old Scholar instructed.

“Don’t worry Senior Brother, leave it to me.” Chen Xi nodded and agreed without the slightest hesitation.

“Little Junior Brother, there are still numerous Innate Bone Markings I gathered from all over the three dimensions in the recent years within the sect, and there are many formidable Divine Techniques amongst them. Little Junior Brother, take all of them and study them. Hahaha!” Tie Yunhai roared with laughter as he instructed.

“Junior Brother Chen Xi, you have to take good care of Dao Emperor Academy after I leave. It’s something the Dao Emperor established painstakingly, and you must not allow others to encroach on it. When you head to the Last Days Domain, you should find a reliable candidate to take over the academy as well. In this way, it would be passed down forever, and we wouldn’t let down the Dao Emperor’s kindness of founding the academy.” At this moment, Meng Xinghe spoke as well, and he passed an extremely old bronze jade seal to Chen Xi.

This jade seal was only the size of a fist, and it was branded with grand diagrams of the sun, moon, stars, the land, and so on and so forth. It emanated boundless energy of Karmic Luck that assaulted the face.

This was the Ultimate Treasure of Dao Emperor Academy, the Nineflow Jade Seal. It was a vital treasure that held down Karmic Luck, and it was passed down for boundless years. Moreover, this jade seal had long since been nurtured by the Karmic Luck of the Heaven Dao into a Divine Artifact, and it was unfathomably profound.

Only the Dean of Dao Emperor Academy could possess this jade seal.

When they saw this scene, Hua Jiankong, Zuoqiu Feiming, Xuanyuan Shao, and the others were shocked in their hearts, and the gazes they shot at Chen Xi couldn’t help but become slightly complicated. Because Meng Xinghe’s actions of passing this jade seal to Chen Xi was equivalent to telling the entire Immortal Dimension that Chen Xi would succeed his position as Dean of Dao Emperor Academy!

Chen Xi didn’t know what the Nineflow Jade Seal meant. However, even if Meng Xinghe didn’t do this, he would still so all of this for Meng Xinghe because the Dao Emperor, Ji Yu, was his own Martial Uncle.

“Chen Xi, now that you’ve already stepped foot into the Half-step Immortal King Realm, these are all the experiences and comprehensions I obtained over the years after advancing into the Immortal King Realm. I haven’t taken any disciples, so it’s useless if I keep it. So why not give it to you.” Midnight Immortal King spoke at this moment as well, and she passed over a light violet colored jade slip to Chen Xi.

“You’re…leaving as well?” This time, Chen Xi was stunned instead because he was clearly aware that Midnight Immortal King hadn’t advanced into the Godrank Realm.

“I’m only a trace away from advancing, and I’m able to sense that the Last Days Vortex is where my fortuitous encounter resides.” Midnight Immortal King Grinned as she spoke.

Chen Xi couldn’t help but glance at his Third Senior Brother, Tie Yunhai.

Tie Yunhai pondered deeply for a moment before he said, “If one enters the Last Days Domain by one’s self, then all living beings in the world can arrive there. However, the tribulations one would encounter while entering at this moment with all the experts at the Godrank Realm isn’t something that just anyone can endure.”

As he spoke, he briefly sized up Midnight Immortal King, and then he nodded and said, “You already possess the foundation to become a god. If you’re determined to take this path, then come along with me.”

“Thank you, Daoist Brother, for helping me achieve this wish of mine!” Midnight Immortal King bowed with delight as she spoke.

At this moment, even Hua Jiankong, Xuanyuan Shao, Xuanyuan Fengchen, Xuanyuan Tabei, and Zuoqiu Feiming were extremely tempted as well.

Even though this calamity was extremely terrifying, and there was only a slim chance of survival while there was only a trace of fortuitous encounter to be found. However, if they were able to follow by the side of Oracle Mountain’s Third Lord, Tie Yunhai, and the others, then they would undoubtedly be much safer, and their chances of obtaining fortune would improve greatly!

Even if they were existences at the Immortal King Realm, but what if they advanced into the Godrank Realm within the Last Days Domain? What if they entered the ancient God Domain?

Sometimes, even if one was clearly aware that the path ahead was covered in brambles and death, but for the sake of obtaining that trace of fortune to attain the Dao, no one would be able to refuse the temptation of such fortune.

Tie Yunhai swept Hua Jiankong and the others with his gaze, and then he said, “All of you have to consider this carefully.”

All of their eyes lit up. He didn’t refuse?

They practically didn’t hesitate to agree, and their expressions were extremely firm. The calamity was extremely terrifying while the Last Days Domain was extremely terrifying as well, but for the sake of going even further on their path towards the Grand Dao, they intended to fight for it this time!

Beneath the calamity, all gods fought for supremacy, and they set out to vie for that trace of fortuitous encounter. If one didn’t vie, then one was bound to have no choice but to stop on one’s path towards the Grand Dao, feel powerless and frustrated, and then accomplish nothing from then onwards!

All the Immortal Kings present here were figures that possessed great determination and willpower, so they would naturally not miss such an opportunity.

“You can’t. Your own Immortal King Foundation has been exhausted too greatly, so you’ll only be courting death if you followed us.” Suddenly, Tie Yunhai spoke as he gazed at Zuoqiu Feiming, and he wasn’t tactful with his words at all.

In an instant, Zuoqiu Feiming’s countenance turned dim and ghastly pale. He’d tried to detonate himself twice during the violent battles earlier, and even though he didn’t succeed in the end, it had damaged his foundation.

He himself understood this, but when he thought about how he was about to miss the opportunity to obtain that trace of a fortuitous encounter, he couldn’t help but feel an extreme sense of loss.”

“Senior, you’ll still have a chance in the future.” Chen Xi consoled.

Zuoqiu Feiming nodded and forced out a smile, yet he was still grievous in his heart. I will indeed have a chance in the future. But at that time, how could I possibly have the chance to follow Oracle Mountain’s Third Lord all along the way?

While they spoke with each other, Tie Yunhai had been constantly resisting the Divine Chains of Order that attacked without end. Up until the moment, the strength of the Divine Chains of Order had become more and more terrifying, and they fluttered throughout the sky and drowned the heavens and the earth!

Bang! Bang! Bang!

The divine formation fluctuated violently, and it was on the verge of collapse.

This caused Tie Yunhai’s expression to instantly become murderous and solemn, and it was filled with an awe-inspiring aura, “Fourth Brother, Little Junior Sister, are both of you prepared?”

It’s finally time! The hearts of everyone shook, and they didn’t dare let their thoughts run wild any longer while they accumulated strength and waited.

“Wait!” Li Yang seemed to have thought of something, and she withdrew a folded net. This treasure was crystalline, translucent, and emanated strands of piercingly cold starlight. It seemed ethereal and dreamlike, and it was light to the point it seemed as if it didn’t exist. Moreover, it was covered in an aura that enchanted the mind.

“Little Junior Brother, take this Overarching Heaven Net. In a moment, when we head to the Last Days Domain, utilize this treasure to capture the shattered Divine Chains of Order…” Li Yang passed the treasure over to Chen Xi as she said via voice transmission, “Remember, you must grasp this opportunity. This is an unprecedented calamity in the three dimensions, and it’s similarly an unprecedentedly great Karmic Luck. Perhaps you’ll be able to rely on this opportunity to establish your Immortal King Foundation, otherwise once you miss this opportunity, you’ll have to wait bitterly for great Karmic Luck to descend again, and who knows how long that will take.”

Chen Xi was shocked in his heart, and he was both moved and pleasantly surprised.

He recognized this treasure. It was refined from Starsoul Sand and Chaotic Five-colored Rocks, and it was a Natural Spirit Treasure left behind by the Master of Oracle Mountain, Fuxi. As it was said, the net of the heavens captures all. Even the Dao Insight, Laws, Karmic Luck, and other ethereal things in the heavens and the earth could be captured by this treasure!

All those years ago when a great and unexpected change occurred in the heavens and the earth while he was in the Dark Reverie, Li Yang had relied on this net to help him capture a strand of the shattered energy of the Heaven Dao, allowing him to completely establish his foundation to ascend into the Heavenly Immortal Realm.

Now, Li Yang obviously intended to make Chen Xi rely on this treasure to capture the Divine Chains of Order in order to seize the Karmic Luck within them and establish his foundation to advance into the Immortal King Realm!

How could Chen Xi not be moved and pleasantly surprised by such a present and her concern?

“Little Junior Sister really is thoughtful. Everyone, let’s begin!” Tie Yunhai roared with laughter and didn’t hesitate any longer to suddenly remove the divine formation, and then he held the enormous hammer in his hand while soaring up into the sky.

Rumble!

When the divine formation vanished, countless thick and large Divine Chains of Orders instantly enveloped down, and they seemed like a myriad of iron cages that intended to restrain everyone.

“Get the fuck away!” Tie Yunhai roared loudly while his gaze was like a bolt of lightning. He emanated monstrous divine might while a terrifying aura of divinity rumbled on his bronze skin, and he seemed like a god that was defying the heavens as he fought.

With a single smash of his hammer, the myriad of Divine Chains of Order that surged and rumble over were actually shattered inch by inch!

This scene was simply mighty and ferocious to the limit.

All of the others that wholeheartedly desired to enter the Last Days Domain were shocked in their hearts. They didn’t dare hesitate to follow closely behind Tie Yunhai, and they were deeply afraid that they would lose this chance if they were a bit too slow.

“Little Junior Brother, make your move!” At practically the exact same moment, Li Yang cried out in a clear voice. She reminded Chen Xi to make a move and gather the shattered fragments of the Divine Chains of Order.
