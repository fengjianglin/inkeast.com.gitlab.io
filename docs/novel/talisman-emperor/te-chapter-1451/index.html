Chapter 1451 The Fall of An Immortal King

Action Cooking Crafting Fantasy Mystery Xuanhuan Chinese Ongoing 2017/09/22

Talisman Emperor

The battle was still going on.

Chen Xi was silent, murderous, swift, and fierce. As the battle went on, not only did his imposing aura not weaken, it rose steadily instead, and he revealed an aura of sweeping through the heavens and the earth.

If they didn’t witness it with their own eyes, it would be absolutely impossible for them to imagine that Chen Xi was already formidable to such an extent, and he could be considered a peerless genius.

In the battlefield, a rain of blood poured down from the sky while shrill cries shook the heavens.

It was like a bloody purgatory had descended into the world.

In the end, Wei Xing’s heart felt cold, and he didn’t dare hesitate at all to shout in a grim voice and order the other higher-ups in Zuoqiu Feng’s faction to join the battle.

There were more than thirty higher-ups in Zuoqiu Feng’s faction, and every single one of them was at the Half-step Immortal King Realm. They were originally unwilling to join the battle, but they were clearly aware that once Chen Xi was allowed to continue slaughtering the others like this, then it would be their turn once all those dogs of the Sovereign Sect had perished.

Thus, they joined the battlefield in the end.

In an instant, Chen Xi’s opponents increased by over thirty. Moreover, the addition of these elders from the Zuoqiu Clan did indeed greatly increase the pressure upon Chen Xi.

“These damnable bastards! They’re actually attacking A’Xue’s son with the dogs of the Sovereign Sect!”

“They’re simply shameless to the extreme!”

“I really wish for nothing more than to annihilate these bastards!”

When they witnessed this scene, Zuoqiu Feiming and the others were angered to the point their entire bodies trembled, and they felt both furious and sad in their hearts. Because all of them were originally clansmen that had the same bloodline flowing within them, yet they’d now turned hostile against each other and were killing each other…

How sad was that!?

It was even to the extent that for the sake of killing their clansmen, they didn’t hesitate to rely on the strength of the infamous Sovereign Sect. If the Ancestors of the Zuoqiu Clan saw this scene, they would probably be angered to death by this scene.

…

All of this did indeed greatly increase the pressure Chen Xi felt.

However, he wasn’t fearful but delighted instead.

Compared to these important pawns of the Sovereign Sect, he hoped even more to be able to personally annihilate these bastards of the Zuoqiu Clan!

If it wasn’t for them, would my mother, Zuoqiu Xue, be imprisoned? And how could the Chen Clan have possibly been completely slaughtered and almost annihilated?

Chen Xi had come here precisely for the sake of taking revenge on the Zuoqiu Clan. At this moment, when they actually took the initiative to fight him, Chen Xi naturally wouldn’t refuse this superb opportunity to take revenge.

Kill!

In the next moment, Chen Xi focused on these higher-ups of the Zuoqiu Clan, and he attacked ruthlessly and mercilessly. He didn’t give them any chance to survive!

Bang! 

An elder of the Zuoqiu Clan wasn’t able to dodge in time, and his neck was directly twisted apart by a palm strike from Chen Xi. He transformed into a headless corpse that shot out and perished on the spot.

“Damnable bastard! If we knew this would happen, we shouldn’t have spared your life all those years ago!” Another black clothed old man roared furiously, and he was madly enraged. His arms stretched out as he executed the profundities of space, and it formed a myriad of shapeless palm shadows that smashed down at Chen Xi.

From the beginning until the end, Chen Xi remained silent, indifferent, cold, and murderous. He took a step forward, causing space to shatter into pieces, whereas his entire body suddenly vanished on the spot.

In the next moment, his figure appeared before the black clothed old man, and he suddenly stretched out his hand to grab the old man’s neck. After that, he twisted fiercely and forcefully twisted the old man’s head off.

Swish!

A strand of thick blood sprayed out from the old man’s neck, and it sprayed all over Chen Xi’s body, dying his clothes red. It caused him to seem even more indifferent and cruel, and he revealed a bloody and merciless aura.

Bang!

Chen Xi casually crushed the head, and then his figure flashed. He left an afterimage in space while he charged towards another elder of the Zuoqiu Clan at the side.

At this moment, the hatred that had accumulated throughout the years in Chen Xi’s heart had erupted like a volcano. It surged throughout his body, and it caused every inch of his skin to tremble.

He needed to vent!

Because he’d exercised forbearance for too many years, and he’d exhausted an innumerable amount of sweat and blood for this! Moreover, he’d experienced countless slaughters and battles for this!

Now, this day had finally come, so how could he possibly restrain himself?

If he didn’t vent, then all this hatred would transform into a knot in his heart that was impossible to undo, and it was even to the extent that it would become an inner demon that severed his path towards the Dao!

Kill! 

Kill!

Kill!

At this moment, Chen Xi had even forgotten the battle between Immortal Kings that was being carried out above the sky, and he’d forgotten exactly how many opponents he had. There was only a single thought in his heart — Vent!

Vent his hatred!

Only the blood and death of his enemies could calm the hatred in his heart!

Expanse after expanse of rains of blood that contained severed limbs poured down while shrill and miserable cries resounded like a song of death. It surged ceaselessly in the surroundings, and it caused Chen Xi’s battle intent to grow exuberant, burn like lava, and seem as if it could incinerate the sky!

At this moment, no matter if it was Wei Xing and the others from the Sovereign Sect, or the elders of the Zuoqiu Clan, all of them noticed to their shock that Chen Xi seemed as if he’d become a completely different person.

His eyes were scarlet red like blood while his dense jet black hair fluttered, and the imposing aura he emanated grew in strength and viciousness. It seemed like a primeval ferocious beast had awakened inside him, and it intended to swallow his enemies!

Under such circumstances, they ceaselessly retreated in defeat while death arrived without end. No matter how they struggled and resisted, they were actually unable to obstruct Chen Xi at all.

It was too terrifying!

It was horrifying!

Everyone, including Wei Xing, felt their souls almost leave their bodies, and their will to fight collapsed inch by inch. They were on the verge of being unable to endure this lethal pressure.

Who would have imagined that all of this was actually caused by Chen Xi alone?

Actually, they were utterly unaware that if it was in terms of cultivation, Chen Xi’s cultivation was over a hundred times deeper than ordinary Half-step Immortal Kings. Moreover, he even possessed the assistance of the Dark Parasol Sapling that guaranteed his Immortal Force wouldn’t be exhausted.

In terms of his cultivation in the Dao Heart, Chen Xi had attained the Heart Infant Realm a long time ago when he was at the Saint Immortal Realm, and it could compare with a true Immortal King. It was utterly not something that a Half-step Immortal King could compare to, and it guaranteed that he possessed sufficient reserves to endure for a long time in battle.

In terms of his cultivation in the Sword Dao, he’d attained perfection in the Sword God Realm. Even most of the seniors in Dao Emperor Academy had been left far behind by Chen Xi.

In terms of his cultivation in Dao Insights, merely the Talisman Saint Dao he’d established himself was sufficient to crush all Saint Immortals in the world. Now that he’d advanced into the Half-step Immortal King Realm, his Talisman Saint Dao had fused with the three supreme Laws of time, space, and life and death. Thus, it was obvious how terrifying his Talisman Saint Dao was.

As for combat experience, combat will, and other aspects. Even if Chen Xi still hadn’t stepped foot into the peak, it wasn’t something that an ordinary expert could compare to.

When all of this was included together, it transformed into the terrifying combat strength that Chen Xi possessed now. Under such circumstances, how could Chen Xi be afraid when facing a group of opponents that possessed the same cultivation realm as him?

It was even to the extent that under the effect of his hatred, the combat strength Chen Xi revealed was even slightly stronger than normal!

On the other hand, even though Wei Xing and the others were Half-step Immortal Kings, how could they compare to Chen Xi? In other words, they didn’t have the qualifications to compare themselves with Chen Xi at all!

…

Once the will to fight had collapsed, they were bound to be utterly defeated.

As they faced Chen Xi that seemed like a Fiendgod that was impossible to shake at all, Wei Xing and the other Half-step Immortal Kings felt a wisp of dense terror in their hearts.

At this moment, they were like camels that were on the verge of death, and only the weight of a single straw would be sufficient to completely collapse them!

“Ah!!!” However, right at this moment, an extremely miserable shrill cry resounded from above the sky, and it was so ear piercing that it didn’t just cause the hearts of Wei Xing and the others to tremble, even the heart of Chen Xi that had fallen into an enraged and almost mad state shook abruptly at this moment, causing him to recover some clarity.

It wasn’t just that, even Zuoqiu Feiming and the others in the distance shot their gazes towards the sky in succession, and they revealed astounded expressions.

Swish!

A golden rain of blood started to pour down from above the sky, and it was dazzling, blazing, and illuminated the heavens and the earth.

“Ancestor Huanglin!” Zuoqiu Feng’s sorrowful and furious howl resounded from above the sky.

Everyone came to an understanding when they heard this. The golden and dazzling blood of an Immortal King that poured down actually belonged to Zuoqiu Huanglin, and Zuoqiu Huanglin himself had transformed into mangled corpse that floated in midair now…

An Immortal King had actually fallen!

Everyone gasped.

Obviously, that extremely shrill and miserable cry from before had come from Zuoqiu Huanglin.

On the other hand, the person that caused all of this was naturally Midnight Immortal King!

At this moment, her clothes fluttered while her figure floated there, and her peerlessly pretty face revealed an otherworldly and proud expression. Her entire body was enveloped by a wisp of light violet divine radiance, and she seemed like a female god.

It was truly difficult to imagine exactly what technique she’d utilized to be actually able to kill Zuoqiu Huanglin while facing two opponents by herself.

“Do you need my help?” Dian Dian grinned in midair as she looked at Chen Xi.

“If it’s possible, then go help him first.” Chen Xi shook his head, and then shot his gaze towards the sky. At the place he gazed at, Hua Jiankong was fighting three opponents by himself. However, at this point in the battle, he’d started to faintly show signs of being suppressed.

It wasn’t just Hua Jiankong’s battle, Xuanyuan Shao, Xuanyuan Fengchen, and Xuanyuan Tabei’s battles were still going on as well. Even if Zuoqiu Huanglin had perished, it didn’t affect them at all.

But comparatively speaking, Hua Jiankong’s situation was slightly bad.

So Chen Xi suggested that Dian Dian went to help Hua Jiankong.

“I’ll listen to you.” Dian Dian agreed, and then she glanced inadvertently at Zuoqiu Feng before she said, “This old fellow helped you resist a strike earlier, and that was the reason I was able to kill him. Now, there’s no one to help you anymore.”

As she spoke, her fair hand shook, and then two violet battle axes soared up. One of them shot towards Zuoqiu Feng while the other tore through the layer upon layers of space to assault Hua Jiankong’s opponent.

Chen Xi’s felt reassured when he saw this. The situation was already leaning towards their side, and what he had to do now was to seize this opportunity to deal with all these fellows before him!

When he thought up to here, he didn’t hesitate any longer. However, right at the instant he was about to make a move, his gaze glance inadvertently, and he suddenly noticed that Zuoqiu Huanglin’s corpse was actually enveloped by a strand of strange energy at this moment. It seemed like his energy, essence, spirit, and even flesh and thoughts were being condensed and revived gradually…

The Calamity Resurrection Pill!

Chen Xi had once witnessed this scene when he killed Bing Shitian. So how could he not understand that Zuoqiu Huanglin had definitely consumed this pill that allowed one to be instantly resurrected!?

Chen Xi practically instinctively intended to flash up into the sky and execute the energy of Terminus to completely annihilate Zuoqiu Huanglin. Unfortunately, he was a step too late in the end.

Right when he realized this, Zuoqiu Huanglin had already come back to life.

This scene instantly shocked everyone in the surroundings!
