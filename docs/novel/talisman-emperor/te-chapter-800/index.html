At the bank of the river shaped like the character ‘道’.

Suddenly, under the focused gazes of everyone present here, the drawing that blotted out the sky and was floating in midair all along emanated boundless immortal radiance that shot into the nine heavens, and it instantly dispelled the dark veil of night!

“The Universal Devil Suppression Sword Formation has been activated!”

“Could it be that the victor is about to be decided?”

“What a terrifying scene. Once a slaughter formation like this is activated, how many people in the world are capable of escaping it?”

Everyone was shocked and exploded into an uproar. All of them had heavy expressions while they stared with eyes wide open fixedly at the drawings that formed the Universal Devil Suppression Sword Formation, as they were deeply afraid that they would miss the slightest detail.

“The Universal Devil Suppression Sword Formation has actually been activated. Looks like the Grand Five Element Heavenflow Slaughter Formation wasn’t capable of crushing Chen Xi.” Leng Chan’er sighed with emotion, and she had a slightly complicated expression on her face. A figure like Chen Xi is really a peerless figure that leads ahead of all his peers in the world. Unfortunately, he went against Lord Bing Shitian, so he’s destined to be an enemy.

After that, she recovered her composure and smiled as she spoke to the nearby Elder Yun Zhu. “There’s no need for Martial Uncle to make a move this time.”

Elder Yun Zhu stood with his hands behind his back and replied indifferently. “Not to mention that little kid, even I only have 40% confidence in escaping 13 Earthly Immortal Realm experts and the Universal Devil Suppression Sword Formation.”

Leng Chan’er smiled, and then she looked at the nearby Wen Tianshuo. “Marquis, it won’t be a moment before Chen Xi will undoubtedly die. Why don’t we seize this opportunity to look into some matters related to the treasure map?”

Wen Tianshuo had always been staring fixedly at the eight terrifying sword drawings in midair, and he seemed to be lost in thought. He suddenly recovered his senses when he heard this, and then nodded repeatedly. “That’s for the best, that’s for the best.”

At this moment, he finally heaved a long sigh of relief when he found out that Chen Xi would die for sure.

Truthfully speaking, he’d always been in a state of anxiety and fear when cooperating with the members of the Heavenflow Dao Sect to trap Chen Xi in this hopeless situation.

It couldn’t be helped, Chen Xi was truly too terrifying. Not only did Chen Xi possess a heaven defying strength, even his background was terrifying to the extreme, and his Marquis Wen’s Estate would surely be subjected to the danger of annihilation if Chen Xi didn’t die.

It was even to the extent that even if he fled into the protection of the Heavenflow Dao Sect, he would surely become a thorn in the side of Chen Xi and the Nine Radiance Sword Sect, and days like that were absolutely horrible.

But now that he found out that Chen Xi would die without a doubt, it could be considered as a great consolation that allowed him to be much more at ease.

Subsequently, Leng Chan’er paid no further attention to the situation in the Universal Devil Suppression Sword Formation, and she started look into some details related to the treasure map that led to the Sky Dao Palace’s treasure vault with Wen Tianshuo.

They revealed a carefree appearance as if they were just waiting for Chen Xi to be slaughtered before instantly going to unearth the treasure vault.

 

    …

Pu!

Blood sprayed out from Chen Xi’s mouth as he staggered, and he was heavily injured by that strike, causing his entire body to be bathed in blood. He was like a trapped beast in a hopeless situation, and he seemed as if he would be crushed in the next moment.

He gritted his teeth and circulated the Shaman Energy to repair his body. At the same time, the Blackhole World within his Dantian rumbled as it circulated in full force, causing his entire body to seem like a wisp of blazing light as he charged forward once again.

The Talisman Armament in his hand soared through the sky while spraying out both horizontal and vertical dazzling Creation Sword Qi that seemed like a river of stars descending from the nine heavens. He fought while bathed in blood, and he emanated a bold and fearless imposing aura.

This battle was the most dangerous battle he’d encountered since leaving the sect to temper himself this time, because Priest Zi Yun and the others were utterly incapable of comparing with these geezers from the Heavenflow Dao Sect.

Even though their cultivations were roughly comparable, in terms of combat strength and cooperation, they were utterly not on the same level.

The reason for this was the Universal Devil Suppression Sword Formation. This grand formation was formed from eight Immortal Artifacts, and it was a true Immortal Formation. If it was utilized in the hands of a Heavenly Immortal, then it would even possess the might to annihilate a world!

At this moment, it was executed under the joint forces of Elder Jing Kong and the others, so even though its might was incapable of annihilating a world, it was sufficient to trap and kill an existence at the sixth level of the Earthly Immortal Realm.

“Hmph! You can be considered to be extraordinary since you were able to struggle until now, and if you were given a period of time, you would surely grow into an overlord. But, unfortunately, you’ll surely die here today!” Elder Jing Kong sneered while the Immortal Sword in his hand swept out repeatedly while combined with the Universal Devil Suppression Sword Formation, and it sprayed out a myriad of vast and murderous strands of terrifying sword qi, causing it to seem like a rainstorm descending from the sky and crashing down at Chen Xi.

Bang! Bang! Bang!

Chen Xi suffered a heavy strike, causing his figure to sway indeterminately while his countenance grew even paler. At this moment, he was like a tiny leaf on the ocean, and he was struck by the violent waves, causing him to in a precious and extremely dangerous situation.

However, to the surprise of all the Earthly Immortal Realm experts, Chen Xi who seemed as if he would die in the next moment actually recovered every single time and persisted for an entire 10 minutes of time!

“This little bastard’s cultivation is so deep. The True Essence within his body actually seems boundless, and its resiliency is so strong as well. It’s as if it’s being reproduced endlessly. No wonder he was able to surmount a realm to do battle and kill Priest Zi Yun and the others.”

“Kill! We must kill this little bastard today. The more he’s like this, the greater the threat he poses. If he’s allowed to mature, then he’ll absolutely be a great enemy of our Heavenflow Dao Sect!”

“Exactly. Since such a monstrous and heaven defying genius can’t be of use to us, then only by killing him would we be able to be rid of all future trouble!”

Chen Xi’s extremely tenacious vitality caused all the Earthly Immortal Realm experts to be shocked, and the more he was like this, the greater the killing intent in their hearts blazed.

He’s only at the Nether Transformation Realm now. If he’s allowed to advance into the Earthly Immortal Realm, then how extraordinary would he be?

Such evil has to be annihilated as soon as possible!

“Cold lights rise when the world is at war, and rustling sword howls eliminate all devils!” Elder Jing Kong shouted loudly while directing everyone to execute one of the three ultimate moves of the Universal Devil Suppression Sword Formation – Universal Destruction!

Bang!

The clouds roiled in the surroundings as sword howls surged out like a tide. The space in the surroundings shattered abruptly before transforming in a myriad of peerless sharp strands of sword qi before sweeping out towards Chen Xi who was at the center!

When facing this terrifying ultimate move that sealed up and enveloped the surroundings, Chen Xi had no place to dodge at all. Even if he possessed the protection of the Immortal Artifact, Nethermist Feathered Armor, he was still shaken to the point of bleeding from all seven apertures while numerous terrifying injuries were sliced open all over his body by the sharp sword qi, causing blood to flow, and it was an extremely shocking scene.

Instantly, his entire body was drowned by the terrifying sword qi that seemed like a tempest.

“Is he dead?”

“he ought to be dead!”

“Haha! If this strike was unable to kill him, then wouldn’t it seem like we’re too useless?”

All the Earthly Immortal Realm experts stopped attacking as they looked at the center of the battlefield with proud and relaxed expressions.

“This Chen Xi can be considered to be a peerless genius. Unfortunately, he was still too young… Let’s go, I presume Lord Bing Shitian will probably be very happen when he finds out about this.” Elder Jing Kong smiled unhurriedly before instructing indifferently.

Bang!However, right at this moment, a peerless and terrifying fluctuation suddenly stretched out from the center of the battlefield, and it seemed like an unmatched ferocious beast from the primeval times had awoken from its deep sleep and looked down upon the world!

That’s…

Instantly, the faces of Jing Kong and all the other Earthly Immortal Realm experts froze, and then they revealed expressions of disbelief.

Within their fields of vision, a tall figure walked out from within a great expanse of chaos, and his entire body was bathed in blood. Every single step he took seemed like a Fiendgod striking a heavenly drum, and it emitted resounding thumps that shook the space and surged throughout the surroundings.

He was exactly Chen Xi!

But unlike before, his jet black and dense hair had actually become silver like snow, and it was extremely dazzling. Coupled with his handsome appearance, it caused him to carry a murderous, experienced, and chilly aura.

His figure was straight like a spear and carried the Talisman Armament in his hand. Moreover, his footsteps were like thunder, while his white hair seemed like a flowing waterfall! At this moment, Chen Xi’s aura had completely doubled!

Merely the aura he emitted caused all the Earthly Immortal Realm experts to feel apprehensive in their hearts. Earlier, his combat strength was already comparable with the 4th level of the Earthly Immortal Realm, and now that it had actually doubled explosively, how terrifying would he be?

Could it be that he’d always been concealing his strength?

“The Godslaughter Burst! The Yazi Clan’s ultimate technique!” An Earthly Immortal Realm expert seemed to have noticed a clue, and he couldn’t help but exclaim involuntarily with a voice that was filled with disbelief.

The Godslaughter Burst!

The hearts of the other Earthly Immortal Realm experts jerked, and their expressions instantly darkened to the extreme.

This terrifying Dao Art came from the primeval ferocious beast, Yazi, and once it was executed, it was capable of condensing the spirit, energy, and essence within the entire body and cause one’s strength to double explosively through a secret technique!

Unlike the multiplying combat strength grasped by cultivators at the Nether Transformation Realm, if a cultivator that executed this Dao Art already possessed 10 times combat strength, then the cultivator would be able to exert 20 times his combat strength!

During the primeval times, the ferocious beast Yazi had once slaughter a true god, allowing it to make a peerless reputation for itself, and it relied exactly on this terrifying Dao Art that was almost heaven defying.

Obviously, the imposing aura Chen Xi revealed at this moment entirely conformed with the characteristics of the Godslaughter Burst.

“Attack! Even though this technique is formidable, it possesses an extremely great flaw. Every time it’s executed, it’ll exhaust an enormous amount of spirit, energy, and essence. Moreover, it can only be sustained for an extremely short period of time. Once this period of time passes, he’ll be returned back to normal and would even suffer backlash!”

“Right! Quickly attack and kill this little bastard!”

All the Earthly Immortal Realm experts howled successively with rage before attacking once more, and they utilized the Universal Devil Suppression Sword Formation to blast down upon Chen Xi once again.

“You group of old dogs! Do all of you think I’d give you the chance?” Amidst his indifferent and murderous voice, Chen Xi’s entire body suddenly vanished on the spot. In the next moment, he’d already arrived before an Earthly Immortal Realm expert, and the Talisman Armament soared through the sky.

Pu! 

A head was slashed off!

A golden rain of blood fell. Even until his death, the Earthly Immortal Realm expert didn’t understand how Chen Xi’s strengths would become so terrifying.

“Junior Brother Ding Rong!” The other Earthly Immortal Realm experts roared with rage and shock, and they were furious to the point blood oozed out from their eyes that were filled with disbelief.

“Die!”

His white hair and clothes fluttered in the wind. At this moment, Chen Xi was like a god of devils that had walked out from a mountain of corpses and a sea of blood, and he intended to use the sword in his hand to sate himself with the blood of his enemies!

Bang!

Another Earthly Immortal Realm expert was slaughtered. His body was slashed into two before exploding open in midair, and it transformed into bloody froth that sprayed through the sky, causing it to be a horrifying, beautiful, and shocking scene.

Kill!

Chen Xi moved forward with large strides and an indifferent expression. He killed one in every 10 steps, and no one was capable of stopping him. Everywhere he passed, numerous cries that were shrill and miserable to the extreme resounded out while strings of scarlet red blood sprayed towards the surroundings.

The entire heavens and the earth were covered in miserable shrill cries, rains of fresh blood, and severed limbs flying about. It was like a field of slaughter in purgatory, and the targets of the slaughter were the blood and souls of Earthly Immortals!

Everywhere the tip of his blade reached, no one was capable of stopping it!

In merely the time of a few breaths, there were nine Earthly Immortal Realm experts that had their lives reaped like weeds, and they fell on the spot.

These terrifying scenes shocked Elder Jing Kong and the others to the point they couldn’t endure the shock and terror in their hearts any longer, and they cried out repeatedly. “How could this be possible!? How could this be possible!?”
