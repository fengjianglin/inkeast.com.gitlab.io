Chen Xi had fought the group of genius experts for a long time earlier and had fought the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree madly now, and he’d slashed the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree to the point of shaking endlessly with great difficulty and was about to launch a counterattack. However, at this moment, the True Essence within his body had actually dried up!

Everyone present noticed this scene, some sighed regretfully in their hearts while others took pleasure in Chen Xi’s misfortune. Normally speaking, so long as a Golden Core Realm cultivator possessed sufficient spirit pills, they would entirely not have to worry about the situation of having their True Essence dried up.

Chen Xi was similarly capable of this. But the might of the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree was too vast and covered the heavens and the earth, and it pressured him to the point he didn’t even have the time to consume a spirit pill. Thus, such an embarrassing situation had been created.

However, to everyone’s astonishment, right when they thought Chen Xi didn’t have the strength to go against it any longer, Chen Xi had instantly changed his method of combat. Shaman Energy surged and gushed in his body as he executed the Heavenly Transformation, Deity Transformation, and Stellar Lightningform before continuing his battle with the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree.

Bang!

An enormous palm print that covered an area of 300m soared through the sky, and it was coiled with brilliant starlight. Moreover, the five elements, Yin, and Yang circulated within it as it slapped down.

Divine Ability — Grand Astral Palm.

This Divine Ability was from the Master of the Manor. It possesses boundless stars and boundless Dao Insights, causing its might to be boundless as well, and it even surpassed the Steller Lightningform created by his Third Senior Brother.

Moreover, Chen Xi spent the most time on this Divine Ability, and compared to before, the might of the Grand Astral Palm was even stronger. Besides the five elements, Yin, Yang, lightning, wind, star, and the other Dao Insights that numbered over 10, there were also the rare Grand Dao Insights of Devour and Slaughter. At the instant it appeared, it was like a hand that could hold up the sky, and it caused the sky to go dim.

Merely the terrifying fluctuation emitted from the palm print had shattered space itself and pressed down onto the ground to the point it exploded open inch by inch, and countless cracks that were like spider webs split open on the grounds.

Besides that, along with the myriad of stars on the striations of the palm starting to circulate, various Dao Insight energies rumbled and seemed to have extracted the light, air flow, and spirit energy in an area of 500km and condensed it into this palm, causing its enormous impetus to shock everyone present to the point their pupils constricted.

Rumble! Rumble!

Waves of sounds that were like thunderclaps surged out from within the Grand Astral Palm, and it shook the world.

Everyone noticed to their astonishment that large expanses of clouds instantly appeared in the area the enormous palm print enveloped. The clouds covered the sky densely and contained Second-Wood Divine Lightning, Seventh-Gold Divine Lightning, Fifth-Earth Divine Lightning, Third-Fire Divine Lightning, Ninth-Water Divine Lightning interweaved and surging within it. Multicolored arcs of lightning rumbled like the roar of enormous dragons and shook the surroundings.

This was one of Chen Xi’s trump cards, and he hadn’t utilized it since entering the Primeval Battlefield. At this moment, it shocked everyone present as soon as it emerged into the world.

“What Divine Ability is this? How terrifying!”

“Dao Insights tolerating each other and actually developing the Divine Lightning of the five elements within the palm! My god! Could this be one of the existences that are ranked in the top 100 in the Divine Ability Gold Rankings of the three dimensions?”

“His Sword Dao cultivation attaining the state of the Enlightened Sword Heart was already sufficiently shocking, yet never had I imagined that this fellow’s body refinement cultivation is actually so formidable. Is this fellow human?”

Everyone looked at the 57m tall giant that had three heads and six arms from afar, and a tempestuous storm couldn’t help but arise in their hearts.

Chen Xi had too many trump cards, too many to the point it caused others to feel horrified, and they had no choice but to refresh their appraisal of him over and over again. According to their current estimations, they even suspected that even if Chen Xi didn’t advance to the Rebirth Realm, his strength would be sufficient to allow him to have a place in the final test of the Primeval Battlefield.

On the other side, the expressions of Qin Xiao and Bi Lingyun instantly became extremely heavy. Obviously, the ability Chen Xi had revealed caused them to feel extremely fearful as well.

As for Pei Yu, the formidableness of Chen Xi’s strength caused the killing intent in his heart to burn even more brightly. He knew that if he didn’t eliminate Chen Xi now, then Chen Xi would absolutely become a great calamity for him in the future.

Bang! Bang! Bang!

The Divine Lightning of the five elements descended like a hail storm as green, yellow, crimson, gold, and blue lightning transformed into a multicolored ocean of lightning that enveloped the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree.

Bang!

The Heavenly Immortal’s Decree trembled violently as its glow instantly dimmed down, and it was blasted flying by the terrifying Divine Lightning of the five elements.

Bang!

The Divine Lightning of the five elements blasted down ceaselessly, causing the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree to be blasted off successively and became dimmer and dimmer as it suffered strike after strike, and the supreme brilliance that enveloped its surface showed signs of being extinguished.

Everyone felt their scalps go numb. This Divine Ability was too terrifying and actually showed signs of crushing the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree, and this was something that was simply unbelievable.

“You’re courting death!” Pei Yu was furious when he saw this. Never had he imagined that after he withdrew such a terrifying trump card, not only was he unable to crush Chen Xi, Chen Xi was about to destroy the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree.

“With my blood, I offer sacrifice to the gods. So long as my enemy remains, the slaughter shall never end!” In the next moment, Pei Yu sat cross-legged on the ground and a boundless bloody glow exploded out from him and gushed into the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree, whereas his appearance and skin actually aged greatly in an instant, and even his hair became grey.

Everyone was shocked. He actually forced Pei Yu to sacrifice his own lifespan and Blood Essence!?

Om!

The ‘戮’ character on the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree was filled with a bloody glow that was scarlet red, and it resounded out with clear sounds as it emitted an aura of eternity. Moreover, its killing intent surged, causing it to become much stronger than before. It was like it had awakened from its sleep, and it was rumbling as it transformed into a dazzling golden crow.

At the same time, golden beams of light exploded out like a rain of sunlight that poured down. This was the profundity of the Laws grasped by the Heavenly Immortal, and was its furious attack after recovering its essence, energy, and spirit.

Heavenly Immortals stood proudly above the sky, and their wills were supreme, inviolable, and allowed no disrespect. Even though it was only the writing of a Heavenly Immortal, it had its own dignity. At this moment, being offended by Chen Xi had caused it to immediately explode with rage.

In merely an instant, the Grand Astral Palm was penetrated by the boundless golden beams of light, causing it to be obliterated. Moreover, the beams of light didn’t lose momentum as they directly crushed down towards Chen Xi.

It has become stronger again… Chen Xi puckered his lips and had to admit in his heart that this Heavenly Immortal’s Decree was truly too terrifying, and he had no choice but to avoid it.

But the golden beams of light were violent like the ocean and present everywhere as they covered the heavens and the earth as they descended down upon him, and they directly drowned him beneath them and caused him to be unable to avoid them.

Pu! Pu! Pu!

Chen Xi’s entire body instantly had densely packed bloody holes penetrated upon his entire body, causing him to be riddled with holes and covered in blood, and he’d suffered a severe heavy injury. If it wasn’t for him protecting his head and heart in time, he’d probably have been killed beneath this attack.

During these past few years of battles, Chen Xi had never experienced such a tragic battle where he was almost killed. The will contained within the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree was truly too terrifying and formidable, and it caused him to be in an extremely passive position.

Rumble!

When it saw Chen Xi had actually survived, the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree became even more furious, and it caused golden beams of light to pour down like line after line of rivers that struck once more at Chen Xi.

Chen Xi gritted his teeth. He already had no path of retreat and was forced into a hopeless situation, there was no one for him to count on nor any way for him to escape, and he could only rely on himself to fight until the end.

Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang! Bang!

At this moment, Chen Xi exerted all the Shaman Energy in his entire body to execute five Grand Astral Palms at the same time, and they blotted out the sky as they fiercely crushed towards the golden beams of light that covered the sky.

Both attacks collided and exploded out with boundless brilliant lights, causing it to seem like a volcano had erupted or a group of stars had fallen from the sky, and it was an extremely violent scene that caused the entire heavens and the earth to fall into chaos.

Everyone present was astonished, and their eyes were pierced by these brilliant lights to the point of almost being impossible to open.

On the other hand, the genius experts of the Darqin, Darjin, and Century Pool Dynasty, and the genius disciples of the various other Dynasties like the Skywolf Dynasty felt deep unease in their hearts. Chen Xi’s strength was too strong and was actually able to go against the furious strike of the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree, and it had far exceeded their expectations. 

They felt anxious in their hearts as they stared nervously at the battlefield, and if it wasn’t for the battlefield being too terrifying and would affect them, they’d probably have made a move long ago. Now, they only hoped that the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree would be able to annihilate Chen Xi right away.

Bang!

A figure was blasted out from the blazing brilliant light, and the figure staggered as streams of blood flowed out from all over the figure’s body. The figure didn’t have an inch of skin that was intact, and even the figure’s tattered clothes and disheveled hair was tainted with blood, causing his appearance to be extremely miserable.

It was Chen Xi.

Even though he’d blocked the strike of the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree in the end, he’d suffered a heavy injury that was fatal, and if his body hadn’t been cultivated to the point of being capable of re-growing itself, he would probably be utterly incapable of standing up.

“The day will come where I’ll surely head to the Immortal Dimension to slaughter you!” Chen Xi gritted his teeth. His face was tainted with blood, causing his appearance to be blurred, and only his pair of eyes revealed a hateful, unyielding, and firm expression.

Yes, even if it was now, he’d still persisted and had not given up, as if he were to give up now, then he would surely die a graveless death.

The hearts of everyone went cold without reason or rhyme as they looked at this tall and unyielding figure. What a tenacious vitality! What a shocking will to fight! If he’s able to survive, then he’ll probably really be able to enter the Immortal Dimension and slaughter the owner of this Heavenly Immortal’s Decree?

After that, everyone couldn’t help but be shocked by their absurd thoughts, and then they shook their heads. How could that be possible? No matter how terrifying the strength Chen Xi possesses is, or how supreme his fighting spirit is, he’s about to fall before the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree and perish. He’s bound to be buried here and be unable to see tomorrow’s sunrise. So how could he possibly fight his way into the Immortal Dimension?

“Eh, the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree has dimmed down!” Right at this moment, someone noticed the unusual state of the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree.

“Could it be that both Chen Xi and the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree were injured in that collision from before?” Everyone raised their eyes and noticed the unusualness in the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree, causing them to be unable to help but be horrified.

On the other side, Pei Yu’s countenance was already pale to the extreme and without the slightest sign of rosiness. No matter how proud and confident he was, he couldn’t help but be terrified at this moment. Why is it like this? I’ve already exhausted everything, yet I’m actually unable to annihilate Chen Xi?

“NO! I want you dead! No one can save you today!” Pei Yu suddenly stated, shouting madly as infinite bloody glows gushed out from his body, causing his long hair to turn snow white abruptly, and his young appearance became extremely aged as his vitality dimmed.

Bang!

The Heavenly Immortal’s Decree glowed once more and even started burning, only leaving behind the ‘戮’ that had turned scarlet red and emitted a sound of slaughter that shook the heavens, and it sounded like the chanting of gods as it transformed into a bolt of lightning that directly shot towards Chen Xi.

“This is… He actually forced out all the energy within the Heavenly Immortal’s Decree for the sake of a final devastating strike!” As they looked at the ‘戮’ character that descended from the sky while emitting boundless piercingly cold killing intent, everyone who felt the terrifying might contained within it felt suffocated and as if they had fallen into an icy pit.

Moreover, they were able to sense that Chen Xi would probably be unable to escape the calamity this time.
