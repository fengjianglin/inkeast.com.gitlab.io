Chen Xi and the others were stunned when they saw the delicate and graceful figure clearly.

This woman had beautiful jet black hair, a fine and high nose, teeth that was like jade, sexy red lips, and a snow white neck that seemed like the neck of a crane. Coupled with her curvy body that caused one’s blood to surge, she revealed charm that could conquer all.

This woman walked over with graceful steps, and every single move she made naturally carried an enchanting and amorous aura. She was like a disaster that charmed a country to its collapse, and her eyes were watery and seemed capable of sucking away one’s soul with a single glance.

Especially those youths, they were unable to help but feel slightly astounded and reveal a wisp of infatuation when they saw this peerless beauty walk out from the distant sky.

“Eh. No wonder, it’s actually a pure blooded Nine-Tailed Fox?” A’xiu’s clear eyes swept over as she spoke with a light smile, and her voice was chilly like a gurgling spring as it rumbled throughout the heavens and the earth.

The consciousness of the youths cleared up instantly, allowing them to awaken from their infatuation, and then all of them revealed furious expressions. Dammit! We were actually affected by the enchantment of another!

“Little Sister, you have a good eye. You actually recognized my identity.” The woman’s gaze flickered as she spoke with surprise, and the combination of her bright red lips and jade white teeth shook the hearts of all.

Chen Xi was shocked in his heart because it was too strange that a pure blooded Nine-Tailed Fox would suddenly appear in the wilderness. Moreover, besides peerless charm, this woman actually had strands of Immortal Energy surging on her body. Obviously, she was an Earthly Immortal Realm expert!

Most alarming to him was this woman seemed to have been waiting here since a long time ago, and if it wasn’t for A’xiu noticing this woman’s tracks, the consequences were unimaginable.

“You’re just a demon woman. What’s so hard to recognize about that?” said A’xiu with a grin.

“Little Sister, Big Sister’s name is Xueyan, not demon woman.” The woman’s voice was gentle and hoarse, and it felt like something was scratching at one’s heart when one heard it, causing one’s blood to boil.

Xueyan?

Chen Xi frowned and was unable to recall who this woman was. But based on the situation before him, she’d obviously come with ill intent!

“Little Brother Chen Xi, we’ve finally met.” Suddenly, Xueyan’s eyes turned to descend onto Chen Xi, and her white and smooth face revealed a wisp of radiant beauty.

“You recognize me?” Chen Xi’s gaze was clear yet suffused with a wisp of a chilly sheen. Every single word and action of this woman emitted charm that drew one’s heart and soul towards her. If it wasn’t for his Dao Heart being tempered to the point of being firm like a rock, his mind would have probably been affected.

“Of course. Presently, who in the Dark Reverie doesn’t know Little Brother Chen Xi?” Xueyan smiled as she spoke, and then her beautiful brows knit together. “But I’m truly very curious. Your Blackhole World was clearly crippled, so why has your cultivation actually recovered?” 

Chen Xi’s eyes narrowed slightly as he said with an icy cold voice, “Looks like you were sent over by Bing Shitian.”

His Blackhole World had been destroyed by Bing Shitian, and even Meng Wei and the others didn’t know about this matter. However, it was revealed with a single sentence of this woman, so who else but Bing Shitian could have told her?

This Xueyan was probably lying in ambush here for the sake of killing me!

They’re really like ghosts that never leave…

Chen Xi’s heart was coiled with killing intent while the hatred that was hidden within his heart surged out, and his entire body was suffused with a dense and almost material killing intent.

Since the plot against Qing Yu, to the destruction of his Blackhole World, and the current harming of Huo Molei and the others. All of this was related to Bing Shitian.

This caused Chen Xi to feel extreme hatred, and never had he imagined that a Heavenly Immortal, a great figure from the Heavenflow Dao Sect, would actually be so despicable and shameless!

Meng Wei and Mo Ya acutely sensed the killing intent on Chen Xi’s body, and all of them revealed solemn expressions as they prepared to do battle.

Xueyan acted as if she didn’t notice and said with a light smile, “Little Brother, even if your strength has recovered now. As far as I’m concerned, you’re still too weak. So it’s better if you follow me obediently because my heart would ache if we fight and I injure you.”

Chen Xi puckered his lips and had an extremely icy cold expression as he instructed. “Big Brother Meng Wei, all of you stay out of this and leave it to me.”

Meng Wei and the others were stunned and slightly bewildered. But when they saw Chen Xi’s serious and solemn expression, they chose to obey and silently moved back in the end.

“I never imagined that Little Brother is so noble and righteous. I’m almost unable to refrain from falling for you.” Xueyan smiled beautifully and had a composed appearance as if the situation was within her control.

“Believe it or not, I’ll tear your mouth apart if you continue talking and calling him Little Brother!” A’xiu spoke angrily like a beast that was on the verge of exploding with rage.

Xueyan was stunned and laughed with ridicule. “Little Sister, don’t be so fierce. I’m not trying to steal your lover.”

“A’xiu, stand back,” said Chen Xi.

“You don’t want me to help?” A’xiu frowned.

Chen Xi shook his head with a firm expression.

Xueyan couldn’t help but sigh faintly when she saw this. “After speaking so much to you, you’re still going to make a move in the end. You’ve truly hurt my heart.”

Bang!

As she spoke, her entire body was swiftly suffused with a terrifying imposing aura while immortal radiance fluttered about and the tune of the Dao rumbled, and it shook space itself to the point of wailing.

In merely an instant, her entire body seemed to have transformed into a scorching sun while her aura that belonged to an Earthly Immortal Realm expert rose to the limit, causing even the heavens and the earth to be cast into a shadow!

“Be careful. This demon woman has overcome the first level of the heavenly tribulation, the Azure Lightning Tribulation. She suppresses you in terms of cultivation realms.” A’xiu quickly sent him a voice transmission.

There were nine levels in the Earthly Immortal Realm, and one would experience a tribulation after advancing a level.

The first tribulation was called the Azure Lightning Tribulation. As the saying goes, the Grand Dao is like a clear sky that allowed one to rise rapidly. After one experienced this heavenly tribulation, the vitality of a cultivator would undergo a transformation, causing True Essence to completely transform into Immortal Energy, and the cultivator would leave the ranks of cultivators and be called an Earthly Immortal.

Obviously, this pure blooded Nine-Tailed Fox called Xueyan was a 1st level Earthly Immortal Realm expert!

Hiss!

It took long to write in words, yet was only an instant. Xueyan held a jade sword as she swept it out lightly, causing space to be torn apart as it instantly arrived before Chen Xi. It was a very simple attack, yet it fully brought forth the might of an Earthly Immortal Realm expert.

Under the envelopment of this sword strike, her peerlessly fierce Immortal Energy emanated boundless radiance that slashed apart the surroundings, and its speed was swift to the limit.

Swish!

Chen Xi moved as well. His tall figure surged with talisman markings and flowed with a divine glow while resounding rumbling of the Dao sounded out. His entire body was like a shocking dragon emerging from its lair, and then the Wings of Disruption flapped, causing him to avoid the sword light in the next moment.

Bang!

At the same time that he dodged, Chen Xi’s gaze was like a bolt of lightning as he executed the Grand Confinement Dao Arts, and it formed a shapeless force of talisman markings that sealed the surrounding space.

Xueyan’s figure instantly slowed down, and then she chuckled. “Sealing space? Even though your Dao Art is profound, your strength is too weak.” As she spoke, her jade sword swept out with a twist, and it collapsed this force field, allowing her to easily escape this confined space.

Chen Xi wasn’t surprised in the slightest, and his figure was like a shuttle as he seemed to transform into talisman markings that covered the entire sky and surged like waves while a variety of peak-grade Dao Arts sprayed out from his hands!

The Myriad Netherwave Palm, Skycontrol Burial Sword, Nine Spirit Transformations, Violetlotus Goldshadow Barrier, 12 Lotus Platform Deliverance, and the various Dao Art profundities contained with the Allheaven Truth. All of them were Dao Arts with exceedingly terrifying might.

At the instant they were executed, it caused an area of 5,000km in the surroundings to sometimes resound with waves of thunderclaps, sometimes be full of tempests, sometimes have mountains collapse while rivers ran dry, sometimes have the plants on it transform into ash while the earth split apart…

The entire heavens and the earth fell into great chaos. Meng Wei, Mo Ya, and the others were shocked and retreated in unison, yet they were still unable to avoid being affected.

Fortunately, A’xiu made a move at the critical moment and protected all of them. Otherwise, the lives of those youths would surely be in danger.

Meanwhile, Chen Xi had already exerted all his strength. He’d exerted his entire six times combat strength while the Blackhole World constructed from the five Divine Talismans rumbled as it circulated, causing him to seem like the embodiment of talisman markings, and every single move he made were filled with talisman markings and Dao Arts.

Similarly, this was the first time he’d encountered a formidable opponent since leaving the Ninth Hell, a Nine-Tailed Fox that was already ranked amongst Earthly Immortal Realm experts!

The enormous gap in their cultivation realms caused Chen Xi to utterly not dare gold back!

He was completely fearless as he fought with all his strength, and the flames of rage and hatred that was suppressed within his heart for a long time surged throughout his body like lava, stimulating every single inch of his skin to the point it burned and seethed.

He seemed as if he’d gone mad!

But it just so happened that his mind was calm like ice while his expression was indifferent yet murderous, and he was like a flash of lightning that tore apart the pitch black night and was peerlessly fierce.

Xueyan’s relaxed expression gradually revealed a wisp of surprise as she was inwardly shocked by Chen Xi’s heaven defying combat strength.

She was truly unable to imagine how a little fellow at the Nether Transformation Realm was actually capable of holding on for so long before her. Moreover, his courage and strength rose steadily as the battle went on.

Even though she was capable of easily dealing with Chen Xi’s string of attacks, she was unable to feel happy at all. Conversely, her expression gradually became solemn and serious.

The little fellow before her couldn’t be judged by convention because he was simply a freak. He’s able to persist until now with a cultivation at the Nether Transformation Realm, then how terrifying would he be once he grows?

When she thought up to here, exceedingly intense killing intent arose within Xueyan’s heart.

Kill!

This scourge must be completely eliminated this time, otherwise, if he’s allowed to grow, then he’ll absolutely be an enormous threat to my Heavenflow Dao Sect!

Swish! Swish! Swish!

Killing intent arose in Xueyan’s heart, causing her to stop holding back, and then an immortal radiance surged out and shot into the sky. Instantly, her body split into nine, and they transformed into nine exactly alike figures.

These nine figures were either wearing a beautiful smile, icy cold, delicate and attractive, or indifferent and proud… Every single figure was like her true body, and every single figure emanated a formidable strength and a monstrous imposing aura that was exactly similar, causing it to be utterly impossible to distinguish which was which.

At the instant they appeared, they took the position of the Nine Palaces, and they completely surrounded Chen Xi!

In merely an instant, Chen Xi felt terrifying pressure press down upon him, and it was as if he was facing nine Earthly Immortals by himself, causing the seething vital blood in his entire body to become sluggish.

“Little Brother, your strength is really not bad that you’re actually capable of forcing me to execute the Ninesplit Grace. Amongst those of the same generation, you can be called a peerless genius, and you’re even not inferior to the monstrous geniuses of some powers that seclude themselves from the outside world. Unfortunately, the more you’re like this, the more Big Sister has to kill you…” Amidst her gentle and hoarse voice, a figure suddenly charged out from amongst the nine figures Xueyan had transformed into, and its fingers squeezed together like a hook that tore the world apart, and it directly arrived before Chen Xi and grabbed fiercely at his Dantian.
