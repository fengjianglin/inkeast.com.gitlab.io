Divinity effused out and instantly filled the heavens and the earth.

At this moment, All thirty three levels of the Sovereign Sect fell into great terror. Everything within it wailed and trembled, and they seemed to be submitting, yet seemed to be on the verge of collapse as well.

This was the true might of the Divine Dao!

This sort of might had already exceeded the scope of the three dimensions, and it was so terrifying that it was sufficient to rebuild the world, recreate the surrounding landscape, and transform the ordinary into extraordinary. It was almighty!

Rumble!

The heavens and the earth fell beneath a wave of rumbling. Space was unable to maintain its stability any longer, and it started to surge and explode into pieces before whistling towards the surroundings in the form of torrents. The heavens and the earth were in chaos!

At this instant, Chen Xi’s eyes focused and a wisp of vigilance arose in his heart.

Swoosh!

His figure suddenly flashed, and it flickered through the layer upon layer of shattered space to move far away into the distance and avoid the pressure emanated from this energy of divinity.

After that, he suddenly took a deep breath before the universe within his body started to rumble. The Infinite Divine Talisman circulated at full strength while the energy of Primeval Dao Quintessence carried a surging aura of divinity as it effused out from within his body, causing his entire body to be suffused by divine radiance and coiled by a myriad of mysterious talismans.

At this moment, Chen Xi didn’t dare hesitate at all to utilize his full strength, and he exerted all the ability he possessed after he’d stepped foot into the Halfgod Realm.

He was clearly aware that this battle would definitely be dangerous and uncertain!

Since he’d cultivated until now, it was the first time he was about to vie for supremacy with actual gods, and it was even more than one!

…

Swoosh! Swoosh! Swoosh!

Right amidst this expanse of terrifying chaos, space fluctuated as three mighty figures that were suffused in divine light appeared. Shockingly, they were Yin Huaikong, Elder Kong Zhao, and Daoist Tuo Kong.

However, they were unlike before. Every single move they made was suffused with the energy of divinity, and as they stood there casually, they seemed like three overlords that controlled the heavens and the earth and possessed supreme might.

Obviously, for the sake of crushing Chen Xi, they’d undone the seals on their cultivations and recovered their strengths at the Godrank Realm!

“Damnable Bastard! You’ve actually killed of those Junior Brothers and Sister of mine in that little bit of time!” As soon as Yin Huaikong appeared, he noticed that those thirty six Elite Disciples had been annihilated on the spot. This caused his expression to instantly turn gloomy as he emanated killing intent.

He’d just left for less than an eighth of an hour, yet Chen Xi had actually annihilated thirty six existences at the Peak Rank of the Immortal King Realm by himself. How could Yin Huaikong not be infuriated by this?

All of these disciples were the central pillars of the Sovereign Sect in taking control of the three dimensions!

Now that they’d died miserably here, such losses were extremely heavy to the Sovereign Sect, and it was even to the point that it had upset the Sovereign Sect’s plans to sweep through the three dimensions!

“They were just a bunch of useless trash, so what if they were killed? Could it be that only your Sovereign Sect is allowed to act willfully?” Chen Xi grunted coldly. Even if he was facing three existences at the Godrank Realm, he still remained fearless. Conversely, the battle intent within his entire body seethed and surged like lava, causing the imposing aura he emanated to grow even stronger.

“There’s no need to waste your breath on this kid. Let’s make the best use of our time and kill him.” Kong Zhao spoke indifferently and casually. He wore a blood red robe, and his face was emaciated and gloomy. His voice hadn’t finished resounding through the air when his hand suddenly stretched out and slapped towards Chen Xi who stood in the distance.

He’s actually attacked just like that, and he didn’t hesitate in the slightest.

Rumble!

His hand soared into the sky and enveloped down like an expanse of sky. The Divinity that descended from it locked down the surroundings, and its might could be said to be boundless.

“Hmph!” Chen Xi grunted coldly while his figure shot into the sky like a Roc, and the Dao Calamity Sword that was suffused with a surging blood red glow soared into the sky.

Swish!

It actually broke the might of that palm strike apart.

Amidst the divine radiance that erupted from it, Chen Xi didn’t retreat but pushed forward instead, and he charged towards Kong Zhao.

“It really is the Halfgod Realm. Unfortunately, with this little bit of ability, you’ll definitely perish today.” Kong Zhao had an indifferent expression as his figure suddenly flashed. A divine blade floated into appearance within his palm. The blade was completely jet black and shaped like a crescent moon, and it was suffused with an aura of divinity that caused one’s heart to palpitate as it slashed down.

This blade was called Moon Slaughter. It was a Natural Spirit Treasure that was peerlessly sharp, and it was invincible before both gods and devils!

Bang!

The two of them collided head-on and the energy of divinity rumbled. It was like a world obliterating thunderbolt had erupted, and a terrifying after shock swept through the heavens and the earth. Everywhere it passed, time and space were obliterated while all things perished.

Under the might of this strike, the figures of Chen Xi and Kong Zhao just shook, and they were actually evenly matched.

This caused Kong Zhao’s expression to turn gloomy as he realized how terrifying Chen Xi’s combat strength was.

On the other hand, when Yin Huaikong and Daoist Tuo Kong saw this, they knew that merely Kong Zhao alone would be utterly unable to harm Chen Xi. Thus, they practically didn’t hesitate to move out ferociously.

They’d undone the seals on their cultivation and exposed their strength at the Godrank Realm, so they had to make the best use of their time to annihilate Chen Xi. Otherwise once time passed, they would be taken away forcefully by the Divine Chains of Order.

That wasn’t something they were willing to see happen.

Om~

A horsetail whisk that flowed with divine radiance and emanated a seething fiery glow revealed a myriad of mysterious phenomena as it swept over. It seemed like a universe covered by an ocean of flames that was enveloping down, and it possessed terrifying might.

This was Daoist Tuo Kong’s Divine Artifact, the Firecloud Horsetail Whisk, and it was a Natural Spirit Treasure. According to legend, a Quintessence Fire Spirit from within the Chaos had transformed into it, and it was suffused with divine flames. Merely a single strand of these flames was capable of incinerating a large world, and it was extremely terrifying.

Swish!

A black, narrow, and long sword flashed through the sky. It was a clean, straightforward, and extremely simple attack, yet it seemed like a strand of sharp glow that came from within the quintessence of darkness, and it revealed a terrifying murderous aura.

This was Yin Huaikong’s Dark Calamity Sword. Even though it was an Artificial Spirit Treasure, its destructive capability was even more formidable than some Natural Spirit Treasures.

After all, no matter if it was Natural Spirit Treasures or Artificial Spirit Treasures, each of them possessed their own profound effects, and in terms of their overall might, it depended on the strength of the expert that utilized the Divine Artifact.

For example, the Dark Calamity Sword was refined by the Sect Master of the Sovereign Sect after exhausting ten thousand years to collect precious treasures throughout the world. Its destructive capability was so formidable that it was not inferior to Kong Zhao’s Moon Slaughter and Tuo Kong’s Firecloud Horsetail Whisk.

At this instant, Yin Huaikong and Daoist Tuo Kong had moved out in unison, and they’d withdrawn their respective Divine Artifacts to attack Chen Xi. It seemed as if any other ordinary Immortal King was in Chen Xi’s place, that Immortal King would probably be powerless to resist them before being scared out of his wits.

Chen Xi similarly felt extraordinarily pressured, but under the effect of this pressure, not only did he not evade, he took the initiative to charge forward once more.

Swoosh! Swoosh!

In his right hand, the Dao Calamity Sword swept out, and it emanated a myriad of clear colored sword lotuses that covered the heavens and the earth.

In his left hand, the Talisman Armament swept through the sky, and it revealed numerous divine talisman diagrams as it shot through the sky.

The attacks of the two swords respectively collided head on with Daoist Tuo Kong and Yin Huaikong. In an instant, rumbling resounded throughout the heavens and the earth, and it was like a myriad of mountains had simultaneously collided and collapsed into pieces at this moment.

Stomp! Stomp! Stomp!

Chen Xi’s figure shook as he uncontrollably took three steps back in space. Moreover, every step he took caused space to shatter into pieces and numerous black holes split open within space.

On the other hand, Yin Huaikong and Daoist Tuo Kong didn’t move a single step, and they were completely unharmed.

Obviously, Chen Xi was clearly in a disadvantageous position during this collision, and he was struck to the point of moving backwards.

“Kill!”

“Attack together!”

“We can’t let this drag on any longer!”

When they saw this, Yin Huaikong, Daoist Tuo Kong, and Elder Kong Zhao, who possessed extremely abundant combat experience, grabbed onto this opportunity to attack jointly once more, and they intended to annihilate Chen Xi in one go.

It was right at this moment that a wisp of a faint smile suffused the corners of Chen Xi’s mouth instead. Even though he was in a disadvantageous position earlier, he’d already resisted the joint attacks of two gods after all.

Moreover, it was under the condition that it was a head-on collision!

This allowed Chen Xi who was fighting existences at the Godrank Realm for the first time to finally realize that merely based on his current combat strength, he was indeed already capable of surmounting a realm and fighting existences at the Godrank Realm.

This was more than enough!

Rumble!

The battle erupted completely. Chen Xi fell into a disadvantageous position as he was vying for supremacy against three gods all by himself. They’d transformed the entire Sovereign Realm into a battlefield, and they fought up to the sky and down to the earth. They fought to the point even the heavens, earth, sun, and moon dimmed down and were lusterless.

Such a rare scene of battle truly made it seem as if they’d returned to the primeval times when the gods fought for supremacy, and it revealed all sorts of tragic and terrifying phenomena.

If there was someone else here, that person would definitely be extremely moved by Chen Xi’s combat strength because he was actually able to fight three gods head-on to such an extent while at the Halfgod Realm. Even if Chen Xi lost in the end, it was still be sufficient for him to be considered as possessing peerless combat strength that shone through the ages, and it would shock all the living beings of the three dimensions!

…

After a short while, the situation of the battle started to become disadvantageous for Chen Xi.

He was going against three existences at the Godrank Realm, after all. Moreover, they possessed various Divine Artifacts and possessed supreme divine might. So when they fought at full force, it was obvious how terrifying they were.

Even if it was a true god that was fighting them, that god would find them difficult to resist.

On the other hand, even though Chen Xi had stepped foot into the Halfgod Realm, possessed the ability to surmount a realm and do battle, and possessed a combat strength that could rival the Godrank Realm, he wasn’t only facing a single expert at the Godrank Realm. There were three!

What did this mean?

It meant that he wasn’t just surmounting a realm to do battle at this moment, he was even undergoing a battle against numerous opponents at a realm higher than his own!

Under such circumstances, no matter how heaven defying his combat strength was, he couldn’t avoid falling into a disadvantageous position along with the passage of time, and he faintly showed signs of being suppressed.

However, even then, the combat strength Chen Xi revealed caused Yin Huaikong and the others to be extremely moved, and the shock they felt constantly grew as the battle continued.

Less than a year ago, this kid before their eyes had only just stepped foot into the Immortal King Realm. Now, not only had his cultivation broken through once more to advance into the Halfgod Realm, his combat strength was so formidable that he was actually able to fight three gods head-on all by himself. Such a terrifying speed of cultivation was simply unimaginable and inconceivable!

If he was given another period of time to grow, then wouldn’t even existences at the Godrank Realm be unable to do anything to him?

Along with the passage of time and as the battle continued, they were still unable to crush Chen Xi. This caused the hearts of Yin Huaikong and the others to feel even heavier. They had no choice but to admit that Chen Xi was simply a freak that couldn’t be judged by convention, and he was a peerless genius that could be said to be unprecedented to the point that very few like him could be found in the world from ancient times until now.

Such an understanding caused the killing intent within the hearts of Yin Huaikong and the others to grow even more blazing, and they pressed down step by step in a resolute manner with the intention of eliminating Chen Xi.

As far as they were concerned, if they didn’t annihilate Chen Xi this time, then the consequences would simply be unimaginable. In the future, Chen Xi was bound to become a latent threat that no one would be able to suppress!

“Kill!”

“We must kill him this time!”

“This kid is obviously trying to buy time. Once the Divine Chains of Order descend, then this battle is bound to end in our defeat. We must annihilate this kid in one go!”

Yin Huaikong and the others spoke via voice transmission, and all of them had aroused killing intent in their hearts. The might of their attacks suddenly grew once more, and they became even more terrifying, causing Chen Xi to have no choice but to dodge repeatedly and stagger back.

The situation started to become even more disadvantageous to Chen Xi…
