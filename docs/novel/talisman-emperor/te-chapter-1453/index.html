Chapter 1453 Silent And Coldblooded Revenge

Action Cooking Crafting Fantasy Mystery Xuanhuan Chinese Ongoing 2017/09/22

Talisman Emperor

Zuoqiu Feng’s howl resounded throughout the surroundings.

Ancestors?

It naturally referred to the two existences at the Godrank Realm within the Zuoqiu Clan — Zuoqiu Beiyong and Zuoqiu Lenghua!

When they heard this word, the spirits of Zuoqiu Huanglin and the others was refreshed, and a wisp of hope had rekindled within their hearts that were originally drowned in terror.

Wei Xing and the other members of the Sovereign Sect revealed a wisp of hope as well.

Even though the enemy had three existences at the Godrank Realm, so long as the two ancestors of the Zuoqiu Clan made an appearance, then even if they were unable to turn the situation around, it would be sufficient to resolve the danger to their lives!

It was even to the extent that…it should be possible for the two ancestors to rescue them and flee, right?

…

When they heard Zuoqiu Feng’s cry for help, Zuoqiu Feiming and the others seemed to have realized something as well, and it caused their expressions to immediately turn gloomy while they were extremely resentful in their hearts.

Even though these two ancestors hadn’t made a move themselves until now in the internal strife of the Zuoqiu Clan, yet they’d always been supporting Zuoqiu Feng’s faction from behind the scenes. Under such circumstances, it was utterly impossible for Zuoqiu Feiming and the others to have a favorable impression of these two ancestors.

Chen Xi wasn’t aware of all of this. However, when he found out about all of this from Zuoqiu Feiming, he couldn’t help but be surprised in his heart. Never had he imagined that the Zuoqiu Clan actually had two existences at the Godrank Realm!

Fortunately, Senior Zhao Taici and the others were called over. Otherwise, the consequences would be unimaginable. Chen Xi glanced at Zhao Taici, Ao Jiuhui, and Chi Cangsheng that stood at his side, and he felt fortunate in his heart.

Moreover, he’d already perceived that the reason Zhao Taici and the others hadn’t made a move until now was obviously because they were waiting for the appearance of Zuoqiu Beiyong and Zuoqiu Lenghua.

However, to Chen Xi’s surprise, there was utterly no movement in the surroundings after Zuoqiu Feng’s cry for help resounded out, and no one came to rescue them after quite some time passed!

What’s going on?

It wasn’t just Chen Xi that was bewildered. Hua Jiankong, Midnight Immortal King, Xuanyuan Shao, and the others frowned in their hearts as well. It has already come to this, yet those two gods of the Zuoqiu Clan still aren’t making an appearance. Could it be that they intend to watch as Zuoqiu Feng and the others are slaughtered?

No one responded to his cry for help even after a long time had passed!

This caused the countenances of Zuoqiu Feng and the others to turn ghastly pale. The wisp of hope that had arisen in their hearts was instantly replaced by boundless despair.

Why is this happening?

Where’re the two Ancestors?

Could it be that they intend to abandon us?

At this moment, the last pillar of support within Zuoqiu Feng’s heart had collapsed with a bang, and he couldn’t help but roar loudly. “Why!? Why!? Ancestors, quickly make an appearance! Could it be that the both of you intend to watch as my Zuoqiu Clan is annihilated?!”

His almost mad shouts resounded through the heavens and the earth, and it reverberated without end, yet there was still no one that responded.

Zhao Taici frowned when she saw this, and her peerlessly beautiful and youthful face revealed a wisp of impatience. “Forget it. Since they’re hiding and don’t intend to make an appearance, then let’s make a move and annihilate these fellows first.”

Aoi Jiuhui and Chi Cangsheng nodded as well.

As soon as these words were spoken, it was like the final straw that crushed the camel, and it caused Zuoqiu Feng, Wei Xing, and the others to collapse.

“Flee!”

“Quickly flee!”

“Dammit! Dammit!”

A wave of panicked shouts resounded. No matter if it was Zuoqiu Feng and his clansmen or Wei Xing and the members of the Sovereign Sect. At this moment, all of them had simultaneously chosen to flee.

They seemed as if they truly wished for nothing more than for their parents to have given birth to them with two extra legs.

The reason for this was none other than existences at the Godrank Realm were too terrifying. Even if they were Immortal Kings, they could only flee before existences at the Godrank Realm because the gap between them was like the difference between a hawk and an ant.

One was a supreme existence in the three dimensions while the other had long since surpassed the three dimensions and weren’t part of the five elements. When the two of them were compared, the difference was obvious.

So they could only flee!

“If enmity isn’t severed, then it’ll only lead to more worries. Everyone, it’s better if all of you leave your lives behind!” All of this seemed to have been within Zhao Taici’s expectations, and she suddenly sighed lightly before she stretched out her hand and pointed it towards the sky in the next moment.

Bang!

It was just a casual movement, yet it was like a divine decree had been sent down from the heavens, and a strand of the aura of divinity suddenly suffused the heavens and the earth.

Time, space, light, dust, and even the aura of the Grand Dao and the Immortal Energy in the world. Everything within the entire world seemed to have been restrained by a shapeless and obscure aura of divinity, and it seemed as if everything had fallen into an absolutely motionless and frozen state at this instant!

With a single point, the world had been frozen, and the Grand Dao submitted!

Such world shocking ability could only be possessed by the gods of legend.

At this moment, Zuoqiu Feng, Wei Xing, and the others that were originally tearing space apart to teleport away seemed like fish that were frozen in ice, and their figures suddenly stopped moving before they were unable to move at all!

When looked at from afar, all of them maintained all sorts of fleeing postures, yet they were like numerous motionless statues, and it was an extremely amusing sight.

However, when this scene entered Chen Xi’s eyes, it caused him to feel extremely shocked.

After all, these were many Immortal Kings amongst those experts, and every single one of them possessed supreme strength in the Immortal Dimension. Yet now, they were actually unable to resist a casual point from Zhao Taici!

How terrifying was such might?

In the past, when he witnessed the battles between Immortal Kings in the God Attainment Region, it had already caused Chen Xi to be shocked, and he felt tiny before them. Now, when he witnessed the attack of an existence at the Godrank Realm, the shock he felt at that moment was simply beyond compare.

Yes, this was the first time that Chen Xi had witnessed the strength of a ‘god!’

Even though he didn’t experience it himself, as he gazed at those motionless figures in the distance and looked at those Immortal Kings that seemed like shackled prisoners that were unable to struggle at all, it was sufficient to prove exactly how terrifying the strength of gods was.

Actually, it was precisely because of this that such a strength couldn’t be possessed in the three dimensions, and it had exceeded the scope of the three dimensions to arrive at a completely new height. Thus, its might naturally wouldn’t be ordinary.

It wasn’t just Chen Xi, even Hua Jiankong, Xuanyuan Shao, and the others revealed shocked expressions, and they felt a wisp of fear towards the might of gods.

Only Midnight Immortal King revealed burning anticipation in her eyes. Because she was currently only a trace away from becoming a god. Thus, she had a better understanding of how terrifying the might of gods was.

Bang! Bang! Bang!

A wave of explosions resounded without end. Everyone could clearly see that no matter what cultivation they possessed, the figures of Wei Xing and the mysterious cloaked figures by his side exploded into pieces like glass at this moment. All of them exploded into pieces, transformed into a thick rain of blood, and were disintegrated by strands of shapeless energy of divinity.

It was completed in a mere breath of time!

Wei Xing, the nine Core Pawns of the Sovereign Sect that were at the Immortal King Realm, and the 25 Major Pawns had all perished at the same moment!

This scene wasn’t just terrifying, it was simply astounding!

Nine Immortal Kings had perished in an instant. If word of this were to spread in the Immortal Dimension, then it would definitely cause a monstrous uproar!

Even Hua Jiankong and the others couldn’t help but be shocked when they witnessed this scene. It wasn’t that they pitied Wei Xing and the others, and it was instead because they were Immortal Kings as well. They were existences that were respected and worshipped no matter where they went in the Immortal Dimension, and they possessed supreme might. Yet when facing a god, all their pride, arrogance, and supremeness didn’t exist at all.

This was the gap between them!

To the myriad of living beings in the three dimensions, they were Immortal Kings, existences that could only be looked up to. Yet when all of these Immortal Kings faced the gods, they could only look up to the gods as well, and they were no different than the myriad of living beings that looked up to them.

“I’ll leave the rest to you.” Suddenly, Zhao Taici turned around and looked at Chen Xi.

Along with the death of Wei Xing and the others, only Zuoqiu Feiming, Zuoqiu Huanglin, and over twenty elders of the Zuoqiu Clan remained.

They were restrained by a strand of divinity, so they were still conscious yet were unable to move at all. They were even worse off than lambs that were waiting to be slaughtered. At the very least, the lambs could still struggle, yet they weren’t even able to move a single finger!

Chen Xi’s heart shook, and then he instantly understood what Zhao Taici meant. He took a deep breath and said solemnly, “Thank you for fulfilling my wish, Senior!”

As soon as he finished speaking, he turned around and strode towards Zuoqiu Feng and the others. This enmity was between him and the Zuoqiu Clan in the end! So, it should naturally be personally dealt with by him. To kill or to torture, it was all in his hands.

Because this was a knot in his heart, a shackle on his Dao, and only by breaking free from it would he be able to eliminate all his inner demons!

…

When he saw Chen Xi walking over, step by step, a wisp of despair couldn’t help but surge out from the hearts of Zuoqiu Feng and the others. They were both furious and terrified, yet they just so happened to be unable to make a sound or move at all.

In other words, they could only watch as death approached step by step towards them!

This sort of feeling where their fate was controlled in the hands of another was something they hadn’t experienced in countless years. Yet now, they could only wait for death to arrive, and this sort of feeling was utterly indescribable unless one experienced it on one’s own.

It was even to the extent that this sort of helplessness, rage, despair, and sorrow caused them to be on the verge of collapsing. If it was possible, they would rather die grandly in battle than wait for death to arrive in such an unjust manner.

This sort of method wasn’t just cruel, it was simply ruthless!

It seemed to be a silent death without any torture, yet the impact and humiliation they felt in their hearts was like suffering torture in purgatory, and they truly wished for nothing more than to commit suicide and end their lives as soon as possible.

But the sad part was that…even their strength to commit suicide had been taken away at this moment!

Pu!

Chen Xi began his revenge. An elder of the Zuoqiu Clan was killed, and Chen Xi reaped the elder’s head and carried it in his hand while the elder’s corpse crashed to the ground.

At this moment, Chen Xi’s expression was calm and without any emotion at all. He seemed to have completely transformed into an emotionless executioner, and he reaped bloody head after blood head with every step he took.

From the beginning until the end, he didn’t speak a single word, nor did he reveal a trace of emotion from being able to take revenge. He seemed to be extremely calm and even indifferent.

This enmity had accumulated in his heart for so many years, so was there anything left to say?

If wasting his breath could resurrect those fallen members of the Chen Clan, if pity could calm the hatred in his heart, was there any need for him to fight and slaughter until today?

So he wasn’t willing to say anything!

He would absolutely not feel pity for them!

All this hatred had to be washed away with blood and death in the end, and there was no other way!

It was even to the extent that for the sake of avoiding any unexpected events from occurring at this moment, his movements seemed to be extremely decisive, precise, and ruthless. He didn’t hesitate at all, nor did he waste even a shred of time.

Because all he wanted was to take revenge!

Only that.
