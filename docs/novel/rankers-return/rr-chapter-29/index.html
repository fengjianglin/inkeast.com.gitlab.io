Ranker's Return

The bomb that Hyeonu threw was a nuclear bomb. It bombed the chat room. The chat room was in shock.

-What did he just say just now? Did I hear incorrectly?

- ㄴㄴ It seems like you heard it well. It is a solo raid today.

- ㅁㅊㄷㅁㅊㅇ  Isn’t this something no one else has done?

-Successful Raid has gifted you 99 gold coins.

- ㅁㅊㄷㅁㅊㅇ has gifted you 99 gold coins.

“Thank you for the gold coins. Hahat!” Hyeonu showed a small but intense reaction to the gold coins pouring in. “Additionally, I’m going to be hunting the boss later. I hope you don’t give me gold coins during that time because I won’t be able to see the boss.”

He bowed toward the viewers.

“I would be grateful if you give everything after I have caught the boss. For people who are watching for my reactions, I’m sorry if I can’t do too much. I also don’t think I will be able to chat.”

Hyeonu turned off the chat room after saying this. Now was the time to focus on the raid. Hyeonu stepped toward the home of the huge guy he found yesterday.


***


It had been overwhelming at first sight. He had learned from the status message that it was a variant desert dragon. It was literally a sub-type of a dragon. Nevertheless, Hyeonu was confident. Of course, he couldn’t hunt it in one blow like a goblin. If he could, he would be the strongest in Arena. Still, it was now possible for him to hunt it.

‘The damage is enough if I use Magic Power Control.’

“Everyone, it’s that guy. Isn’t it huge? Still, don’t worry. In a few hours, it will be lying on the floor in a terrible state.”

‘It has a circle on its back as well.’

Hyeonu summoned Tang-E and received the buffs.


[You have received Bear’s Momentum.]

[Physique has increased.]

[Strength has increased.]

[You have received the Forest’s Blessing.]

[Defense has increased.]

[Health will continue to recover.]


His body tensed up as he gathered all his strength.

“I will start!” Hyeonu shouted those words without knowing who he was talking to.


[Master of Combat is activated.]

[Your stats have increased.]

[The stat ‘fighting energy’ has caused your stats to rise.]

[The other party is stronger than you.]

[There will be an additional increase in stats.]


‘Now!’

The moment the desert dragon took a deep breath, Hyeonu released his tension. Hyeonu’s movements were like a flash of lightning. The beginning was the Crescent Moon Cut. The three-meter-long blue beam of energy flew toward the desert dragon.

“Kuooh!” The cries of the desert dragon rang out everywhere along with a huge explosion. Hyeonu had already stepped on the desert dragon’s body and landed on its back. He thrust his longsword into the white spot he noticed earlier.

‘From now on, it is starting for real.’

The desert dragon was attacked on its reverse scale and started to go crazy. From now on, it was clear that he would be logged out the moment he was hit even once. Hyeonu’s concentration gradually increased.


***


There wasn’t a single comment in the chat window since Hyeonu started his solo raid. Hyeonu hadn’t banned them from chatting. The tens of thousands of viewers were just focused on Hyeonu. Hyeonu’s movements were phenomenal to this extent.

-Wow... It doesn’t make sense.

-A solo raid... Who is capable of this?

-If he succeeds, the one with the best control in Arena will be Alley Leader.

At this moment, Hyeonu was in a crisis. The desert dragon used Breath. It had a trajectory that would cover all the routes Hyeonu could move to. In the eyes of the audience, Hyeonu would be directly hit by Breath. 

In an instant, Hyeonu’s point of view changed. 

Hyeonu was a monster streamer born out of capitalism.


***


He instinctively selected to change the point of view when the desert dragon let out the Breath. Hyeonu didn’t know why. He just felt like he had to do it.

‘Phew, I thought I was going to die.’

He naturally escaped the Breath. It was because Hyeonu inducted the desert dragon to use Breath. Hyeonu’s goal was shortly after the desert dragon used Breath.

‘This is the other big weakness.’

The desert lizard’s most vulnerable moment was after using a Breath. The moment it breathed out without any vigilance for five seconds was when Hyeonu resumed the battle. By the time the desert dragon noticed something and moved, Hyeonu had already climbed onto its back. The blue light once again dug into the desert dragon’s reverse scale. It ripped open the original longsword wound that Hyung had dealt.

“Kyao!”

The desert dragon’s front paws moved quickly. It was to hit the human, that was dealing pain to it, like swatting a fly. Hyeonu swung his longsword to stop the attack of the dragon’s forefoot. It was just that the Giant skills were gone, and Hyeonu’s strength was no longer comparable to that of the desert dragon.

Hyeonu was forcefully thrown back several meters. He stood straight up and held his posture. However, the desert dragon’s massive appearance already appeared before Hyeonu’s eyes. The desert dragon was roaring when a sound rang out from somewhere.

“Master dude! Now!” Tang-E had fired lightning. Tang-E’s magic power was sufficient to inflict a blow on this guy. In particular, it would be a critical hit when aimed at the reverse scale.

‘Now it feels like the old days.’

Hyeonu was able to breathe once Tang-E joined in. From now on, he wouldn’t be able to borrow the strength of his past experiences. It was time to carve out a new path. He had to hit the dragon using everything in his body without any reservation. This would be the starting point for the raid’s success.


***


It had been three hours since the desert dragon raid began. Even so, neither the party doing the raid nor the viewers who were watching felt it.

-Wow! Three hours have passed already.

-The time machine is working properly.

-I’m tired just from hunting for three hours. I might vomit if I had to fight for three hours continuously.

This moment was the highlight and the climax. It wasn’t an intense and dramatic scene. Hyeonu just had an opportunity to end the battle that lasted for three hours. The desert dragon was finally exhausted. The past three hours of fighting left huge injuries on the desert dragon. It reached the point where the reverse scale was hard to see and the desert dragon’s entire body was torn apart by Hyeonu’s longsword.

“Tang-E!” Hyeonu urgently called out to Tang-E. He couldn’t miss this opportunity.

“Understood!” Tang-E’s lightning flew into the tattered reverse scales.

“Kiyaaak!” This was the dragon’s first ferocious cry. It was the howl of a pain-afflicted beast. At this moment, Hyeonu flew forth in the form of a ray of light, scraping up all the remaining magic power in his body.

‘This...’

“It is the end!”

Hyeonu’s longsword flew toward the desert dragon’s neck. The moment the desert dragon’s neck was hit, a giant blue light emerged. This was the Crescent Moon Cut. It was larger than its initial size and had little difficulty cutting off the desert dragon’s neck.

Now, it was the end of over three hours of fighting.


***


[A variant desert dragon has been killed.]

[Experience has been acquired.]

[You have leveled up.]

[All health and magic power have been restored.]

[You have leveled up.]

[All health and magic power have been restored.]

[You have leveled up.]

[All health and magic power have been restored.]

[Your fighting energy stat has increased by 10.]

[The title ‘Solo Raider’ has been created.]

[The title ‘Desert Dragon Slayer’ has been created.]

[The skill proficiency of Weapons Mastery has increased to D.]

[The skill proficiency of Crescent Moon Cut has increased to F+.]


The desert dragon left a lot for Hyeonu. It generously gave him three levels, skill proficiency increases, and even a title. However, there was more.


[Desert Dragon’s Essence]

[-A bracelet made by gathering everything from the desert dragon.

Rating: Unique

Restrictions: Desert Dragon Slayer

Effect: Magic Power +100, Physique +100, ‘Fire Breath’ is available (Cooldown Time: 24 hours.)]


“A unique item?” Hyeonu muttered as he forgot he was streaming. The viewers heard these words and exploded with curiosity. However, there was no chat window in front of Hyeonu’s eyes.

-Ah, Alley Leader! Aren’t you looking at the chat?

-I’m dizzy!!! Look at the chat!!!

Hyeonu spent a long time checking his skills and titles and only remembered he was on air after 15 minutes. “I’m sorry. I forgot that I was broadcasting. The chat has been turned back on.”

-I was dying from frustration.

- ㄹㅇ Praise Alley Leader. This isn’t the fighting power of a human.

-1 Alley Leader = 1 Desert Dragon. 

-Gold Coins Flowing has gifted you 99 gold coins.

The chat room was rejuvenated the moment Hyeonu turned it on. Then gold coins started to pour in. It was like a cannon celebrating Hyeonu’s raid success.

-Good at it Alone has given you 999 gold coins.

-Today’s Video has gifted you 999 gold coins.

“Thank you for the gold coins. I will buy items and skills with this and show you many other things.” Hyeonu bowed his head at the bomb of gold coins. Then there was one thing that caught Hyeonu’s eyes.

-Alley Leader-nim, where did you get Crescent Moon Cut?

“Crescent Moon Cut was received after clearing a quest. I didn’t get it from the random skill book like everyone else.”

Hyeonu talked with the audience for around 30 minutes afterward.

“I hope you enjoyed it. I’ll end the streaming here today, and I’ll see you next time.”

Hyeonu’s second streaming was over. The result was a great success.


***


Hyeonu’s great challenge received a lot of attention. It was a different sort of interesting compared to his other videos so far. This live stream started to attract attention not only from A-World but also the big broadcasters involved in Arena. As they became more interested in Hyeonu, the large guilds contracted with existing broadcast channels were bound to become more vigilant, jealous, and filled with the desire to keep Hyeonu in check.

However, some people weren’t affected.

“Junggu, is this the end?”

Gang Junggu replied to Kim Seokjung’s question, “Yes Hyung-nim. All we have to do now is kill the black magicians.”

“By the way, I heard that the Phoenix members are often seen at Ella Fortress these days? What’s going on?”

“It looks like they are working on a magic tower scenario. It will eventually overlap just like last time.”

“Really?” Kim Seokjung was troubled by Gang Junggu’s words. It was clear that he was considering the value of the Phoenix Guild. Should he discard them or take them?

‘Numbers will be important when it comes to a fight against black magicians.’

“Let’s do it together this time, Junggu. It is too big to share anyway.”

“I understand.”

Kim Seokjung decided to join hands with the Phoenix Guild. It was because he thought this scenario was a serious one.

“I don’t know what that little brother is doing. It was fun when doing the quest together.”
