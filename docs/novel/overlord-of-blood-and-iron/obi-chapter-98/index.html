Chapter 98. Real man Kang Chul-In Leyy3's Thoughts

Action Comedy Fantasy Kingdom Building Superpowers Korean Ongoing 2019/12/14

Overlord of Blood and Iron

“I’m guessing it’s those adventurers that came back from Pangaea,” Kang Chul-In mused.

“How’d you know?”

“Well, it’s obvious.” 

Though Old Man Kwon was already 80 years old and liked to ride supercars and had a secret romance with a famous old actress…

He was still a terrifying man.

If there was someone that dared to fight back against someone like Old Man Kwon?

It would have to be adventurers.

To adventurers that had superhuman strength, law and order didn’t matter to them as they could just run away after breaking and destroying everything.

“Well, I guess you’re right. There are clans that are trying to disrupt our business. Just this week, 7 of my bodyguards have gone missing. I think it might be a warning of sorts.”

It was a warning. 

This was clearly a warning message, threatening that they would do something like a terror attack against the Galaxy Corporation if they dared to continue and expand their business.

“The Unique Clan and the Omega Clan.” 

Kang Chul-In knew what groups and clans were bothering Old Man Kwon. 

“The Unique Clan’s headquarters are located in Mapo-gu, around Hongdae. Look for a club/lounge that goes by the name of ‘Insane’ and look at the VIP rooms there.” 

Kang Chul-In knew a lot about the Unique Clan, too much in fact. After all, it was a clan that he had smashed in his past life.

“As for the Omega Clan… I’m not entirely sure. Those people are quite secretive and mysterious.”

“To deal with them, I will lend you the 30 Adventurers that I mentioned to you. Then, you can try to use them and take care of the Omega Clan.” 

Kang Chul-In knew that Billy Halford and the other adventurers were quite skilled as they survived during the hunt of the Peacock Dragon. With adventurers like them, it wouldn’t big a big problem to take down the Omega Clan.

“What…?”

Old Man Kwon was startled.

“I thought you were running a territory in the Pangaean continent… How do you know so much about this? Huhu… Oh, do you have some sort of informant that you use?”

“Don’t ask me.” 

“Haha, come on. Why don’t you introduce me to that informant of yours too? What do you want as payment? Ferrari? Or a Porsche?” 

Out of Old Man Kwon’s mouth came some car brands that would make one’s jaw open in awe.

“Oh! Thinking about it now, even though you do have a Mustang, a Lamborghini, and a Jaguar… You don’t have a Ferrari! If you’re a real man, don’t you think you need to drive a Ferrari at least once?” 

Using a Ferrari as bait, Old Man Kwon looked at Kang Chul-In as if to say “How’s that? You have to tell now, don’t you?” 

But…

“If you know, you’ll get hurt.”

Kang Chul-In still didn’t tell him.

“Sigh… You’re too resilient.”

Due to this, Old Man Kwon had to lick his lips in disappointment. If he asked, he would get hurt, and no sane man would ask knowing this.

“A Bugatti Veyron.”

“Huh?”  Old Man Kwon’s eyes turned bigger at the sudden request.

“What, so you are going to tell me the informant?”

“Of course not.” 

Kang Chul-In himself was the informant that Old Man Kwon really wanted to meet, and Kang Chul-In had no intention of introducing Old Man Kwon to his past memories. 

“Just tell me.”

Kang Chul-In suddenly turned into a thief.

“What…?”

“To be precise, a Bugatti Veyron Super…”

“Don’t tell me… a Supersport?”

“Haha, you know me well.” 

The price of this car was incredible.

A Bugatti Veyron Supersport was one of the derivatives of the Bugatti Veyron and was incredibly expensive, pricing out at over 2 million dollars. 

“P-Please, give me mercy… It’s too expensive…”

“The hair growth serum is still selling right now, I know that you don’t have problems with money.” 

“Fine! Fine. Just give me 3 days.” 

“Hmm… Actually, just give me a list of all the supercars you want. I think that’d be better, for the future.”

Old Man Kwon spoke as if he was pained.

“Around how many more are you aiming to collect?” 

“Hmm... maybe 10?” 

“Oh, also… can you make me another ID card for someone the age of 9?”

“9 years old?”

“It’s for my daughter. It would be even better if it was double-nationality, for Korea and the U.S. Her surname, of course, has to be Kang.”

It was for Arshelly.

“D-Daughter? Y-You were a father?” 

“Don’t ask.” 

Kang Chul-In turned his head around.

“Haha, I thought you were still unmarried, but… Ha… Well then, what about your wife…?”

As Old Man Kwon was going to ask about Kang Chul-In’s non-existent wife, Old Man Kwon suddenly lost his balance.

BANG! 

With an extremely large noise, Galaxy Corporation’s building shook.

Cr-Crack! 

The windows cracked…

“Old man, duck!”

Kang Chul-In ran like lightning towards Old Man Kwon to protect him. As he arrived next to Old Man Kwon, a bunch of the chandeliers and other things hanging on the ceiling fell down.

“W-What the hell?” 

“Is it an earthquake?”

“No, it’s not.”

Kang Chul-In shook his head and cleaned up all the shattered glass on the floor.

“It’s a terrorist attack.”

“A terrorist attack?” 

“Some crazy people are threatening us right now.”

But Kang Chul-In remained calm even when this happened.

It’s probably the Omega Clan.

Though the Unique Clan did disrupt a lot of things even in Kang Chul-In’s past life, they did not do terrorism on such a big scale.

Meanwhile, the Omega Clan was different.

They did not care about the method and just got things done.

“I’m guessing that they used some kind of explosives to do that,” Kang Chul-In explained.

“No, it can’t be. That kind of terrorism hasn’t occurred in Korea before. There’s no way that…”

But before Old Man Kwon could even finish his words…

“President!”

“President, we have a report!”

A bunch of bodyguards came to Old Man Kwon and surrounded him.

“There’s trouble! Apparently, according to security, there were shots from an RPG-7 from the top of the building next to us.”

This was when Kang Chul-In’s words turned out to be true.

 

-----

 

Kang Chul-In didn’t need to do anything.

T-These… I’ll do this with my own strength. Don’t worry.

Becoming extremely angry, Old Man Kwon reverted to “Kwon Hyung-woo”, the absolute head of the dark Korean underworld.

Agreeing to this, Kang Chul-In gave Old Man Kwon the right to command Billy Halford and the other adventurers until the case was finished.

It was at that moment, when Kang Chul-In was driving his Jaguar V-8 while thinking about his future plans.

- Mr. Chul-In~

 

A message from Lee Chae-Rin came.

- We’re at B.I Spa-Land right now. Do you want to come?

- No.

Kang Chul-In answered lazily. He didn’t really like answering in long texts while driving.

- (sad face)

Lee Chae-Rin sent a sad face emoji to him.

Spa? I have no time, or need for that.

Kang Chul-In had already set up an extremely luxurious Hinoki Bath in his new house and could relax in there. There was no real reason for him to waste his money and…

- Son! 

A message from his mother also came.

- Come quickly~ I’m waiting ^^

 

And finally…

- Dad~~

 

Arshelly sent him a selfie of her wearing a Korean-style Sheep-head using the towel, asking him to come. It seemed like the 3 of them had all went to “B.I Spa-Land” together.
