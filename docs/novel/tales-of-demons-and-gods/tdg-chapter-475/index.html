Chapter 475 - Tied Together

The disciples of the Divine Feathers Sect had no other choice.

Before coming to the Heavenly Note Sect, Nie Li had already issued them a death order. If they do not get a Dao Companion from the Heavenly Note Sect, then they wouldn’t be allowed to return.

If they couldn’t accomplish their mission, wouldn’t that be terrible? Didn’t that mean that they would become homeless?

In the beginning, everyone wasn’t that anxious as there was ample time for them, so they had been seeking for their admiring companions. The Heavenly Note Sect was truly a well-famed cultivation sect, since the female disciples in there were all beauties that dazzled their eyes.

But on the second day, there was already a disciple that got together with a female disciple of the Heavenly Note Sect. The two of them were paired together, which ignited the envy in others.

Thereafter, it was said that there were even disciples that spent the night in the room of the female disciples of the Heavenly Note Sect, so how could they hold themselves back?!

But they still weren’t too anxious, wasn’t the good stuff usually left to the back?

On the third day, another three disciples found their Dao Companion and five on the fourth day. Reaching the fifth day, there were already thirteen disciples.

Sometimes, love was like a plague that spreads around.

If others found their Dao Companion and not you, wouldn’t you be anxious about it?

Not to mention that they had been given a death order when they were sent over, so how would they not dare to accomplish it?

When those clumsy people saw the beauties in the Heavenly Note Sect, they wouldn’t be able to speak a single word, so how were they going to find their Dao Companion? But even if they had to die, they still had to complete the task given to them by the Sect Master!

Thus, all sorts of love books and techniques of wooing girls were spread amongst the disciples of the Divine Feathers Sect.

Furthermore, in order to woo the female disciples in the Heavenly Note Sect, they had used all sorts of methods. Even sneaking into the rooms of those female disciples.

There’s a phrase ‘even barbaric women would be fearful of a tangling man’, seeing as their sisters were finding their other half, then the other female disciples would yield after making a show of resistance.

Henceforth, the entire Heavenly Note Sect had been turned into a matchmaking session.

In the hall of the Heavenly Note Sect

Nangong Xianyin nearly collapsed and she was furious. She never imagined that allowing the Divine Feathers Sect to help guard the Heavenly Note Sect would turn into such a situation.

“Is what he say the truth?” Nangong Xianyin asked Ye Ziyun.

Ye Ziyun’s face blushed from embarrassment as she answered, “Yes, Sect Master. Nie Li said it this way.”

“He… this is simply…” Nangong Xianyun nearly erupted, since she had no idea what words to use to describe what she was feeling. If she said something wrong and it was delivered to Nie Li, then it might be a troublesome matter for her.

After all, the Divine Feathers Sect was powerful now and even if Nangong Xianyin was furious, she had to swallow it down.

“Are there any words the Sect Master would like to say that needs to be delivered?” Ye Ziyun looked at Nangong Xianyin.

Nangong Xianyin pondered, then she softened. “Since the Divine Feathers Sect is unwilling to leave, then am I allowed to move a portion of my disciples to other places?”

“Sect Master, Nie Li has considered this matter. He said that the Demon God’s Sect is currently capturing those of the Righteous Sects, and the disciples of the Heavenly Note Sect are still too weak. One or two might not be a problem, but if a group of them goes out at once, they would be easily attacked by the Demon God’s Sect.” Ye Ziyun said seriously.

“Send out one or two?” Nangong Xianyin’s brows twitched. With the current situation in the Heavenly Note Sect, what’s the use of just sending one or two?

“Furthermore, Nie Li had said that since the Divine Feathers Sect has come to the Heavenly Note Sect, we have to express something. No matter what the Heavenly Note Sect wants, be it peerless Cultivation Techniques, peerless pills or even Sacred Elixirs, the Divine Feathers Sect is willing to supply them. But that is only for one of our own.” Ye Ziyun said as she somewhat blushed with shame.

“One of us?” Nangong Xianyin was stunned.

“Correct, that means that those that are close to the disciples of the Divine Feathers Sect’s disciples. Nie Li said that the girls in the Heavenly Note Sect are pretending to be noble, giving unpleasant eyes to the disciples of the Divine Feathers Sect. And the Divine Feathers Sect is unwilling to have dealings with these kinds of people,” said Ye Ziyun.

“Nie Li, that fellow… he’s simply… shameless!” Nangong Xianyin’s face had turned red. If she didn’t know that Ye Ziyun was Nie Li’s fiancée, she would have flipped out by now. So even if the Divine Feathers Sect was powerful, she still couldn’t help cursing out.

This move was simply too ruthless from Nie Li. What did he mean by pretending to be noble? That’s called keeping themselves out of trouble.

Over the next period of time, those female disciples of the Heavenly Note Sect that were keeping themselves out of trouble would definitely suffer some suppression. As for those female disciples that were close to the disciples of the Divine Feathers Sect, there were gifts of peerless Cultivation Techniques, peerless pills or even Sacred Elixirs. Didn’t that mean that they were trying to empty out the Heavenly Note Sect?

“What else did he say? This is simply too much, did he really think that there’s no one in my Heavenly Note Sect and we’re easy to bully?” Nangong Xianyin cursed with anger as she continued, “I will now summon the disciples of my Heavenly Note Sect and go all-out with him.”

“Nie Li still said these words. Sect Master Nangong, what’s the point of this? First of all, the Divine Feathers Sect and Heavenly Note Sect are not enemies. The Divine Feathers Sect only wanted to protect the Heavenly Note Sect. No matter what happened to the disciples of the Heavenly Note Sect, they will always be part of the Heavenly Note Sect. And if Sect Master Nangong doesn’t chase them away, they will not leave.”

Ye Ziyun carefully observed Nangong Xianyin’s expression. Nie Li had even expected such a reaction from Nangong Xianyin. When she saw that Nangong Xianyin did not flip, she continued, “Union of Yin and Yang is the great path of humanity. Since the female disciples of the Heavenly Note Sect and the male disciples of the Divine Feathers Sect have an affinity with each other and love each other, our Divine Feathers Sect will not be willing to break them up. So why is Sect Master Nangong going to break up the mandarin ducks?”

“But there are rules and ancestor’s teachings in the Heavenly Note Sect!” Nangong Xianyin spoke out solemnly.

“Nie Li even said that the so-called “rules and ancestor’s teachings” were all made by an impulse of the ancestors and yet, thereafter generations followed them like creeds. Think about it, just how does it benefit the Heavenly Note Sect? With the population in the Heavenly Note Sect decreasing and is now the weakest amongst the Righteous Sects, if the Divine Feathers Sect was to keep ourselves out of this matter, then who knows what outcome there might be. If the Demon God’s Sect launched a war with the Righteous Sects, the first to be annihilated would definitely be the Heavenly Note Sect. If the Heavenly Note Sect joins together with the Divine Feathers Sect, then wouldn’t it be a good thing for the Heavenly Note Sect’s population to rise?” Ye Ziyun saw the contemplation on Nangong Xianyin’s face as she continued, “Nie Li also said that as long as the Heavenly Note Sect doesn’t chase their disciples away, the Divine Feathers Sect will not take a single disciple of the Heavenly Note Sect away. As for the relationship between the disciples, it won’t take too long to come from the Divine Feathers Sect and it’s also a good time to have frequent interactions.”

Nangong Xianyun sunk into a long contemplation with her expression constantly changing. After all, such a decision was too tough for her to make.
