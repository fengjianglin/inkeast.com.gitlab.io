Chapter 482 - Divine Soul Dao Technique Thyaeria's Thoughts

Novel Chinese Ongoing 2015/07/12

Tales of Demons & Gods

“This…” The other Sect Masters of the various Divine Sects looked at each other and were shocked by Nie Li’s words.

So it turned out that the legend about the Sage Emperor’s existence was real, and he’s trying to refine the entire Draconic Ruins Realm. Just how powerful was that sort of existence?

“Take this Sacred Elixir, for example. Do you think that you can find it elsewhere aside from the Ancestral God Lands?” Nie Li smiled as he looked at everyone.

“That means that you’re someone from the Ancestral God Lands?” Everyone looked at Nie Li in shock.

“All of you aren’t stupid, so how can I impartially distribute so many Sacred Elixirs to everyone if it wasn’t for the life and death of the Draconic Ruins Realm? Who would be stupid enough to do that?” Nie Li said as he looked at everyone.

Everyone exchanged a look as they pondered for a long time.

It looked like Nie Li truly was someone from the Ancestral God Lands, and since he’s someone from the Ancestral God Lands, they should unite together and obey Nie Li for survival.

Lu Piao and company blinked their eyes with puzzlement. ‘Since when did Nie Li become someone from the Ancestral God Lands?’

However, it seemed like Nie Li did not admit his identity, but it was a conclusion that everyone came to.

“My Sky Origin Sect is willing to follow the lead of Sect Master Nie!” After a brief consideration, the Sect Master of the Sky Origin Sect cupped his hands together at Nie Li as he continued, “If Sect Master Nie has ways to deal with the Sage Emperor, we’re willing to cooperate as much as possible.”

“Our Phaseless Sect is willing to follow Sect Master Nie’s lead!”

……

The Sect Masters of the other Divine Sects called out after they’ve thought it through. Nie Li has given them so many Sacred Elixirs, and they’re not able to take out anything more precious than the Sacred Elixirs. Thus, they could only come to a conclusion that was to deal with the Sage Emperor, which made sense!

When Nie Li conversed with the Demon Lord, their discussion had also mentioned the Sage Emperor.

“My… Heavenly Note Sect is the same…” Nangong Xianyin weakly said after some pondering.

“With everyone’s cooperation, we will certainly be able to defeat the Sage Emperor and get through the calamity!” Nie Li smiled. He had already anticipated such a response.

“Sect Master Nie, what should we do now?” The Sect Masters asked.

“We have a few things that I need all of you to do. Although all of you have disciples in your sects, and the six Divine Sects have at least a million disciples, it’s far from enough. I need all of you to absorb at least tens of millions of disciples.” Nie Li said.

“We’ve already absorbed all the talented ones from the various Lower Planes into our sects, and only those weaker ones are left. So it’s useless even if we’ve absorbed them, how should we teach them?” The Sect Master of the Phaseless Sect asked.

“I will give you guys a few cultivation techniques. All of you make a copy of them and hand them to those disciples. Furthermore, all the disciples have to cultivate a cultivation technique, the «Divine Soul Dao Technique».” Nie Li replied.

“What sort of cultivation technique is that?” Asked the Sect Masters.

“Only those that cultivated the «Divine Soul Dao Technique» can step into the Ancestral God Lands. There’s one benefit in this cultivation technique, and it will greatly increase one’s cultivation speed.” Nie Li smiled.

“Since that’s the case, then we will heed Sect Master Nie’s words!” The Sect Masters all agreed.

“I will leave for a period, but I have to ask all the Sect Masters to have the task I’ve assigned done as soon as possible.” Nie Li said as he cupped his hands towards everyone.

“Don’t worry!” Everyone responded.

Over the next few days, Nie Li copied a few cultivation techniques and handed them to the various Sect Masters of the Divine Sects, which they swiftly made copies of them. Looking at all of the various powerful cultivation techniques, the Sect Masters couldn’t help sighing in astonishment.

The majority of them were stronger than their secret cultivation techniques, and with this matter, all of the Sect Masters’ belief in Nie Li being someone from the Ancestral Gods Land strengthened.

Just these cultivation techniques along were priceless!

Ye Ziyun and company also swiftly packed their stuff and made preparations to return to the Tiny World.

“Nie Li, what’s the Divine Soul Dao Technique? Since it can greatly increase the cultivation speed, why didn’t you let us practice it?” Everyone asked in puzzlement.

“You guys are not suitable for the Divine Soul Dao Technique. It’s just one of the helpless options to confront the Sage Emperor. This Divine Soul Dao Technique was something that I created, and everyone that cultivated it could increase their cultivation speed. But in the process of that, a portion of their Soul Energy will be separated…” Nie Li explained after a brief hesitation.

“Separate a portion of the Soul Energy?” Du Ze was puzzled before he asked, “What for?”

“Soul Energy can be absorbed by those who have cultivated the Divine Soul Binding Technique.” Nie Li continued his explanation, “In the future, I will pass you guys the Divine Soul Binding Technique.”

When everyone heard that, they couldn’t help widening their eyes.

“If I told them about it, I’m afraid that they would be suspicious and not dare to cultivate it.” Nie Li bitterly smiled as he continued, “To face the Sage Emperor, I need everyone’s strength to be focused on a few people to step into the Deity Realm as soon as possible.”

“What a terrifying and powerful cultivation technique…” Everyone couldn’t help being shocked. They were dumbfounded for a long time.

“Nie Li, what happens if tens of millions cultivate the Divine Soul Dao Technique?” Du Ze couldn’t help asking.

“If tens of millions cultivate it, we will only require a day to reach the Martial Ancestor Realm.” Nie Li explained.

“One day?” Everyone couldn’t help taking a cold breath from the shock.

“Then, will there be any side effects?”

“Yeah, it’s too powerful.”

“There’s none for the time being. But the effect of the Divine Soul Binding Technique will only last for thirty years before it slowly dissipates.” Nie Li answered.

“So, that’s the case.” After a brief pondering, Lu Piao continued, “It’s still powerful.”

“Nie Li, you intend to fight with the Sage Emperor within thirty years?” Du Ze couldn’t help looking at Nie Li as he asked.

“Indeed, we only have thirty years at most, and that’s also our only chance.” Nie Li nodded his head.

“Duan Jian is willing to follow Master into war with the Sage Emperor, regardless of life or death.” Duan Jian said decisively.

Looking at Lu Piao and company, Nie Li could see the fire blazing in their eyes.

‘In my previous life, I had to witness every single one of them leaving my side. In this lifetime, I won’t let it happen again, and I had to win because of them!’ Nie Li solemnly swore in his heart.
