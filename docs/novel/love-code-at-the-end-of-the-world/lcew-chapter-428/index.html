"He Lei?!" Raffles' surprise voice instantly made my heart hang by a thread. He Lei got hurt?!

I looked over at once but saw that He Lei wasn't injured.

He stood by the door of the spaceship, staring at me with a pained expression. His head was lowered in agony, his hand seemed to be holding onto something.

My heart hung precariously on a thread. His expression made me insecure. I had a bad feeling that was suffocating me.

Slowly he walked over and stood before me. I saw his eyes welled up with sorrowful tears.

"He Lei? What's wrong?"

He slowly extended his tightly clenched fist to me. "I'm sorry, Luo Bing." He took my hand and put something on my palm. "I'm sorry." As he turned around in pain, tears flowed down his cheeks.

One glance at my palm and my body quivered. Something seemed to have sucked away all my energy and I couldn't stand straight. My chest was aching as though an explosion had shattered my heart. It was so painful that I couldn't breathe!

"No, no, I don't believe this! I don't believe this!" In my hand was Harry's Silver Moon badge!

"Harry!" Raffles grabbed the badge. "No way. Harry wouldn't die! He wouldn't die!" He roared in agony, "What happened?! I want to know the ins and outs!"

"He wouldn't!" My head buzzed and went blank. "I absolutely won't believe that Harry died!"

"I'm sorry! Luo Bing!" He Lei turned and hugged me tightly. He choked with tears. "Harry was covering everyone as they retreated. He was hit then. I'm sorry. I'm sorry..."

"I want to kill all of them! I want to kill all of them!" My body was shaking in anger as tears blurred my vision.

I pushed He Lei away and walked straight. I wanted to kill them! I wanted to kill them! No matter whether it was the universe or a black hole, nothing was going to stop me from going to Steel Ghost City!

"Luo Bing! Luo Bing!" He Lei pulled at me but I swung him away. I pointed at his face as tears wetted my cheeks. Everyone in the surrounding stopped to look at me.

"Don't stop me!" I pointed at He Lei's anxious face.

Raffles ran over hastily. "Lil Bing! Don't act on impulse! You can't be in danger again!"

"You are not allowed to stop me too!" I roared in pain. "None of you can stop me!" I pressed on the Gold Moon badge on my chest. In an instant, my spaceship landed behind me like a giant standing at my back. 

"Luo Bing! We want to take revenge for Harry too!" He Lei instantly appeared in front of me and held my hand. "But what difference is there between you going in alone and sending yourself to your death?! Harry wouldn't want you to do that! He loves you! You are the most important person to him! He would want you to be safe!"

Harry!

Harry!

"Harry promised me to come back alive..." I roared and wailed in tears.

Anger rushed up to my brain. I didn't care. I was going to kill the person who had killed Harry. All of them had to die!!!

My body instantly burned. Immediately blue light spots exploded from my body!

"Ah!" He Lei was sent flying off and Raffles quickly caught him. The clothes around his chest had turned into ashes and his skin had started rotting. He Lei covered his chest in pain while Raffles looked at me. “Lil Bing…!" he hurriedly called after me.

I took a step back before everyone's astonished gaze. The robot carried me and put me into the cabin. Then, it took off and left Silver Moon City!

"Luo Bing, don't act on impulse." Cang Yu's solemn face appeared.

I gave him a blank look then cut off his image directly.

I leaped into the dark universe and charged towards the crimson planet. Under the moonlight, I landed at the entrance of Steel Ghost City. Lights shone upon me in an instant. Then, my spaceship started to cramp up. It was a metahuman's attack!

It must be the metahumans in Steel Ghost City!

*Bang!* The spaceship activated its protection program and ejected me out. I fell onto the ground while the lights concentrated on me. I stumbled as I got to my feet. My body swayed in the breeze while only one thing reverberated in my mind!

I want to kill all of them!

I want to kill all of them!!

I want to kill all of them!!!

"Ah!!" I roared in agony, my tears flowing down my cheeks. They killed Harry! They killed Harry! 

I want to take revenge and kill them all!

Harry!

Harry!

My Harry!

My dearest Harry!

My Harry who always gave way to me and spoiled me!

How could he just die like that?

How could he not keep his word?!

He promised me to come back alive...

He promised to be with me forever...

I don't believe it!

I don't!!!

My heart hurt!

I couldn't breath!

I could barely breathe!

Why does my heart hurt? It hurts so much that I can’t breath!

"Just one person? Hahaha... Let's see how we are going to play with him until he dies."

"Don't let him die so quickly. Let's have some fun."

"Me first!"

Suddenly, I felt a piercing pain on my arm. I glanced towards the pain blankly. Tears blurred my vision. I could see blood, blood that was flowing down my arm.

But I didn't feel pain.

Because my heart hurt so much.

I couldn't feel any other pain.

"I am going to kill all of you." I walked towards their city gate.

"What did he say?"

"It sounded like he wanted to kill all of us."

"With just him? Alone? Hahahaha..."

"Silver Moon City is the strongest at boasting... Hahaha..."

Blue light spots began to fill my body and my fingers started to turn translucent. There were blue lights flowing rapidly inside me! Now I was no longer bleeding. The wound had disappeared in the blue light. My entire arm turned translucent and shimmered blue too.

Suddenly, a cloud of yellow liquid charged at me. With a raise of my hand, a light wall formed around me. The cloud of liquid instantly turned into ashes in the light. Some dropped onto the ground. "Psst!" It evaporated into a cloud of disgusting green smoke that gave out a foul smell.

I retracted my hand blankly and continued to march towards the city gate. I wanted to take revenge for Harry! I would kill whoever stood in my way!

"Wow! I'll play with you!" A brawny person landed in front of me. He was wearing armor with black thorns on it. Spreading his arms, he suddenly expanded like Harry. His features turned ferocious while blank thorns popped out on his back. He became a huge monster and swung his arm at me!

I raised my hand again. The arm instantly burned the moment it touched the light wall around me!

I retracted my hand blankly. They can't touch me.

"Ah!!!" His shrill scream tore through the silence of the night sky. I glanced at him blankly and he vanished in my hands just like the spirits. Drowning in the blue light spots, he swiftly turned into ashes.
