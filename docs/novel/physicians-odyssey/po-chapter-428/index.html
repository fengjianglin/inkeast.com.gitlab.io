“Then… I’ll let you touch twice!” Gu Rushan gnashed her teeth as she made the decision. Firstly, she had agreed to Su Tao’s request.

Secondly, her butt was really aching, and she did not have any strength to resist.

If he really tried to violate her, how should she react?

But at this moment, Su Tao was focused entirely on the treatment, and he didn’t know of the turmoil going on in Gu Rushan’s heart.

“Done! Try walking a few steps!” Su Tao stood up and slightly arched his body. He realised that there was a reaction at a specific location on his body.

“Yep, didn’t I just tell you that I’m a doctor? Treating these kinds of injuries is nothing!” Su Tao smiled. Although he has some fame in the medical industry, it wasn’t in the same world as commoners, so it was natural that Gu Rushan wouldn’t know anything about him.

In fact, Su Tao only had to tell Gu Rushan that he’s the founder of the Three Flavour Cosmetics International for the latter to look at him in a new light. After all, any woman with taste would have heard a thing or two about the Beautifying Cream and Restoring Serum.

“So it turns out that you weren’t messing around when you treated that man in the morning.” Gu Rushan subconsciously pulled the hem of her pajamas. Since Su Tao touched her intending to treat her injury, then she wouldn’t haggle about his actions earlier.

“His condition was sudden cardiac death, and he would probably be in the ICU by now if it wasn’t for me.” Su Tao explained with a smile.

Gu Rushan was briefly stunned before she earnestly requested, “Please pretend to be my boyfriend tomorrow as it has something to do with the man you saved today. His girlfriend mistook us as a couple, and wanted to treat us to a meal to thank us.”

“Why didn’t you tell her that we’re just neighbors? Why do you want to lie to her?” Su Tao asked as he furrowed his brows together.

After a brief pondering, Gu Rushan did not reveal the reason in the end and locked her brows together. “You’ve already touched my butt, so why are you asking so much? No matter what, you have to help me tomorrow and accompany me to a meal. Furthermore, you have to pay attention to our relationship and don’t spill the beans!”

Su Tao knew that there must be a reason for it, which he sighed. Gu Rushan was scheming a little too much. But at the same time, it was understandable since she had to be clever as a drifter here. Otherwise, how was she going to survive in the brutal world here?

“Alright, I’ll help you!” Su Tao agreed.

Gu Rushan was the first person he got to know upon arriving in Beijing, and he admired her quite a bit. Even if she would occasionally appear to be shrewish, she’s also a clever woman.

Seeing that Su Tao finally agreed to her request, Gu Rushan briefly pondered before she said, “We can’t reveal any flaws tomorrow, and we have to match our lines. At the same time, we need to have a basic understanding of each other.”

“Is there a need for that? Isn’t it just a meal? Worse comes to worst. I’ll just try not to speak tomorrow!” Su Tao bitterly smiled. He suddenly realised that it wasn’t an easy task.

“No way! The meal tomorrow is significant for me, and you have to treat it seriously!” Gu Rushan forcefully said.

“So, what do I need to understand?” Su Tao weakly asked.

“My name is Gu Rushan, and I majored in dance at Shanzhou Province’s Art and Drama Institute. My father is a university professor while my mother is a public servant. It has been five years since I came to Beijing, and I’m currently working as a dance instructor at a dance school. I like to eat durian, my favorite color is purple, my favorite actor is Chen Mingdao, and my favorite singer is Na Ying…” Gu Rushan started incessantly rambling about her information.

“I think I really owed it to you in my previous life.” Su Tao rolled his head as he felt his head aching. How did he get himself such an exotic neighbor?

Then again, it was also due to Gu Rushan’s attractive looks, which was why Su Tao was willing to help. If she wasn’t a gorgeous woman, he wouldn’t even want to get himself involved in so much trouble.

“That’s where you’re wrong!” Gu Rushan smiled. “This is a transaction, and we will have nothing to do with each other after tomorrow!”

“Is there a need to be so heartless?” Su Tao rolled his eyes.

“There’s no relationship, to begin with, so how did the heartless come about?” Gu Rushan stabbed.

Sniffing his nose, Su Tao sourly replied, “Even a fake relationship is still a relationship. I’m already so sharp-tongued, but I actually can’t beat you in a war of words.”

“Quibbling is an innate talent for women, and you can just suck it up! Now, it’s your turn!” Gu Rushan smiled.

But just when Su Tao wanted to introduce himself, he saw Gu Rushan waving her hand and interrupted, “You’re twenty-five this year, and you’re from Huainan Province’s Hanzhou City. You originate from a TCM family, so your medical skills are pretty good. We got to know each other three years ago, and our relationship is between an ordinary friend and a couple.”

Su Tao was teased by the role that Gu Rushan’s role for him as it was more or less similar to his actual situation and curiously asked, “What’s between an ordinary friend and a couple?”

“You’re the one chasing after me, but I’ve not agreed to date you!” Gu Rushan blushed.

“I’ve already seen your butt, so shouldn’t I be promoted at the very least?” Su Tao wryly smiled.

“What nonsense are you talking about?” Gu Rushan glared at Su Tao with indignance. “Remember the role that I’ve assigned you, and don’t make a mistake tomorrow!”

When she finished, she immediately sent Su Tao out, which left him a little dumbfounded. After that, the door was opened again and she placed his medical box outside then tossed a complacent gaze to Su Tao before she closed the door.

After Su Tao returned to his apartment, Gu Rushan, who was leaning against the door, finally couldn’t hold herself back and chuckled. She had no idea where the joy came from, or perhaps it was from seeing how deflated Su Tao was.

“Hmph! Who asked you to take advantage of me? Even if you’re treating me, you still can’t do that!”

Thinking about that, Gu Rushan couldn’t help letting out a sigh and she felt at a loss. Someone took the treasure that she has protected for over twenty years.
