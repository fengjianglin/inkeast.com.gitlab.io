Action Comedy Kingdom Building Modern Setting Political Intrigue Romance Wuxia Chinese Ongoing 2019/01/16

Physician’s Odyssey

“Senior Brother, I…” Mo Sun’er nervously stuttered. She could feel the beads of sweat dripping from her forehead.

Looking at the beads of sweat that came along with a fragrance, Wang Guofeng couldn’t help taking a deep breath. It was a sweet flavor and compared to those gold diggers, Mo Sun’er was too valuable.

He was determined to make Mo Sun’er into his woman.

“Don’t you have something to say?” Wang Guofeng smiled casually. “I know that you might be startled, but I have no idea when I started to have feelings for you. I’ve said in the past that I will always treat you as a sister of mine, but I couldn’t accomplish that. I’ve realised that you’ve already walked into my heart. After I ended my engagement with Ruochen, I actually felt relieved since I can now openly pursue you.”

Wang Guofeng wouldn’t put in such an effort to sweet talk with girls in the past, but he has changed. After having a taste of more women around him, he had a few practices and swiftly became an expert playboy.

Naturally, sweet talk wouldn’t work for someone like Liu Ruochen. On the contrary, she would even feel disgusted instead.

But Mo Sun’er wasn’t even twenty. She might be smart, but she wasn’t experienced with men, not to mention that she admired him for a long time. So she had no resistance and the ability to distinguish.

“Ah!” Mo Sun’er was at a loss for words. “Senior Brother, I will feel guilty if you say that.”

“What is there for you to feel guilty about? Silly lass, don’t call me Senior Brother in the future. Just call me Guofeng. This way, we will be closer.” Wang Guofeng gently patted Mo Sun’er’s forehead then caressed her hair.

As Mo Sun’er felt her nose turning sour, tears dripped down from her cheeks as she shook her head. “Guo… feng… I can’t say it!”

Looking at Mo Sun’er’s pitiful looks, Wang Guofeng knew that she has been startled by him, so he gently said, “Then, just call me Senior Brother. You can call me however you like.”

“I still can’t accept this fact!” Mo Sun’er finally pulled her hands back and pinched herself on the cheek. “Wouldn’t I be the one to ruin your relationship with my Senior Sister?”

Wang Guofeng was briefly dumbfounded by her logic before he smiled. “Silly girl, if Ruochen and I really had a firm relationship, why would she leave me? This was initially an impossible relationship, and I realised that you are my true love after she ended it. Right now, I don’t hold anything against her; on the contrary, I even feel that I should thank her.”

Mo Sun’er was completely touched by Wang Guofeng as the latter looked at her with affection. Looking at his face, she was gradually being captivated and her heart started to race. Even her skin was basking in a blessing at this moment.

So it was true that the true love of her Senior Brother wasn’t her Senior Sister, but her.

“No!” Mo Sun’er shook her head and bit on her rosy lips. “We can’t do this. My master and Senior Sister won’t agree to it!”

“Sun’er, you have to bulk up your courage and tell me, do you like me?” Wang Guofeng asked as his tone turned firm.

“I do… but…” Mo Sun’er knitted her brows as she hesitated.

“It’s good, then!” Wang Guofeng placed his finger on her lips and smiled. “I will handle your Senior Sister. You master definitely won’t refuse us since, in this way, the crack between the Medical Dao Sect and Aqua Cloud Stream will be restored.”

As a smile appeared on Mo Sun’er’s lips, she replied, “But you have to promise me something. You can’t reveal this matter until I’ve found an appropriate time to tell my master and Senior Sister about it!”

Embracing Mo Sun’er, Wang Guofeng smiled. “Alright, I’ll promise you as long as you stop crying. You’ve always been a cry baby since young, and it’s heartbreaking!”

Feeling the warmth from Wang Guofeng’s chest, a smile crept on Mo Sun’er’s face as she finally felt a lock open in her heart.

“Why don’t you stay here for the night?” Wang Guofeng softly suggested.

“No way!” Mo Sun’er’s expression suddenly became nervous. Who knows what would happen when a man and woman share a room? She might have feelings for Wang Guofeng, but she wouldn’t take that step unless she obtained the approval from her Senior Sister and master. She might be young, but she had her bottom line.

“Don’t misunderstand! I will get two rooms and you can stay here! It’s not early now, and I’ll be worried for you if you go back alone!” Wang Guofeng immediately found an excuse after his probing.

“Senior Brother, you look down on me too much! I’m not a kid, I can just flag a cab. There’s no need for you to send me back. I’ll send you a message when I reach the dorm.” Mo Sun’er smiled.

Letting out a sigh, Wang Guofeng smiled as well. “Alright. We’ll just chat a little more before you return. It’s getting late now!”

“Alright!” Mo Sun’er looked at Wang Guofeng and felt that it was too much of a pity for her Senior Sister. How could she give up on such a gentleman? But thinking in another manner, this could be considered her fate that Wang Guofeng was her destined prince.

“There’s something that I would like to ask you!” Wang Guofeng took a sip of red wine.

“What is it?” Mo Sun’er nodded as her expression turned solemn.

“Have you ever thought of becoming the Sect Master of the Aqua Cloud Stream?” Wang Guofeng asked with a smile that even looked as if he was joking around.

“I’ve never thought about it!” Mo Sun’er replied with her brows knitted. “Under Senior Sister’s management, the sect has been developing well. Everyone trusts and support her, that includes me.”

Shaking his head, Wang Guofeng sighed, “Ruochen does have some talent in management, but she’s lacking in potential compared to you when it comes to medical skills. The Aqua Cloud Stream is a prestigious TCM sect, and the Sect Master must be talented in medical skills. I feel that you can replace her in just a few years!”

Looking at Wang Guofeng in a complicated light, Mo Sun’er lowered her voice, “Senior Brother, I’ve really never thought about it!”

“Alright, I’m just asking casually.” Wang Guofeng was slowly trying to brainwash Mo Sun’er. To subdue her, he would have to remove the two barriers in her mind, her master and Senior Sister.

Caressing Mo Sun’er’s cheeks, Wang Guofeng gently said, “Sun’er, you’ve always had a talent in this, and I believe that you can soar into the sky like me!”

A smile plastered on Mo Sun’er’s lips as she replied, “Since you’re so encouraging, then I have to work hard!”

Lowering his head, Wang Guofeng looked at Mo Sun’er’s face and felt her heart throbbing. Mo Sun’er also had a premonition as she slowly closed her eyes.

When Wang Guofeng slowly approached Mo Sun’er, a manly scent blew onto Mo Sun’er’s face that made her heart itch.

This was her first kiss, and was she really giving it to the man she likes today?

All of a sudden, the doorbell rang while their lips were only a centimeter away.

Mo Sun’er opened her eyes and she burst into laughter. With the romantic atmosphere ruined, Wang Guofeng also found it hard to kiss her. In his heart, a myriad grass mud horse galloped and he cursed that person who ruined his plan.

When he opened the door, fuming in anger, he knitted his brows when he saw the visitor and raged, “What are you doing here?”

The visitor was Su Tao, who flung his nose as he faintly responded, “I’m here to pick someone up!”

“What a joke!” Wang Guofeng became unhappy as he asked, “Based on what are you here to pick someone up? Do you have that qualification?”

“Ruochen sent Mo Sun’er to the Three Flavour Hall, and I have the qualifications to be responsible for her!” Su Tao’s expression calmed down as he declared. “I’ll count to three, and if you don’t get out of my way, don’t blame me for being impolite!”

“I’ll get out of the way, but I can’t guarantee that Sun’er will leave with you!” Wang Guofeng knew that Su Tao was skilled, and he would definitely suffer if he fought. Not to mention that this was Hanzhou City, Su Tao’s territory.

Mo Sun’er came to the entrance and glanced at Su Tao, “I already intended to leave, is there a need for such an action?”

Letting out a sigh, Su Tao responded, “Society is complicated, and human hearts are vile. I’m afraid that you might be too kind and got fooled by a baddie.”

“Baddie? Are you referring to yourself?” Mo Sun’er mocked. “I’ve known Senior Brother longer than I’ve known you.”

“The length of time doesn’t mean getting to know a person fully. Furthermore, people change, and the kindness you met yesterday might hold an ill motive for you today.” Su Tao patiently explained since Mo Sun’er was too pure. Wang Guofeng was no longer the man he used to be and after experiencing failure, Wang Guofeng has fallen deeper to collude with Bai Fan. He was worried that Wang Guofeng might use Mo Sun’er as part of his plans to strike the Three Flavour Hall!

“Shut your mouth!” Mo Sun’er flew into a rage. “What has my matters got to do with you?!”

“Of course it does, and I have already told you the reason. I don’t want to repeat myself, and if it wasn’t for Ruochen, do you think that I would care about you?” Su Tao continued to provoke Mo Sun’er since rage could cause someone to lose their rationale. “If you don’t come with me, I’ll give a call to your Senior Sister now and tell her that her beloved Junior Sister is messing around with her ex-fiancé in the middle of the night!”

When a person loses their rationale, they would be controlled by their hormones and they could be easily manipulated.

“You’re insane!” Mo Sun’er’s lips trembled. Su Tao grasped onto her fatal point. Turning around, she went to retrieve her purse before leaving for the lift.

Turning to look at Wang Guofeng, Su Tao smiled. “I’m sorry, but it turns out that she’s coming with me!”

Looking at the pleased smile on Su Tao’s face, Wang Guofeng’s countenance turned dark before he slammed the door.
