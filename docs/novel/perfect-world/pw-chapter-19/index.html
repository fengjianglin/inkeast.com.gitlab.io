Chapter 19 - Battle for Supremacy

The Green Scaled Eagle released a sideways glance. The chilly and intimidating scales on its body flickered with cold radiance, as if they were polished from metal. Divine splendor was being released from its eyes as it overlook the scene below. 

“Aunt Green, hurry! There’s not enough time.” The little guy on the mountain peak waved his little hands while shouting loudly. 

Violent winds whistled as the Green Scaled Eagle rapidly descended. When its wings spread out, it reached a length of roughly fifty or sixty meters across. Like a black fog pressing downwards, it cast a terrifying large shadow. 

With a whoosh sound, Shi Hao jumped five or six meters into the air, landing on the back of the eagle. He immediately felt a hard surface. Every single one of her scales was as cold and hard as metal.

“Little guy, don’t put yourself in danger!” The village chief yelled loudly. Everything happened too quickly, and he didn’t even have time to stop him before Shi Hao jumped up. 

“Grandpa Chief, don’t worry, we won’t place ourselves in danger. We are only going to take action when there is a chance.” Shi Hao waved his little hands, indicating that there was no need to worry.

“Little guy, you have to be careful! “ Shi Linghu yelled out. He knew that he couldn’t stop the little guy from going and could only loudly warn him.

“Uncle, I know. Grandpa chief, you guys go back first. This place is too dangerous. There are too many clans moving around, so it’ll be hard to find a good chance.”

The villagers became silent. This was the truth. Right now, even if all of the robust men got together, they would still be caught in the bloodbath of hundreds of crazy and vicious beasts.

“We’re leaving!” Chief Shi Yunfeng waved his hand and gave out this order. If they did not leave now, they might bring a great disaster onto themselves. 

“Little guy you have to be careful!” The villagers all shouted together to warn him. 

“I know, Grandpa Chief, you guys be careful too.”

The Green Scaled Eagle turned around, and then its body then departed from the mountain peak for the distance. Its speed was extremely fast, bringing about deafening winds. When these winds scrapped against one’s face, it would bring about stinging pain, making it almost impossible to open their eyes. The little guy laid flat on its back, his two little hands tightly grabbing the scales that shone with cold luster. He squinted his eyes as he watched the world below. 

“Aunt Green Scaled Eagle, be careful, there are many vicious beasts down there.”

The Green Scaled Eagle was exceptionally fast. With a flap of its wings, it quickly crossed over from one mountain peak to the other. The forest below them began to collapse. Enormous trees were snapping apart. Broken branches and withered leaves flew everywhere.

Several powerful and vicious beasts were tearing at each other. There were over a hundred vicious birds beating their wings in the air as well. Nothing was left unharmed, and even the mountain boulders were crushed apart. It was absolute chaos. 

The beasts roared, and the cries of birds echoed throughout the mountains. Blood splashed all over the ground, dyeing the mountains a deep red. There were many enormous beasts that collapsed. Their corpses were trampled on until they turned into bloody paste. This area was a bloody mess!

Terrifying creatures crowded together in front of the collapsed mountain, fighting madly against each other. Blood splashed several dozen feet into the air. They all pushed and shoved about, trying to dig out the enormous stones to find the Suan Ni’s corpse. 

There was a striped lynx that was several meters long. The powerful creature had a horn on its head. This bloodthirsty and crazy lynx could tear apart the body of an enormous beast with a single attack. The lynx’s two claws were incomparably sharp as well. Each time cold light flashed past, there would inevitably be blood that splashed outwards. 

AOOO…

An especially large Kui released a thunderous roar. Its huge body measured an enormous twenty meters, making it look like a small mountain. Its weapon were the powerful sound waves it could release, and wherever it rippled past, ferocious beasts were knocked out and blood was spilt. 

Peng

An eight or nine meter long silver pangolin with a large and sharp horn on its head appeared. It was incredibly powerful, and whenever it leapt up, the mountain rock below him would collapse. It directly made its way over towards that mountain. 

…

There were too many different species. These were powerful descendents, because if they didn’t have enough power, they wouldn’t have dared to come here. They were all intelligent races. 

The huge beasts fought for supremacy. A group of tyrannical creatures violently clashed. They all wanted to obtain the Suan Ni’s corpse, because after they devoured it, it would give them the power to become the overlord of the mountain forest. 

Hu!

A mighty gust of wind whistled past as the Green Scaled Eagle quickly rushed over. With both of its huge metallic claws stretched forth, it grabbed straight for the eight or nine meter long silver pangolin. The reason the Green Scaled Eagle went for this target was because the pangolin was likely to be the first to obtain the Suan Ni’s corpse. Half of its body had already been submerged within the rocky mountain. 

Pu. Even though the pangolin’s skin was as hard as iron, it was still penetrated by the Green Scaled Eagle’s cold and flickering claws. Blood splashed outwards. 

An angry roar rang throughout the mountains. The silver pangolin went mad. It frantically smashed its tail about, and at the same time, it bore through the pile of rocks. While standing on its hind legs, it pierced its two meter long horn towards the Green Scaled Eagle’s chest. 

However, the Green Scaled Eagle was a creature that had even forced Stone Village’s people to use their ancestral artifacts. One must know that these were precious artifacts made from the kings of archaic descendants, and these treasures had been passed down from generation to generation.

Both its wings flapped in the sky, and it rose up in a flash. Of course, its two claws did not let go. It continued to hold the pangolin by its tail as it rapidly ascended into the clouds.

While this was going on, deafening winds blew about. The pangolin’s struggles were completely useless. Without the support of the ground below it, it couldn’t inflict any damage to this vicious bird. 

Suddenly, after ascending to the clouds, the Green Scaled Eagle released its huge claws. The pangolin dropped rapidly from the sky like a streak of silver light.

Bang!

A miserable screech rang throughout the mountains. Soon after, smoke and dust rushed into the skies. That eight or nine meter long huge pangolin was smashed down, breaking the bones and tendons of many powerful creatures. Then, it turned into a bloody paste itself. It wasn’t powerful enough, no matter how hard its body was. 

“Big aunt, you’re so strong!” The little guy gasped in admiration.

The Green Scaled Eagle dove down towards the mountain again. It was a predator at the top of the food chain in the surrounding mountain region, so it naturally looked down on these beasts. 

Weng! This time, it didn’t use the strength of its physical body and instead spat out a streak of azure moonlight. There were a few differences between this attack and the little guy’s silver moon. The diameter of this beam was two meters long, and it was aimed straight at the twenty meter long Kui.

With the activation of this ancestral precious technique, the area was suddenly thrown into chaos. Many vicious birds and fierce beasts became terrified and fled towards all directions. However, there were also those that were especially ferocious. They became even more blood-thirsty, throwing themselves forward to get rid of this frightening new contender first. 

Pu

The blue moon shone brilliantly. It was incomparably sharp, directly hacking off the Kui beast’s massive head. Blood rushed more than ten meters into the air. Its enormous headless body collapsed with a tremendous rumbling sound. The ground shook greatly, and blood flowed like a small river. 

Meow!

A sound that made people’s head shiver sounded. It came from that huge several meter long leopard that was hiding behind a mountain boulder that was on relatively higher ground. It fiercely leapt outwards, jumping towards the Green Scaled Eagle from the back. The large black horn pierced towards the back of the Green Scaled Eagle’s head, and its sharp, half-meter long claws directly tore towards the eagle’s back. 

This was a perfect ambush, and it really was about to succeed. It was likely that not even the steel-like scales could defend against these attacks, because this was after all a lynx descendant that carried the blood of an ancient beast within it. 

At the same time, the sound of wings flapping could be heard. Seven or eight vicious birds descended from all directions. They all grabbed towards the Green Scaled Eagle, because they felt that it was the biggest threat. They acted together to eliminate this overlord of the sky.

A hazy light emerged, and a silver moon rose. Symbols flickered about. The lunar palace could be seen, accompanied by ancient trees. The moon disk released precious splendor, and with a weng sound, it sliced through the air. The lynx’s horn was broken, and its head was hacked apart. A miserable cry sounded as its body dropped. 

“Auntie, don’t worry. Leave the backside to me.” Little Shi Hao spoke with his immature voice. 

The Green Scale Eagle released a loud shriek. It moved its wings and swept through the other vicious birds. Scales and feathers swirled in the air, and blood flowed continuously. It tore apart five or six large vicious birds, some of which were even larger than itself. However, they were all killed purely by the Green Scaled Eagle’s powerful physical strength. 

This was the descendant of the archaic demonic bird. Without using precious techniques and only with its physical body, it could sweep through the mountain forest with its strength. 

The wind whistled. When the Green Scaled Eagle rushed downwards this time, most of the other fierce beasts moved out of the way, not wanting to provoke it. The eagle’s attacks were truly tyrannical.

“Auntie, let’s dig out these mountain rocks. The Suan Ni is just under it,” said the little guy. 

The Green Scaled Eagle landed, and with just a swipe, it immediately split apart a huge boulder weighing a thousand jin. With its wings spread, it swept them flying with hu sounds. It began to dig quickly, because this place was extremely dangerous. Not even creatures as powerful as itself dared to stay for too long. 

Rubble flew through the air, and soon, a deep hole was dug. Suddenly, purple light flashed outwards. A shining whip thrashed out directly towards the Green Scaled Eagle. 

Qiang!

The purple light whip landed on the Green Scaled Eagle’s left wing, causing sparks to fly out from the collision. The vicious bird released a cry of anger from the pain it was feeling from its metallic wings. 

“Ya, it’s a purple snake!” The little guy cried out in alarm.

This snake was too fast to react to. After attacking, it quickly retreated again. It was only the thickness of an adult’s thigh, and its length was about six to seven meters. Compared to the enormous beasts, it appeared rather fine and graceful.

However, it was extremely strong. The purple scales covering its body flickered with radiance and flowed with light. It was extremely sturdy, with a forceful bite, it even crushed the Green Scaled Eagle’s scales that were even harder than iron. 

In addition, there was black blood dripping from the Green Scaled Eagle’s wound. It was clear that it had been poisoned. 

Pu!

The archaic demonic bird’s descendant’s movements were straightforward as well. It spat out a small crescent moon from its mouth, and with a pu sound, several jin of scales and flesh were sliced off. Blood immediately splashed outwards. 

“Auntie!” The little guy was shocked.

No one could have thought that such a terrifying snake hid within the mountains. It was clearly extremely powerful, as it dared to fight against the green vicious bird. 

The Green Scaled Eagle cried out. Symbols flickered around its wound, and the bleeding was quickly stopped, stabilizing the injury. Then, its eyes released two streaks of cold electricity as it stared at the large snake that flowed with purple golden radiance.

Hissss….

The snake was rather perceptive and possessed high levels of intelligence. It confronted the Green Scaled Eagle. 

Moonlight appeared, and light scattered downwards. The Green Scaled Eagle activated its precious technique, sending the moon hacking towards the purple golden reptile. The sound of compressed air erupted with wuwu sounds. 

What was quite astonishing was that this purple golden snake was extremely fast. It raised its body upwards, and with a spring of its tail, it quickly flew more than twenty meters outwards, avoiding this attack. 

The Green Scaled Eagle brandished its wings and went in for the kill. It opened its beak, and once again, the blue moonlight appeared, this time even more lustrous as it whirled towards the violet snake.

Dang!

It finally hit its mark. However, the purple golden snake actually wasn’t sliced in half, only having a large amount of its scales hacked off and causing bits of blood to flow out. 

It struggled around in pain. Then, it quickly leapt outwards, retreating over twenty meters away.

Chi

The little guy took action. A pure and holy silver moon disk appeared in his right hand. A myriad of multicolored light erupted, and auspicious energy rose. With a chi sound, it flew outwards, slicing down right on that strange snake’s injury. 

Many scales had already separated from the snake’s body, causing its defensive capabilities to sharply decline. Bloody light immediately splashed outwards, however this strange snake still wasn’t hacked apart. Shi Hao’s attack only managed to make a thumb length cut. This snake’s flesh and bones were extremely tough, allowing it to stubbornly block these attacks. 

The Green Scaled Eagle’s gaze was ice-cold. As long as it didn’t enter the mountain range depths, it would rarely be injured, yet today, it actually was actually hurt like this. It naturally didn’t want to spare the snake and chased in pursuit. 

Suddenly, a bloody radiance shot over from the side. It reached two meters in length, and dazzling scarlet multicolored light flickered about around it. The assault was extremely sudden and impossible to defend against effectively as it landed on the Green Scaled Eagle’s back. 

This was a blood sable that was over two meters long. Its entire body seemed like it was made out of sparkling red agate, and it also had a pair of scarlet wings. Despite its body seeming rather small when compared to the enormous beasts here, it was actually especially powerful. 

It had extraordinary strength, and its movements were as fast as lightning. When it rushed past, it almost tore open the little guy’s chest and stomach. It then directly trampled on his chest before taking into the sky, and then with a slash, it carved a half meter long gash along the Green Scaled Eagle’s neck. 

The little guy released a soft shout. The silver moon reappeared, forcing the blood sable back. 

The vicious bird trembled. Its entire body radiated light, and it now thoroughly erupted with rage. It had suffered injuries again and again, cause it to seeth with anger. It began to chase after those two unusual beasts. 

The violet snake and the blood sable’s teamwork was impeccable, as if practiced. This left the little guy amazed. These creatures were actually so intelligent. 

Hong!

A silver alligator rushed over from the lake. When it arrived, it immediately smashed apart a mountain boulder. 

At the same time, seven or eight other tyrannical creatures appeared. They were far more powerful than ordinary beasts, and these creatures formed a terrifying confrontation. 

In the end, as if they all came to a tacit understanding, they stopped their attacks and began to quickly dig up the mountain rocks. They were going to wait until the Suan Ni’s precious body was visible before they fought again. 

The Green Scaled Eagle also stopped its pursuit. Its body was covered with bloodstains. Its terrifying green scales flickered with cold light. 

“Aunt Green, are you okay?” The little guy began to feel worried. There were several hundred enormous beasts here, and there were quite a few who weren’t weaker than the Green Scaled Eagle. 

Boom!

The silver alligator seemed to have gone mad as it continuously tore apart the enormous rocks. Then, suddenly, a wisp of golden multicolored light blossomed, as if a small sun was rising and shining between the rocks. 

It was a robust and powerful leg that was as thick of a pillar and covered in golden beast fur. It was absolutely dazzling, as if it was cast from gold. Its ferocious aura overflowed into the heavens!

The precious body of the archaic descendant Suan Ni had finally appeared. Primordial symbols flickered about brilliantly on its body. All of the creatures were greatly affected by this sight. Their eyes blazed with passion, and all of them threw themselves forward.
