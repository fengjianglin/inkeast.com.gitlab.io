Chapter 1110 - Preparations For All Out War

Of course, Su Lan and Chen Nuo still felt some worry. If this fella ended up starting a commotion, provoking Princess Yao Yue’s rage, then things wouldn’t be too good.

This fella wouldn’t truly have these types of thoughts, right?

Along the way, the two of them were a bit skeptical, not having much faith in him. It was because no matter how they looked at it, this fella’s way of doing things was a bit ridiculous.

“I think it’s still better if we don’t go.” Su Lan said.

“We’re already almost there, so don’t get cold feet now. As Wang Xi’s follower, you all should figure out her thoughts. Being scared of the slightest thing like this isn’t good.” Shi Hao said.

The two of them shot him a look of disdain, always feeling like this fella wasn’t some good person. The more he showed this kind of ‘kindness’, the more he acted ‘sincerely’, the more they doubted him.

“Yi, we’ve arrived. This is it right?” Shi Hao said with a smile.

Su Lan and Chen Nuo felt helpless, because they already arrived before a divine cliff, precisely Qi Lin’s cave. This mountain was massive, releasing scarlet multicolored light. It was entirely bright red like cornelian.

“You two go and issue a challenge, tell Qi Lin that we came to issue a challenge.” Shi Hao said.

“Why do we have to go and challenge? I’m pretty sure we already said that you had to do things on your own.” Su Lan said angrily. 

Chen Nuo looked at him. Don’t tell me this fella really wanted to drag them with him into the water?

Su Lan and Chen Nuo exchanged a look, not saying anything, and then turning around to leave. They felt like staying by his side was not safe at all. Of course, they didn’t go too far, hiding in the distance to watch the situation.

Shi Hao didn’t stop them. He wandered about this sparkling red giant mountain. He discovered a cave, and then he walked forward, transmitting sound quite respectfully for a meeting.

When Qi Lin appeared and saw that it was him, she was first stunned, and then she felt alarm, asking, “Do you need something?”

“Young miss Qi Lin, are you not going to invite me into your cave for a cup of tea?” Shi Hao said with a smile.

Qi Lin was extremely alarmed, truly not daring to let Shi Hao in, fearing that he might shout out. Her actions previously were a good lesson to learn from, she definitely didn’t want to have the same favor returned.

This fella before her already had a bad reputation, so if she had some type of relationship with him, inviting him into her cave, even if nothing happened, it would be difficult to explain things.

“If you have anything to say, just say it here.” Qi Lin said, raising her sharp chin, putting on a haughty appearance.

“This isn’t a place for discussion.” Shi Hao said while rubbing his hands.

“Your reputation is quite bad, I do not dare invite you into my cave. If you have something, then just say it quickly.” Qi Lin raised her head as she spoke, not showing him any face.

“My bad reputation, wasn’t it all granted by you?” Shi Hao said.

“You… what are you trying to do?” Qi Lin was on guard, feeling a bit worried. She then said, “Don’t forget that you are only Wang Xi’s follower, a servant, not an official disciple. You don’t have the qualification to enter my cultivation ground.”

“Since you aren’t letting me enter your cave to plunder, I meant look around, then we’ll just settle things outside.” Shi Hao said with a dry cough.

“What? You want to enter my cave to rob me?!” Qi Lin widened her eyes, definitely not believing that he made a mistake just now. This fella was too daring, actually carrying these types of thoughts.

“Just come, I will challenge you here!” Shi Hao said, eyes becoming fierce. He muttered, “In a bit, I’ll enter the cave and move house!”

“You… really are daring. Do you even know what you are saying?” Qi Lin shouted.

“Of course I know. Doesn’t the academy encourage exchanging of pointers and challenges between disciples? I am just responding to their call.” Shi Hao calmly said.

“Who do you think you are? Not even an official disciple, you don’t have the qualifications!” Qi Lin felt a bit lacking in confidence, because she had heard that this fella wasn’t ordinary at all.

“If someone who isn’t even an official disciple can defeat you, how would you even have the shame to occupy this divine mountain? Hurry up and gift the cave to me already.” Shi Hao pressed forward.

He took action, his palm and fingers shining, lightning interweaving, forming purple-colored lightning spheres one after another. They were like stars, becoming more and more numerous, producing a miniature starry domain.

Hong!

Shi Hao struck out, the lightning seemingly forming the cosmos, profound and massive. Those stars were all produced from lightning, blasting towards that woman.

Qi Lin was alarmeds. The other person’s random strike already brought about this type of frightening scene, if he cultivated immortal energy, there was no way she could compare to him.

She quickly took action, directly activating immortal energy to protect herself and resist the lightning.

Following a numb feeling, Qi Lin moved sideways, her body greatly shaken, a trace of blood appearing at the corners of her lips.

“Do you know who I am? I am Princess Yao Yue’s bosom friend! If you take action rudely, you will bring about a great disaster onto yourself!” Qi Lin said.

At this stage, she was a bit fearful, worried that Shi Hao would go crazy and take her life.

“Princess Yao Yue’s best friend? Then do you know my identity? Right, you should understand that because you previously said that I am her man, now, this ‘man’ and ‘bosom friend’ have come to settle things!”

“You… stop acting randomly!” Qi Lin’s expression changed.

Chi!

Lightning shot out like rainbows, forming a starry stream, crashing down. The power became even greater.

Even though Qi Lin used immortal energy, there was still no way she was Shi Hao’s opponent. She was directly blasted until she spat out blood, suffering injuries that weren’t light.

“Let’s go, to your cave!”

In the distance, Su Lan and Chen Nuo were shocked, secretly sighing that his strength improved again, becoming more and more terrifying.

“What is he doing? Is he robbing her?!” Chen Nuo was shocked.

“Too lowly, completely looting this place!” Su Lan was stupefied.

In fact, Shi Hao even kindly invited the distant Su Lan and Chen Nuo in the distance to come with him and eat roast meat.

It was because he cooked Qi Lin’s mount, extremely savage, directly carrying out this outside of her cave.

“Do you know what you are doing? Princess Yao Yue will take full action against you!” Qi Lin threatened.

“Oh? Is that so? Hurry and have her look for Wang Xi then, waited for her a long time already. I only came here to seize an advantage.” Shi Hao spoke extremely irresponsibly.

“Hey, what are you randomly spouting? My clan’s young miss never intended to fight a decisive battle against Princess Yao Yue!” Su Lan was angered to the extreme, rushing over.

“It seems like you don’t understand, how can this type of thing be endured? My trip here was tacitly accepted by your young miss. The two fairies will inevitably have a full on battle!” Shi Hao said seriously.

“Is what you said true?!” Su Lan was extremely doubtful.

“Of course it’s true, but the battle might take place in Immortal Battlefield. It’s not easy for Wang Xi to take action right now, so I’m helping her.” Shi Hao said. 

“My phoenix blood!” Qi Lin cried out in alarm.

Right now, Shi Hao was not polite at all, searching through her body, including her bosom, groping all around, finding four bottles of phoenix blood.

“As Princess Yao Yue’s bosom friend, you have good stuff as expected. Is there any more?” Shi Hao laughed loudly.

“None, none! Stop randomly touching!” Qi Lin screamed.

Su Lan and Chen Nuo both stopped, no longer saying anything, because they suddenly felt like this unreliable fella’s way of doing things made them release a bit of pent-up resentment.

It was precisely because Qi Lin spoke randomly that lead to a series of trouble. Now that she was being dealt with, it really did match what the two of them desired.

Peng!

Qi Lin was thrown onto the ground, directly fainting.

This was a female cultivator, a genius who cultivated one strand of immortal energy, yet before Shi Hao, she was completely powerless, thrown on the ground like a scarecrow.

“Let’s go, lead me to where Feng Xingtian and Lu Tuo reside.” Shi Hao called over to Su Lan and Chen Nuo.

“Ah, what are you trying to do?” After these two asked this, they felt their scalps becoming numb. Was this fella really recruited into the academy?

“Its nothing, just going over to take a look. Your young miss said that once we entered Immortal Battlefield, there’ll be an all out battle! I’m going to help her take a look at her enemies, sizing up that battle’s result.” Shi Hao spoke calmly.

All out battle, this was the young miss’ attitude? They just felt like it was impossible, just this fella before them randomly making enemies.

“Let’s go, take a look!” Shi Hao said. When they passed by Princess Yao Yue’s pure land along the way, he only stopped briefly, not issuing a challenge.

Lu Tuo and Feng Xingtian’s caves were shrouded in mist and primal chaos, unable to see through from a single glance.

What allowed Su Lan and Chen Nuo to feel a bit more relieved was that this fella wasn’t arrogant enough to the point where he went to challenge those two great young supreme beings. When he arrived in front of their pure lands, he also just looked around a bit, and then left.

“I heard that Yuan Qing brought over a descendant of his into the academy, this is clearly against the rules. Where is he? Bring me over to give him a look too.” Shi Hao said, asking Su Lan and Chen Nuo if they knew anything about that person’s circumstances.

“He is called Yuan Feng, a genius who cultivated two strands of immortal energy. It was just because of a few matters that he missed out on heavenly deity institution’s disciple selection, which was why his elder personally brought him over.” Chen Nuo said. 

“Hey, what is your intention? The ones you’re provoking are all tough individuals, first Princess Yao Yue’s bosom friend, then Lu Tuo, Feng Xingtian and others, now wanting to deal with Yuan Qing, a supreme being’s descendant, don’t tell me you want to trigger the greatest chaotic battle?” Su Lan was worried, feeling that the situation wasn’t good.

“You’re thinking too much, I just want to scout out the enemies in the young miss’ place.” Shi Hao said with a smile.

“You shouldn’t speak nonsense, pushing everything onto the young miss. She definitely wouldn’t act like this, this is just the final preparations for your own plans.” Su Lan said.

Soon after, they arrived at a mountain region. The peaks were jade-green in color, immortal energy rising in spirals, as if they arrived in an immortal realm.

“Wu, this dao earth belongs to Yuan Feng…” Shi Hao said. He then saw a youngster whose gaze was brilliant like a blazing son, looking towards this direction.

That was precisely Yuan Feng. He stared at Shi Hao, eyes like an exceptional sword.

“Wu, we ran into each other just like this!” Shi Hao said to himself.
