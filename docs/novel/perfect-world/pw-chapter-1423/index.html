Chapter 1423 - Extreme Recklessness

Ka!

Nine thousand streaks of lightning hacked down at the same time, blasting Shi Hao until his entire body was jerking randomly, some areas even becoming badly mangled, bones visible.

The lightning in the Tenth Heaven was originally peaceful, even if it was going crazy because he was trying to seize lightning tribulation liquid, it shouldn’t be like this. All of this was because he wanted to bring the lightning pool itself away.

“Move!”

Shi Hao used his great divine abilities. His Sea of Reincarnation shone, moreover activating Kun Peng technique, Willow Deity methods, and other things, going all out to seize this ancient pool.

Everyone was stupefied, really not knowing what to say. Not long ago, they still saw him display heaven reaching divine might, forcing back all lightning tribulation, incredibly heroic.

But now, it actually became like this.

“Just now, Huang killed Wang Family and Jin Family’s elders, appearing so dashing and handsome, but now he… is stealing the lightning pool, something doesn’t seem right…” A beautiful young lady muttered quietly.

What something doesn’t seem right? They are basically two different people! Shi Hao had the appearance of a money grubber, carrying the lightning pool, not willing to let go no matter what, using all of his strength to move it.

“Hey, Huang, what are you doing? Can you not be like this? It really is a bit embarrassing.” The Lunar Jade Rabbit felt embarrassed for him. This was just too reckless.

“The Lightning Pool is formed from lightning, not real, only the lightning tribulation liquid is a real heavenly treasure produced in destruction, able to be brought away!” The Heavenly Horned Ant said, thinking that Shi Hao didn’t know this.

“I don’t need you all to tell me this, I know what I am doing.” Shi Hao remained unmoved. He was sure that this was a true lightning pool, because after all the different tests, he still felt like there was no mistake.

Of course, during this process, he didn’t waste any time, using the bone cauldron to collect Lightning Tribulation Liquid, fearing that he might end up with nothing if he couldn’t bring away the Lightning Tribulation Liquid.

Sure enough, he could only bring away half of the Lightning Tribulation Liquid, perhaps not even that much, just like before.

However, right at this time, the lightning pool was about to escape, rumbling as it moved.

Even though things were already like this, heavenly tribulation about to end, the lightning was still crazy, even more fierce than the great tribulation Shi Hao faced in the Nine Heavens Ten Earths, leaving him seriously injured.

Not even he himself thought that at the crucial point, he originally should’ve made it through, but in the end, he had his flesh hacked apart, entire body charred black.

“Get up!” Shi Hao shouted, still trying his hardest.

Then, he felt the void shake, great cracks extending outwards. Moreover, the lightning radiance became much heavier, the entire heavenly tribulation about to enter the cracks, about to disappear here.

“Seal!”

Shi Hao shouted, using everything he had. Six Dao Reincarnations Heavenly Art was activated, Imperishable Scripture sounding. The True Primordial Record operated, at the same time, his Sea of Reincarnation shone, also suppressing this pool.

At this time, the lightning pool dragged him along, about to enter the great crack in the heavens above.

“No! Hurry and stop! If you enter, you will undoubtedly die!” Cao Yusheng shouted loudly.

Many people were stupefied, and then they were horrified.

It was because according to rumors. If they really found the source of lightning tribulation, then it would inevitably result in the eradication of body and spirit, flesh dying, dao extinguished, there has never been an exception.

Ever since ancient times, there had never been anyone who went to the land of origin and came back alive.

Lightning tribulation wasn’t produced by rain clouds, normally always abruptly descending from the sky dome. However, there would always be a source. Right now, Shi Hao was about to enter, it naturally made others turn pale from shock.

“Stinky brat, get back here!” Right at this time, even Great Elder Meng Tianzheng was startled. He felt a wave of coldness, fearing that Shi Hao might die from this risk.

This brat was too daring! Many older generation figures rushed over. When they saw this scene, all of them were stupefied. They never saw someone with such nerves!

What was this fella trying to do? Was seizing half a pool of Lightning Tribulation Liquid not greedy enough? He actually wanted to take this little pool too! The older generation figures all felt a bit dizzy. They looked at him, really not knowing how to evaluate him.

Was this a heaven warping genius, or was it a shameless and reckless brat?

“Let me try one last time!” Shi Hao replied, still not giving up.

In the heavenly dome, a few strange cracks appeared. Lightning radiance gathered inside. At this moment, everyone was a bit frightened, at the same time shocked.

This had never happened before, or to be more precise, no one had ever seen it before, only heard legends. Heavenly tribulation actually really did have a source!

In the past, everyone only felt that heavenly tribulation suddenly disappeared, but now, they discovered that in reality, it gathered in a mysterious land!

“Stinky brat, do you not want your life anymore? Stop right now!” Great Elder berated.

Shi Hao was carrying out the final test, but as a result, the lightning tribulation that was about to scatter hid the sky and covered the earth again, everything blasting towards him.

In the heavenly dome above, those strange cracks were accompanied by all types of brilliance, quite striking in the darkness, looking even more real. The cracks were slowly closing.

“Stay!”

Shi Hao roared, using everything he had. His Sea of Reincarnation flourished with radiance, immediately collecting the lightning pool. He did all of this to make the ancient pool stay behind so he could research it.

En?

Everyone was stupefied. He really succeeded, stopping that square foot lightning pool? It actually disappeared, entering his abdomen!

However, it was as if he poked a hornet nest. In the darkness, the cracks accompanied by brilliant light immediately released thousands to tens of thousands of streaks of lightning, all of them terrifying beyond compare.

Immediately afterwards, Shi Hao had nowhere to hide. He was blasted until his body jerked about, hair standing on end, blasted black, and then falling down.

As for his body, it was directly blasted through by many streaks of lightning. Terrifying bloody holes appeared one after another, a ghastly sight. It was just too terrifying.

Even his heart could be seen through the large hole, about to be scorched black. This lightning was unimaginable, chaotic energy crashing down, submerging this place.

“Have you lost it?!” Great Elder felt helpless, feeling alarm, worried that Shi Hao would immediately die.

It was because this lightning tribulation was clearly more terrifying than before, even more resplendent, simply about to destroy all living things, even looking like it was going to spread.

This touched upon heaven’s anger. Huang forcefully seized the lightning pool, triggering a great disaster, lightning continuously hacking down.

Shi Hao grimaced in pain, resisting there. However, he was blasted until his body was scorched black, about to fall apart. He suffered the greatest danger in his entire life.

Vaguely, the Immortal Killing Stage condensed again, extremely indistinct, faintly discernible, about to hack down on him again, kill him here!

Everyone was speechless. This person really was too good at provoking trouble!

Since the ancient times, just how many people triggered the appearance of the Immortal Killing Stage? However, in one day, it actually appeared twice in a single lightning tribulation. The god of longevity truly didn’t want him to live a long life!

Shi Hao’s only hope was that even though lightning continuously hacked down, those cracks were still closing, not stopping.

Those cracks became smaller and smaller, completely closing soon after.

However, the lightning seemed to have a grudge against him, locking onto him, frantically gushing out. In the end, it became even more intense, like water at the eye of the sea, frantically surging.

“No! I already went through the Immortal Killing Stage once, you don’t have to appear again! We can see each other again next time, actually, it’ll be better if we never meet again!” Shi Hao screamed.

It was because he saw the most dangerous thing was gradually taking form. The Immortal Killing Stage became a bit more real, that dark red blade about to become clearly visible.

Hong!

Lightning poured down like rain, hacking down with pilipala sounds, every single streak merging with primal chaos. It was too terrifying. Shi Hao was blasted until his body was badly mangled, bones even exposed, the scene too horrible to endure.

Even though he was smiling, joking around, his real situation really was dangerous.

Great Elder Meng Tianzheng was worried, but outsiders couldn’t interfere, unable to stop this. It was because if powerful experts took action, they might provoke terrifying lightning onto themselves.

“If you can’t hold on, then just return that pool!” Fatty Cao shouted.

“Isn’t it just a stupid pool? Hurry up and throw it away!” The Lunar Jade Rabbit and Heavenly Horned Ant shouted.

The Emperor Butterfly flapped its wings, expressing its agreement. Right now, one’s life was the most important, it wasn’t worth the risk.

Hong!

At the final crucial point, lightning blasted Shi Hao streak after streak, just too terrifying, to the extent where even the crown of his head split apart.

If it was anyone else, they would have long died.

Shi Hao displayed the Imperishable Scripture to the limit, moreover using the True Primordial Record’s power of turning the rotten to the miraculous to stop the lightning tribulation, resisting with everything he had.

His Sea of Reincarnation shone. After putting away the lightning pool, it continuously flickered about, but it just didn’t release the lightning pool, fully subduing it.

“You… really are going to take form?!” Shi Hao stared at the Immortal Killing Platform, looking at the dark red guillotine on it. He really was a bit terrified.

“En?” He raised his head, seeing that the final cracks were about to close as well. The lightning that forcefully squeezed out became fewer, the opening too small, not much able to come out anymore.

However, what did rush out was extremely fast and ferocious.

Qiang!

On the Immortal Killing Stage, that dark red guillotine was fully formed. It released cold light, carrying scarlet multicolored light, terrifying beyond compare, about to hack down on Shi Hao again.

This time, Shi Hao had no choice but to make a decision. Was he going to persevere on?

Suddenly, someone shouted, “The cracks closed, the time has been reached!”

At the same time, on the Immortal Killing Stage, the guillotine’s radiance was brilliant, scarlet multicolored light heaven overflowing. In the end, it rushed into the heavens, entering the cracks. It was because it was time, now withdrawing.

Kacha!

The great cracks closed, a large portion of the lightning cut off outside. Apart from this, the Immortal Killing Stage was also like this, breaking down, the guillotine leaving behind a streak of essence energy before dissipating into this world.

“Stay behind for me!”

Shi Hao roared. Under everyone’s shocked gaze, he opened his mouth, swallowing the remaining lightning radiance, wishing to use this to refine his body.

Moreover, at this time, his abdomen shone, releasing the lightning pool, using it to collect the remaining light left behind by the Immortal Killing Stage’s guillotine after it scattered.

He had to collect that light. Regardless of whether he used it to refine a sword core or refine it into his body, it would grant him unimaginable power. In the future, he could use it to cut through everything.

Right at this time, a group of elders threw themselves towards Shi Hao, every single one of their eyes red, their target the lightning pool.

It was because this thing was too rare, from past until now, no one had ever acted like this, forcefully making a lightning pool stay behind!

“Let me take a look! I want to see just what kind of thing this is! How could there be a real body left behind, actually not produced by lightning?!”

“Let me study it a bit first, see just what kind of shocking mysteries it has!”

The group of elders went crazy, almost running into each other, about to break out into a fight. They wanted to be the first to study it in their hands.

Compared to heaven-defying creatures like the Lightning Spirits, this lightning pool was even more enticing, making them temporarily forget about the Lightning Spirits. It was because from past until now, this was the first time someone forcefully made a lightning pool stay behind.
