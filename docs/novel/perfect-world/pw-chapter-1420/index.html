Chapter 1420 - Revisiting

Lightning Spirits, they were like worms, the length of chopsticks. If one didn’t examine them too carefully, they were just like snakes. The one who approached was dark red colored, entire body as if cast from blood-colored Immortal Told, giving one a cold and hard feeling.

Shi Hao didn’t recognize this type of creature, but he felt that this bug was extremely dangerous, not normal.

Otherwise, when the heavenly tribulation struck its body, it would have directly been blasted to bloody chunks, how could it still coldly watch him? This was an extremely frightening creature.

“You actually dare do this!” 

Shi Hao’s breathing was hurried, inwardly shocked and furious. This was just too sinister, a strange creature appearing when he was crossing tribulation, wishing to harm him, it really was sinister.

The lightning ocean surged, lightning shooting out streak after streak. The noises here were earsplitting, normal people unable to look through it at all, unable to see the situation near the lightning pool.

It was because this place was full of electrical arcs, extremely dazzling, one would feel a stinging pain even if Heavenly Eyes were used.

However, this didn’t stop them from realizing that something happened, that there was an issue with Shi Hao.

“What is going on, Huang seems to be angry?”

Everyone was shocked. The main reason was that the Lightning Spirit was too small, and it was like a streak of red electricity itself. It wasn’t easy to detect as it moved about.

Right now, it was like a snake, raising its head, opening its mouth, revealing sharp teeth. Lightning flashed through its eyes, directly swallowing up the lightning.

This lightning tribulation couldn’t harm it, it devoured the electrical light like food, incredibly greedy.

Of course, what it wanted the most was still the simple and ancient pool where Shi Hao was, staring at it. As a Lightning Spirit, it understood well that this was the greatest natural luck.

Chi!

This dark red lightning moved, attacking. It directly rushed at Shi Hao, vicious and decisive.

Originally, it was dark red colored like metal, rather ordinary, not drawing everyone’s attention. However, when it truly attacked, everything changed.

It was incomparably resplendent, like scarlet red divine diamond, bright red and replete, releasing endless streaks of scarlet multicolored light, too brilliant. Its entire body was perfectly straight, like a chopstick length immortal spear, shooting at Shi Hao like a streak of lightning!

Xiu!

It was too fast, instantly piercing through the void. It arrived at the lightning pool’s side in the blink of an eye, wishing to enter the space between Shi Hao’s brows, kill him in one strike, penetrate his forehead and destroy his primordial spirit.

It was clear that this Lightning Spirit was extremely brutal. When killing, it was simple and direct.

Shi Hao was cold, his reaction extremely fast. With a turn of his head, he operated the Imperishable Scripture, two fingers shining, becoming a light golden color like Flood Dragon shears as they pressed towards that Lightning Spirit.

However, there was a streak of light that was even faster. In the heavenly dome above, a streak of lightning shot straight down. It wasn’t that thick, but it was extremely shocking, carrying primal chaos energy, scarlet red in color.

Dang!

At this moment, sparks flew in all directions. The lightning struck that creature, making its perfectly straight body tremble, immediately flying out, unable to remain like a blood-colored immortal spear anymore.

The Lightning Spirit was greatly alarmed, releasing low is is roaring noises. It was because what it was least afraid of was lightning, heavenly tribulation normally couldn’t injure it, yet now, its body was bleeding, injured.

The lightning pool was impassable!

Chi!

Immediately afterwards, this dark red Lightning Spirit moved again, still shooting straight out, wishing to enter the lightning pool, seize the Lightning Tribulation Liquid and kill Shi Hao.

Dang!

It was another streak of lightning radiance. This time, it was silvery-white in color, accompanied by chaotic energy. It was even more terrifying, blasting the Lightning Spirit until it flipped out. Blood splashed in all directions from its body, divine skin damaged, burning it until it was charred black.

This left it horrified. It had never suffered so greatly in heavenly tribulation before, actually becoming seriously injured.

This was something inconceivable. It actually couldn’t approach the lightning pool! Even if Shi Hao himself didn’t do anything, it still couldn’t approach that place.

“What is that thing? It can actually move in lightning, resist the most powerful heavenly tribulation? It is too shocking, what kind of monster is this? Is it comparable to Huang?”

At this moment, everyone finally discovered something strange, seeing that creature that wasn’t that large. All of them were extremely alarmed. Was that thing a living creature or something produced by lightning? It actually competed with Shi Hao over the lightning pool.

Hong!

In the sky, streak after streak of lightning crashed down, blasting the Lightning Spirit’s body. It was targeted, suffering attacks.

It was because Shi Hao could already be considered to have completed tribulation, now currently obtaining natural luck, already obtaining the acknowledgment of heavenly tribulation.

The Lightning Spirit suddenly rushed over murderously, wishing to seize the lightning pool, so it naturally suffered fierce attacks. The heavenly tribulation suppressed this intruder on the spot.

At this moment, the Lightning Spirit panicked, because this heavenly tribulation could actually harm it, make it continuously bleed. If this continued, it might die here.

In the distance, Wang Xi’s grandfather was stupefied. Not even the Lightning Spirit could harm Shi Hao?

Moreover, from the Lightning Spirit’s reaction, he understood how terrifying this heavenly tribulation was. Not even a Lightning Spirit could handle it, yet Huang actually made it through!

Just how freakish was this? Huang’s resistance to lightning was even more ridiculous than a Lightning Spirit!

“Is there no more heavenly reasoning? How come it didn’t directly hack him to death?!” Wang Xi’s grandfather said to himself.

“I was also hoping that he could directly die here.” Jin Zhan’s grandfather nodded.

Unfortunately, they were destined to not have their wishes fulfilled. Right now, Shi Hao stood up, walking out from the lightning pool, pressing towards the Lightning Spirit.

This creature left him extremely shocked. No one understood better than him how powerful this heavenly tribulation was, but this snake-like creature could actually resist it, not dying even now.

Xiu!

When the Lightning Spirit saw Shi Hao leave, its eyes shone with a vicious light. It felt like without the lightning pool’s protection, it could kill this person. It turned into a blood spear again, shooting out.

Dang!

It collided with Shi Hao’s palm. The noise was earsplitting, as if two pieces of divine steel collided, leaving Shi Hao shocked. This bug was even more powerful than he had imagined.

As for this Lightning Spirit, it was somewhat stupefied. Its previously unstoppable self was actually struck until it was confused and disoriented, becoming a bit dizzy.

Shi Hao’s eyes became cold. The Imperishable Scripture supported him, right hand reaching out as if he was catching a dragon.

The dark red Lightning Spirit was angry, continuously crying out, calling over its companions for help.

However, at this moment, Shi Hao was furious, wishing to immediately eliminate it, not treating it like a worm, but more like a true enemy, attacking at full force.

Dazzling metal sparks flew about. Shi Hao continuously took action, attacking fiercely several times, finally suppressing this bug. His hands were like scissors, almost cutting this Lightning Spirit in half.

In the distance, Wang Family’s people were petrified. Just how much of a monster was this youngster? The Lightning Spirit was subdued this easily?

Wang Family’s higher level all knew just how much care was used to nurture the Lightning Spirits to this state. It was heaven-defying, yet in the end, it became a captive.

This Lightning Spirit didn’t only seize one person’s natural luck. If it wanted to become stronger, then it needed heavenly tribulation, needed the precious liquid in the lightning pool!

In the past, it had seized others’ dao fruits!

Now, Shi Hao just happened to be facing a great tribulation, able to rise into the Self Severing Realm at any time. If the Lightning Spirits seized his natural luck, then the ones who would advance would be these three divine bugs.

“No, the Lightning Spirits cannot be allowed to fall into his hands!” Wang Family’s people clenched their teeth. It was because the Lightning Spirits were too precious, there might only be these three in the entire world. They were priceless!

If they really were nurtured, then they would be unmatched creatures!

It could be said that these three bugs would most likely become Bug Emperors.

The dark red Lightning Spirit screamed endlessly, struggling intensely.

Hong!

Suddenly, world shocking lightning erupted. In the distance, two figures flashed, one gold, one silver, moreover bringing with them an endless great army as they slaughtered their way forward.

This left everyone shocked. There were two strange bugs that appeared, actually ordering around the lightning creatures in the heavenly tribulation as they charged forward, attacking Shi Hao.

Everyone became speechless. This was too strange, wasn’t the tribulation complete? Why did this stuff end up appearing?

They still didn’t know that these Lightning Spirits were living creatures, not something produced by lightning. It was only because none of them recognized these as Lightning Spirits!

There were also many people who suspected that might be a big problem!

Hong!

Shi Hao charged forward boldly. Now that his body had recovered, returning to its peak, he naturally didn’t fear those creatures he had already defeated before. It was because it was precisely the Nine Heavens Ten Earths he slaughtered through, fighting intensely with these lightning formed creatures before.

As long as there were no emperor races youth who took action, the lightning tribulation didn’t pose any problems for him.

“The two of you, get over here! You want to escape? No way!” Shi Hao shouted.

He took action fiercely, pressing towards the two Lightning Spirits. As it turned out, the two creatures suffered greatly, not able to do anything to Shi Hao even after bringing great armies over, in the end running for their lives.

“Perfect, you all can lead the way!” Shi Hao said with a cold laugh.

He stirred on lightning radiance, following just behind them. No matter how fast the two divine bugs were, they still couldn’t shake off Shi Hao. He continuously pursued them.

Chi!

Shi Hao bathed in lightning radiance. With a single step, he crossed many li, instantly arriving before Wang Xi, Jin Zhan, and the others.

“Huang, what do you want to do?” Wang Xi released a light scoff.

“Get lost!” Shi Hao said coldly. He didn’t look at Wang Xi, instead staring at the elder at her side, feeling that these two Lightning Spirits were precisely on his body.

“Unbridled!” Jin Zhan shouted, taking a step out, reaching out a hand. It was surrounded by white mist, about to slap over.

“You, get lost too, or else I will kill you on the spot!” Shi Hao shouted coldly.
