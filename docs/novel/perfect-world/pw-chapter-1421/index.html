Chapter 1421 - Angry Slaughter

At this moment, Shi Hao was filled with rage, about to unleash a great slaughter!

When he learned that the two bugs were hiding behind Wang Xi’s grandfather, murderous rage overflowed into the heavens. As expected, this was done by Wang Family, actually harboring such vile motives, wishing to harm him to death while he was facing tribulation.

The enemy was already this despicable, there was nothing else to say. He naturally couldn’t hold back, now completely erupting with anger.

What Wang Xi, what Jin Zhan? He would directly kill whoever stood in his path. All those who dared stop him, he would directly kill.

He already raised his palm, starting to form imprints, about to directly take action against Wang Xi and Jin Zhan. Slaughtering geniuses, isn’t this what Wang Family liked to do? Then, he would just give a demonstration right here!

This was especially the case when Jin Zhan now took action against him, about to attack him, sending a hand slapping over, wishing to contend against him, so he acted even less politely!

“Zhan’er, withdraw for now!” Jin Zhan’s grandfather stepped up at the crucial moment, pushing him back, making his palm hit nothing, having no choice but to exercise restraint.

“Grandfather, why are you doing this?” Jin Zhan was unwilling to stop right here, his expression cold, insisting on attacking.

It was because no one dared speak these words in front of his face before. Just now, Huang actually didn’t treat him with any importance, directly stating for him to get lost, or else he would kill him.

Just how domineering was this? Forget about Jin Zhan, even everyone else was stupefied.

The glorious Jin Zhan, known as a heaven’s pride of a generation, was actually berated by another. Meanwhile, his grandfather stopped him, not allowing him to fight but ordering him to withdraw instead. This left everyone shocked!

“Shi Hao, what happened?” Cao Yusheng shouted. He naturally knew that something major happened, or else why would Shi Hao rush out from the lightning pool, directly rush towards Wang Family?

“They released a strange type of bug, wishing to harm me!” Shi Hao said with an overcast voice.

“What?!” There were immediately people who were stunned, their minds shaken.

Shi Hao exposed them here in response to Cao Yusheng naturally out of security considerations, avoiding some people being driven to desperation, doing things without regard for anything.

Now that he spoke out, he believed that there would definitely be great figures who would be alarmed. The heavenly tribulation disturbance here was extremely great, it would definitely draw some eyes.

As long as there were great figures to stop Self Release Realm people, Shi Hao wouldn’t have to worry anymore, able to calmly deal with the others. The ones who wished to harm him must pay the price!

“Too far! Acting this ruthless, trying to harm Huang while in heavenly tribulation, all of you are completely deranged! Huang fought against the other side, obtaining great fame through killing, yet you all wish to harm him to death?” Cao Yusheng screamed, not scared of making things bigger, doing this precisely to stir up all sides.

“Do you all still have any character?” The Heavenly Horned Ant shouted.

As for the little rabbit, she put in even more effort, continuously scolding them, screaming loudly, startling the experts of all sides.

The others also felt hatred, the masses stirred up, many people felt like this was going too far. Even if there were grudges between the two, they still couldn’t harm him like this.

Now, when they should be unanimously aiming their forces at the other side, there was someone who acted viciously like this, wishing to harm their side’s young supreme being to death, it really was inexcusable.

“You are speaking venomous slander!” Wang Xi’s grandfather shouted, his expression extremely cold. At this point, he wouldn’t admit it even if he died, or else he was finished.

In reality, he already knew that things weren’t good, he might experience a fate even worse than death today.

“Old thing, you secretly tried to harm me, and now, you instead blame me for accusing you wrongly?” Shi Hao shouted coldly, pressing forward.

“Huang, you better not go too far!” In the back, Jin Zhan said with a sunken voice.

“Get lost, stand obediently to the side. If not for your grandfather stopping you, I would have definitely killed you!” Shi Hao said, extremely annoyed. Right now, he hated Wang Family bitterly, not in the mood to listen to Jin Zhan.

Wang Xi wanted to say something, but she was stopped by her grandfather. He secretly told her to immediately leave, that she couldn’t stay here.

“Huang, even if you wish to unjustly accuse others of crime and sin, add filth to my Wang Family, you don’t need to be in this much of a rush, right? You should do it only after being completely certain of victory.” Wang Xi’s grandfather spoke in a cold and deep manner, appearing extremely calm.

This was a half threatening, half justification. At this moment, there was no way he would bow his head and admit, he had to stick it through to its end.

He knew that his clansmen might not step out for him, about to abandon him, but he definitely couldn’t admit it himself.

At the same time, there were powerful clansmen who were secretly displaying methods to bring away that stone case, bring away the Lightning Spirits. It couldn’t be lost outside.

Hong!

Suddenly, an explosion sounded in the void, magical force shaking. A wave of mysterious power severed this place, the magical force trying to bring the stone case away scattered.

It was clear that someone secretly took action, stopping Wang Family’s upper levels.

Right now, unless someone came, completely ripping apart all face to seize the stone case, there was no other way.

If they dared admit that the Lightning Spirits were Wang Family’s, that it came from their clan, then other experts might be drawn out to deliver a heavy blow, make Wang Family nurse a grievance.

“You old bastard, even though half your body is already about to be buried, you still don’t want any face?” Shi Hao said.

He took a large step forward, carrying lightning radiance, shouting, “Get out here right now!” He forced back those two Lightning Spirits, stirring lightning, blasting forward.

“Open the stone case, let the Lightning Spirits go!” Someone transmitted sound.

Wang Xi’s grandfather’s expression paled. He knew that he was abandoned by his clansmen, all hope now turning to dust. However, he still had to do things as he was told.

Chi!

Suddenly, two streaks of radiance rushed into the heavens, one gold, one silver, wishing to run.

“You want to run? It won’t be that easy!”

Shi Hao shouted. He looked at these lightning creatures, feeling that they were extremely powerful. If they were properly raised, they really would be top level battle servants. They were just too formidable.

“Youngster generation, you bully others too intolerably. You clearly drove the strange bugs to rush towards me, trying to frame me. This old one does not agree, I will stake it all against you!” Wang Xi’s grandfather shouted, attacking forward, wishing to stop Shi Hao and give the two Lightning Spirits time to run.

“Old bastard, your shamelessness really has reached a level that is rarely seen.” Shi Hao erupted, a fist smashing out, making the heavens collapse and earth split. Even though he didn’t directly enter the Self Severing Realm, he still wasn’t weaker than the creatures of this level.

Wang Xi’s grandfather was a Self Severing Realm expert. Right now, even though he attacked with all of his strength, he still suffered a great loss, actually blasted by Shi Hao’s fist until he coughed out blood, continuously falling back.

“Younger generation, aren’t you bullying others too much? Where it is possible, you should let others off!” Jin Zhan’s grandfather spoke out. The instant Shi Hao rushed into the heavens, he sent a fist down. Jin Zhan’s grandfather took the opportunity to take action, help Wang Xi’s grandfather.

Shi Hao didn’t see the result of that fist, instead shooting out like lightning, chasing after the two Lightning Spirits, wishing to capture them.

This battle shocked many people.

“Could these be the legendary Lightning Spirits?” In Emperor City, there were finally old monsters who recognized the Lightning Spirits, expressions filled with shock.

Wang Family’s people released a sigh. They knew that there was no way these three divine bugs would return to Wang Family, just the two words Lightning Spirit alone were enough to shock those powerful figures. They believed that Meng Tianzheng would most likely even step out!

Chi!

Shi Hao caught up to one, the other one wanted to run.

As a result, a large hand secretly reached out, wishing to seize it.

Peng! 

Suddenly, a faint light appeared, someone reaching out a finger, blasting aside that large hand. Moreover, there was a voice that sounded.

“Since these divine bugs came after Huang, appearing because of his tribulation, then he ought to be the one to subdue them.” A great figure came to this decision.

The one who secretly wished to fight over the Lighting Spirits sighed, having no choice but to withdraw.

“Ya, it moved!” Right at this time, the Lunar Jade Rabbit was shocked. It was because among the things Shi Hao entrusted to them, there was one cocoon that moved.

Moreover, it quickly broke out, golden multicolored light blooming. A creature rushed out from the cocoon!

The Emperor Butterfly appeared in the world, actually waking at this time.

A single sleep lasted many years. It previously obtained great natural luck, absorbing the essence of an ancient Emperor Butterfly, so it had always been transforming within its cocoon, waiting to soar into the heavens.

Even Shi Hao didn’t expect it to revive today.

“Hand it over to me.” The Emperor Butterfly transmitted sound.

Chi!

It was too fast. The Emperor Butterfly spread its wings, shining with incomparable splendor. It was as if a golden sun blossomed here. It spread its golden wings, rushing into the heavens, catching up to the other Lightning Spirit, herding it towards Shi Hao.

Kacha!

That Lightning Spirit attacked, releasing lightning, but with a slight shake of the Emperor Butterfly’s wings, all of the lightning radiance was scattered, unable to injure it.

Everyone was stunned. What kind of heaven-defying creature was this, actually able to fight against the Lightning Spirit?

Shi Hao was stunned. The golden and silver Lightning Spirits were both handed over to it. He wanted to see just how powerful the Emperor Butterfly really was.

The two Lightning Spirits were furious. They were arrogant, had their own dignity, but they were actually looked down upon like this. They all attacked, stirring on lightning.

Hong!

However, something even more terrifying happened. When the Emperor Butterfly flapped its wings, the void collapsed. It was as if seas were roaring, earth quaking, world shaking.

Everyone was horrified. Space was destroyed, continuously extending, simply about to engulf the heavenly dome!

The two Lightning Spirits’ attacks couldn’t harm it!

Chi!

The two Lightning Spirits suddenly accelerated, throwing themselves over fiercely. Both of them opened their mouths, revealing their sharp fangs to bite at the Emperor Butterfly.

That Emperor Butterfly looked gentle, beautiful and perfect, but right now, the divine might it displayed was enough to leave everyone shocked.

Qiang!

A pair of golden wings smashed down ferociously, each striking one Lightning Spirit’s body, releasing a resounding noise. They were directly crushed beneath!

Everyone was a bit stupefied. A butterfly actually suppressed two Lightning Spirits known for their viciousness, seizing the advantage!

When he saw this scene, Shi Hao completely relaxed, turning around to rush murderously at Wang Xi’s grandfather.

He shouted, “Old thing, it’s time to settle accounts between us. Your Wang Family acts just like this, huh? For the sake of dealing with me, they don’t even want face anymore, daring to do anything!”

“Younger generation, if you continue to slander my Wang Family, don’t blame my clan for being impolite to you!” Wang Xi’s grandfather shouted, showing strength while weak inside.

“Little friend, do not go too far.” Jin Zhan’s grandfather also spoke from not too far out.

“Everything has already been done, yet you all don’t even dare admit it, so Wang Family was nothing more than this. Moreover, you all said that you’ll act impolite, yet you are still talking about what going too far? Today, I will show you impoliteness, take two heads first!” Shi Hao shouted.

He took action, not holding back at all. He stirred on lightning radiance, rushing forward to attack Wang Xi’s grandfather. Towards Self Severing Realm individuals, he now didn’t feel any fear.

Hong!

Just the heavenly tribulation lightning radiance was enough to make Self Severing Realm individuals’ expressions change, let alone his current shocking fighting strength.

“You…” Even though Wang Xi’s grandfather was trying to protect himself, he was still immediately injured, coughing out large mouthfuls of blood.

“Stop!” Jin Zhan’s grandfather shouted, wishing to stop him.

“I still haven’t settled accounts with you, yet you dare interfere. You can just come over too!” Shi Hao released a roar. He brandished his arm, a stellar stream-like streak of lightning radiance rushed over, blasting this person.

“You dare!” In the distance, Jin Zhan was furious. He was enduring this whole time, but now that he saw Shi Hao act like this, even daring to attack Jin Family’s clan elder, he finally erupted.

“Come back!” In the distance, a shout sounded, stopping Jin Zhan!

Hong!

Right at this time, Shi Hao used the greatest strength to fight against the two elders, occupying the advantage, moreover seriously injuring them, making the two elders cough out large amounts of blood.

“Younger generation, you!” Wang Xi’s grandfather shouted angrily.

Dong!

Another fist smashed over, moreover accompanied with lightning radiance. Wang Xi’s grandfather couldn’t hold on anymore, entire body splitting apart like porcelain, body breaking down.

“Tell me right here why you harmed me, and I can leave you with your life!” Shi Hao said.

“You… are dreaming.” Wang Xi’s grandfather didn’t dare betray his family.

“Then I’ll send you on your way.” Shi Hao condensed a magical imprint, looking down from above, pressing downwards. What proof did he need left? It was just that he wanted to humiliate Wang Family.

Since the other party didn’t want to talk, then he would just kill him with full strength.

Pu!

A streak of bloody light erupted. Wang Xi’s grandfather was killed right here, completely exploding.

“Also you, I am too lazy to even ask. I’ll just directly send you on your way!” Shi Hao said to Jin Zhan’s grandfather.

There was no suspense. Within shocking fist radiance, Jin Zhan’s grandfather also released a loud shot, killed, blood dyeing the skies red.

“You dare!” In the distance, Jin Zhan’s eyes were scarlet red, killing intent overflowing.

There was also Wang Xi who stood in the distance, face full of shock.
