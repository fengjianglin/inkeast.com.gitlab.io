Chapter 15 – The Little Guy’s Might

The youngster revealed a startled expression. This was only a child, yet he was able to brush away the arrow with a single hand. The youth couldn’t help but feel that this was a bit inconceivable.

You have to understand that this youth could lift five or six copper cauldrons just by raising his two hands. When he fired these arrows that could split rocks and crack metal, that tyrannical strength was not something an ordinary person could resist. It was enough to kill a Dragon Horned Elephant.

The people from Wolf Village were all shocked. Nobody knew better than they did how scary Bei Feng was, as he was gifted with superior natural talent and an extremely rare divine strength. How could such a young child block Bei Feng’s iron arrow?!

“You are courting death!!” The young Bei Feng lightly scoffed. Beams of light shot out from his eyes as if they were streaks of lightning. He loudly exhaled, drew his bow, and began to continuously shoot out arrows.

Chi, Chi…

Iron arrows pierced through the vast sky with wuwu sounds, as if they were ghastly sounds from the ninth level of hell. The thick arrow shafts formed heart-trembling metallic glints as they rapidly flew over; it was extremely terrifying.

Bei Feng continuously drew his bow until he released eight iron arrows. As they whistled through the air, each and every arrow was extremely terrifying, targeting only the little guy’s vitals!

Like a spiritual ape, Shi Hao dropped down on all fours. With natural movements, he quickly avoided five arrows, and then with a few chops, the remaining three metal arrows were redirected. These three arrows entered a mountain boulder and released loud keng qiang noises. 

This performance completely shocked everyone there!

Even Stone Village’s people were stupefied, as Shi Hao’s display far exceeded their expectations. To do all this at such a young age really was a bit unbelievable.

Bei Feng’s mouth lightly shouted, and he furiously drew his bow. The iron arrows poured down like torrential rain. Thick and long iron arrows slammed down one after another into the mountain forest.

Although Shi Hao was quite young, his speed was incredibly fast. Leaping like a tiger and soaring like a dragon, he evaded the rain of arrows. At the same time, he used both hands to smack at the arrows, knocking the arrow shafts into each other. As the arrows collided with each other, dang dang sounds rang out, causing the arrows in the sky to tremble.

His speed did not resemble that of a child at all, and the gap between them was closed in the blink of an eye. His motions made him look like a young and imposing Golden-Winged Peng.

Dong!

The chaotic rain of arrows became more and more concentrated. The little guy shouted loudly and kicked a thousand jin boulder along the way. With a loud bang, it soared through the air straight towards the cold-faced youth, Bei Feng.

“How can he have this much strength? This child isn’t even four years old yet!”

Everyone was horrified. This truly left them stupefied. Only a few individuals were able to see that when Shi Hao released that tremendous strength, a flash of symbols appeared. It all happened too quickly

The thousand jin rock covered the sky, and then it smashed straight down.

Bei Feng’s eyes suddenly became cold and he stopped releasing arrows. He spun his large bow that had the height of half a person, and with a hong sound, this heavy rock was instantly ruptured. Its fragments flew out in all directions!

Meanwhile, Shi Hao used this chance to rush forward. In an angry voice, he said, “Why are you such a bully? Why do you have to shoot at Uncle Ah’Fu and steal the food we need to survive?”

Bei Feng released a cold snort. He dropped his large bow and then brought out a dark red-colored lance forged from bloodweave iron. With both hands gripping the lance, he used all of his strength to fling it towards the little guy’s chest. 

However, at this moment, Shi Hao’s body was in mid-air and currently facing forward, making it rather difficult for him to dodge. The hearts of the onlookers from Stone Village almost burst out from their chests. They all hated Bei Feng to down to their bones, because his actions were too cruel. 

Qiang!

The ringing sound of metal rang through the air. Shi Hao’s eyes shone as his right hand began to move, forming a beautiful arc and releasing clear splendor. With a qiang sound, the lance was severed into two pieces. Meanwhile, those white and tender little hands weren’t injured in the slightest. 

The result left everyone dumbstruck. The young Shi Hao was facing the tyrannical Bei Feng, yet he was not at a disadvantage at all. After he closed the distance between them, his performance was incredible.

Wu…

Bei Feng furiously flung his broken lance, sending it straight at the little guy’s right eye in a vicious and decisive manner. At such close range, this attack was extremely dangerous.

Shi Hao tilted his head to dodge. While his body was still in midair, his right leg directly spun around before kicking towards the side of Bei Feng’s face, bringing with it a strong gust of wind. Despite his small stature, his body was quite graceful. His movements were flowing naturally like a spiritual swallow that flew glided across the sky.

Peng…

Bei Feng used his left arm to defend himself. A loud and heavy sound could be heard, and the nearby trees were so shaken that leaves flew about chaotically as if the winds of fall had swept through this place. 

“Such strong power!”

The Wolf Villagers revealed a look of disbelief. They were fully aware of the astounding divine strength that Bei Feng possessed. He was still young, and within a circumference of a thousand li, few could match him. However, he currently did not have the advantage.

Shi Hao dropped to the floor. He was also still young, and was much shorter than Bei Feng, but he was not afraid at all. His small face was fuming with anger. His eyes were large and wide as he confronted his opponent. 

Both sides stopped fighting. They were more interested in the confrontation between the formidable youth and the shocking child, waiting to see the results of the fight.

Bei Feng’s face grew gloomy, and a hint of coldness seeped out of his eyes. He was already fourteen years old, but the previous collision against a child who wasn’t even four years of age did not result in his decisive victory. To him, this was a complete humiliation.

Hong!

Bei Feng’s body was tall and slender. He suddenly moved his leg, and it swept out like a metal stump. The terrifying strength released rumbling sound, as if an enormous monster was the one attacking instead. 

Shi Hao fell back. He grew up in the mountain forest, so even though he had never experienced a real battle, he still knew a lot. He avoided this attack, and with a single leap, he backed up several meters. 

However, Bei Feng had killed many fierce beasts, slaughtered unusual birds, and shot formidable humans to death. He was greatly experienced in combat, and as such, he naturally understood how to take the initiative and press his advantage. 

His body leapt high into the air, travelling several meters in an instant. His right leg spun around, and then it hacked down once again like a metal whip. It was extremely powerful, and just the wind it produced made the faces of the crowd feel pain.

There were several ancient trees behind him, so he didn’t have any room for retreat, making it hard for him to avoid this attack. With his back against the enormous tree, his two hands interweaved, and then he forcefully raised them upwards. Several symbols flickered before disappearing. 

Boom!

Like a collision between two huge beasts, rocks and sand were flew everywhere. Dust and smoke rushed to the sky, and leaves instantly withered. Both sides were heavily shaken. When Bei Feng’s legs pressed down, a one meter wide tree trunk behind Shi Hao was directly cut in half.

With a honglong sound, broken pieces of the ancient tree flew high up into the skies. When they fell back into this mountain region, a tremendous noise was released. 

This time, Wolf Village’s people could clearly see that there were symbols flickering within Shi Hao’s palms. This was the power that enabled him to stop the exceptionally talented Bei Feng’s tremendous attack that possessed several thousand jin of weight. 

“Precious artifact, that child holds a rare precious artifact!” They all shouted out.

They refused to believe that a child like Shi Hao was able to come into contact with the powerful bone text profound mysteries. They all believed that this power came from the beast teeth bracelet around his wrist because there was sparkling light flowing about its surface. 

The eyes of Wolf Village’s people all turned red from greed. Even though this wasn’t some clan guarding treasure, it was still quite a good precious artifact. To be able to increase a young child’s strength by that much, it was definitely not ordinary. 

After snapping apart the enormous tree with a single kick, Bei Feng did not stop his forceful approach. Instead, his assault became even more ferocious. Both legs swept out like iron pillars, causing enormous trees to shatter one after another. Broken branches and withered leaves flew about everywhere. 

Shi Hao did not back down and began to retaliate. Even though he was small in stature, every time he jumped, he could reach a height of several meters. When he pounced forward, he looked just like a young Peng spreading its wings!

Hong!

The wind around Bei Feng’s fist flew forward like thunder, and after Shi Hao dodged his strike, it struck the several thousand jin mountain boulder behind him. It directly exploded, sending rocky fragments weighing hundreds to thousands of jin flying in all directions. 

This scene was simply astonishing, as a youth that was just over ten had this kind of fearful strength. All the onlookers gasped.

The little guy was still small, and even though his power could already be considered astonishingly great compared to his peers, his strength was still less than Bei Feng’s in the end. With the assistance of the bone text’s mysterious powers, however, it didn’t seem like he he had too much to be scared about.

The two of them moved extremely quickly, one like a diving falcon and the other like a rising hare. Each strike was extremely terrifying. Fists flew about with thunderous force. Loud rumbling sounds could be heard, and vibrations that caused trees to fall and boulders to split spread everywhere. 

After exchanging ten strikes, Bei Feng’s expression turned morbid. He was a genius blessed with talent from the heavens. He couldn’t believe that he did not even have the upper hand against a child who was not even four yet. How could he endure this?

Peng!

This time, he attacked with full strength. He suddenly crouched down and lowered his head, and behind him, an expanse of arrows tore through the air. Cold light flickered about as they attacked at the little guy’s eyes and face. 

Everyone from Stone Village cried in in alarm; this all happened too suddenly! Immediately after, they all erupted with fury. Bei Feng was just too cruel. He was just dealing with a child, yet he didn’t show any morals and acted so viciously. 

The little guy was startled, but he was not scared. He waved both hands, and mysterious patterns appeared at his fingertip, forming a small crescent moon. He held the moon in the middle of his palms and threw it with all his might. Specks of light spread out, and it broke through every arrow. 

The people of Stone Villages all released a breath of relief. The situation just now truly covered their bodies in cold sweat.

“This youngster is too vicious. He will definitely be a disaster in the future once he grows up!” Shi Feijiao angrily said.

Bei Feng cursed inwardly, he still didn’t manage to hit Shi Hao yet. He fiercely jumped six or seven meters high, and from this advantageous position, he stomped straight towards the little guy with an unblockable force.

Hu!

Shi Hao’s eyes became clear. He took the initiative to meet his opponent. Both his arms spread outwards like an eagle soaring into the sky, displaying a domineering and forceful movement that greatly resembled a Green Scaled Eagle piercing into the clouds. 

Hong!

The crescent moon in the palm of his hands shone once again. It turned into a beam of clear splendor and flew out straight towards Bei Feng. It smashed heavily into his body and created a powerful explosion. 

Pu

This time, Bei Feng spat out blood. His body flew out horizontally, smashing many branches off of the huge trees before dropping onto the floor. The little guy immediately followed in pursuit!
