AST 2156 - Forging Sword, Mo Situ

"Mmmm. I've already waited for so many years. I won't mind waiting for a little longer. Even if I can't get my revenge, I don't want to see anything happen to you." Tantai Lingyan said softly.

"Your man is very powerful, it's just that I'm too young. If I were to be of the same age as them, I'd be able to crush them with a single finger."

"Shameless!" The corners of Tantai Lingyan's lips curled up into a happy smile.

"Your cry earlier was really great. Even my bones had turned weak." Qing Shui smiled and said.

"You aren't allowed to say this..."

Tantai Lingyan had wanted to say that it was her bones which had turned weak. Earlier on, she realized that she wasn't even able to move at all, and her body felt so limp that it was as if she didn't have any bones at all. She felt so good that she was almost on the verge of fainting.

Qing Shui's hands could let even those restless women fall wholeheartedly for him, even if they didn't go all the way. Qing Shui's hands would definitely make them over 100 times more comfortable. Of course, in terms of mental impact, the actual act would still have a stronger impact.

"Alright, alright, I won't say it any more. You felt good, but I still have to hold it in." Qing Shui rested her body slightly, and his lower part bulged up against her hips.

"Qing Shui, give me a little more time. I still can't do it at the moment, but I'll be able to attain a breakthrough soon, and when that time comes, I'll be yours." Tantai Lingyan said apologetically.

Qing Shui smiled, "I'm fine. When I'm with a goddess like you, I'll become hard countless times a day. I can't possibly do it every single time I turn hard. Otherwise, we'll be spending our days only in bed."

"Shameless!" Tantai Lingyan didn't seem to detest hearing Qing Shui's lewd words. Only Qing Shui could say such things before her.

"This isn't shameless. This is a sacred act that all men and women would have to do. It's a legacy, and everyone comes to this world through this. Lingyan, do you think that we should have a son or a daughter in the future?" Qing Shui chuckled.

"Who's going to give birth to your child?" Tantai Lingyan said softly. She thought fondly of this possibility but was unable to describe what she was feeling now.

"You don't like children?" Qing Shui asked.

"I don't know. This matter seems to be very far away from me, and I've never thought about it before."

"Think of a child calling you mother." Qing Shui smiled and said.

Tantai Lingyan shivered as she thought of her younger days and her parents who had doted on her.

"He is someone you gave birth to after ten months of pregnancy and is connected to you by blood. Your blood will flow in his body; he'll be our child, the product of our relationship." Qing Shui hugged her tightly and said.

"We'll have a kid." Tantai Lingyan smiled.

"Where's your weapon? Give it to me. I'll strengthen it for you." Qing Shui thought of the Treasure Basin and that Coldfrost from the Realm of the Violet Jade Immortal. It was the Divine Sword that was most suited to the ice element.

Tantai Lingyan handed her weapon to Qing Shui.

When they returned to the Imperial Cuisine Hall, it was already in the afternoon. Qing Shui led Tantai Lingyan to the second floor; this floor belonged to Qing Shui. The floor above them belonged to Nuo Lan.

"You're back!" Nuo Lan greeted Qing Shui when she saw him.

When they came back earlier, Nuo Lan had gotten to know Tantai Lingyan. Despite so, she was unwilling to disturb Qing Shui since Tantai Lingyan and Qing Shui were husband and wife.

Therefore, she left after greeting them. After all, she had her own things to do. Being in charge of the Divine Palace's intelligence wasn't such a leisurely job.

There were two bedrooms on the second floor; thus, Tantai Lingyan took the other one. She said that she wanted to have some rest.

Qing Shui smiled and nodded, "I know that you're tired. I'll call you when the food is ready."

Tantai Lingyan blushed and nodded. She understood Qing Shui and thus, knew what he was teasing her about.

Qing Shui entered the Realm of the Violet Jade Immortal.

He took out that Coldfrost.

Qing Shui had always felt that the Goddess' Sword, which Tantai Lingyan was using now, wasn't really suited for the Glacier Divine Sword. At the very least, it was lacking some of the additional reinforcements from the Glacier Divine Sword.

He felt that this Coldfrost was the most suitable material for it. However, if the Goddess Divine Set were to be used separately, its ability would be reduced. Therefore, Qing Shui gave up on the idea of changing her sword. After all, changing the sword would produce similar results, or the sword could become even weaker than before. 

However, Qing Shui now planned on merging the Coldfrost into the Goddess' Sword.

He first put the Goddess' Sword into the Treasure Basin to raise its abilities.

The Coldfrost's abilities had been raised previously.

Qing Shui had placed the Goddess' Sword into the Treasure Basin the moment they came back. Although not much time had passed outside, a long time had passed in the Realm of the Violet Jade Immortal.

However, only after the sword had stayed another month or so in the Realm of the Violet Jade Immortal, did Qing Shui took out the sword and started the merging process.

The process was considered quite smooth. After all, the Ancient Art of Forging was quite powerful.

The Goddess' Sword still remained the Goddess' Sword, but it now had the greatest attribute that belonged to the Coldfrost—the prowess of ice element techniques was doubled.

The prowess of the Goddess' Sword wasn't bad to begin with, and now, it was even more suitable for performing the Glacier Divine Sword.

Qing Shui looked at the Hundred Treasure Chest. It still needed another three days before the next tier would be revealed.

This was something which Qing Shui had discovered at a later time. He had no idea when the Hundred Treasure Chest will be activated. Back then, it was a month in the real world. When Qing Shui brought it into the Realm of the Violet Jade Immortal, he discovered that it would still take the same amount of time it would in the outside world. This made Qing Shui feel that this Hundred Treasure Chest wasn't something ordinary. To think that it wouldn't be affected by the time flow of the Realm of the Violet Jade Immortal.

Qing Shui was full of anticipation. The first tier had already brought him so many Divine Square Cauldrons. He wondered if there would still be more Divine Square Cauldrons this time around.

Right now, the thing that Qing Shui liked the most were the Divine Square Cauldrons and the Divine Weapon Crystals. If he could raise the grade of the Divine Weapon Flying Sword, he would be able to take revenge for Tantai Lingyan by himself.

Qing Shui wanted the three days to pass by very quickly.

At the same time, in the Northern King Domain… 

Right now, the ones taking charge of the Demon Gate were the Mo Clan. It had been the Beiwang Clan [2] in the past, but now, the Beiwang Clan had declined. However, there were also people who claimed that the Beiwang Clan was more powerful than they were in the past but had just chosen to keep a low profile.

The wound on Mo Tianzhi's leg had been bandaged. However, unless he could find some miraculous medicinal pills that could allow a person to regrow his bones and flesh, he would have to spend the rest of his life with only one leg.

"Situ, he's our child. Your Complete Annulus Pill has been stored there for so long. Why don't you let Tianzhi have it? Do you want to see him remain a cripple like this?" A beautiful married lady said, feeling pained.

The person who was addressed as Situ was Mo Situ—the current person in charge of Demon Gate and the head of the Mo Clan. He seemed to be a middle-aged man. He nodded, "Yunzhi, go and get it."

"Yes!" A thin old man bowed and took his leave.

There were over ten people present now. After all, this was a huge matter to the Mo Clan since one of their members was forced to chop off his own leg in the Northern King Domain. No matter who was in the right or wrong, this was like a slap in their faces. Therefore, there was no way that this matter could be allowed to rest just like that.

Very soon, that thin old man came, bringing along a box made from jade.

Mo Situ opened it, and a refreshing fragrance leaked out, filling up the room.

Just the smell alone would make one intoxicated. This was Mo Situ's life-saving medicinal pill, but he had to give it up to his son now. This thing was very precious and if it wasn't because his woman had pleaded him to, and also because other people had gotten to know of it, he would choose to kill this son of his. He had a number of children, but only a few of this medicinal pill. There would be one less with each use.

Mo Tianzhi saw killing intent flashed in his father's eyes. He understood that although he was considered one of the most outstanding ones amongst his father's many children, he wasn't the best. If it wasn't because his mother was favored, he probably wouldn't have any chances of getting the medicinal pill.

"Thank you, Father!" Mo Tianzhi said seriously. When he lowered his head, there was a hint of a loss in his gaze, in addition to an indescribable feeling of dejection. Aristocratic clans were heartless. Sometimes, a human might be worse off compared to even an animal.

 

[1] The Mo in Mo Clan translates to the word 'Demon'.

[2] Beiwang translates to 'Northern King'.
