Don't listen to the naysayers gchan7127's Thoughts

Novel Romance Surprise 2019/04/23

Grace Time

Today, I randomly came across this video and it inspired me to write another post. Please watch before you proceed! 

I found this video so motivational. I completely agree with everything Arnold Schwarzenegger said. 

In order to achieve your goals, you need a vision. You need to know exactly what you're working towards, and do something daily to get yourself closer to your goal. 

Everything I have accomplished so far was done because I refused to give up and listen to the naysayers. 

Countless of people would tell me to give up, or just not to bother because the chances of success is so low. 

Perhaps, they didn't think it was possible or worth all the sacrifices. 

But if there's something that you really want to achieve, you need to think of a plan and execute it. 

I think many of us have no trouble coming up with excuses to stop us from moving forward. 

Your reasons may be valid, but it's not going to get you where you want to be. 

You may have to put in consistent effort for years before you see any results. 

In fact, you may never get the results that you desire. 

But you'll be closer with every action that you take.

Don't give up!

What are you going to do today to turn your dreams into reality?
